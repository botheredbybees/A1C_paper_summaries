## FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing - Week 6 Activities

This week's module focuses on puppet therapy, emphasizing the use of puppets in externalizing emotions and enhancing emotional regulation among children. It includes a required reading by Carter (2017) from 'Puppet Play Therapy: A Practical Guidebook,' detailing the symbolic role of puppets in therapeutic settings. The discussion activities involve analyzing photographs of puppets and creating expressive new eyes for sock puppets, with an emphasis on understanding how puppet gaze affects engagement and emotional expression. Students are also encouraged to continue their research on specific events and survivor accounts for Assessment Task 2.
