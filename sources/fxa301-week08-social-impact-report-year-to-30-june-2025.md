## Social Impact Report: Year to 30 June 2025

The Clown Doctors Australia Social Impact Report for the year ending 30 June 2025 highlights the organization's mission of enhancing wellbeing through humor and creative engagement in healthcare settings. The report showcases the activities of both Clown Doctors and Laughter Care Specialists, who provide support to children in hospitals and older adults in aged-care facilities across Australia. Key findings reveal that these programs contribute significantly to emotional safety, moments of authentic human connection, and improved quality of care. Healthcare workers reported increased patient receptivity to treatment, reduced need for chemical interventions, and a more positive overall environment when Clown Doctors are present. Additionally, families and carers expressed greater relief, joy, and emotional resilience due to the programs' uplifting presence. The report underscores the organization's commitment to expanding its reach and deepening its impact through an innovative Social Impact Measurement Framework.
