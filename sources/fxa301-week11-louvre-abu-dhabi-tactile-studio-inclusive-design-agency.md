## Louvre Abu Dhabi - Tactile Studio - Inclusive Design Agency

The document details the Louvre Abu Dhabi's initiative to enhance accessibility for all visitors, particularly those with visual impairments. The project involved designing tactile stations and exhibition panels that cater to diverse audiences through multiple languages (Arabic, French, English) and writing systems including Braille. These stations provide an interactive sensory exploration of over 14 major artworks from different periods and cultures, aiming to bridge geographical and cultural divides. Each station offers a short introduction followed by detailed tactile transcriptions of the art pieces, complemented by audio guides. The production quality is exceptionally high, featuring ultra-high-definition 3D prints with direct tone printing and matte finishes. This inclusive approach not only benefits those with visual impairments but also enriches the experience for all visitors, underscoring the importance of accessibility in creative arts and health.
