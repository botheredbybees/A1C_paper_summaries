## Week 10 Lecture

This week's lecture concludes the content for The Photo Essay course and introduces a Study Week where students will not receive new content but can engage with tutors through Discussion Boards and Zoom sessions. The lecture encourages participants to share valuable resources in the Sharing Resources activity on the Discussion Board. It also suggests continuing engagement from previous weeks, particularly emphasizing the Three Picture Story discussion board as beneficial for the photo essay project. Additional recommendations are provided for further studies within the diploma of creative arts and health, including face-to-face photography classes offered through the art school in Hobart and Inveresk. The lecture concludes with an expression of appreciation for students' engagement and enthusiasm during the semester.
