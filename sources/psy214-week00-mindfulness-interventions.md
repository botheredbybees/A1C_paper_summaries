## Mindfulness Interventions

The article evaluates the growing evidence of mindfulness intervention randomized controlled trials (RCTs) over the past two decades, focusing on their effects and applications in various domains such as physical health, mental health, cognitive and affective outcomes, and interpersonal relationships. It also discusses potential risks associated with these interventions, mechanisms underlying their efficacy, and considerations for dosing or duration to achieve benefits. Mindfulness interventions aim to foster greater attention and awareness of present moment experiences, which have been shown to improve various aspects of well-being and health. The relevance to creative arts and health lies in the application of mindfulness practices to enhance performance and resilience in artistic pursuits while also addressing mental health concerns.
