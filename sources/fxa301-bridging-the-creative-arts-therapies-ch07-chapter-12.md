## Sowing the Seeds of Art Therapy in India: One Seed at a Time

This chapter details the development of art therapy in India, focusing on challenges, cultural perspectives, and collaborative efforts to establish trainings and licensure standards. Authors Sangeeta Prasad, Susan A. Anand, Girija Kaimal, and Sumathi Prathap describe their initiatives, including an inaugural MFA program established in 2021, emphasizing the importance of culturally relevant curriculum development. The chapter highlights issues such as funding limitations, professional recognition, and ethical considerations in providing art therapy to diverse populations.
