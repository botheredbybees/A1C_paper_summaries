## FXA202 Week 9 Web Lecture: The Photo Essay - Storytelling with Image and Text

The document provides feedback on the photo essay assignment (AT2) for FXA202, focusing on storytelling techniques and image formatting. Key aspects include clarity in story tone, effective use of images and captions to progress the narrative, appropriate image size and quality without excessive editing or compression, and proper alignment between background simplicity and how words interact with images. It also discusses caption improvement tips such as reading them aloud for flow and coherence, getting feedback from others, and avoiding overly complex word order. The lecture concludes by stressing the importance of completing consent forms correctly and ensuring content relates to the arts and health theme explored in subsequent tasks.
