## Introduction to the field of creative arts, wellbeing, and health: achievements and current challenges

This chapter introduces the field of creative arts in relation to health and well-being from a global perspective. It traces the historical development of the role of arts and artists in society since classical times, highlighting the recent surge of interest and activity worldwide. The authors discuss the importance of robust evidence for future developments in this field, noting the establishment of specialized journals and academic sources over the last 15 years. They also describe the growth of national and regional arts-for-health organizations that promote networking, training, resource development, advocacy, and lobbying for increased funding and support. Examples include Arts Health Australia and Arts Health Network Canada, showcasing their missions and impacts in primary care, community health, and medical education.
