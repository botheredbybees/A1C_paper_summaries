## Developing Partnerships and Briefs in Arts-Health Projects

The chapter discusses the formation of partnerships between arts organizations and health entities for collaborative projects. It highlights two primary methods of initiating such collaborations: arts organizations independently identifying a health challenge and seeking a partner, or health organizations reaching out to artists for specific interventions. Key aspects include developing a case for support to articulate the proposed intervention's value, identifying strategic health partners aligned with the identified challenges, and preparing briefs for potential projects. The chapter also explores various approaches to tender processes and collaboration models, emphasizing the importance of thorough planning, clear communication, and equitable evaluation methods.
