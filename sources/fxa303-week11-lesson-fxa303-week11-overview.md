## FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing - Week 11

This week's content in FXA303 focuses on the role of arts-based programs in supporting mental health among disadvantaged youth. The reading discusses recent evidence identifying characteristics of such programs that can effectively support young people's mental wellbeing. Students are encouraged to engage with a discussion forum where they must describe a group of disadvantaged young individuals and propose an arts program tailored for their specific needs, considering barriers to participation and potential benefits. References include various scholarly articles emphasizing the therapeutic impact of art, music, dance, and other creative activities on the mental health of vulnerable populations.
