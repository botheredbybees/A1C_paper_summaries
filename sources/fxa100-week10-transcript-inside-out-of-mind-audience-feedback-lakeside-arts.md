## Audience Feedback on 'Inside Out of Mind' Performance

The transcript captures audience reactions to a performance called 'Inside Out of Mind', focusing on the portrayal of dementia care from both staff and patient perspectives. The audience appreciated the realistic depiction of the challenges faced by healthcare providers and patients, highlighting the sensitivity and complexity involved in dementia care. They noted the effectiveness of portraying distressing behaviors caused by noise levels and praised the initiative to engage patients through life story work, emphasizing the importance of listening to those with dementia.
