## FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing - Week 5 Overview

In this week of FXA303 Creative Arts, the focus shifts to Module 2 with an emphasis on practical engagement through play and creativity. The module explores how playful activities such as puppetry and clowning can aid mental wellbeing, particularly in supporting children and adolescents dealing with trauma or strong emotions. Students are provided background information on puppets' historical context and technical aspects of various puppet types, along with methods for using them effectively in educational settings. Learning objectives include describing early puppet forms and mechanisms used to facilitate expression, recognizing signs of vicarious stress related to engaging with others’ traumatic experiences, and strategies for self-management.
