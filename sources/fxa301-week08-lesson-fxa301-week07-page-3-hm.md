## Case Studies of Engagement with Literature and Creative Writing

This lesson explores case studies of shared reading groups for chronic pain management and creative writing for wellbeing. It discusses the benefits of inclusive book clubs where participants read aloud without advance preparation, facilitated by trained leaders. Research shows these activities can improve mental and physical health, particularly in patients with depression or chronic pain. Shared reading is introduced in hospitals to provide emotional expression opportunities. Creative writing therapy enhances psychological well-being through narrative development, reflecting Pennebaker's research on expressive writing as a therapeutic tool. Both methods engage various brain regions and foster social connections.
