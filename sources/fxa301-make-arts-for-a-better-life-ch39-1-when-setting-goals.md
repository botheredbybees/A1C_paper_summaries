## Celebrating and Integrating Arts for Community Development

This chapter discusses the final step in the Make Arts process, which focuses on celebrating and integrating community arts to ensure continuity. It emphasizes the importance of recognizing cultural changes and maintaining fragile artistic traditions while encouraging sustainable development through results-based management (RBM). The authors highlight strategies for embedding the cycle into community life, fostering diversity, and nurturing new artistry that can positively impact society.
