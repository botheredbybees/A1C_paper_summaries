## Indigenous Arts and Mental Wellbeing

The lesson discusses the role of creative arts in enhancing mental wellbeing among indigenous communities that have experienced historical trauma due to colonialism. It highlights successful programs such as 'Young, Strong and Deadly' and 'Rekindling', which use cultural practices like storytelling and dance to strengthen identity and connection with community. The importance of co-designing initiatives with indigenous communities is emphasized for effective support of mental health.
