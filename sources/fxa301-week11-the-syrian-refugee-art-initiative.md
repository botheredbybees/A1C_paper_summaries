## The Syrian Refugee Art Initiative

The Syrian Refugee Art Initiative, spearheaded by Joel Artista and his organization Artolution, aims to provide educational and creative activities for Syrian refugees in Jordan. Since 2013, Artista has been facilitating mural art projects with youth in refugee camps like Za’atari and Azraq, fostering a sense of community and resilience among traumatized populations. By collaborating with local artists such as the Jasmine Necklace collective and Mohammed Hassan Ibrahim, this initiative seeks to empower Syrian refugees through arts-based programming that focuses on healing and social cohesion. The project also involves partnerships with international organizations like the International Rescue Committee (IRC), UNICEF, and the Norwegian Refugee Council to ensure sustainable support for these art initiatives.
