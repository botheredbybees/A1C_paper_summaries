## Arts on prescription: observed changes in anxiety, depression, and well-being across referral cycles

This study evaluates the impact of Arts on Prescription (AoP) interventions as part of social prescribing in primary health care, focusing specifically on anxiety, depression, and well-being improvements. The research is an observational longitudinal study conducted with a community-based AoP intervention in the South West UK from 2017 to 2019, involving 245 participants across two referral cycles. Results show significant positive changes in all three measures after initial and re-referral periods for both the whole sample and a sub-sample identified as having multimorbidity. Importantly, no participant variables accounted for variance in outcomes. The study provides further evidence supporting AoP interventions, highlighting their effectiveness even among those with complex health needs.
