## Internet Science: 6th International Conference, INSCI 2019

The document provides the proceedings of the 6th International Conference on Internet Science (INSCI 2019), which took place in Perpignan, France, from December 2 to 5, 2019. The conference focuses on interdisciplinary studies of the internet as a societal and technological artifact that influences various aspects of human life. It explores social, cognitive, technical, data mining, and well-being dimensions through contributions by academics from diverse fields such as computer science, sociology, anthropology, economics, law, political science, literature, history, and teaching techniques. The proceedings include 30 selected papers organized into thematic chapters addressing the conference’s overarching theme of 'The Internet and Geopolitical Crossroads.' This work is highly relevant to creative arts and health by examining how internet platforms can support social engagement, collective intelligence, and well-being, particularly in relation to global issues such as sustainability, migration, health challenges, and citizen participation.
