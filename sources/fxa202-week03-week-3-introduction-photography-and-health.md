## Week 3 Introduction - Photography and Health

The Week 3 lecture of the course 'FXA202 The Photo Essay: Storytelling with Image and Text' focuses on the interplay between photography, social media usage, mental health, and wellbeing. It highlights how social media can both positively impact connections through visual culture and negatively affect mental health due to excessive use or comparison with curated online personas. Dr Linda Erceg introduces topics such as nomophobia (fear of being without one's phone) and discusses strategies for mitigating the adverse effects of social media, including mindfulness techniques in photography walks. The lecture also touches upon therapeutic photography as a self-initiated activity and its potential benefits compared to professional phototherapy sessions guided by therapists. This content is relevant to creative arts and health studies, emphasizing personal expression through images and their role in mental wellbeing.
