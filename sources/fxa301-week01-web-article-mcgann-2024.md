## Creative Australia: Advancing Arts through Research

The Creative Australia website details its commitment to advancing the arts through rigorous research and analysis. Featured sections include 'Investments and Opportunities', 'First Nations Arts', 'Partnerships & Philanthropy', 'Research', 'Resources', 'News and Events', and 'About Us'. Key research highlights are the reports on increasing recreational reading, diversity data in the creative sector, and a national survey of street dance communities. The website aims to support open discourse about creativity and its societal impact.
