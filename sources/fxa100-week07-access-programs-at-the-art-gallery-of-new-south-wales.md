## Access Programs at the Art Gallery of New South Wales

The document outlines various access programs offered by the Art Gallery of New South Wales to engage diverse audiences, particularly those with disabilities or special needs. Programs include Auslan-interpreted tours for deaf visitors, tactile and sensory tours for individuals who are blind or have low vision, audio-described tours, tailored visits for people with intellectual disabilities, and a structured program specifically designed for dementia patients and their caregivers. The Art and Dementia program offers interactive engagement with artworks, promoting lifelong learning and reducing stigma around dementia. These initiatives foster inclusivity in the arts, enhancing accessibility and creativity across different community groups.
