## Improving Your Composition and Design

This lesson focuses on improving the visual impact of photographs through effective composition and design. It covers nine compositional guidelines: rule of thirds, leading lines, symmetry, pattern and repetition, framing, creating a point of interest, using negative space effectively, experimenting with point of view, and filling the frame. Each guideline is explained with examples from renowned photographers such as Steve McCurry and Bruce Wunderlich.
