## FXA302 Perspectives of the Arts on Health and Wellbeing

This unit examines the role of creative arts in health and wellbeing from historical to contemporary contexts. It is divided into three modules: Module 1 explores artistic contributions to healing and medicine through history; Module 2 focuses on creativity and mental/physical illness in the nineteenth and early twentieth centuries, featuring case studies of notable artists like Beethoven and Van Gogh; Module 3 looks at modern expressions of health themes by twentieth and twenty-first century creators. Assessment includes quizzes, poster presentations, and a final essay integrating research with creative responses to public awareness scenarios.
