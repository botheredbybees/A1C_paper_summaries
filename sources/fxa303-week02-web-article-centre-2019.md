## Centre Stage +20 Report

The Centre Stage +20 Report, published by Youth Theatre Ireland in November 2019, is the third national study of youth theatre activity in Ireland. It builds upon previous research conducted in 1998 and 2009. The report evaluates various aspects of youth theatre including structures, funding, resources, practice, programming, members, leaders, and outcomes of participation. It aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of the current state of youth theatre and identify key priorities for its future development.
