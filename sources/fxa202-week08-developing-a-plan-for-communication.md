## Developing a Plan for Communication

The document discusses the importance of developing a communication plan to effectively convey messages about community issues and organizational accomplishments. It outlines the components of a communication plan, including identifying the purpose, audience, message content, and channels for dissemination. The text emphasizes that effective planning helps in targeting communications accurately, making efforts more efficient and lasting. Steps to develop such plans are provided, stressing the importance of considering resources, obstacles, media connections, and evaluation methods. Communication is described as an ongoing activity crucial at all stages of an organization's development.
