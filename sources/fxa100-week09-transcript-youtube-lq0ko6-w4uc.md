## Benefits and Essence of Dance Therapy

The transcript discusses the benefits and essence of dance therapy, a form of expressive arts therapy where individuals share experiences and emotions through movement. It highlights how dance therapy reaches people on an emotional level that traditional talk therapy might not achieve. Participants are encouraged to express their feelings and memories through movements, which can lead to discussions among the group and foster closer relationships. The therapy is psychotherapeutic and aims to improve physical, mental, social, cognitive, and emotional well-being in participants. Dance therapists like Gina build rapport with residents by understanding their unique needs and backgrounds, often bringing people out of isolation into a supportive environment.
