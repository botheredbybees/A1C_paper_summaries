## Advancing Practice in Arts in Health: Artist Workforce Development and Certification

This document outlines the need for systematic artist workforce development and professional certification in the field of Arts in Health. It argues that as the integration of arts into health and wellbeing support increases, there is a critical demand for qualified artists with clear career pathways and professional recognition. The paper proposes establishing an 'Arts in Health Practitioner-Certified' role through visualizing new roles within the broader ecosystem, defining foundational competencies, and creating career architecture. Benefits to participants include enhanced safety protocols and improved service quality, while employers benefit from standardized hiring criteria. Additionally, this initiative aims to enhance visibility and legitimacy for the field of Arts in Health, providing clear pathways for artists' professional advancement.
