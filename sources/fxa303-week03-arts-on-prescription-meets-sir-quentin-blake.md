## Arts On Prescription Meets Sir Quentin Blake

The document discusses the Arts On Prescription project and its collaboration with Sir Quentin Blake during the unprecedented time of the Covid-19 pandemic. The Lockdown History Project was initiated to encourage local families to document their daily lives through lockdown in a creative and engaging manner. Sir Quentin Blake, known for his illustrations, agreed to contribute by creating some illustrations and designing a unique logo for the project. This initiative aims to create a lasting historical record of how people lived during this challenging period.
