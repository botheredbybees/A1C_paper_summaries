## The Complexities of Implementing an LGBT Inclusion Scheme in Residential Care

The article explores the implementation of an LGBT inclusion scheme, 'Pink Passkey', in two nursing homes in the Netherlands to understand its impact on creating safe and inclusive environments for LGBT residents. Drawing from critical organizational diversity studies, the research highlights that while the Pink Passkey fosters sustained attention to sexual and gender diversity over time, it also faces challenges such as resistance from staff and limitations in inclusivity towards less represented identities like bisexual and transgender individuals. The study reveals positive changes associated with long-term, comprehensive approaches but underscores inherent partiality and continued opposition. Additionally, it addresses the issue of involuntary same-sex expressions due to dementia or Parkinson's disease, advocating for care professionals’ ability to recognize and respond appropriately.
