## Making Art with Communities - A Work Guide

The document 'Making Art with Communities - A Work Guide' outlines the processes and protocols for collaborating between artists and diverse Victorian communities to create impactful arts projects. It underscores the transformative power of art in enhancing community well-being, engagement, and social cohesion, especially among marginalized groups like youth and disadvantaged populations. The guide emphasizes ethical considerations such as confidentiality, accountability, cultural sensitivity, and respect for diversity. It provides practical advice on selecting artists and organizations, developing project concepts, managing budgets, and implementing risk management strategies. Highlighted are the benefits of community-based arts projects in fostering confidence, pride, and a sense of belonging among participants, along with promoting skill development and new career pathways.
