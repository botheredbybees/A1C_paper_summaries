## Creative Arts and Disadvantaged Youth

The lesson discusses the benefits of creative arts programs for disadvantaged youth, including those experiencing homelessness or involved in the justice system. It highlights how such programs can foster mental well-being through self-expression, skill development, and community engagement. Specific examples are given from research studies and case studies involving music, visual arts, and dance therapies. The lesson emphasizes the importance of trauma-informed practices, individual agency, and cultural relevance in these programs.
