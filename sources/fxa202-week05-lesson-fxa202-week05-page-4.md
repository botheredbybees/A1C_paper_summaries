## LAW122 Legal Systems Week 7 Activities

This week's LAW122 lesson focuses on developing visual storytelling skills through a 'Day in Your Life' activity. Students are encouraged to capture elements of character, place, and detail across a single day, selecting three compelling images that form a cohesive narrative. Tips for creating collages using PowerPoint or free collage apps like Canva and Fotor are provided, along with instructions on how to present these stories effectively through linear or non-linear arrangements. Students are also asked to provide captions that enhance the visual content and engage critically in discussions with peers.
