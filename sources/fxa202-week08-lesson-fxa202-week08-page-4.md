## LAW122 Legal Systems Week 8 Activities

Week 8 of the LAW122 Legal Systems course focuses on image editing and photo processing. Students are encouraged to explore various software options for editing images, such as Snapseed and Photoshop. The assignment involves selecting an image, making simple edits like straightening horizons, cropping, adjusting brightness and contrast, and altering color saturation, then sharing the 'before' and 'after' versions on a discussion board along with a commentary explaining the rationale behind each edit. Additionally, students can experiment with more creative approaches such as image compositing or mixed media collages.
