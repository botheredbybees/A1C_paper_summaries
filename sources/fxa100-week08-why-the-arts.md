## Why the Arts?

The text explores the concept of creativity in the context of dementia care, arguing that creativity is not limited to those with exceptional intelligence but can be a characteristic of everyone's life. The authors define creativity as an activity pursued for its own sake, involving a making process and giving pleasure. They emphasize the importance of creativity in providing people with dementia opportunities for self-expression and enhancing their well-being. By engaging in creative activities, individuals can rediscover aspects of their identity and feel more connected to their inner selves. The text also highlights that early experiences with arts can shape perceptions of creativity, making it crucial to approach these activities without restrictive assumptions.
