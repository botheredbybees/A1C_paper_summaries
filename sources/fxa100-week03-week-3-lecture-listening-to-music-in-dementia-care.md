## Week 3 Lecture: Listening to Music in Dementia Care

The lecture focuses on the therapeutic effects and cognitive benefits of listening to music for individuals with dementia. Dr Brendan Lamb discusses internationally recognized programs like 'Music and Memory' and 'The Power of Music and Dementia,' which utilize personalized playlists and live musical performances respectively, highlighting their effectiveness in stimulating memory and emotion. The lecture delves into how music can activate neural pathways and release dopamine, enhancing mood and cognitive engagement. It also explores the concept of rhythmic entrainment, demonstrating its ability to stimulate motor responses and improve overall well-being. Dr Lamb emphasizes that individualized playlists tailored to personal preferences are more effective in providing cognitive stimulation and unlocking memories for dementia patients.
