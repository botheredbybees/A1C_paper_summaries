## I Remember Better When I Paint - Film Clips

The content is a collection of film clips from the documentary titled 'I Remember Better When I Paint'. The documentary uses art as a means to explore and enhance memory function in individuals with Alzheimer's disease. It showcases how engaging in painting activities can lead to improved recall and emotional well-being for patients. Through interviews, artwork examples, and personal stories, the video highlights the transformative impact of artistic expression on cognitive health.
