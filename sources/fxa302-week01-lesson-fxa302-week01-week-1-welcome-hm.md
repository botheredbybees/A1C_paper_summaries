## FXA302 Perspectives of the Arts on Health and Wellbeing - Week 1 Introduction

The lesson introduces the concept that engagement with creative arts can significantly enhance both mental health and physical wellbeing. It highlights two key areas: art therapy for trauma recovery among refugees, veterans, and emergency service workers, and dance programs aiding Parkinson's disease patients. The course covers expressive and reflective characteristics of creativity through case studies, fostering empathetic understanding. Health Humanities is introduced as an interdisciplinary field combining humanities, creative arts, and medicine to provide novel approaches in healthcare education.
