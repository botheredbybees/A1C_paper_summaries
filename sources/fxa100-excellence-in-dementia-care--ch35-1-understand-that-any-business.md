## Building Dementia-friendly Communities

This chapter discusses the development and implementation of dementia-friendly communities with a focus on reducing costs associated with long-term residential care while promoting independence for individuals living with dementia. It emphasizes education, awareness-raising initiatives, and collaboration between local government, emergency services, businesses, voluntary sectors, and community groups to support people with dementia. The text highlights the importance of understanding business needs, legal obligations under the Equalities Act 2010, and involving faith organizations in creating inclusive environments. Additionally, it covers strategies for raising awareness within communities, providing practical suggestions on how neighbors can help those with dementia, addressing cultural barriers like stigma in South Asian communities, and offers guidance for setting up project steering groups to ensure a shared vision of success.
