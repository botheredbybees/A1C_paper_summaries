## FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing - Week 8 Activities

Week 8 of the FXA303 Creative Arts course focuses on social clowning's impact on mental wellbeing. Students are required to read Ford et al.'s (2014) study on the benefits of Clown Doctors for children, families, and medical staff in healthcare settings. The module includes a discussion task where students identify an area in their community lacking social clowning services but needing such intervention for mental health improvement. Additionally, it invites students to consider how they would present as clowns, including costume and persona, if involved in such a project. Supplementary readings provide further context on therapeutic clowning's historical and theoretical foundations.
