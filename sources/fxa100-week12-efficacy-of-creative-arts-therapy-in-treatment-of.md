## Efficacy of Creative Arts Therapy in Treatment of Alzheimer’s Disease and Dementia: A Systematic Literature Review

This systematic literature review evaluates the effectiveness of creative arts therapies, including visual arts, music, storytelling, drama, and dance, for persons with Alzheimer’s disease and dementia. Analyzing 112 articles, the authors find that while these therapies are effective in treating behavioral and emotional challenges associated with the diseases, they do not impact cognitive decline. The review highlights significant challenges such as small sample sizes and difficulty quantifying findings. Despite these limitations, creative arts therapy is shown to provide considerable benefits by enhancing quality of life and offering social support for both patients and their caregivers.
