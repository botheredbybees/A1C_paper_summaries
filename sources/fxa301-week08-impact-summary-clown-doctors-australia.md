## Impact Summary - Clown Doctors Australia

The document presents the annual social impact summary of Clown Doctors Australia for the year ending June 30, 2025. The organization's mission is to enhance well-being through humor and creativity in healthcare settings. Key programs include Clown Doctors and Laughter Care initiatives aimed at improving quality of care, fostering connections, and promoting hope and joy among patients, families, and healthcare workers.

The report highlights the positive outcomes from their Social Impact Measurement (SIM) Framework, which evaluates the impact across four domains: connection, hope, empowerment, and quality of care. Key findings indicate that Clown Doctors significantly improve patient receptiveness to treatment and reduce reliance on chemical interventions, while Laughter Care Specialists enhance the emotional well-being of aged care residents.

Quantitative data shows over 300,000 people benefited from their programs in hospitals, clinics, and aged-care facilities. Social Return on Investment (SROI) analyses reveal that every dollar invested generates substantial social value, with returns ranging up to $9.90 for specialist clinic interventions.

The organization's commitment to evidence-based practices is reinforced by published research in peer-reviewed journals, emphasizing the transformative role of clown doctors and laughter care specialists in healthcare environments.
