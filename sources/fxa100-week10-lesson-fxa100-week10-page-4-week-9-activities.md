## Activities for Week 10: Dementia and the Arts

This week's module for FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care includes a discussion on how arts can be used in dementia care. Students are required to read articles by Nicci Gerrard and Michelle Douglass discussing the depiction of dementia in the arts, particularly in drama and films like 'Still Alice'. They are then asked to analyze Gjengedal et al.'s study, focusing on concepts such as recognition, touchedness, knowledge, or aesthetic expression. The module also includes references to works by Brooker, Caillois, Dunn, and others that provide insights into dementia care through the arts, emphasizing person-centered approaches and playful engagement.
