## Epilogue: Ruminations on Music Psychology

This chapter presents reflections on the field of music psychology, emphasizing its broad scope and interdisciplinary nature. The authors discuss how the definition and practice of music psychology are influenced by various disciplines such as cognitive neuroscience, anthropology, and philosophy. Key topics include understanding who conducts music psychology research, the interaction between philosophy and music psychology, and the importance of considering a wide range of perspectives in the field. The chapter also highlights the need for researchers to think critically about what constitutes music psychology while embracing interdisciplinary approaches that expand our understanding of human musical behavior.
