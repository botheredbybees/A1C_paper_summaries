## Coping Notes Slides

The document provides an overview of coping mechanisms in the context of stress, self-care, and mindfulness. It introduces the concept of coping from both everyday language and psychological perspectives. The slides outline various coping taxonomies including approach versus avoidance strategies, and problem-focused versus emotion-focused coping methods. Additionally, it discusses overlaps between these categories and emphasizes that there is no one-size-fits-all best way to cope; rather, the effectiveness depends on context and fit. It also distinguishes between coping strategies (specific behaviors or cognitions) and styles (general tendencies towards a particular type of coping). The relevance to creative arts and health lies in understanding how individuals can better manage stress through tailored coping mechanisms.
