## TEDx Talk by John Gerdy on Investment in Arts

In his TEDx talk at West Chester, John Gerdy discusses the value of investing in arts within communities. He outlines four key areas where such investments are crucial: education, community building, economic growth, and public health. For education, he emphasizes that creativity is essential to address complex issues in a fast-paced world, with music and arts as potent tools for fostering creativity. Community building is highlighted through initiatives like 'Keys for the City' in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, which successfully brought people together through freely accessible street pianos. Economically, the arts contribute significantly to local economies by attracting creative individuals and providing direct job opportunities. Public health benefits include therapeutic uses of music and other art forms, enhancing quality of life for seniors. Gerdy encourages advocacy and action from community members who believe in these values.
