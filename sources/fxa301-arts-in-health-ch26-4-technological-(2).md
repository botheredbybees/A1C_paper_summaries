## Understanding the Environment Through PESTLE Analysis

This chapter explores how to conduct a PESTLE analysis in healthcare settings to identify opportunities for arts interventions. It discusses various factors such as political pressures, economic shifts, societal trends, technological advancements, legal requirements, and environmental changes that can influence the development of new health initiatives. The text emphasizes understanding target populations' needs through methods like process mapping, shadowing, and discovery interviews, and applying Kolb’s reflective cycle to guide intervention design.
