## Testing the Implementation of the Veder Contact Method: A Theatre-Based Communication Method in Dementia Care

This study evaluates the implementation of the Veder Contact Method (VCM), a person-centered care approach using theatrical, poetic, and musical communication in dementia care settings. The research was conducted as a quasi-experimental study involving caregivers and residents across experimental and control wards over one year. Caregivers on wards with high implementation scores exhibited improved communicative behavior and positively impacted some aspects of resident well-being, such as positive affect and social relations. However, no significant differences were observed in caregiver attitudes or resident mood compared to the control groups. The study highlights the importance of distinguishing between successful and less successful implementations for understanding key factors influencing method efficacy.
