## Creating Our Future: Spotlight on Health and Wellbeing

The document 'Creating Our Future: Spotlight on Health and Wellbeing' highlights the growing recognition of arts and creativity in promoting health and wellbeing among Australians. It underscores the positive impact of creative activities such as visual arts, music, dance, and drama across various healthcare sectors including primary care, hospital settings, rehabilitation, and general wellbeing enhancement. The report emphasizes that arts interventions can yield significant financial returns on investment and address critical societal challenges like aging, loneliness, chronic conditions, and mental health issues. Evidence from studies and reports indicates substantial benefits in areas such as improved mental well-being, reduced anxiety and depression, increased social inclusion, and trauma recovery. Additionally, it discusses the increase in public support for arts funding aimed at enhancing health outcomes and the pivotal role of arts during crises like the bushfires and the COVID-19 pandemic in maintaining community connections and supporting psychological resilience.
