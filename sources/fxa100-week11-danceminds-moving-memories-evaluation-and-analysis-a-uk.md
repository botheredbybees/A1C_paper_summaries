## Dancemind’s ‘Moving Memories’ Evaluation and analysis; a UK based dance and health project for people living with dementia and their care-staff

This paper discusses a year-long dance and health project named 'Moving Memories' conducted by Dancemind in Northamptonshire, UK, targeting older people with dementia attending Thackley Green Specialist Care Centre. The study evaluates the approaches used for developing, delivering, and assessing the program's impact on participants' well-being and provides insights into the role of dance artists in such projects. Key areas covered include mentoring for care staff, ethnographic evaluation methods, and the project's influence on the health and social interactions of those with dementia and their caregivers. The research emphasizes the significance of movement and dance in fostering person-centered care, reducing challenging behaviors, and enhancing group identity among individuals affected by dementia.
