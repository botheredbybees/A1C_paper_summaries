## The daily digital practice as a form of self-care: Using photography for everyday well-being

This article explores how daily digital practices, such as the 'photo-a-day' initiative, contribute to well-being through self-care and community engagement. The study uses a practice theory framework to analyze photo-a-day activities and their impact on participants' well-being, emphasizing mindfulness, social interaction, and personal reflection. Despite the simplicity of committing to take one photograph daily, the article reveals that this routine offers complex benefits by integrating into other aspects of life, fostering creativity, and enhancing awareness. The research also critiques traditional views of well-being as an individual trait, highlighting its relational nature and dynamic evolution.
