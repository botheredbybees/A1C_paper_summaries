## The Evolution of Healthy Humor: From Clown Care to Independent Healthcare Clowing

This chapter details the origins and development of Healthy Humor, an organization that evolved from the Big Apple Circus' Clown Care Unit. Founders Karen Mittleman, Deborah Pliskin, and Dina Plotkin share their experiences transitioning from circus-funded outreach to establishing Healthy Humor as a standalone entity in 2016. The chapter highlights challenges faced during this transition, such as fundraising, operational independence, and securing partnerships within healthcare institutions. It also discusses the mentorship program implemented by Healthy Humor to train new artists and the organization's efforts to collaborate with Child Life specialists in hospitals.
