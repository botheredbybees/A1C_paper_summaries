## Bridging Arts and Healthcare Communities

This chapter explores the integration of arts into healthcare environments to improve patient experiences and outcomes. The Center for Performing Arts Medicine (CPAM) at Houston Methodist supports various initiatives such as specialized healthcare education, art therapy, and research to harness the therapeutic potential of arts. It highlights how purposeful art-based activities can transform stressful hospital settings into more soothing and healing environments. Key aspects include live music performances, visual arts displays, and participatory art programs designed to meet diverse audience needs and reduce patient anxiety. The chapter also emphasizes the importance of curating appropriate art that is easily consumable regardless of patients' mental states and environmental factors.
