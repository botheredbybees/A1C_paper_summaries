## Theatre of Wandering: Rehearsal and devising notes [Part 2]

This article by David Slater, an associate of Entelechy Arts, details the collaborative process behind the creation of 'Theatre of Wandering', a project commissioned for Coventry UK City of Culture in 2021. The piece focuses on co-production and community engagement to explore themes of care and dementia through theatre-making. Initially rooted in narratives from Japan involving an older man searching for his wife with dementia and a younger individual returning home after years away, the project evolved as it incorporated diverse voices and perspectives from across Coventry, including artists, healthcare providers, people living with dementia, and community members. The inclusive nature of these collaborations necessitated flexible scheduling and various venues to accommodate participants' schedules and health needs. This process facilitated the development of a multifaceted narrative that questioned societal attitudes towards care and difference. Ultimately, 'Theatre of Wandering' succeeded in weaving complex personal stories into a communal performance, emphasizing the interdependence between community members and their shared environments.
