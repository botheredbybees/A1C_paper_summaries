## FXA302 Perspectives of the Arts on Health and Wellbeing - Week 3 Activities

Week 3 of FXA302 focuses on integrating visual arts into health education. Students explore paintings and sculptures by Bonnard, Goya, Rodin, and Dubuffet online. They also read an article evaluating art programs for nursing students and watch a video about Penn State’s use of Impressionist Art in medical studies. Discussions involve analyzing anatomical drawings or public health posters from pre-1600s to reflect on the visual imagery's power. The week includes web lectures, drop-in sessions, and preparation for an assessment quiz covering weeks 1-4.
