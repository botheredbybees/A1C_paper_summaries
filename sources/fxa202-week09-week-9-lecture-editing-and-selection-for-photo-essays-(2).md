## Week 9 Lecture: Editing and Selection for Photo Essays

The document focuses on the importance of journaling as a tool for both creative expression and personal wellbeing. It emphasizes the value of reflective writing, especially when uncensored and experimental, as an aid to mental health and artistic development. The lecture also provides practical tips for improving captions in photo essays by encouraging students to consider how their written content complements and enhances visual storytelling. Students are instructed to post a 'Three Picture Story' on the Discussion Board, sharing three key moments from their developing photo essay along with related captions. This activity aims to facilitate constructive feedback among peers and instructors, thereby enhancing the quality of each student's final submission for Assessment Task 3.
