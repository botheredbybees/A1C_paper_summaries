## Developmental Aspects of Synesthesia Across the Adult Lifespan

This research investigates age-related changes in the consistency of synaesthetic experiences, specifically focusing on grapheme-color synesthesia where letters and digits evoke color perceptions. The study involved over 400 participants with this type of synesthesia. Results indicate a decline in the number of consistent grapheme-to-color associations as individuals age.
