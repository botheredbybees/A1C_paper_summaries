## Week 3 Lecture - Photography and Health

In LAW122's Week 3 lecture on photography and health, Dr. Linda Erceg discusses the impact of social media on mental well-being and introduces therapeutic photography as a form of expression. The lecture explores how photography can both positively influence mental health through mindfulness activities and negatively affect it due to excessive use of social media platforms like Instagram. It highlights issues such as nomophobia and body image pressures illustrated by celebrities' images, while also presenting resources for healthy social media usage. Students are encouraged to engage in a 'Mindfulness Photo Walk' and participate in discussion boards.
