## Artsmark and Sandwell Youth Offending Service Follow-Up

The document discusses the integration of creative arts into the Sandwell Youth Offending Service (SYOS) as part of its Artsmark journey, which began in late 2017. The initiative aimed to provide young people with alternative methods for self-expression compared to traditional approaches, aiming to enhance engagement, raise aspirations, and improve relationships with parents or carers. By implementing creative arts programs such as drama groups and workshops, SYOS observed significant positive impacts on the youth involved, including increased educational re-engagement and personal development. The project's success has led SYOS to become the first Youth Offending Service to receive an Artsmark award. Testimonials from participants highlight how these arts-based interventions have positively changed their lives, offering a sense of purpose and direction, particularly in areas such as performing arts. This initiative underscores the transformative potential of creative arts in addressing mental wellbeing among young offenders.
