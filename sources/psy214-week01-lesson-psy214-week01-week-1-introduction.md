## Introduction to stress and coping

The content introduces the concept of stress as part of a fully online unit designed like an interactive textbook with short videos. It covers definitions and theories related to stress, including stressors, biological responses, and the transactional model of stress and coping. The lesson also includes reflections on personal experiences of stress and its impact, encouraging students to consider both positive and negative aspects of stress.
