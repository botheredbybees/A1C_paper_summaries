## Remembrance

In Emma Barratt's poignant photo essay, 'Remembrance,' the author reflects on life through a series of vignettes that capture both the beauty and struggle of existence. The narrative revolves around themes of memory loss due to illness, the transient nature of human connection, and the resilience found in facing life's challenges alone. Through vivid imagery and metaphorical language, Barratt explores how her world has become an empty stage where she performs for no one but herself. This essay emphasizes the importance of personal remembrance and the persistence of hope even amidst overwhelming circumstances. It highlights the therapeutic aspect of storytelling as a means to reclaim fragmented memories and find purpose in everyday life.
