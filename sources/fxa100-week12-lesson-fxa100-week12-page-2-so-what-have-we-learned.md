## FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care

This lesson discusses the application of various art forms in dementia care. It covers music's role in improving quality of life through custom playlists and rhythmic activities; visual arts' impact on self-expression and cognitive stimulation; drama therapy's ability to provide a voice for those with dementia; dance and movement therapies' neurological benefits beyond physical fitness; and recent initiatives like the Music Champions project and calligraphy therapy research. Each art form is explored in its capacity to enhance emotional, social, and mental health aspects of living with dementia.
