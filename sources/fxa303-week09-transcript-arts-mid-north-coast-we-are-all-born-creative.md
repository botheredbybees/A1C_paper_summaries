## We Are All Born Creative: Art Workshops for Resilience

The transcript captures a project titled 'We Are All Born Creative' led by Linda Kerhoven, featuring Karen Kirkovan's workshops for children at Coomba Park and Coomba Aquatic Club in the Mid North Coast area of New South Wales. The project highlights children engaging in various creative activities such as painting, cutting out materials, playing with parachutes to create patterns, dancing, and making art pieces. Parents and educators emphasize the importance of these sessions for fostering creativity, resilience, and emotional expression among children who have faced numerous challenges including bushfires, flooding, and the pandemic. The workshops encourage a child-led approach that allows kids to explore their imaginations freely without technological distractions.
