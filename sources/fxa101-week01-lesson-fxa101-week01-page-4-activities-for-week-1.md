## Activities for Week 1

The content outlines activities and instructions for the first week of the FXA101 Creativity and Ageing course. Students are introduced to recent research on creativity's positive impact on brain health and ageing, with a focus on an article from The Conversation. A creative introduction exercise is assigned, requiring students to select three objects representing themselves and discuss their significance in an online forum. Additionally, the week includes mandatory web lectures covering course material, assessment tasks, and engagement opportunities via Zoom.
