## Making Space for Your Own Creativity

This chapter emphasizes the importance of personal creativity for individuals working in dementia care. It discusses how engaging in creative activities can help caregivers process emotions, prevent burnout, and enhance their ability to connect empathetically with those they assist. The authors reference studies indicating that creative pursuits like massage, yoga, music, and writing significantly reduce stress among care staff. They also highlight theories suggesting creativity connects people to their unconscious resources, promoting personal growth and emotional well-being. The chapter encourages caregivers to explore various media and offers practical exercises to inspire creativity.
