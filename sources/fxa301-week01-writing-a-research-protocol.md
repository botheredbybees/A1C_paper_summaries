## Writing a Research Protocol

This chapter discusses the importance and structure of research protocols in arts interventions, comparing them to 'show bibles' used in film and television productions. It outlines key components such as rationale, methods, and feasibility for ensuring internal consistency and external validity. Protocols are crucial for ethical approval processes and maintaining transparency across stakeholder groups. The chapter provides a template protocol with detailed sections covering the rationale, design, and ethical considerations necessary for various types of research studies.
