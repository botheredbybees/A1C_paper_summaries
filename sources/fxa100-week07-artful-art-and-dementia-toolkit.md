## Artful: Art and Dementia Toolkit

The Artful: Art and Dementia Toolkit, an initiative by the Museum of Contemporary Art Australia (MCA), is designed to engage individuals with dementia through creative art-making activities. The toolkit offers ten interactive sessions that can be conducted at home or in healthcare settings. Each activity focuses on different aspects of artistic expression such as painting to music, printmaking, and continuous line drawing, inspired by artworks from the MCA Collection. The program emphasizes that no prior experience is required and aims to enhance wellbeing through increased self-confidence, social interaction, memory recall, and motor skill development. Developed based on a successful in-person program since 2015, this digital version offers flexibility for personal use or institutional adoption with multiple licenses available per team location. Research collaboration between the MCA and academic institutions has shown positive impacts on neuroplasticity and wellbeing among participants.
