## A Systematic Review of the Efficacy of Creative Arts Therapies in the Treatment of Adults With PTSD

This systematic review evaluates the efficacy of creative arts therapies, including music therapy, art therapy, dance/movement therapy, and drama therapy, in treating posttraumatic stress disorder (PTSD) among adults. The review identifies a significant gap in research quality; from an initial yield of 1,918 records, only seven studies met the inclusion criteria after rigorous screening. These studies indicated low to very low evidence supporting the efficacy of these therapies, with no available data for dance/movement therapy. The findings underscore the need for higher-quality scientific trials to establish the effectiveness of creative arts therapies in PTSD treatment and aid informed decision-making by both consumers and funders.
