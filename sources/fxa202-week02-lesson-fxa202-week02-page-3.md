## Using Colour Effectively in Photography

The lesson focuses on the effective use of color in photography to convey mood and enhance visual appeal. It introduces three key terms for understanding color: hue (color name), saturation (intensity), and brightness. The text emphasizes considering these aspects before taking photographs rather than solely relying on post-processing. Strategies include adding a pop of color, pairing complementary colors, using analogous color schemes, and breaking established rules to create unique visual effects. Examples from photographers like Steve McCurry and Eric Kim illustrate the principles discussed.
