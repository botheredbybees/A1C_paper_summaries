## Glossary - Choose Art

The document provides a glossary of terms related to accessibility and inclusivity in arts activities, events, and projects. It covers definitions such as access support workers, accessible toilets, alt text for images, assistance animals, and various symbols indicating facilities or services available for people with disabilities. The glossary includes specific details about art form categories, age classifications, contemporary and emerging arts, deaf arts, disability arts, and other related concepts crucial to ensuring inclusive practices in the creative arts industry.
