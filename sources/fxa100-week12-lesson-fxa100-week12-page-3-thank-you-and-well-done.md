## Well Done for Completing This Semester

This week's lesson in FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care acknowledges the completion of the semester content. It thanks students for their participation and encourages them to provide feedback through eValuate surveys. The instructor also expresses gratitude towards the Creative Arts and Health team for their contributions, including Associate Professor Anne-Marie Forbes and Catherine Shearer. Students are invited to support the teaching staff by nominating them or leaving positive comments. References provided discuss various arts-based interventions in dementia care such as music therapy, theatre communication methods, and art therapy. These resources highlight the therapeutic benefits of engaging people with dementia through creative activities.
