## Preparing for Creative Project 1

This lesson introduces the first creative project in the FXA101 course, aimed at exploring personal creativity through familiar media and techniques. The project requires students to create an artefact based on one of three themes: 'Air', 'Cell', or 'Sea'. Additionally, each item used in the making process must be employed in an unconventional way. The lesson outlines criteria for the creative work and provides tips for brainstorming and planning, emphasizing the importance of journaling throughout the process. Students are encouraged to collaborate with peers and seek guidance from tutors during weekly discussions.
