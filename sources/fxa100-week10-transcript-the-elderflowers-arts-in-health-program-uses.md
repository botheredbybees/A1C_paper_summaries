## Elderflowers Arts-in-Health Program Transcript

The transcript captures a session of the Elderflowers arts-in-health program run by Hearts & Minds in Scotland. The initiative uses theatre and playful activities to improve quality of life for elderly people with dementia and their families in hospital settings. Activities include puppetry, storytelling, and interactive sessions designed to stimulate communication and creativity. Participants engage through verbal and non-verbal interactions facilitated by performers known as 'clown doctors'. The program aims to provide a sense of humor and fun amidst challenging medical conditions.
