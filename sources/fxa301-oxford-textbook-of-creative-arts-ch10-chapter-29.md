## The Arts and Older People: A Global Perspective

Chapter 29 discusses the role of participatory arts in the context of global aging trends. It highlights how older adults can be active participants in art to enhance their health, happiness, and social engagement. The chapter emphasizes demographic shifts, particularly in developing countries like India and China, predicting a tripling of the world's population aged 60 or above by 2050. Challenges such as ageist attitudes and marginalization of arts in education systems are discussed alongside potential benefits of participatory arts for health promotion and societal well-being.
