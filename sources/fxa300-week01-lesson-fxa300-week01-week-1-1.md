## Introduction to FXA300 Music, Mind and Body

This module introduces the course 'Music, Mind and Body' by Dr Brendan Lamb. It outlines the learning objectives for the first week, including understanding earworms, empathy in music listening, and Juslin and Västfjäll's BRECVEMA model. The course aims to explore how music impacts the human brain, mood, creativity, and health across various life stages. It also emphasizes developing critical thinking skills through research methods and experimental design in music psychology.
