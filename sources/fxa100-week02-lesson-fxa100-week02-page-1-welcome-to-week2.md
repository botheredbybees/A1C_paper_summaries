## FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care - Week 2 Introduction

In this week's module of the FXA100 course on The Arts and Dementia Care, Dr Brendan Lamb introduces students to the topic 'Music and the Brain'. The lesson outlines key learning outcomes such as understanding how parts of the ear and brain function in hearing, listing common components of music, detailing brain areas involved when listening to music, and reflecting on music's role in pain alleviation and rehabilitative care. A SBS video titled 'Dementia's silver lining: a creative flair' is recommended for viewing to set the context of how arts can transform lives in dementia care settings.
