## The Creative Arts in Dementia Care

This transcript details a documentary called 'I Remember Better When I Paint,' which explores the impact of creative arts on individuals with Alzheimer's disease. Hilda Heer, a renowned artist suffering from Alzheimer's, found renewed connection and identity through painting. Her story inspired her daughter to establish the Hildes Foundation, providing scholarships for students to engage in art therapy with memory-impaired patients. The project showcases how artistic expression can help patients maintain cognitive function and emotional well-being.
