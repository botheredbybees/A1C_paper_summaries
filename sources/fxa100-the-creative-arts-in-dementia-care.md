## Creative Arts in Dementia Care

This excerpt discusses the importance of acknowledging and expressing all emotions in dementia care through creative arts therapies. It highlights how experiencing a range of emotions, from sadness to joy, is crucial for personal growth and emotional well-being. The text references Sebastian Barry's novel 'The Secret Scripture' as an example of how emotions can be complex and profound even in challenging circumstances like dementia. It also emphasizes the role of various art forms—songs, music, creative movement, dance, images, and poetry—to help individuals express their stories and connect emotionally with themselves and others. The chapter suggests that these arts provide a non-judgmental outlet for personal truths and can facilitate healing and contact with one's inner life.
