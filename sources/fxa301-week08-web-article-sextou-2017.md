## Drama Is for Life! Recreational Drama Activities for the Elderly in the UK

This article explores the application of recreational dramatic activities for older adults (aged 55 and above) under the umbrella term Applied Theatre, focusing on its benefits in enhancing health and wellbeing in Western societies. The study highlights that loneliness is a significant issue among the elderly population and aims to address this through drama-based interventions. Data was collected from participants attending drama classes at a community hall in Southeast England using mixed methods including semi-structured interviews and observations. Findings indicate that these activities contribute positively to the happiness, social integration, and improved interaction with others for older adults.
