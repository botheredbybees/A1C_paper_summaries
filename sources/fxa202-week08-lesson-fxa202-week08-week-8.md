## LAW122 Legal Systems - Week 8: Editing and Selecting Images

This lesson in LAW122 focuses on enhancing photo essays through image editing. It covers the importance of digital editing for improving composition and design, exploring various techniques such as collage, mixed media, and digital compositing. Dr Linda Erceg introduces practical activities like a mindfulness photo walk and documenting daily life to inspire students. The lesson also emphasizes the benefits of engaging deeply with images through physical manipulation or software tools. References are provided to books on therapeutic photography for further reading.
