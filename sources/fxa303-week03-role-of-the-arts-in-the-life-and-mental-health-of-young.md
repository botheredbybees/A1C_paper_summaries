## Role of the arts in the life and mental health of young people that participate in artistic organizations in Colombia: a qualitative study

This study investigates the role of artistic engagement in mental health and life experiences among young people participating in Colombian art organizations. The qualitative research methodology includes interviews with youth to understand how arts activities influence their well-being, coping mechanisms, and overall quality of life. Key findings highlight that involvement in artistic communities provides a supportive environment for emotional expression, skill development, and social connection. Participants reported reduced stress levels and improved self-esteem as outcomes of engaging in creative practices. The study also reveals the importance of access to arts education programs within both community organizations and schools. These insights are relevant to the discussion on how similar initiatives can be implemented to benefit adolescents' mental health and healthy brain development in other contexts, such as Australia.
