## Visual Arts to Develop Observational Skills

This lesson explores how visual arts are used in healthcare education to develop observational and communication skills, foster critical thinking, and enhance empathy. It discusses the integration of Arts into health curricula through dedicated intercalated programs and emphasizes the benefits of such training for medical professionals. The methods include Artful Thinking and Visual Thinking Strategies (VTS) to improve visual literacy and analytical skills. These approaches encourage close observation of artworks, group discussions, and critical engagement with cultural perspectives.
