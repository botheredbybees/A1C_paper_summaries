## 7 Art Initiatives That Are Transforming The Lives Of Refugees

This web article by Lauren Parater, an Associate Innovation Engagement Officer at the UNHCR, highlights seven art initiatives that are aiding refugees worldwide. The primary objective of these programs is to provide a platform for self-expression and personal development through various artistic mediums such as photography, painting, and murals. One initiative, 'Dreams of Humanity,' features portraits and photographs by Syrian refugee children in Iraq, aiming to give them the ability to share their stories with the world. Another example is the Za'atari Project, which involves workshops in Za'atari refugee camp where young refugees create public murals and learn about educational topics like hygiene and sanitation issues. These art projects not only serve as a therapeutic tool but also help raise awareness of the struggles faced by refugees. Through these initiatives, refugees are empowered to express their emotions and hopes, contributing positively to both their personal growth and societal understanding.
