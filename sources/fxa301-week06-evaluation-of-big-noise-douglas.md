## Evaluation of Big Noise Douglas

The document 'Evaluation of Big Noise Douglas' presents a comprehensive analysis of the Big Noise programme in Douglas, Scotland, implemented by Sistema Scotland. The study evaluates the programme's structure, delivery, and impact on children aged P1-P6 (Primary School Years) as well as their parents and community. Key findings indicate that despite challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, the program maintained robustness and adaptability in its delivery methods. Children participating in Big Noise reported a strong affinity for music education and experienced improved engagement with learning and emotional wellbeing. The programme also fostered social skills development and healthier behaviors among participants. Notably, the initiative positively influenced parental involvement in their children's schooling and community awareness of the program. Recommendations include strategic improvements to enhance inclusivity and operational adjustments to further support sustainable growth.
