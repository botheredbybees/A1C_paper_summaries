## FXA300 Music, Mind and Body Week 2 Activities

This week's activities in the FXA300 Music, Mind and Body course include recommended readings from Chapter 9 of 'Music in the Human Experience: An Introduction to Music Psychology' by Donald A. Hodges and Gary E. McPherson. The lesson provides links to University of Tasmania resources for reading articles and books. Students are directed to engage with a discussion forum based on UNICEF's article about how music affects an infant's brain, followed by questions that encourage personal reflection and peer interaction regarding the use of music with infants in their communities. Additionally, students are advised to maintain a glossary of terms and take notes for study purposes. The week concludes with a reminder to collaborate with peers for studying and preparing for the first online quiz.
