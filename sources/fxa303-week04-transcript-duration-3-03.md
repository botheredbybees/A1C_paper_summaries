## Art Therapy for Children with Autism Spectrum Disorders

The transcript discusses the 'Images of Self' art therapy program designed for children diagnosed with autism spectrum disorders. The program addresses various issues such as negative self-thoughts, insecurity, depression, anger, fear, difficulty adapting to change, and social interaction problems. Art therapy involves creating art with specific materials to facilitate communication, skill development, emotional awareness, and understanding of autism symptoms. Therapists guide children through the process, providing support for emotional expression and insight into personal strengths and weaknesses. The program aims to improve flexibility and social behavior in participants.
