## Creative Writing Workshop Experiences in Jurby, Isle of Man

This transcript details the experiences and impact of a creative writing workshop facilitated by Janet Leigh in Jurby, Isle of Man. The workshop is funded by the Isle of Man Arts Council and focuses on well-being through creative expression. Participants describe it as therapeutic, inspiring personal growth, and fostering community bonds. The facilitator emphasizes confidentiality and support within the group setting, encouraging self-expression and connection among participants from various backgrounds.
