## Statistical analysis of educational outcomes among Big Noise Raploch participants

This report presents the statistical analysis of educational outcomes for participants in Sistema Scotland's Big Noise programme in Raploch. The study highlights that almost all Big Noise participants (98%) achieved a positive post-school destination compared to 84% of non-participants from similar socio-demographic backgrounds. Although the mean educational attainment tariff score was lower among Big Noise participants, adjusted analysis suggests a positive impact on attainment scores despite not being statistically significant. The findings indicate that Big Noise participation is associated with higher employment rates and reduced unemployment post-school. This evaluation underscores the programme's potential to promote education, well-being, healthy behaviors, and positive choices for young people from disadvantaged backgrounds.
