## Contracting Artists for Health Projects

This chapter discusses the importance of proper contracting when artists or organizations are selected to work in healthcare settings. It emphasizes the need for tailored contracts that cover various aspects such as project scope, payment terms, timeline, and responsibilities. The chapter also addresses issues related to ethical guidelines, health and safety regulations, intellectual property rights, and ownership of project ideas. Additionally, it covers preparatory measures like induction days, preparation booklets, shadowing sessions, and the role of supervisors. It highlights the benefits of training programs for developing future artists and provides guidance on monitoring, evaluating, and funding arts in healthcare projects.
