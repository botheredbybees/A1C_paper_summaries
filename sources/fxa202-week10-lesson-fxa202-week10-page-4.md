## Completing Your Photo Essay

This LAW122 legal systems course module provides guidance on completing a photo essay. It emphasizes the importance of dispassionate engagement and clarity in editing. The text explains formatting requirements for the photo essay including page count, image sizes, and caption placement. Tips are given on how to prevent image compression while working on the project and instructions are provided for saving files as PDFs. Inspirational examples from other students' work are included.
