## Impact of dance interventions on the symptoms of dementia: A mixed-methods systematic review

This systematic review investigates the impact of dance interventions on symptoms associated with Alzheimer’s disease and related dementias (ADRD) among individuals in residential care settings. The authors conducted a comprehensive search across seven databases to identify relevant studies published before June 2022, focusing on quantitative and qualitative research methodologies. They found that only two quantitative and two qualitative studies met their inclusion criteria. These studies indicated that dance interventions can reduce levels of agitation, aggression, and behavioral and psychological symptoms in individuals with ADRD. The review highlights the potential for creative arts-based interventions like dance to enhance emotional expression, improve relationships, and alleviate distressing symptoms in people with dementia. However, the small number of included studies limits definitive conclusions about the broader efficacy and mechanisms of such interventions.
