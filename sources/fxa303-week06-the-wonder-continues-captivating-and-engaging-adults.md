## The Wonder Continues: Captivating and Engaging Adults Through Therapeutic Puppet Play and Puppet Therapy

Joanne F. Vizzini's chapter, 'The Wonder Continues,' explores the therapeutic and educational potential of puppetry in adult settings. Initially focusing on children with learning disabilities, Vizzini expands her practice to address emotional issues among adults through puppet therapy. The author advocates for using puppets as a tool to facilitate dialogue and engagement about sensitive topics such as family conflict, stress management, and cultural differences. She emphasizes the importance of creating culturally sensitive puppet characters that resonate with diverse audiences. Puppetry, according to Vizzini, offers an accessible medium for adults to reconnect with their childlike wonder and participate in therapeutic activities alongside children. The chapter highlights the effectiveness of puppet therapy in various community settings including churches, schools, and prisons.
