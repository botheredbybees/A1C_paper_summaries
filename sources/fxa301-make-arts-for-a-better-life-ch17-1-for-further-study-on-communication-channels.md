## Designing Creative Activities to Enhance Community Well-being and Identity

This chapter from the book 'Make Arts for a Better Life: A Guide for Working with Communities' discusses how to design creative activities that foster community well-being, identity, and sustainability through artistic programs. It outlines steps to consider when planning such activities, including identifying opportunities and barriers, engaging participants, and leveraging scientific rigor. The authors also provide examples of sparking activities like commissioning, workshops, and mentoring, detailing their potential impact on health, well-being, and human rights.
