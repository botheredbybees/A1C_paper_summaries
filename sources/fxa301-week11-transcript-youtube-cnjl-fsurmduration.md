## Immersion, Inclusion, and Music Technologies in Virtual Reality

This YouTube video transcript discusses a project funded by the Arts and Humanities Research Council focusing on immersion, inclusion, and music technologies, particularly virtual reality. The project aims to explore how new and emerging technologies like VR impact music creation and audience experiences. It emphasizes the potential for extending immersive experiences in an inclusive manner, contrary to the usual isolating nature of virtual environments. Collaboration with Music NI, a charity supporting disabled musicians, showcases how these individuals interact within the innovative medium, fostering a more accessible and diverse musical environment.
