## The Role of the Arts in Healthcare: Transforming Lives, Creating Community

The Laurie M. Tisch Illumination Fund's 'Arts in Health Initiative' Progress Report from 2018 to 2024 details the foundation’s mission and initiatives aimed at fostering healthy, vibrant communities through arts integration into healthcare settings. The report highlights research findings indicating that over 80% of Americans believe the arts can help address mental health stigma, trauma recovery, and coping with aging-related diseases such as Alzheimer's and Parkinson's disease. Laurie M. Tisch, President of the Illumination Fund, underscores her family’s longstanding commitment to improving access and opportunity for all New Yorkers through equitable philanthropy. The report documents the foundation’s support for approximately 15 organizations that use various art forms—visual arts, theater, dance, music—to improve mental health outcomes and quality of life for those with age-related diseases. This initiative reflects a broader recognition within creative arts and health sectors of the intrinsic value of arts participation in promoting social cohesion and community well-being.
