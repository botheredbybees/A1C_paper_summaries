## Transcript: Yale Medicine Child Study Center Autism Treatment

The transcript discusses the treatment of a child with autism named Victoria at the Yale Medicine Child Study Center. Pam Ventola, a Clinical Psychologist and Assistant Professor, describes pivotal response treatment (PRT) as an individualized behavioral therapy aimed at enhancing language development and social interaction skills in children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). The method involves setting clear goals for each child based on their specific needs, using positive reinforcement through activities like blowing bubbles to encourage communication. Victoria's parents were actively involved throughout her treatment, which helped build their confidence in supporting her developmental progress.
