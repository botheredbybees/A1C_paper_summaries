## FXA100 Week 7 Lecture

The document outlines the content and learning outcomes for a lecture in FXA100: The Arts and Dementia Care, focusing on gallery and museum programs designed for people with dementia. It covers various initiatives such as ARTZ (Artists for Alzheimer’s), Meet Me at MoMA, and the National Art Gallery of Australia's programs for individuals with dementia and their carers. Key themes include reminiscence-based benefits, creativity stimulation through art interactions, and practical planning recommendations for conducting successful gallery visits. The lecture emphasizes the importance of accessibility, tailored experiences, and fostering emotional engagement to enhance independence and quality of life for those living with dementia.
