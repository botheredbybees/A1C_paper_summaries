## Community Services – The Music Therapy Center of California

The document provides an overview of the services offered by The Music Therapy Center of California, focusing on individual and group music therapy sessions for various needs such as dementia, autism, language delays, and other developmental challenges. Individual sessions are customized to meet specific emotional, social, communication, and development needs in a home environment using neurologic music therapy techniques. Group sessions aim at addressing health and wellness, sensorimotor skills, cognitive coherence, and appropriate social interaction through cost-effective means. Specialized programs like Jam Sessions for teens with special needs and free Music Playgroups for young children emphasize peer engagement and creativity to enhance communication and social skills. The document highlights the importance of music therapy in enhancing mood, attention, learning, and interaction, suggesting a positive impact on mental wellbeing.
