## Research-Informed Approaches to Visual Arts Programmes: Dementia & Imagination

The 'Research-Informed Approaches to Visual Arts Programmes' handbook by the Dementia & Imagination project provides guidelines and recommendations for artists working with individuals affected by dementia. The handbook aims not to prescribe specific exercises but rather offers foundational principles derived from extensive research conducted across North Wales, Derbyshire, and Newcastle in the UK. Key components include an introduction of the project's objectives, such as creating dementia-friendly communities through visual arts interventions, and practical advice for artists on delivering effective programs. Central to the handbook are guiding principles that emphasize improving participants' wellbeing by enhancing engagement, social connections, and quality of life while also educating staff and carers about living with dementia. The document underscores the importance of understanding dementia, personal resilience, multi-sensory stimulation, and imagination in arts-based activities for individuals with dementia.
