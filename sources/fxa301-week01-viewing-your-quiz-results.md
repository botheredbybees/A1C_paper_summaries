## Viewing Your Quiz Results

The document provides a step-by-step guide for students on how to navigate through MyLO, the University of Tasmania's learning management system, to view their quiz results. The process includes accessing unit materials from the home page and using specific navigation tools within Assessments to reach Quiz List pages where individual attempt scores can be reviewed if released by the lecturer.
