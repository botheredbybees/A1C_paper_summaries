## Creative Arts, Mental Health and Trauma

This week's lesson in FXA301 Arts in the Community explores the therapeutic use of creative arts for individuals with mental health disorders or PTSD. It covers characteristics and objectives of such therapies, including developing coping strategies and promoting well-being through engagement with art. The role of neurochemicals released during artistic activities is discussed, along with Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi's concept of 'flow'—a state where an individual is completely absorbed in a task, leading to improved mental health outcomes.
