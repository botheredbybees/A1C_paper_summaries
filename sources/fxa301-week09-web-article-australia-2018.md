## Creating Pathways: Insights on Support for Artists with Disability

The Australia Council report 'Creating Pathways: Insights on Support for Artists with Disability' outlines the findings and insights from research conducted in 2017-18. It emphasizes that every Australian should have equal access to cultural life through art, regardless of their background or circumstances. The document highlights the importance of artists with disabilities as key contributors to Australia's arts scene and acknowledges the unique perspectives they bring. The Council is committed to fostering diversity and addressing barriers in society that affect artistic practice and participation for individuals with disabilities.
