## National Dementia Data Improvement Plan 2023–2034

The National Dementia Data Improvement Plan 2023–2034 aims to enhance data quality and availability for dementia in Australia over a decade. With projections of over one million Australians living with dementia by 2065, the plan addresses critical gaps in understanding through specific goals: robust prevalence and incidence data, regular national monitoring, improved data for priority populations, harmonized data across sources, and broader data linkages. Key achievements include new data linkages (e.g., ADNeT-AIHW linkage), development of analysis methods, and baseline data from surveys like the Dementia Awareness Survey and Living with Dementia Survey. The plan's flexible design allows continuous updates based on evolving dementia data infrastructure and policy needs, ensuring effective service provision for those affected by dementia.
