## MAC.ART: McAdam Aged Care Art Recreation Therapy Program

The MAC.ART program is an innovative initiative by Dr. Julie Gross McAdam to enhance the quality of life for individuals living in aged care facilities, particularly those with dementia. Established in 2001, this program uses art and recreation therapy to engage residents creatively and socially. The program draws on participants' personal histories to create large-scale communal artworks that celebrate their stories and contributions. MAC.ART aims to challenge the stereotype of passive and unstimulating aged care environments by fostering vibrant, engaged communities where artists thrive. Dr. McAdam’s work has received international recognition for its impact on dementia-specific programming in both Australia and North America.
