## FXA302 Week 1 Introduction

The document provides an introduction and overview for FXA302, a course on Perspectives of the Arts in Health and Wellbeing. It outlines the structure of the unit, including four pages of learning materials per week, with each page covering different aspects such as module content and interdisciplinary study of health humanities. The text emphasizes the integration of narrative medicine and various artistic disciplines into healthcare studies. Students are encouraged to engage in weekly discussions, which serve as a platform for meeting their tutor and peers. The course aims to broaden perspectives on how arts interact with healthcare, reflecting personal experiences of illness, disability, and wellbeing through creative works such as music, drama, and visual art.
