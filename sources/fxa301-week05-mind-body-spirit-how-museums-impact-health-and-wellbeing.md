## Mind, Body, Spirit: How Museums Impact Health and Wellbeing

The publication 'Mind, Body, Spirit' explores how museums contribute to public health and wellbeing in a changing societal context. It highlights the growing importance of a holistic approach to health that addresses multiple factors such as lifestyle, social networks, and socio-economic conditions. The report details findings from an action research project funded by Arts Council England, demonstrating how museums can engage with health and wellbeing through their collections, programs, and community outreach. Key points include addressing health inequalities, fostering resilience, and promoting positive wellbeing. The document underscores the potential for museums to play a significant role in public health initiatives, advocating for these institutions to integrate health and wellbeing into core activities.
