## Editorial: The State of the Art in Creative Arts Therapies

The article serves as an editorial for a special Research Topic on the state of creative arts therapies, focusing on dance movement therapy (DMT), drama therapy, psychodrama, film therapy, music therapy, and art therapy. It highlights new clinical practices, scientific evidence, methodologies, theoretical concepts, and notions related to these therapeutic modalities. Contributions include studies that explore how specific motor components contribute to the recognition of emotions in DMT; methodological advancements in assessing unscripted movement; reviews on the effectiveness of DMT for various populations including adults with depression and cancer patients; and investigations into drama therapy techniques like the mirror game. The piece underscores the importance and therapeutic impact of creative arts therapies across different clinical settings.
