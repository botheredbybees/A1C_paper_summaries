## FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing - Week 5 Activities

This week's lesson in FXA303 focuses on the mental wellbeing of arts practitioners working in applied therapy and puppetry in healthcare contexts. The required reading is 'Applied Theatre, Puppetry and Emotional Skills in Healthcare: A Cross-Disciplinary Pedagogical Framework' by Sextou et al., which explores a cross-disciplinary framework for teaching emotional skills through theatre and puppetry in healthcare settings. Students are asked to choose a puppet from a TV show or live performance, describe it, and discuss how the puppeteer manipulates it. They must also create their own sock puppet that interacts with the chosen puppet and include an image of it in their discussion post. Additionally, students should prepare for Quiz 1, which closes on March 30th, 2026.
