## LAW122 Legal Systems - Week 9: Editing Writing and Shaping Your Story

This week's lecture in LAW122 focuses on refining writing skills for Assessment Task 3. The content emphasizes the value of journalling for wellbeing and encourages students to reflect on their thoughts without censorship. Dr Linda Erceg introduces tips for editing and selecting captions and images for a photo essay, emphasizing that perfection is not necessary. Resources are provided including an article by Melanie Hudson titled 'Guide to starting a reflective journal for wellbeing' and another from PositivePsychology.com on the benefits of journalling. The lecture also highlights the importance of recognizing the difference between first drafts and final edits, encouraging continuous practice as the best way to improve writing skills.
