## How Do We Realize the Potential of the Arts to Support Health and Wellbeing in the United States?

This report summarizes key learnings from a caucus held prior to the Healing, Bridging, Thriving Summit on Arts and Culture in Communities co-hosted by the White House Domestic Policy Council and the National Endowment for the Arts. The event aimed to explore how arts can contribute to health and wellbeing at both individual and community levels. Leading figures across various sectors discussed the role of policy, public awareness, and practical pathways for integrating arts into clinical and public health initiatives in the United States. Central themes included removing institutional silos, recognizing the interconnectedness of art and healing, and enabling artists and cultural organizations to contribute more effectively to national health goals.
