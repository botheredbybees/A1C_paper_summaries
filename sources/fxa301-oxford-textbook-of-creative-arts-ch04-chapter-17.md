## Arts and Health in Australia

This chapter explores the evolution of arts and health practices in Australia from the 1970s to the present day. Key developments include the establishment of the National Arts and Health Framework (NAHF) in 2013, which aims to strengthen collaboration between the healthcare and cultural sectors. The author discusses various initiatives such as artist residencies in hospitals, art therapy programs, medical illustration advancements, and community-based arts projects that promote social inclusion and mental health. Challenges include securing funding for therapeutic and community-oriented arts programs.
