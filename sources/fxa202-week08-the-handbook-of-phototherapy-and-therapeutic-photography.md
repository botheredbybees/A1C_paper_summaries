## The Handbook of Phototherapy and Therapeutic Photography

Del Loewenthal's comprehensive book, 'The Handbook of Phototherapy and Therapeutic Photography,' provides a detailed examination of the various ways photographs can be used in therapy across different disciplines. Rich with theoretical insights, case studies, and practical exercises, the text explores the transformative potential of photography in enhancing clients' well-being through visual communication. Key themes include ethical considerations, training, research methodologies, and diverse therapeutic approaches such as photo-cards, re-enactment phototherapy, and portraiture. The handbook is highly recommended for psychotherapists, researchers, and practitioners interested in integrating photography into their practice for personal growth and self-discovery.
