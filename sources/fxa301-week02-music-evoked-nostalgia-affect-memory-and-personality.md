## Music-Evoked Nostalgia: Affect, Memory, and Personality

This study explores the psychological impact of music-evoked nostalgia, focusing on its affective and mnemonic components as well as individual differences in experience. Participants listened to randomly selected music excerpts and rated their nostalgic feelings. The strength of nostalgia was linked to autobiographical salience, emotional arousal, familiarity with the song, and the presence of mixed emotions. Individual factors such as personality traits (nostalgia proneness, mood state) also influenced the intensity of nostalgic experiences. Nostalgia is characterized by a blend of joy and sadness and serves to counteract negative affect. The research provides insight into how music can evoke bittersweet emotional responses tied to personal memories, highlighting its relevance in understanding human emotions and their relation to autobiographical memory.
