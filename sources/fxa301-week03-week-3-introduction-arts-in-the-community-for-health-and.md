## Week 3 Introduction: Arts in the Community for Health and Wellbeing

This week's introduction to FXA301 course focuses on the intersection of arts, health, and wellbeing from an international perspective. The lecture discusses how policy frameworks have been established in various countries like the United States, the United Kingdom, and Australia based on evidence supporting the role of arts in enhancing health and wellbeing. Students are provided with learning materials that include summaries of these frameworks and their practical implementations within communities. The course also explores how policies focused on arts, health, and wellbeing interconnect with other government initiatives. Emphasis is placed on understanding the implications of such policies for different community groups.
