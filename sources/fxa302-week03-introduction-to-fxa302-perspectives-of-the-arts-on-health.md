## Introduction to FXA302: Perspectives of the Arts on Health and Wellbeing

The document provides an introduction to the course FXA302, which explores the interdisciplinary study of health humanities. It outlines the structure of weekly learning materials and introduces key concepts such as narrative medicine. The course aims to examine how creative arts engage with healthcare and reflect personal experiences of illness, disability, and wellbeing through music, drama, and visual art. Students are encouraged to participate in discussions to meet their tutors and peers. The content promises a stimulating journey that broadens perspectives on the intersection between health and the arts.
