## Statistical Power Analysis for the Behavioral Sciences by Jacob Cohen

Sylvia Wassertheil's review of Jacob Cohen's 'Statistical Power Analysis for the Behavioral Sciences' highlights the book's significance in addressing a common issue in health research: the selection of appropriate sample sizes. The review underscores how improper sample size determination can lead to inconclusive or excessively costly studies, emphasizing the importance of power analysis in planning efficient and cost-effective research designs. Wassertheil praises Cohen's comprehensive approach to defining effect size and providing tables for determining sample size based on desired power levels, significance criteria, and effect sizes. The review also notes the book's clear explanations and illustrative examples that make it accessible and useful for researchers across various disciplines. Overall, the review commends Cohen's work as a valuable resource for those aiming to enhance their statistical understanding and research methodologies in behavioral sciences and health fields.
