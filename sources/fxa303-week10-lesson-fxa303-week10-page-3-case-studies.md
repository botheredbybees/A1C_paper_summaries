## Arts, Mental Wellbeing and Refugees

This lesson discusses the psychological impact of migration on children and young people from war-torn regions or refugee camps. It highlights the challenges they face in adapting to a new culture and language while dealing with trauma and stress. The content emphasizes that creative arts programs are beneficial for their mental health, helping them build community connections and foster hope for the future. Case studies include projects like 'My Safe Place' at Milpera State School, the 'Journey Stories' initiative in Coffs Harbour, and the hip-hop project 'Akolkol Dastan Gesa'. These programs aim to improve social inclusion and mental health outcomes through arts engagement.
