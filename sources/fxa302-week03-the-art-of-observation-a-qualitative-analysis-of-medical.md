## The Art of Observation: A Qualitative Analysis of Medical Students’ Experiences

The study explores the impact of arts-based interventions on medical students' observation skills, empathy, and well-being through a qualitative analysis of evaluations from students who completed 'The Art of Observation' course at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center. The research reveals three main findings: enhanced observational skills, improved physician socialization, and reduced burnout symptoms. Students developed nuanced understanding and deep reflection on artworks which translated to better clinical observation skills in medicine. Additionally, engaging with art fostered personal growth and empathy, contributing positively to their professional identity formation and mitigating burnout.
