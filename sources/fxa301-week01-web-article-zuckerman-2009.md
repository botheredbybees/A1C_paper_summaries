## The Human Brain: Facts and Information

This article explores the complexities of the human brain, detailing its structure and function. It highlights that despite weighing only three pounds on average, the human brain is more intricate than any other known structure in the universe. The piece explains the anatomy of the brain, including the cerebrum, cerebellum, diencephalon, and brain stem, each responsible for different bodily functions such as movement, sensation, reflexes, and consciousness. Special emphasis is placed on the blood-brain barrier, which serves as a protective layer against harmful substances while allowing essential nutrients to pass through. Additionally, the article addresses common neurological disorders like Alzheimer’s disease and mental illnesses including anxiety, depression, and ADHD.
