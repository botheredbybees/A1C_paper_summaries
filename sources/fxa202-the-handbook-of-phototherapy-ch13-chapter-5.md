## Chapter 5: Phototherapy Using Talking Picture Books

Chapter 5 explores the therapeutic use of photobooks, specifically focusing on 'Talking Picture Books,' a method where clients create books for actual or imagined recipients using personal photographs and reflections. The chapter details two forms of book creation: Talking Picture Books, which involve personal narratives with photos, and storybooks that tell stories through sequences of photographs. Case studies illustrate the process used by prison inmates to write photobooks for their children, emphasizing emotional development and therapeutic benefits. Key points include the ease and popularity of this approach in both individual and group settings, alongside detailed templates and exercises for readers to apply the method.
