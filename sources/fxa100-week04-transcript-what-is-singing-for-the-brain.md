## Transcript: What is Singing for the Brain?

The transcript describes 'Singing for the Brain,' a singing group designed to engage people with dementia and their carers. Sue emphasizes that participation does not require singing ability; it is about wanting to sing and connect socially. The session includes tea, coffee, gentle chair exercises, and singing familiar songs. Participants bond over shared experiences, reducing feelings of isolation and providing emotional support.
