## Week 7: Galleries and Museums

In Week 7 of the FXA100 course on 'The Arts and Dementia Care,' students explore gallery and museum outreach programs designed to benefit individuals with dementia. The week discusses how arts engagement can range from creating art to visiting exhibitions, emphasizing that even those unfamiliar with art can derive enjoyment and cognitive stimulation. Programs like ARTZ (Artists for Alzheimer's) are highlighted as foundational models setting standards in the field. The lesson also delves into practical considerations for carers planning visits, such as reminiscence opportunities, sensory experiences, and ensuring a safe environment.
