## Engaging in Community Music: An Introduction

The book 'Engaging in Community Music: An Introduction' by Lee Higgins and Lee Willingham provides a comprehensive overview of the emerging field of community music. Designed for undergraduate and graduate students, it details processes involved in designing, initiating, executing, and evaluating community music practices. The text includes interviews with 26 practitioners across various countries, case studies from diverse cultural groups, and research on topics such as life-long learning, music in prisons, and business strategies related to community music. Key features include succinct explanations of concepts supported by rich theoretical underpinnings, practical 'how-to' activities, and reflections on best practices within the field. This text is crucial for understanding the role of music in fostering social inclusion, empathy, and well-being among communities.
