## FXA302 Perspectives of the Arts on Health and Wellbeing - Week 2

This week's FXA302 lesson focuses on the application of ancient Greek literature and music to contemporary issues such as PTSD in veterans through tragic plays. The reading assignment includes articles discussing healing through tragedy and recreating sounds from ancient Greek music. Students are asked to analyze a piece of art or music that evokes either relaxing or disturbing emotions, identifying elements contributing to their response and considering environmental influences. Additional resources include references to historical texts on mental health practices in ancient Greece.
