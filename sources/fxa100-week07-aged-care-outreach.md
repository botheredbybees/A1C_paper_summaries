## Aged care outreach

The document 'Aged care outreach' by Museums Victoria provides a detailed overview of reminiscing kits designed for aged care settings. These kits, containing various social history items such as music memorabilia, sports paraphernalia, childhood games, and cultural artifacts like an Italian reminiscing kit, are intended to stimulate memories among elderly individuals. Each kit focuses on specific themes like rock 'n' roll culture, sporting events, childhood activities, dance hall days, domestic life, and transport from the mid-20th century onwards. The kits aim to engage seniors in conversations about their past experiences through interactive and tangible objects. This approach is relevant for enhancing cognitive stimulation and social interaction among dementia patients or elderly individuals in care facilities.
