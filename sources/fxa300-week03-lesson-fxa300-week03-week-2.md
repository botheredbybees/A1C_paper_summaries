## Music and Memory

In this week's lesson on 'Music and Memory', Dr Brendan Lamb introduces the concept of memory types within cognitive psychology. The focus is on distinguishing between explicit and implicit long-term memories through examples related to music listening and practice. Key topics include Baddeley and Hitch’s (1974) working memory model, as well as methodologies like Krumhansl and Kessler's probe tone method for understanding auditory processing in context with Halpern's hierarchy of contour, rhythm, and tonality.
