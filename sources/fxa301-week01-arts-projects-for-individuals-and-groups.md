## Arts Projects for Individuals and Groups

The document 'Arts Projects for Individuals and Groups' outlines funding opportunities provided by Creative Australia, offering grants between $10,000 to $50,000 for a range of activities in various arts practices. The funding aims to support professional skills development, creation of new work, research, collaboration, and community engagement projects both nationally and internationally. Applications must align with specified eligibility criteria and address three assessment criteria including quality, viability, and impact. The document also emphasizes the importance of accessibility, adherence to First Nations cultural protocols, and compliance with child safety frameworks.
