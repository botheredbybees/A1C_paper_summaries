## Activities for Week 9 - FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care

This week's lesson in FXA100 focuses on dramatherapy for dementia care, emphasizing its potential to improve quality of life. The required reading by Jaaniste et al. (2015) discusses the efficacy of drama therapy techniques and their role in enhancing well-being among older adults with dementia. Students are reminded about the quiz deadline and prompted to reflect on stressors common in aging without dementia, considering how dramatherapy can be adapted as a coping mechanism. Additionally, students should start gathering research for Assessment Task 4 (AT4) on creative art forms used in dementia care.
