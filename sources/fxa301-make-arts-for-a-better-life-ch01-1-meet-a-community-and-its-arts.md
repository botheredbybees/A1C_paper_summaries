## Meeting Community Needs Through Ethnomusicology

This chapter discusses the importance of ethnomusicologists in enhancing community life through arts-based initiatives. It outlines a guide with seven steps to help individuals and communities engage with locally accessible arts for practical goals, emphasizing relationship-building and ethical engagement. The text highlights ethnomusicologists' roles in applied work, their responsibility towards contributing to societal betterment, and the interdisciplinary nature of such efforts. It also references global initiatives like the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) that promote arts-based approaches in community development.
