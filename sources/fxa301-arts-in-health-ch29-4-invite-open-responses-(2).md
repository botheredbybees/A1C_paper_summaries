## Research Related Projects

This chapter outlines strategies for conducting research related to arts in health projects. It suggests inviting open responses from stakeholders through consultation forms and emphasizes the importance of collecting data across diverse groups while considering demographic representation. The text advises on analyzing consultation form data using descriptive statistics, thematic analysis, and visual aids like bar charts. Additionally, it highlights the need to review existing literature and case studies on similar arts interventions for evidence-based solutions. Another key strategy is developing a problem tree to identify core issues and their broader implications within the healthcare context. The chapter concludes with an introduction to Step 4, which focuses on generating ideas for arts interventions using design thinking, open innovation, and systems thinking approaches.
