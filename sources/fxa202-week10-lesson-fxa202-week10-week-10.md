## Introduction to Week 10 - LAW122 Legal Systems

The lesson introduces students to the content and activities of Week 10 in the LAW122 course. It includes information on academic skills workshops, assessment items deadlines for tasks AT3 (Photo Essay) and AT4 (Critical Reflection), and resources available such as Studiosity and Student Advisers. The lecture by Dr Linda Erceg covers visual storytelling and tips for completing the Photo Essay. Various models of critical reflection are discussed to assist students in preparing their Critical Reflection task, including Jasper's three-step model, Gibbs' six-step model, Johns' Model, and Maclean's Weather Model. Additionally, it provides guidance on using resources from The Conversation and Positive Psychology blog.
