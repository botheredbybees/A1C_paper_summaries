## Tips for making your PowerPoint presentation

This document provides a concise guide on creating and editing a PowerPoint presentation. It begins with instructions to create a new blank presentation in Microsoft PowerPoint, including adding the presenter's name to the first slide. The guide then explains how to add slides by using the 'New Slide' button under the Home tab, and it offers options for choosing different layouts or inserting blank slides.

For incorporating visual elements like pictures or screenshots, the document suggests two methods: either through the Insert tab selecting Picture from file, or directly dragging files from Finder (Mac) or File Explorer (Windows). It emphasizes the importance of using JPG images that are no larger than 5 MB each. The guide also covers resizing and repositioning photos within the presentation.

Instructions for inserting text boxes are provided, detailing how to place them on a slide and adjust their content. Additionally, there is guidance on deleting both text boxes and images if needed. Finally, the document concludes with instructions on saving the PowerPoint file by clicking the save icon.
