## Your Guide to Celebrating Queer and Gender-Diverse Stories in 2024

The Geelong Arts Centre presents a guide to celebrating queer and gender-diverse stories in 2024, showcasing groundbreaking performances that challenge societal norms and foster inclusivity. The lineup includes Overflow, the first Australian theatre production with an all-trans and gender-diverse cast and creative team, directed by Dino Dimitriadis. Trophy Boys, a satirical comedy and drag extravaganza, critiques power and privilege in high school settings. Fourteen delves into the self-discovery of a gay student at an all-boys Catholic school in 1999. Tash York’s Happy Hour combines drag, music, and comedy for an empowering cabaret performance. These performances highlight the transformative power of art in shaping perceptions and promoting empathy, contributing to a more inclusive society.
