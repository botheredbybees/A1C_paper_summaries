## Participant Stories from Arts Gymnasium at Belgrade Theatre Coventry

This transcript features personal stories shared by participants of the Arts Gymnasium program at the Belgrade Theatre in Coventry. The individuals discuss how engaging with drama and theatre activities has positively impacted their mental health, self-confidence, social life, and overall well-being after retirement. Participants highlight the sense of community, respect among peers from diverse backgrounds, and the stimulating nature of creative arts as key benefits.
