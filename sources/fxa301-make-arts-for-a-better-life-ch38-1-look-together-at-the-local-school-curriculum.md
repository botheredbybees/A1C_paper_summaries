## Integrate Local Arts into Educational Curricula

This chapter discusses integrating local arts into educational curricula, emphasizing the importance of collaboration between teachers, community members, and cultural experts to enhance learning experiences through various art forms. It outlines a method for evaluating the success of such integration in schools, using case studies from Mali where 'storytelling cloths' were used to educate communities about HPV vaccination and cervical cancer prevention. The chapter also highlights how local arts can be effectively utilized across different subjects like cultural studies, reading, writing, science, and math. Evaluation methods are discussed as crucial for ensuring the effectiveness of these educational tools.
