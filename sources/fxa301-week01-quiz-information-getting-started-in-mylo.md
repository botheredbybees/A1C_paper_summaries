## Quiz Information - Getting Started in MyLO

The document provides an interactive guide for students on how to submit quizzes in MyLO, the University of Tasmania's learning management system. The guide demonstrates navigating through a quiz and viewing results within MyLO. Additionally, it explains the use of Respondus Lockdown Browser during assessments when required by instructors, ensuring security and preventing plagiarism. This resource is essential for new students to familiarize themselves with the technical aspects of completing online quizzes in an educational setting.
