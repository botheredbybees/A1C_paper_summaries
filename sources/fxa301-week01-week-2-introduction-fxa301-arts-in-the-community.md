## Week 2 Introduction - FXA301 Arts in the Community

This week's introduction for the course FXA301 Arts in the Community provides an overview of the topics to be covered, including terminology and concepts related to the brain and its role in perceiving and responding to different genres of art. The aim is to deepen understanding of how engagement with arts benefits individual health and community wellbeing. Key areas of focus include specific parts of the human brain relevant to perception and creativity, as well as neurochemicals and hormones associated with these processes. Understanding these basics will aid students in engaging more deeply with research on the topic and completing assessment tasks such as quizzes based on this material.
