## LAW122 Legal Systems: Week 1 Introduction

This is the introduction to the LAW122 unit on visual storytelling through photography. The course coordinator welcomes students and provides an overview of the module structure, emphasizing the importance of understanding the interaction between words and images in photo essays. Students are expected to engage with weekly activities including uploading photos to discussion boards, exploring various examples of photo essays, and participating in Zoom lectures and tutorials. Recommended readings include books on visual arts, therapeutic photography, and phototherapy. A time commitment of four hours per week is suggested.
