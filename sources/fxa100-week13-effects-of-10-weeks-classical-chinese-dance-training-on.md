## Effects of 10 Weeks Classical Chinese Dance Training on Flexibility, Balance and Muscle Strength of the Elderly

This study investigates the effectiveness of Classical Chinese Dance as a sports intervention for enhancing physical capabilities in older adults. The research involved 24 participants divided into experimental and control groups, with the experimental group undergoing a 10-week dance training program twice weekly. Key findings indicated significant improvements in upper-body strength, cardiorespiratory endurance, and agility within the experimental group compared to the control group. Additionally, the study highlighted that Classical Chinese Dance not only enhances physical fitness but also fosters mindfulness, improves cognitive function, and promotes emotional expression through synchronized hand gestures and gazes. The results underscore the unique benefits of this dance form for older adults, emphasizing its potential as an accessible intervention in creative arts and health contexts.
