## Journal and Visual Research Guidelines

The document provides guidelines for maintaining a journal in the context of creative arts and design, emphasizing its role as a tool for professional development. It outlines various categories such as documentation, research, critical thinking, and divergent thinking. The text encourages the inclusion of sketches and drawings to aid memory and understanding during lectures and workshops. Additionally, it stresses the importance of visual research in documenting historical and contemporary influences on one's work, including visits to galleries and exhibitions for direct experience with artwork. Critical analysis of artworks is advocated through a structured approach to questioning techniques, composition, expression, and intent. The document also includes a section on referencing sources using the Harvard style, ensuring academic integrity.
