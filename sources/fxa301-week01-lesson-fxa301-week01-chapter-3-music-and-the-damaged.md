## Week 2 Activities: FXA301 Arts in the Community

The content for this week includes weekly lectures on Tuesday from 4:15pm to 5:15pm Hobart time which can be accessed through a Zoom link. Recordings of these sessions are available via MyLO or Echo 360. Students are assigned the reading 'Aesthetic emotions across arts' by Miu et al., focusing on Introduction and Discussion sections. Week 2 discussions focus on the emotions reported by participants while engaging with art forms such as painting and music, and contextual elements influencing emotional responses related to well-being. Additional readings cover topics like brain functioning in perception and memory processes.
