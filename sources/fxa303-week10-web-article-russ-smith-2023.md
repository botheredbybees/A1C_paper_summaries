## Publications - Indigenous Mental Health and Suicide Prevention Clearinghouse

The Indigenous Mental Health and Suicide Prevention Clearinghouse provides an overview of publications focused on social and emotional wellbeing, mental health, and suicide prevention among Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander communities. From July 2021 to June 2025, the clearinghouse released 32 papers that explore various aspects of these issues from a strengths-based, culturally informed perspective. Key themes include flourishing in later life for First Nations people, best practices for Indigenous evaluations concerning mental health and suicide prevention, cultural grounding and community leadership in preventing suicides among First Nations populations, and the relationship between systemic anti-Indigenous racism and mental health outcomes.
