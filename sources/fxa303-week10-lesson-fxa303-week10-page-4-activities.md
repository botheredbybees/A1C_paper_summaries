## FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing Week 10

This week's content in FXA303 focuses on the role of arts and creative projects in supporting mental wellbeing for migrants. The reading by Martiniello (2022) explores how art can aid migrants in managing cultural adjustment stresses, referencing a co-designed arts program tailored to new arrivals' needs. Additional references highlight various studies that investigate the use of indigenous arts in therapy, cultural strengthening programs for Aboriginal youth, and creative arts projects supporting refugee children's mental health. The module encourages students to discuss culturally appropriate art initiatives for recent migrants in Australia.
