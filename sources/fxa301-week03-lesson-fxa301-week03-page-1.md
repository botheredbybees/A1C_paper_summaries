## Arts in the Community for Health and Wellbeing

This week's lesson explores how engagement with creative arts can contribute to better health outcomes and wellbeing as populations age. It highlights the economic benefits of preventative strategies involving arts-based therapies amidst rising healthcare costs. The course discusses theoretical underpinnings, international policy frameworks, and government policies related to arts for health. Students will learn about Arts on Prescription and its mechanisms, citing research supporting the benefits of creative arts engagement.
