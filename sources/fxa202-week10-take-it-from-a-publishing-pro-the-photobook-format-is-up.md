## Take It From a Publishing Pro: The Photobook Format Is Up for Grabs

Lesley Martin, publisher of Aperture's book program and The PhotoBook Review, discusses the bustling photobook scene during autumn, which includes numerous events like the New York Art Book Fair. Martin has jurying responsibilities for selecting books for awards such as the Paris-Photo/Aperture Photobook Awards. She reflects on her experiences with 800 submissions, highlighting significant themes and trends in contemporary photobooks. The article also touches upon self-publishing practices, desert island recommendations of photobooks, and emerging talents like Rob Hornstra. Furthermore, it addresses Japanese design influences and personal preferences regarding paper quality for book production.
