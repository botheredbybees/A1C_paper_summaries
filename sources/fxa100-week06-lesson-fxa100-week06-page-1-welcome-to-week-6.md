## Week 6 Introduction: The Visual Arts and Dementia Care

In this module of FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care, Dr Brendan Lamb introduces the use of visual arts in dementia care settings. The focus is on how painting and drawing are perceived by the brain, particularly how these functions can be affected by various forms of dementia. The lesson covers the principles of Art Therapy and its applications to improve quality of life for people with dementia through creative engagement. It also highlights the role of technology such as tablet devices in viewing and creating art. Additionally, it discusses the benefits of well-planned visits to galleries and museums and explores how professional artists and therapists facilitate these experiences.
