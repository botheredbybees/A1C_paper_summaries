## Step 2: Choose a Goal in Arts for Social Change

This chapter discusses the importance of community members identifying goals for arts-based social change initiatives, particularly in communities facing multiple issues such as poverty and illiteracy alongside health challenges like AIDS. It emphasizes that addressing one problem often involves tackling related issues within a holistic framework. The authors provide guidance on how to choose specific effects from new artistry, select content accurately, and determine appropriate genres capable of conveying this content effectively while also producing desired impacts in the community.
