## Making Basic Adjustments Tutorial at GCFLearnFree

The document provides a comprehensive tutorial on making basic image editing adjustments using Pixlr Express, a free web-based image editor. It covers three primary techniques: cropping, resizing, and rotating images. The tutorial emphasizes the importance of keeping original image files intact before making any edits to avoid losing crucial details or flexibility in future modifications. For cropping, it explains how removing unwanted parts of an image can improve focus on the main subject. Resizing is discussed with cautionary notes about avoiding upscaling images as this leads to loss of quality and detail. The tutorial also highlights the necessity of maintaining aspect ratios when resizing to prevent distortion. Additionally, it offers guidance on rotating improperly oriented images to correct their alignment. Overall, these fundamental editing skills are crucial for photographers and designers who aim to enhance image quality without compromising on resolution or clarity.
