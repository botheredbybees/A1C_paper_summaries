## Music and Dance for Health and Wellbeing

This lesson explores the use of music and dance in healthcare environments to enhance wellbeing. Case studies include 'Music on the Move' at Launceston General Hospital, which involves live music performances by professional musicians to improve patients', visitors', and staff morale. Another case study focuses on Dance for Parkinson's Disease, highlighting how therapeutic dance can significantly impact mobility, motor control, balance, and quality of life for individuals with Parkinson's disease. The lesson also discusses the development of a video game interface that enables people to continue practicing at home, leveraging motion-tracking technology.
