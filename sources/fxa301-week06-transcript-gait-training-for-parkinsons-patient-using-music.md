## Gait Training for Parkinson's Patient Using Music

This transcript describes a gait training session for a patient with Parkinson's disease using music. The therapist, Anicea Gunlock, demonstrates the difference in walking pattern when using music compared to without it. Initially showing a typical shuffling gate pattern characteristic of Parkinson's patients, she then uses music from a cell phone to help improve the patient's walking cadence and rhythm.
