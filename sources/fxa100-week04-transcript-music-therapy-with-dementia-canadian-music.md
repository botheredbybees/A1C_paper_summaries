## Transcript: Music Therapy with Dementia

A transcript from a session at Evergreen residential nursing home discussing the use of music therapy for individuals with dementia. The discussion centers on achieving two primary goals in music therapy: ensuring a positive and successful experience for participants and encouraging self-expression through musical engagement. The facilitator emphasizes the importance of using meaningful and familiar music to validate and reconnect clients with their memories and experiences, thereby enhancing their overall well-being.
