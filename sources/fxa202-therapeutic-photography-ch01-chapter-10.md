## Adults with Autism: A Case Study

Chapter 10 presents a case study of a therapeutic photography program conducted with five adults on the autism spectrum. The chapter highlights how participants explored their identities, fears, and strategies through photographic exercises over six weeks. Key outcomes include enhanced self-efficacy, improved social interaction skills, and increased support among group members. Identity formation and family systems theory are discussed in relation to individual experiences. Challenges such as isolation and obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD) were addressed through the therapeutic process.
