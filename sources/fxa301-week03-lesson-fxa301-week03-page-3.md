## Public Health Perspective and Government Recognition of Creative Arts

The lesson explores the public health perspective on creative arts and their recognition by governments. It highlights that music therapy and art therapy have been recognized for addressing physical and mental health issues but gained broader acknowledgment in recent years due to advocacy from societies such as the Society for Arts in Healthcare. The United States and the UK serve as models with frameworks like 'Creative Health' in the UK, which emphasize the role of arts in healthcare settings and community well-being. In Australia, the National Arts and Health Framework was endorsed to improve health services and outcomes by drawing on international practices. Social prescribing is also discussed as an effective approach to reducing stress, anxiety, and depression through non-medical referrals.
