## Introduction to Music and the Brain

The lesson introduces the concept of earworms through a TED-Ed video by Elizabeth Hellmuth Margulis, explaining why songs get stuck in our heads. It also discusses personality traits influencing preferences for sad music, including absorption and empathy. Dr. Sandra Garrido explores how individual differences affect responses to sad music. Research by Zachary Wallmark shows that empathetic individuals process unfamiliar disliked music differently in the brain's reward system and executive control areas compared to less empathetic people.
