## FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing - Week 4 Activities

Week 4 of the FXA303 course focuses on creative arts therapies for individuals with autism. The week includes required readings and a video discussing how music therapy can affirm neurodiversity and enhance mental wellbeing. Students are instructed to watch a documentary about a music therapist's work with children on the autism spectrum and participate in a discussion by describing an observed therapeutic session, its activities, the role of the therapist, and potential benefits for the child. The content emphasizes the importance of understanding neurodiversity within arts-based therapies.
