## Websites: Social Networks, Blogs & User-Generated Content

This information sheet discusses copyright issues related to websites, social networks, blogs, and other online services that encourage users to generate their own content. It highlights that users typically retain ownership of the material they post or create on these platforms. Website operators have limited permissions for use unless explicit agreements are in place. The document also addresses the legal implications if user-generated content infringes on existing copyrights and suggests strategies to mitigate risks, such as adopting clear copyright notices and terms of service. Understanding these guidelines is crucial for fostering a healthy online community and avoiding potential legal issues.
