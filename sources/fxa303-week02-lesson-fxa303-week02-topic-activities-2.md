## Week 2 Activities

The Week 2 activities include reading chapters from 'Play for Health Across the Lifespan: Stories from the Seven Ages of Play' by Julia Whitaker and Alison Tonkin (pp. 33-49). The e-tivity features a TED talk by Professor Alison Gopnik on developmental psychology, focusing on infants’ perspectives and cognitive capabilities. Students are instructed to identify a creative arts program for infants or toddlers, analyze its nature, target age group, aims related to theoretical understandings of play and development, trauma-informed practices, and potential impact on brain and social-emotional development.
