## Activities for Week 9: FXA301 Arts in the Community

Week 9 of the FXA301 Arts in the Community course includes activities focused on understanding disabilities and their impact on arts engagement. Students are required to watch a video from Independence Australia about communicating with people who have disabilities and read an article by Ashley Clark discussing disability awareness exercises. The e-tivity involves selecting one of four specified groups, researching the benefits of creative arts for similar populations, and answering questions about planning an arts project tailored to their group's needs. Students are also directed to start initial planning for Assessment Task 4 - Project Proposal, which is due on May 29, 2026.
