## Does Music Change a Child's Brain

In this TEDx San Diego presentation from 2015, John Iversen discusses the impact of music on children's brain development. The speaker introduces a study called the Symphony Study which aims to determine how music influences specific areas of the brain and enhances cognitive functions such as math skills and reading. He also envisions a future where doctors measure brain growth alongside physical growth in young patients, suggesting that musical training could help optimize brain development. Iversen highlights findings from his research indicating that rhythmic training improves consistency in performance over time compared to non-musical activities. The talk concludes with the notion that music education is beneficial not only for aspiring musicians but also for those who may be less inclined towards it.
