## Puppets, Expression and Sustainability

This lesson explores the expressive capabilities of puppets in play-based therapies and their role in creative arts therapy. Puppets serve as tools for exploring creativity, emotional expression, and addressing psychological challenges. They are particularly effective in providing separation from self and reducing feelings of judgement, allowing for externalization of problems. In medical settings, puppets offer a form of diversional therapy that can reduce stress levels and facilitate understanding during procedures like phlebotomy. The use of puppets also supports emotional regulation and agency development in children facing challenging situations such as chronic illness or bereavement. Moreover, puppet-based interactions are beneficial for children with behavioral issues, autism, and attentional problems by providing social modeling opportunities.
