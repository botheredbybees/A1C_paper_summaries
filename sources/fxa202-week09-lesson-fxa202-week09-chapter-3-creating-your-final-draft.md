## Editing Your Photo Essay

This lesson focuses on the editing process of a photo essay, emphasizing the importance of selecting key images to tell a compelling visual story. It covers basic adjustments such as choosing photos, rotating and cropping images, adjusting brightness and contrast, changing sequencing, and refining captions. The concept of 'Three Picture Story' is introduced as an exercise for identifying essential elements in storytelling through photography. Students are encouraged to apply this technique by creating three key pages that represent the beginning, middle, and end or highlight establishing, character, and detail shots.
