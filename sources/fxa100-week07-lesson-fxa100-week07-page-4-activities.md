## Activities for Week 7

The content outlines activities and readings for students to engage with the benefits of visual arts in dementia care. It includes required reading by Shan Crosbie on the Australian Journal of Dementia Care discussing the role of art in a gallery setting for people living with dementia, and sections from Patricia Baines' article 'Nurturing the Heart: Creativity, Art Therapy, and Dementia.' The lesson prompts students to participate in an online discussion reflecting on key concepts related to creativity and dementia care. Additionally, it guides students on initiating their research for a program proposal and video pitch involving creative arts interventions in dementia care.
