## The Value of Playwork for Care Home Residents Living With Dementia: A Pilot Study

This study explores the application of playwork principles in dementia care, aiming to enrich the lives of residents with dementia and support staff delivering care. A five-week pilot program was conducted in a UK care home involving undergraduate playwork students and lecturers alongside resident participants living with moderate to severe dementia. The findings suggest that playwork approaches can be adapted for older adults, encouraging agency and fostering emotional well-being, social interaction, and engagement among residents. However, the study highlights challenges in engaging care staff consistently, which affected the sustainability of positive impacts post-programme. The research underscores the potential of playwork to enhance dementia care through a relational approach that respects and values residents' autonomy.
