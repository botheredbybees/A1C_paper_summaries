## Barrier-free Accessibility at the Kunsthistorisches Museum

The webpage outlines the Kunsthistorisches Museum's commitment to providing barrier-free access for visitors with disabilities and those with reduced mobility. Key features include discounted admission, specialized guided tours designed for visually impaired individuals, wheelchair rental services, accessible elevators, and dedicated parking spaces. The museum also offers tactile reliefs and auditory descriptions during tours to enhance the sensory experience of art appreciation. Various educational programs such as 'Friday is BarriereFREITag' promote inclusive access on a regular basis. This initiative reflects the institution's broader goal in making cultural heritage accessible to all, fostering an inclusive environment that supports diverse visitor needs within the creative arts and health sector.
