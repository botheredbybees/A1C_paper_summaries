## Week 5 Activities - FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing

This document outlines the activities and readings for Week 5 of the FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing module at UTAS. The focus is on mental wellbeing in arts practitioners working in applied therapy and puppetry within healthcare contexts. A key reading by Sextou et al. (2020) discusses a cross-disciplinary pedagogical framework for applying theatre and emotional skills in healthcare settings. This work emphasizes the importance of developing emotional competencies among artists who engage in therapeutic interventions using puppets, highlighting how these practices can benefit both practitioners and patients. The text also includes a discussion section where students are encouraged to reflect on their experiences with puppets encountered through television or live performances, fostering an understanding of puppetry techniques and expressive capabilities. This exploration contributes to broader discussions about the role of creative arts in promoting mental health and well-being.
