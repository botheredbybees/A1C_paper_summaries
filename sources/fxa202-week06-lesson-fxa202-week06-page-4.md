## LAW122 Legal Systems Week 5 Activities

The LAW122 Legal Systems course week five activities focus on enhancing caption writing skills and narrative techniques through personal photography. Students are encouraged to find an image from their personal photo bank as a prompt for free writing exercises such as working with memory, old and new themes, and grabbing quotes. The activity emphasizes the exploration of creative approaches in storytelling by considering elements like character, drama, and temporal setting to affect the pace of action. Tips on transferring photos from mobile devices or cameras to computers are provided, including direct download, cloud storage, and email methods. Instructions for converting HEIC images to JPEGs, resizing images for online use, posting images in discussion boards, and suggestions for editing software are also included.
