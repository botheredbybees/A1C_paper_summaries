## Week 1 Welcome

This document provides an overview of Week 1 for a creativity-focused course, emphasizing the importance of creative journals in documenting thoughts and processes throughout various creative projects. The text outlines different methods of maintaining a journal, both traditionally and electronically, suggesting students experiment to find what works best for them. Additionally, it includes guidelines for Creative Project 1 and encourages active participation in weekly discussions, real-time web conferences, informal drop-in sessions, and the Questions for the Teaching Team forum to enhance learning and address any queries.
