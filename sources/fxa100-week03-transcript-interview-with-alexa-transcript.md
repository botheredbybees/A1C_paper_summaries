## Music Therapy for Dementia Care

The transcript discusses the role and impact of music therapy in caring for individuals with dementia. Registered music therapist Alex Morse shares insights from her extensive experience working across various settings including residential aged care facilities, community groups, and private clients' homes. She highlights how music can help retain personhood and identity despite cognitive decline, serving as a powerful communication tool between patients and their families. The discussion includes the use of personalized music playlists to manage agitation and pain without pharmacological intervention. Additionally, Morse emphasizes the importance of understanding each patient's unique musical journey to engage them effectively.
