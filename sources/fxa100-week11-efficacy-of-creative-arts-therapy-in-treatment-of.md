## Efficacy of Creative Arts Therapy in Treatment of Alzheimer’s Disease and Dementia: A Systematic Literature Review

This systematic literature review evaluates the effectiveness of creative arts therapies for individuals with Alzheimer’s disease and dementia, focusing on behavioral and emotional challenges rather than cognitive decline. The study synthesizes data from 112 articles, highlighting that while there is evidence supporting the use of creative arts therapy to mitigate certain symptoms and improve quality of life, the research faces limitations such as small sample sizes and lack of long-term follow-up studies. Creative arts therapies are suggested to provide mental stimulation, a sense of control, and social support for both patients and caregivers. The review underscores that nonpharmacological interventions can be valuable complements to drug therapy in managing dementia symptoms.
