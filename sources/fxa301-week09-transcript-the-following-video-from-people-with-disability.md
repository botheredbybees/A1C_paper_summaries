## Overview of the Social Model of Disability

The video presents an introduction to the Social Model of Disability, emphasizing a shift in perspective away from the Medical Model. The Social Model views disability as a social construct resulting from barriers and prejudices rather than individual pathology. It encourages attitudinal changes towards inclusivity and accessibility, advocating for societal reforms that remove disabling conditions to ensure equal opportunities.
