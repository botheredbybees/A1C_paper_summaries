## The Power of Music

Oliver Sacks' commentary in Brain explores the unique influence of music on human behavior and consciousness. He questions why an alien species would find humanity's preoccupation with music puzzling, emphasizing music's role beyond mere tonal patterns to evoke profound emotional and motor responses. Sacks discusses how music can induce trance states, enhance collective bonding, and provide therapeutic benefits for individuals with neurological disorders such as Parkinson’s disease and Tourette syndrome. He also highlights the ability of familiar and unfamiliar music to elicit strong emotions in people with dementia, enhancing their cognitive focus temporarily. The article underscores the diverse emotional and motor impacts of music, suggesting it is a fundamental human experience that transcends language and concepts.
