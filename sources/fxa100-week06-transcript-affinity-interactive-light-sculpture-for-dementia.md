## Affinity interactive light sculpture for Dementia Awareness Month

The transcript discusses the unveiling of 'Affinity,' a large-scale interactive light sculpture in Melbourne for Dementia Awareness Month. Created by artists in Sydney inspired by personal experiences with dementia, Affinity aims to raise awareness about brain functions and memory sharing through an engaging art installation. The project highlights that over 340 thousand Australians currently live with dementia, and this number is expected to rise significantly by 2050. Emphasizing the importance of understanding dementia, Affinity encourages conversations about the disease's impact and offers resources for support.
