## Defining Arts in Health

The chapter explores the concept of 'arts in health', a field that lacks a standardized definition due to its diverse range of activities and applications. It discusses various models for categorizing arts in health, such as the Arts and Health Diamond by Macnaughton et al., Ansdell's continua model for music therapy, and MacDonald's Venn diagram approach. The chapter outlines seven key areas of arts in health practice, including healthcare environment design, participatory arts programs, everyday life activities, psychotherapy, technology integration, training, and education. It highlights the interdisciplinary nature of arts in health and its potential to influence individual and community well-being.
