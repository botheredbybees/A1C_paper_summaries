## Youth Survey Report 2024

The 2024 Youth Survey Report by Mission Australia highlights the evolving challenges and opportunities for young Australians aged 15 to 19. The report is based on insights from over 17,000 participants who shared their views on national issues such as cost of living, climate change, violence, safety, crime, and mental health. Cost of living emerged as the top concern, reflecting economic challenges and higher costs affecting young people's quality of life. Climate change remained a significant issue but was second in priority due to immediate financial hardships. Violence and discrimination were highlighted, with high rates of unfair treatment reported among Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander youth and gender-diverse individuals. Mental health continued to be critical, with many facing stress, anxiety, loneliness, and depression. The report underscores the need for greater support and inclusivity to help young people achieve their full potential.
