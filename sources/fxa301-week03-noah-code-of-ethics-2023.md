## NOAH Code of Ethics 2023

The National Organization for Arts in Health (NOAH) has released the updated NOAH Code of Ethics 2023, which aims to guide artists and arts administrators working in healthcare and community settings. The document emphasizes five core ethical principles: Respect for Persons, Do No Harm, Integrity, Quality, and Justice & Equity. It provides detailed guidelines on how these principles should be applied in practice, such as ensuring fully-informed consent, protecting individual dignity and privacy, and promoting cultural respect and safety. This code of ethics is part of NOAH's ongoing efforts to professionalize the field of Arts in Health (AIH), alongside developing standards of care and scope of practice documents. The document underscores the importance of respecting personal autonomy, well-being, and community agency while fostering equitable collaboration and continuous professional development.
