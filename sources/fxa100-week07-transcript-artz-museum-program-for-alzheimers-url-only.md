## ARTZ Museum Program for Alzheimer's

The ARTZ Museum Program focuses on offering specialized visits to museums designed specifically for individuals with Alzheimer’s disease and their caregivers. The program aims to provide a meaningful cultural experience that enhances the quality of life by engaging participants in art-related activities such as guided tours, interactive workshops, and creative expression opportunities. These initiatives are intended to stimulate cognitive function, promote emotional well-being, and foster social connections among those affected by dementia.
