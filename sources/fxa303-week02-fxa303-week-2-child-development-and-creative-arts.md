## FXA303 Week 2: Child Development and Creative Arts

This document introduces foundational concepts in child development from neuroscience and developmental psychology, challenging traditional theories with new data from longitudinal studies and brain imaging. It emphasizes the critical period of a child's first 1000 days for neural network formation, underscoring the importance of early experiences in future learning and well-being. Play is highlighted as essential for developing cognitive skills and emotional expression, while creative arts further enhance these benefits by boosting neural complexity. Additional support through drop-in sessions with tutors is offered to facilitate understanding and engagement with course materials.
