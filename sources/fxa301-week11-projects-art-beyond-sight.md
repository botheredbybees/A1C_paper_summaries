## Projects – Art Beyond Sight

The document 'Projects – Art Beyond Sight' outlines various initiatives and programs by the organization Art Beyond Sight, focusing on inclusivity in the arts for individuals with disabilities. Key among these is the Art and Disability Institute (ADI), which provides professional development opportunities for artists and professionals engaging with disability themes or identifying as having disabilities. ADI aims to remove barriers and enhance accessibility within the contemporary art world through education, research, and advocacy. Additionally, the Lab For Learning (L4L) program offers hands-on arts education aimed at fostering independent living skills and employment readiness in disabled students across New York City. The project also highlights the creation of an online toolkit for job seekers and employers to reduce barriers to careers in the arts for people with disabilities, funded by the National Endowment for the Arts (NEA). Art Beyond Sight's work significantly contributes to broader discussions on accessibility and inclusion within cultural institutions and educational settings.
