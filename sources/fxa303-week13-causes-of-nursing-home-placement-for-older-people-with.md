## Causes of nursing home placement for older people with dementia: a systematic review and meta-analysis

This systematic review and meta-analysis examines the factors contributing to nursing home placement for older adults with dementia, focusing on modifiable risk factors that could help delay institutionalization. The study synthesizes data from controlled comparison studies, including cohort and case-control designs, to identify key factors such as cognitive status, behavioral and psychological symptoms of dementia (BPSD), activities of daily living (ADL) impairments, caregiver burden, and physical health conditions. Poorer cognition and BPSD were consistently associated with increased risk of nursing home admission, while the impact of community support services was unclear. The review suggests that strategies aimed at cognitive enhancement, managing BPSD, educating caregivers, and supporting carers could delay institutionalization for individuals with dementia.
