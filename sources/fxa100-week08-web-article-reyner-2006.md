## Successful Crafts & Game Ideas for Alzheimer's Activities

This brochure from S&amp;S Primelife provides guidance on conducting arts and crafts programs for individuals with Alzheimer's disease. It outlines strategies such as simplicity in activity design, constant reassurance, social interaction enhancement, and the importance of sensory stimulation to improve quality of life. The document emphasizes planning activities that patients can complete successfully, focusing on each individual’s response rather than solely on project completion, and maintaining flexibility in approach due to varying cognitive levels and needs.
