## Activities for Week 3 - FXA301 Arts in the Community

Week 3 of the course includes a reading activity featuring an ABC Radio National Life Matters episode titled 'Creative Ageing', which explores why art matters more as we age. The program is available for listening or download and requires students to note down their observations on the benefits of arts engagement for wellbeing. An upcoming quiz will assess knowledge from weeks 1-4, focusing on multiple-choice and short-answer questions. Students are encouraged to prepare their own set of questions for review and participate in discussions about local arts engagement possibilities to improve health and well-being. References include various studies and policy documents emphasizing the role of art in enhancing mental and physical health.
