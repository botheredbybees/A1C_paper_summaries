## Music Therapy Benefits for Dementia Care

This module explores the benefits of music therapy in dementia care. It discusses how creativity with music can enhance enjoyment and engagement, particularly through improvisation techniques tailored to individuals' needs. Music therapy helps establish non-verbal communication for those suffering from aphasia due to dementia. Additionally, it addresses agitation by reducing behavioral issues without medication. The lesson includes case studies like the MJHS Protocol Trial in New York State that showcase the effectiveness of music-assisted care practices.
