## Autism Spectrum Disorder: The Impact of Stressful and Traumatic Life Events and Implications for Clinical Practice

This paper reviews emerging research on the impact of stressful and traumatic life events on mental health in individuals with autism spectrum disorder (ASD). It highlights high rates of comorbidity between ASD and other psychological disorders, indicating that standard behavioral interventions are insufficient for addressing mental health issues. The author suggests a shift towards trauma-focused assessment strategies and clinical interventions to better understand the nature of childhood stress and trauma and improve mental health outcomes in this population. Understanding these factors is crucial for social workers and clinicians working with individuals on the autism spectrum.
