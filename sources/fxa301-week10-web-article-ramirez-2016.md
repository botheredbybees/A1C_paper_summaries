## A Review of Art Therapy Among Military Service Members and Veterans with Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder

This article reviews the use of art therapy in treating PTSD among military personnel and veterans, emphasizing its effectiveness in addressing avoidance and emotional numbing symptoms not well managed by traditional cognitive-behavioral therapies.
