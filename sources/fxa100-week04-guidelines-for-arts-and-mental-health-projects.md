## Guidelines for Arts and Mental Health Projects

The document 'Guidelines for Arts and Mental Health Projects' compiled by Simone Gillam outlines protocols and best practices for integrating arts into mental health initiatives in regional South Australia. The guidelines aim to foster a collaborative environment where artists and mental health professionals work together to improve social inclusion, cultural expression, and personal growth through creative activities. It emphasizes the importance of creating safe and supportive spaces for participants, ensuring mutual respect between all stakeholders, and setting clear expectations regarding confidentiality and group norms. The document serves as a resource for planning projects that enhance community well-being by promoting engagement in arts-related activities without requiring prior artistic experience or skill.
