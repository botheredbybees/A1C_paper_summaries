## Audience Data and Advocacy Tools - Creative Australia

The Audience Data and Advocacy Tools are interactive resources developed by Creative Australia and Lonergan Research to analyze the results of the 2019 National Arts Participation Survey, 'Creating Our Future'. These tools aim to provide insights into Australians' engagement with arts and their attitudes towards it. The tools consist of a Population Segmentation Model and Behavioral and Attitudinal Index dashboards, intended for use in advocacy, audience development, and strategic planning.
