## Improving Farm-Based Livelihoods: Challenges and Realities

This chapter from 'Make Arts for a Better Life' discusses the challenges faced by community development projects, focusing on the failure of a project aimed at improving farm-based livelihoods in Jharkhand, Orissa, and West Bengal despite sincere efforts and adequate training. It highlights how unrealistic goals and existing social structures led to limited participation among the poorest villagers. The chapter emphasizes the importance of realistic goal-setting considering historical contexts and constraints. It also introduces results-based management (RBM) as a planning tool and discusses its application in arts-related projects, advocating for continuous evaluation and integration of effective programs.
