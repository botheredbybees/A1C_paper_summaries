## Can Photography Help Depression?

The article 'Can Photography Help Depression?' explores the potential benefits of photography as a therapeutic tool for individuals suffering from depression. It draws on research suggesting that daily photography practice can enhance overall wellbeing and then delves into interviews with five artists who use photography to manage their mental health conditions. The contributions range from providing structure and motivation, aiding in trauma processing, offering control over uncontrollable situations, fostering introspection, enabling emotional expression, and encouraging connections among people facing similar challenges. Each artist shares unique methods of how photography helps them cope during difficult times and provides insights into the psychological benefits derived from creative engagement with a camera.
