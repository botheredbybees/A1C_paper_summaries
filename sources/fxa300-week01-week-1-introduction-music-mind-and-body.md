## Week 1 Introduction: Music, Mind and Body

In the Week 1 introduction to FXA300 Music, Mind and Body, Dr Brendan Lamb welcomes students and outlines the course's focus on exploring music’s impact on the human brain. Drawing upon his expertise in musicology and neuroscience, Dr Lamb emphasizes that despite our long history of musical expression, much remains unknown about how music influences our neural processes and emotional states. The lecture encourages students to engage with basic musical concepts and critically analyze their listening experiences to understand personal impacts. For Week 1 specifically, the emphasis is on understanding the relationship between music and mood, exploring how various brain functions respond to different types of music.
