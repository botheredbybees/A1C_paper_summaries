## Activities for Week 10: FXA301 Arts in the Community

Week 10 of the FXA301 Arts in the Community course includes a focus on music therapy for mental illness treatment and involves a creative activity where students create a collage to explore their current feelings. The lesson provides references to scholarly articles discussing arts therapies in mental health contexts, such as eating disorders and PTSD. Students are instructed to reflect on their engagement with the making process of their art pieces and share these reflections in the discussion forum. Additionally, this week continues work on Assessment Task 4, which involves developing a creative arts project proposal.
