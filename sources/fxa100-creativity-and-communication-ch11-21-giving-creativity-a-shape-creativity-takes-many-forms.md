## Giving Creativity a Shape

This chapter explores the nature of creative sessions for individuals with dementia, emphasizing that creativity is fluid and responsive to individual needs. It outlines a typical session structure including an invitation phase where care is taken to engage people in a non-threatening way; a beginning marked by welcome, reassurance, and establishing safety; a middle focused on engagement and relationship-building through materials and activities; and an ending characterized by celebration and reflection. The chapter underscores the importance of adapting sessions based on real-time observations and interactions, highlighting strategies such as visual prompts, gentle introductions, and clear yet flexible plans.
