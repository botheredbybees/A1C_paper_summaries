## About the Octaband® — Dance for Connection

The document provides information about the Octaband®, a product created by Octaband LLC in 2005 to promote movement and sensory connection among group members of all ages and abilities. The Octaband® is described as an interactive tool that represents the idea of individuality within a collective group, symbolizing each person's uniqueness while emphasizing their integral role in the community. Its bright colors and stretchy material encourage playfulness and interaction, allowing for both personal autonomy and communal engagement. The product’s adaptability is highlighted through its ability to be manipulated into various forms, such as being rolled up or stretched out. Additionally, the company donates 10% of net profits to dementia research and advocacy, underscoring a commitment to social responsibility and health support.
