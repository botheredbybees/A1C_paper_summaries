## iRemember Loan Resource Flyer

The document describes the 'iRemember' project, a free loan resource developed by the Tasmanian Museum and Art Gallery (TMAG) in collaboration with the Wicking Dementia Research and Education Centre. The project aims to assist people living with dementia and their support networks in engaging in meaningful conversations through the use of real objects from everyday life contexts such as kitchens and sheds. Each memory case contains 16 carefully selected objects designed to evoke memories and stimulate storytelling, accompanied by informational cards that provide context and discussion prompts for users. The resource is intended to enhance communication and enjoyment among people affected by dementia and their care circles.
