## Measuring Arts Participation

The lesson discusses findings from national arts participation surveys conducted by the Australia Council for the Arts in 2016 and subsequent years. It highlights increasing engagement with both online and live events, recognition of First Nations arts, and benefits to wellbeing and social cohesion. The content also includes insights from U.S. and UK studies on the social impact of arts and case studies like Girgarre’s Moosic Muster which transformed a struggling dairy town through music. Emphasis is placed on the role of arts in promoting community health and accessibility.
