## Singing for Better Breathing Project

This transcript describes the 'Singing for Better Breathing' initiative launched in Lambeth and Southwark during summer 2015. The program aims to create singing groups for individuals with chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) who experience breathing difficulties. Participants undergo assessments of their respiratory function, and researchers hope to document significant improvements through regular participation in these singing sessions. Testimonials from participants highlight the physical benefits such as increased lung capacity and reduced frequency of infections, alongside emotional gains including a sense of community and personal empowerment.
