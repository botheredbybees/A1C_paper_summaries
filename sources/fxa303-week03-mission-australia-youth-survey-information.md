## Mission Australia Youth Survey Information

The document provides information about the Mission Australia Youth Survey, an annual survey conducted with young people aged 15 to 19 across Australia. The survey aims to gather insights on sociodemographic information, national issues of importance, mental health and wellbeing, school engagement, housing and financial situation, climate issues, personal concerns such as bullying and body image, social media use, loneliness, alcohol and drug opinions. Participants' responses remain confidential; however, if any answers suggest risk or distress, appropriate authorities are informed to ensure safety and support. The survey data is used for developing programs that address young people's needs in schools and communities.
