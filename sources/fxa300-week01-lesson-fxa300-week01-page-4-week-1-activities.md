## Activities for Week 1 in FXA300 Music, Mind and Body

This week's lesson in the course 'FXA300 Music, Mind and Body' introduces weekly discussion prompts for students to share their backgrounds and experiences related to music. It emphasizes the importance of creating a supportive community through discussions and virtual spaces like the Virtual Coffee Shop and Zoom Room. The module also highlights the use of Studiosity for academic support and suggests routines for effective video watching and note-taking, including activities such as reflecting on key points from videos about how music affects the brain.
