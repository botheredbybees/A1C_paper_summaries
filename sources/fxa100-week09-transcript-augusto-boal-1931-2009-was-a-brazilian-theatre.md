## Transcript: Augusto Boal and Theatre as Political Activism

The transcript discusses the life and work of Augusto Boal, a Brazilian theatre director and politician who used theatre to address political issues. In 1994 municipal elections in Rio de Janeiro, he ran as an independent candidate and won by promoting the Workers' Party instead of himself. Boal's influential book 'Theatre of the Oppressed' highlights his belief that theatre can give a voice to underrepresented groups such as the poor, crime victims, prisoners, etc.
