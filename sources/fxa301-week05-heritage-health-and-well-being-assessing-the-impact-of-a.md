## Heritage, health and well-being: assessing the impact of a heritage focused intervention on health and well-being

This article explores the impact of a heritage-focused intervention on health and well-being in healthcare settings, including hospitals and care homes. The study evaluates whether museum objects can improve patient well-being by promoting relaxation, introspection, and positive social interactions. Key findings suggest that such interventions may indeed contribute to mental and physical health benefits for patients and caregivers alike. The research also highlights the importance of considering individual experiences and motivations in assessing the effectiveness of these heritage-based therapies.
