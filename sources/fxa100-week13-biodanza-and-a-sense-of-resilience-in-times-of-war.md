## Biodanza and a sense of resilience in times of war

The study explores the impact of Biodanza on resilience, affectivity, vitality, and morale during times of war. Conducted in Israel during the Iron Swords war, it included 112 participants with varying levels of biodanza experience. The research aimed to understand how Biodanza contributes to emotional and physical well-being amidst crisis. Key findings indicate that non-verbal human interaction through dance significantly enhances resilience, providing comfort, support, empathy, and a sense of healing and security. The study highlights the importance of vivencia in creating physiological and emotional balance, fostering mental well-being, and increasing vitality among participants. Seasoned dancers reported higher levels of resilience compared to those with less experience.
