## Arts-based research in aged care: Photovoice, documentary photography, and poetry

This chapter details a unique arts-based research methodology that combines photovoice, documentary photography, and poetry to explore the daily lives of aged care residents. The project named 'Inside Aged Care' involved over 100 participants but focuses on detailed insights from ten elderly individuals across different stages of life in an Australian aged care facility. Participants underwent semi-structured interviews and various creative arts tasks to reflect on their experiences, health conditions, and emotions towards aging and living in aged care. The study employs hermeneutic phenomenology and manual inductive reflexive thematic analysis for data collection and interpretation, aiming to provide a rich, poignant description of the lived experiences in aged care settings. This research contributes significantly to understanding the qualitative aspects of aging and the impact of creative arts-based approaches on health and well-being among elderly individuals.
