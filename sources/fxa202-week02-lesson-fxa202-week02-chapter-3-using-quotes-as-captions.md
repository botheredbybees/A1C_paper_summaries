## Using Quotes Effectively in Photo Essays

This lesson focuses on the use of quotes as captions in photo essays. It highlights how direct and indirect quotes can enhance storytelling by adding color and personality to images through dialogue and historical accounts. The importance of attribution for ethical writing is emphasized, along with the suggestion to alternately use both types of quotes for variety and clarity.
