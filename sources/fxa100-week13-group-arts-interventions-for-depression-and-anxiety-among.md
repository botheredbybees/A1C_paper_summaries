## Group Arts Interventions for Depression and Anxiety Among Older Adults: A Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis

This systematic review and meta-analysis evaluates the effectiveness of group arts interventions in reducing depression and anxiety among older adults (over 55 years without dementia). The study, involving 39 controlled trials, reveals a moderate reduction in both conditions, with more pronounced effects observed in care home settings compared to community-based interventions. Despite some publication bias noted for depression studies, the findings affirm the potential of group arts as an effective and inclusive therapeutic approach, particularly considering resource disparities across low-, middle-, and high-income countries.
