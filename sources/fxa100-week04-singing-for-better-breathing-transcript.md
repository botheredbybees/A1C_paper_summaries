## Singing for Better Breathing - Transcript

The document is a transcript discussing the 'Singing for Better Breathing' project initiated in Lambeth and Southwark during summer 2015. The project aims to establish singing groups for individuals suffering from chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) with the goal of assessing whether regular participation in such activities can improve their breathing conditions. Participants share positive experiences, noting enhanced physical capabilities, improved mental outlook, reduced reliance on medication, and decreased instances of respiratory infections. The initiative underscores the potential benefits of engaging COPD patients in social and physically active pursuits to regain a sense of control over their condition and enhance overall well-being.
