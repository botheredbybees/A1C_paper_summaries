## FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing - Week 4 Overview

This week's module concludes the foundational learning on brain and social development in children and adolescents with a focus on creative arts and play. The course delves into how these activities can support mental wellbeing, particularly for neurodiverse individuals, especially those with autism. Key concepts include contrasting medical and social models of disability and understanding autism through its manifestations in brain function, social interactions, and behavior. Creative arts and play are discussed as tools to enhance communication, learning, and emotional self-regulation among neurodiverse children.
