## Power of Music - Music in Herts Dementia Care

Led by Age UK Hertfordshire, the 'Power of Music' initiative aims to integrate music into dementia care through a comprehensive training programme for care professionals in partnership with various organizations such as the Hertfordshire Care Providers Association and local charities specializing in music therapy. The project focuses on creating personalized musical experiences based on residents’ preferences and histories, offering techniques like passive and active listening, singing, playing instruments, reminiscence, and movement-based engagement. Training will be co-produced to ensure practical relevance and sustainability through a Music Champions Network for ongoing peer support and development. An evaluation with the University of Hertfordshire will measure long-term impacts on staff confidence, resident wellbeing, and care home culture.
