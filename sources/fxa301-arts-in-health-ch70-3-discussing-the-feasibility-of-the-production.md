## Research Protocols: Their Importance and Structure

This academic chapter discusses the importance and structure of research protocols in scientific studies. It draws parallels between show bibles in television production and research protocols, highlighting their role in providing a clear rationale for studies, detailing methods to address research questions, and outlining feasibility aspects such as timelines, resources, and ethical considerations. The chapter explains how protocols are internal documents crucial for project management and external communications with stakeholders, funders, and ethics committees. It also outlines the components of a protocol including covering page details, table of contents, synopsis, abbreviations, background rationale, objectives, study design, participant identification, procedures, intervention, analysis, data management, ethical considerations, finance, publication policy, references, and conflicts of interest statements.
