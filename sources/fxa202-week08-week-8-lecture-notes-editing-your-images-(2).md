## Week 8 Lecture Notes: Editing Your Images

The Week 8 Lecture Notes for the course FXA202 at the University of Tasmania emphasize the therapeutic benefits of photography and offer guidance on editing images for a photo essay. The lecture notes highlight activities such as mindfulness, nature walks, and the 'photo a day' challenge to explore mental health through photography. Additionally, the notes suggest reading materials like Neil Gibson's book on therapeutic photography and Del Loewenthal's work on phototherapy in a digital age. Students are encouraged to experiment with creative approaches such as digital compositing or mixed media techniques. The document also discusses various editing software options and provides tips for improving image lighting. For the Week 8 discussion activity, students are tasked with sharing a 'before and after' version of their edited images.
