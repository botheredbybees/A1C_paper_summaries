## Artists in the House Program: Expressing Yourself in Aged Care

The transcript discusses the Artists in the House program aimed at enhancing the quality of life for elderly individuals in nursing homes through various forms of self-expression such as dance, art, music, and singing. The program addresses common issues faced by residents, including memory loss due to Alzheimer's disease, disorientation, and depression. It highlights how engaging with creative activities can help stimulate neural pathways and improve cognitive function even when other faculties are declining.
