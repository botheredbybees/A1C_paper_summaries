## PSY114 PSY214 Stress, Self-Care and Mindfulness - Wrap-up

This lesson provides an overview of the upcoming assessments for the unit PSY114/PSY214 on Stress, Self-Care and Mindfulness. It outlines details about AT1 quizzes, which include a weekly quiz with only one attempt available, emphasizing careful preparation before starting. For AT2 information sheets and AT3 workbooks, students are directed to the Assignment Information document for comprehensive guidelines and requirements. Students are encouraged to begin familiarizing themselves with required readings and mindfulness practices early on. The lesson concludes by reminding participants that Week 2 content will be accessible from Monday at 9 am.
