## Activities for Week 11

This week's lesson focuses on the final required reading from an ABC news article about supported studios and other initiatives enabling artists with intellectual disabilities to achieve their goals. The discussion topic requires students to research barriers and impacts of COVID-19 restrictions on arts engagement for either non-English speaking backgrounds or people with a disability, and suggests strategies to promote engagement. Assessment Task 4 involves creating a project proposal due May 29th, 2026.
