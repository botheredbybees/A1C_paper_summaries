## LAW122 Legal Systems: Activities for Week 4

This week's LAW122 legal systems course activity focuses on visual storytelling through writing captions and sharing images. The task involves taking a picture through a window and composing a caption that conveys elements not visible in the image, incorporating sensory experiences and emotions. Students are instructed to upload their pictures and captions into a discussion board where they will also engage with peers' posts. Tips for transferring photos from phones or cameras to computers, converting HEIC files to JPEG, resizing images, and posting them on the MyLO platform are provided.
