## Week 2 Welcome

The document provides an overview of Week 2 in the Creativity for Life unit at UTAS. It encourages students to continue developing their ideas and experimenting with materials, especially focusing on using objects in novel ways. The module introduces concepts related to creativity and neuroscience, including synaesthesia research by Turner and Littlemore and Lunke and Meier. Students are assured that foundational knowledge is built through intensive learning content in the initial weeks, which will later allow for more relaxed exploration of creative projects.
