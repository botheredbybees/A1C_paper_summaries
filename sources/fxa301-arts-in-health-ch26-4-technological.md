## PESTLE Analysis and Kolb’s Reflective Cycle for Arts in Health Interventions

This chapter discusses PESTLE analysis and its application in healthcare settings for arts-based interventions, highlighting strategic opportunities and barriers. It outlines the importance of understanding legal, technological, social, environmental, economic, and political factors before designing an intervention. The chapter also introduces Kolb’s Reflective Cycle as a framework for experiential learning in arts in health projects, emphasizing concrete experience through process mapping, shadowing target groups, and conducting discovery interviews to gain deep insights into patient experiences.
