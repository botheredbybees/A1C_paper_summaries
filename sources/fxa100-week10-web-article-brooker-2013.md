## Enhancing Student Engagement in Online Learning Environments

This article explores strategies for improving student engagement in online learning environments. It discusses the challenges faced by educators and students alike, such as lack of face-to-face interaction and difficulty maintaining motivation over extended periods without regular feedback or social interaction. The text highlights various approaches to enhance engagement through interactive content, regular assessments, and community building exercises.
