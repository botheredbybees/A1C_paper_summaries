## 2022 Week 10 Lecture Transcript

This lecture transcript provides information about the concluding phase of the photo essay course content at the University of Tasmania. It outlines that Week 11 is designated as a study week where students can engage with tutors but will not have new content released. The document highlights an invitation for students to share valuable resources on the Discussion Board, emphasizing the importance of diverse references for their critical reflection assignments. Additionally, it encourages continued engagement in previous weeks' discussions, particularly referencing Week 9's activity focused on three-picture stories as a resource for the photo essay task. Suggestions are given for further studies beyond this course, including photography classes offered both through the diploma of creative arts and health programs and directly from the university’s art school, covering various levels from introductory to advanced courses in Hobart and Inveresk campuses. The transcript concludes with an expression of appreciation and encouragement from the teaching team towards the students’ engagement throughout the semester.
