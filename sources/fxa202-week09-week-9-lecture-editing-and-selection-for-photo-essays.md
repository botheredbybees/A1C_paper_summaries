## Week 9 Lecture: Editing and Selection for Photo Essays

The lecture for Week 9 of the Photo Essay course focuses on editing and selection as students work to refine their captions and images. The instructor emphasizes the value of journaling for personal well-being, encouraging students to be experimental with reflective writing. Students are reminded that longer passages from journals can serve as inspiration for photo essay captions, which may then be edited down to fit the required length. Practical tips and examples for enhancing writing are provided on subsequent pages. For the Discussion Board activity, students are asked to share a 'Three Picture Story' consisting of three key moments from their photo essays to receive feedback from tutors and peers. Instructions for exporting slides as JPEG files are also included.
