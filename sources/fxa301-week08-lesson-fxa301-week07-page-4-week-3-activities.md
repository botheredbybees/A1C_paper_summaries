## FXA301 Activities for Week 8

This lesson focuses on an exercise involving three poems by William Butler Yeats, William Henry Davies, and Walt Whitman. Students are instructed to listen repeatedly to the recordings of these poems and reflect on their emotional responses, including sound, rhythm, imagery, and sensory effects. The emphasis is not on textual analysis but rather on personal feelings evoked by the poetry. Additionally, students are encouraged to read aloud Les Murray’s 'The Mitchells' and discuss their reflections in a class discussion forum.
