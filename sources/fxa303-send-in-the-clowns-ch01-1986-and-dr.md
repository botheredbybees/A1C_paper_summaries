## Empowerment Through Clowning: Humanitarian Activities in Pediatric Hospitals

The Big Apple Circus Clown Care Unit shares their experiences and techniques in using clowning to empower children in pediatric hospitals, focusing on the psychological benefits of humor and play. The unit's approach includes creating a safe space for expression, teaching therapeutic skills, and fostering a sense of normalcy amid medical treatment.
