## Puppet Display Techniques and Ventriloquism in Play Therapy

This chapter from 'Puppet-Assisted Play Therapy' by Cheryl Hulburd discusses strategies for displaying puppets in therapy settings and the art of ventriloquism to enhance therapeutic interactions. It covers techniques such as positioning puppets, creating engaging displays, and using props like costumes and accessories to make sessions more dynamic. The chapter also delves into developing a distinct puppet voice through proper breathing techniques, mouth positions, and sound substitutions for different letters and words. Hulburd provides exercises to help therapists practice ventriloquism effectively.
