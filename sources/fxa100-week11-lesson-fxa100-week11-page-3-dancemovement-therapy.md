## Dance/Movement Therapy Projects for Dementia

This content discusses several dance/movement therapy initiatives aimed at enhancing the lives of individuals with dementia. It highlights 'Move through Time', a project by Tina Heeley's organization Dancemind, which improved social engagement and confidence among participants in Northamptonshire. Another initiative is 'Moving from Problem to Potential - Dance and Wellness in the US' where Filipa Pereira Stubbs explored dance-based wellness programs across different hospitals. The content also covers circle dancing for people with dementia, emphasizing its adaptability and inclusivity. It concludes by reiterating the week's learning objectives on describing dance therapy benefits, brain areas involved in dance, and overall benefits to older individuals.
