## Final Report: Wisconsin Music & Memory Program – Student Volunteer Program

The document presents a final report on the 'Wisconsin Music & Memory Program – Student Volunteer Program' that was initiated in January 2016 to provide personalized music to nursing home residents. The program aimed to certify an additional 67 Wisconsin nursing homes, with 37 participating specifically through the student volunteer component. This initiative involved high school and college students volunteering at certified nursing homes and engaged local schools and health professionals through outreach activities such as presentations and collaborations with HOSA Future Health Professionals chapters. The report highlights the achievements in increasing dementia care awareness and providing direct benefits to over 1,900 residents by integrating personalized music therapy into daily care routines.
