## Healing Arts - Safe Place for Youth

The Healing Arts Program at Safe Place for Youth (SPY) offers a nurturing environment for creative expression to youth who are experiencing homelessness and have experienced trauma. The program includes various activities such as music groups, writing workshops, open studio sessions, yoga, mindfulness practices, and experiential learning field trips. These activities aim to provide a safe space for emotional healing and mental well-being through creative self-expression. Art creation is used as a tool for recognizing feelings, connecting to identity, building self-esteem, improving decision-making skills, and enhancing impulse control. The program also includes weekly meditation sessions facilitated by community partners like InsightLA and Against the Stream. Yoga sessions focus on strengthening the mind-body connection and addressing physical ailments relevant to youth members. Additionally, music activities such as open mic nights and songwriting sessions are held in partnership with local organizations and professionals. These initiatives collectively foster a sense of community and provide alternative methods for emotional regulation and communication.
