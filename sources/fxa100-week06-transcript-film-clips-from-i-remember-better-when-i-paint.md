## Transcript: Film Clips from 'I Remember Better When I Paint'

The documentary 'I Remember Better When I Paint' focuses on Alzheimer's disease through the lens of creative arts, particularly painting. The story is narrated by Olivia de Havilland and features Berna Heer whose mother was a renowned painter who continued to paint despite her illness. Hilda's daughter enlisted art students from the Art Institute of Chicago to work with her mother, leading to significant improvements in her quality of life and communication abilities. This initiative inspired the creation of the Hilos Foundation which provides scholarships for such interactions.
