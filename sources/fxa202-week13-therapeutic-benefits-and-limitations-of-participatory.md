## Therapeutic benefits and limitations of participatory photography for adults with mental health problems: A systematic search and literature review

This review explores the therapeutic benefits and limitations of participatory photography (PP) for adults with mental health problems, examining existing literature through a systematic search across six academic databases. The study reveals that PP can offer various therapeutic advantages such as empowerment, enhanced self-reflection, strengthened therapeutic relationships, peer support, creative expression, achievement, and enjoyment. However, it also highlights potential ethical issues related to photographing identifiable individuals. The review concludes that while PP shows promise in aiding recovery and fostering empowerment, its suitability varies among different individuals, necessitating careful adaptation for specific contexts.
