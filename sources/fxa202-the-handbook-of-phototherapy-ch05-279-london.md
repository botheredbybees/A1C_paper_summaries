## Training in Phototherapy and Therapeutic Photography

This chapter focuses on training and implementing phototherapy and therapeutic photography practices for professionals in the psychological therapy field, including counselors, psychotherapists, psychologists, arts therapists, and play therapists. It outlines a five-day workshop designed to educate practitioners about the theoretical underpinnings of these methods and how they can be applied both individually and within group settings, particularly with marginalized communities like LGBT+ youth. The chapter provides practical guidance on planning such training sessions, emphasizing the importance of personal supervision and continuous professional development for those engaging in therapeutic photography.
