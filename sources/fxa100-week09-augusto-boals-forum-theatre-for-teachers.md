## Augusto Boal’s FORUM THEATRE for teachers

This document provides workshop notes on Augusto Boal’s Theatre of the Oppressed and specifically focuses on Forum Theatre. The text outlines the principles and development of Boal's approach, emphasizing its role in empowering individuals to address social issues through theatrical techniques. It describes the evolution of Boal’s work from Brazil to other countries due to political circumstances and his later return to establish a Centre for Theatre of the Oppressed in Rio de Janeiro. The notes detail how Forum Theatre involves audience members becoming 'spect-actors' who can intervene during performances to propose alternative solutions, fostering social change through interactive drama. This method encourages critical thinking and collective action among participants.
