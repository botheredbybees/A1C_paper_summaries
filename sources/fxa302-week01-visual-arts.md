## Visual Arts

The chapter explores the integration of visual arts into care home settings and its benefits for both residents and healthcare staff. It highlights various initiatives such as hospital art collections, artist-in-residence programs in hospices, and creative projects commissioned by hospitals to enhance patient well-being. The author presents evidence supporting the positive impacts of visual arts on mental and physical health, including improved self-esteem, reduced anxiety, and enhanced communication for individuals with dementia. Furthermore, it discusses the role of transdisciplinary approaches where artists collaborate directly with care homes to influence care delivery positively. Practical advice is provided for those aiming to implement similar programs, emphasizing the importance of building long-term relationships between cultural institutions and healthcare settings.
