## FXA302 - Week 1 Introduction

The document provides an overview of the first week's materials for FXA302, a course focusing on perspectives of the arts in health and wellbeing. The content includes introductions to interdisciplinary study areas such as health humanities and narrative medicine, with discussions on their origins and key figures. It outlines the structure of the course modules and encourages student engagement through weekly activities like required readings and discussion forums. The text emphasizes the importance of understanding how creative work is influenced by illness and disability, and how these themes are depicted across various art forms such as music, drama, and visual arts. This foundational knowledge sets the stage for a deeper exploration into the intersection of arts and healthcare throughout the semester.
