## Week 3 Overview - FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing

This document provides an overview of Week 3 in the FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing course. It discusses recent advancements in neuroscience that reveal ongoing brain development during adolescence, which presents a critical window for shaping mental health outcomes through environmental influences such as diet, social connections, and experiences. The text highlights how patterns established during this period can have long-term impacts on future well-being and emphasizes the role of creative arts in supporting cognitive functions like expression, abstract thinking, and emotional regulation. It also addresses potential risks associated with underdeveloped decision-making capacities due to hormonal changes during adolescence.
