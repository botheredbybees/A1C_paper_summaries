## Trauma-Informed Care and Practice Organisational Toolkit: Stage 1 - Planning and Audit

The Trauma-Informed Care and Practice Organisational Toolkit (TICPOT) is a resource developed by the Mental Health Coordinating Council Inc. to assist services in Australia implement trauma-informed care and practice (TICP). The document provides an overview of trauma, principles of TICP, and guidelines for planning and conducting an organisational audit. It aims to support staff and services in becoming more aware of and responsive to the needs of individuals impacted by past or current trauma. The toolkit is divided into six organizational domains that assist in identifying areas for quality improvement. This resource emphasizes inclusivity in language use and acknowledges differences across disciplines and systems, aiming to be as inclusive as possible without pathologizing individuals. It provides an essential guide for mental health and human services organizations looking to improve their policies and practices related to trauma-informed care.
