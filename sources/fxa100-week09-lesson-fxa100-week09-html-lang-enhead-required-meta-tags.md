## Week 9: Dramatherapy, Dance Therapy and Applied Theatre in Dementia Care

In this module of FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care, students explore dramatherapy, dance therapy, and applied theatre as therapeutic approaches for individuals living with dementia. The course covers the application of dramatic skills in enhancing awareness and understanding of dementia care. It outlines key learning objectives such as explaining the basics of these therapies, describing elements of Theatre of the Oppressed techniques, and discussing the Veder method's impact on people with dementia. The module is developed based on previous lecturer Matt Delbridge’s structure and includes a time commitment estimate of 8 hours for completing the week’s activities.
