## Implementing Living Room Theatre Activities for People with Dementia on Nursing Home Wards

The study evaluates the implementation of the Veder Method, a new communication approach combining theatrical elements and person-centered care techniques for dementia patients in nursing homes. The method was designed to improve interaction between caregivers and residents by using stimuli like songs, poetry, and reminiscence activities. Semi-structured interviews with stakeholders and focus groups with trained staff revealed that positive resident reactions and increased reciprocity were key motivators for continued use of the Veder Method. Essential factors for successful implementation included an action plan, executive support, visibility within the organization, and a pioneering group to initiate the process. However, high work pressure was identified as a significant barrier. The research provides valuable insights into facilitating the dissemination of effective communication methods in dementia care settings.
