## The Man Who Plays French Horn With His Toes

This article from the BBC Ouch blog features Felix Klieser, a professional French horn player born without arms who uses his feet to perform. Despite his physical limitations, Klieser has toured with Sting, released an album, and secured numerous concert engagements through December 2015. He became an ambassador for OHMI (One-Handed Musical Instrument Trust) which helps musicians with limb differences access adapted instruments. The piece also discusses how Klieser uses his right foot to operate the mute on his horn while his left foot controls the valves, showcasing remarkable adaptations and emotional depth in musical performance. His commitment to music highlights unique challenges faced by disabled individuals in pursuing creative arts, emphasizing resilience and creativity.
