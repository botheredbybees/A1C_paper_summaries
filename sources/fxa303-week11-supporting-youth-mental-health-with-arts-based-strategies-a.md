## Supporting youth mental health with arts-based strategies: a global perspective

This article discusses the growing global crisis of youth mental health, emphasizing the need for innovative and accessible solutions to address this issue across diverse communities. The authors highlight the potential role of arts-based strategies in supporting youth mental health, noting that engaging with local cultural practices can bolster mental health by enhancing community connections, self-expression, and cultural identity. They argue that integrating the arts into healthcare initiatives can provide a scalable solution due to its cost-effectiveness and relevance across different settings. The paper also outlines four key steps for implementing these strategies: elevating youth voices, developing core outcome measures, identifying successful global models, and generating funding pathways. By addressing mental health through arts-based interventions, the article suggests that we can mitigate long-term mental health issues and promote overall well-being among young people.
