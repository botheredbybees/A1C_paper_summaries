## Designing Inclusive Projects for Health and Wellbeing Outcomes

This week's lesson in the FXA301 course focuses on designing inclusive creative arts projects that cater to diverse groups with specific needs. It builds upon principles introduced earlier and emphasizes respectful collaboration and resource utilization for community benefit. Key objectives include locating appropriate resources, analyzing project scenarios critically, and using a proforma for developing collaborative projects tailored to individuals' health requirements. The lesson highlights the transformative power of art in enhancing self-expression and agency among people with disabilities or other challenges.
