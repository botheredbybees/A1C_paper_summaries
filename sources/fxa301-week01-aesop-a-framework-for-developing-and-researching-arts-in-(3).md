## Aesop: A Framework for Developing and Researching Arts in Health Programmes

The article presents Aesop, a framework designed to address the challenges in arts in health research by integrating methodologies from both arts and health disciplines. The authors highlight the growing activity in the field of arts in health but note that this growth is often hindered by confusion and misunderstanding regarding appropriate research methods. They describe how Aesop synthesizes existing research frameworks, such as participatory action research and medical guidelines for complex interventions, to provide a cohesive approach for developing, designing, delivering, and disseminating arts in health projects. The framework aims to facilitate collaboration between artists and researchers, ensuring that studies are robust and acceptable across both the arts and health communities.
