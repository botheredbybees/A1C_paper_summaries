## How Can Photography Help Wellbeing?

This lesson explores the therapeutic benefits of photography on mental health, focusing on digital practices such as photo-a-day challenges and their impact on wellbeing. It references a study by Brewster and Cox (2018) which found that daily photography can enhance self-care, community interaction, reminiscence, mindfulness, exercise, purpose, competence, achievement, managing loneliness and grief, and provide personal narratives. The lesson also discusses Tara Wray's project 'Too Tired for Sunshine' and her use of photography to manage depression. Additionally, it addresses the role of smartphones in memory retention and suggests mindful practices for enhancing memories through photography.
