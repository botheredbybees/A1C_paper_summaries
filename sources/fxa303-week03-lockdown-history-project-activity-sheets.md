## Lockdown History Project Activity Sheets

The document is a collection of activity sheets developed by Arts on Prescription for the 'Lockdown History' project in 2020. The initiative aims to capture how individuals experienced and documented their lives during lockdown periods, providing a historical record for future generations. It includes various activities such as taking photos, writing journals, creating art pieces, and expressing emotions through visual aids like pie charts. Participants are encouraged to share personal stories, recipes, and reflections on daily life changes, fostering community engagement and mental well-being through creative arts.
