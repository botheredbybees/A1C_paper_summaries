## Child-Centered Play Therapy as an Intervention for Children With Autism: A Literature Review

This article presents a systematic review of the play therapy literature to evaluate the effectiveness of child-centered play therapy (CCPT) as an intervention for children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). Despite CCPT being recognized as an evidence-based, effective intervention for various childhood issues, there is limited research specifically addressing its efficacy in treating ASD. The review summarizes four studies that suggest CCPT can improve social and emotional behaviors in children with ASD but highlights the need for more rigorous empirical evidence to conclusively establish CCPT's efficacy. The article emphasizes CCPT’s focus on the therapeutic relationship rather than behavior modification, which could be beneficial for addressing interpersonal dynamics in individuals with ASD.
