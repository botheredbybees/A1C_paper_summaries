## Week 1 Activities: FXA301 Arts in the Community

The course content for Week 1 of FXA301 'Arts in the Community' introduces students to community arts discussions and activities. The materials include a video transcript, readings by Dr Kim Lehman and Angelina Hurley from The Conversation, and an e-tivity featuring a TEDx talk by John Gerdy. Students are instructed to engage with discussion boards, read assigned articles on arts value in society, and watch the TEDx talk for reflection. A true or false quiz is provided based on statistics from National Arts Participation surveys to reinforce learning. Additional resources like lectures via Zoom and studies about social engagement through arts activities supplement the coursework.
