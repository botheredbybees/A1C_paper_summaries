## The Professionalization Agenda for Arts in Health

This chapter discusses the professionalization of arts in health professions in America, emphasizing the importance of education and certification systems similar to those in creative arts therapies and expressive arts therapy. It highlights initiatives such as the NOAH Professionalization Code of Ethics, Standards, and Core Curriculum, which aim to establish a nationally recognized credential for artists working in healthcare settings. The chapter also explores existing educational pathways ranging from on-the-job training to formal degree programs and proposes an expansion of these paths to include diverse cultural perspectives and practices.
