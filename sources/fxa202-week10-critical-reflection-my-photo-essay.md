## Critical Reflection: My Photo Essay

This critical reflection by Melanie Hall explores the creation and therapeutic benefits of a photo essay focusing on her 14-month-old son's perspective. The project aimed to acknowledge and process postnatal depression, anxiety, and stress through photography. Hall discovered that using smartphone photography as a creative outlet helped enhance mindfulness and provided a coping mechanism. She initially planned to focus on manual camera functions but shifted to spontaneous moments captured via phone due to accessibility and the ease of capturing immediate experiences. The photo essay also incorporated various photographic techniques such as composition rules, symmetry, and negative space, enhancing the visual impact of her images. Additionally, Hall learned the importance of writing captions that complement the imagery, providing context beyond what is visible in the photos. This project not only improved her technical photography skills but also demonstrated how creative arts can support mental health recovery.
