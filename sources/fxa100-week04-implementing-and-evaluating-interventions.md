## Implementing and Evaluating Interventions

This chapter outlines the process of designing and evaluating arts in health interventions, focusing on the practical steps required to turn conceptual ideas into tangible actions. The author, Daisy Fancourt, details how to undertake a pilot project effectively, ensuring that an intervention is well-designed for its intended participants and fits seamlessly within healthcare settings. Key considerations include piloting interventions to test their feasibility, gathering formative evaluation data, and demonstrating the potential impact of these initiatives to secure funding and stakeholder support. The chapter underscores the importance of iterative refinement through active experimentation, allowing arts-based projects to evolve into sustainable, impactful health solutions.
