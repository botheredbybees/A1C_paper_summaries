## The Role of Creative Arts in the Community

Dr Brendan Lamb's lecture for FXA301 Arts in the Community introduces students to the significant role of creative arts in enhancing community health and wellbeing. The content highlights how engagement with art forms such as dance, theatre, music therapy, and public arts programs positively impacts individuals' mental and physical health across various life stages. It discusses evidence supporting arts on prescription practices and their potential as a cost-effective preventive measure against social isolation and other health issues. The lesson outlines the structure of weekly learning materials including modules focusing on theoretical understanding, case studies of community art projects, and practical application through student assessments.
