## FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care - Week 4: Participation and Singing

This week's module in FXA100 focuses on participatory music activities for individuals with dementia. It highlights the role of registered music therapists and discusses various forms of music participation suitable for people at different stages of dementia, ranging from listening to active engagement such as singing or playing instruments. The lesson emphasizes the therapeutic benefits of music therapy beyond mere entertainment, including cognitive stimulation, mood improvement, and physical well-being. Historical context is provided on how institutional settings have integrated music into care programs, evolving from initial motivations towards recognized therapeutic outcomes.
