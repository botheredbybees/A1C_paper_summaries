## The Handbook of Phototherapy and Therapeutic Photography

The Handbook of Phototherapy and Therapeutic Photography is a comprehensive resource that explores the use of photography in therapeutic settings across various disciplines, including psychotherapy and counseling. Del Loewenthal, an expert in the field, provides insights into how photographs can be used to facilitate self-understanding, emotional expression, and psychological development. The book covers theoretical approaches, practical techniques, case studies, and research methods, emphasizing the relevance of phototherapy in today's digital age. It is recommended for practitioners, researchers, clients, and self-help activists interested in creative therapeutic practices.
