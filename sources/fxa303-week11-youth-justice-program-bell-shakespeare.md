## Youth Justice Program | Bell Shakespeare

Bell Shakespeare's Youth Justice Program, initiated in 2010, uses Shakespearean texts to empower young people within the youth justice system through workshops and performances. The program aims at fostering self-reflection, boosting self-confidence, and enhancing skills development among participants by engaging them with renowned teaching artists from Bell Shakespeare. Supported by various trusts, foundations, and individual donors, the initiative has expanded its reach across juvenile detention centers in Australia. Participants perform their learned works for audiences including peers, teachers, staff, and family members, thereby gaining valuable public speaking experience and building a sense of community within the challenging environment of youth justice facilities.
