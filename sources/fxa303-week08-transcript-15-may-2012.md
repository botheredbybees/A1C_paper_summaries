## Transcript: 15 May 2012

The transcript documents a speech by Dr. Patch Adams on his journey from suicidal depression to becoming a medical revolutionary and peace activist. He describes founding the Gesundheit Institute with a vision of free healthcare provided in a communal living environment, prioritizing patient happiness and eliminating medical hierarchy. The institute also conducts 'clown trips' to conflict zones and underprivileged areas around the world to promote healing through laughter and human connection.
