## Finger Puppets in Play Therapy

This chapter discusses the use of finger puppets in play therapy for children aged preschool to elementary and beyond, emphasizing their effectiveness as therapeutic tools. Finger puppet techniques provide a means for children to explore and express their thoughts, emotions, and experiences through storytelling. The author explains how these simple yet versatile toys can facilitate communication, foster emotional wellness, enhance social relationships, and increase personal strengths in the context of medical settings or traditional therapy rooms. Specific applications include addressing speech/language delays, learning disorders, attention issues, anxiety, and self-esteem problems, with case studies illustrating how finger puppets aid children in reframing their identities and understanding their situations more positively.
