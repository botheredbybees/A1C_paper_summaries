## Puppet Play Therapy Techniques and Practical Guide

This chapter from the book 'Puppet Play Therapy: A Practical Guidebook' provides guidance on various aspects of using puppets in therapeutic settings. It discusses posture, personality development for puppets, exaggerating characteristics to make them more captivating, facial expressions, and giving each puppet a name to enhance its realism. The duration of performances is recommended to be brief for children's attention spans. Warm-up techniques are suggested to help children become comfortable with the puppets before engaging in therapy activities. Pitfalls such as aggressive behavior from certain types of puppets and considerations for children with severe emotional disturbance or psychosis are noted. Practical advice on storing, displaying, and utilizing puppet theaters is also provided.
