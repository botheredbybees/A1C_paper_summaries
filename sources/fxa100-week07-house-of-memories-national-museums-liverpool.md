## House of Memories | National Museums Liverpool

The House of Memories is a dementia awareness programme initiated by National Museums Liverpool, designed to support caregivers in providing person-centered care for individuals living with dementia. The initiative offers various resources and activities such as training workshops for health and social care professionals, memory strolls at the Museum of Liverpool, and interactive mobile museum experiences that bring engaging sessions directly to communities. Additionally, House of Memories develops customized memory suitcases filled with memorabilia and objects to stimulate conversations about past memories. This program also includes an app called 'My House of Memories' aimed at facilitating shared explorations and reminiscence among people living with dementia and their carers. The ultimate goal is to enhance the quality of life for those affected by dementia while fostering understanding and empathy in society.
