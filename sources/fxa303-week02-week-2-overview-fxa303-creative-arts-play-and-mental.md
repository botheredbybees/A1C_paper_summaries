## Week 2 Overview - FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing

This document provides an overview of the importance of creative play and arts in child development, focusing on their role in fostering healthy brain and social development. It discusses how play contributes to motor skills, cognitive abilities, and self-regulation, which is essential for learning readiness and emotional well-being. The text emphasizes that sensory experiences through creative activities like music and dance can be cathartic and aid in understanding emotions and reactions. Engaging with the arts supports various aspects of child development including fine motor control, communication, and emotional expression. This week's materials aim to provide context by exploring brain development theories and how play influences mental health.
