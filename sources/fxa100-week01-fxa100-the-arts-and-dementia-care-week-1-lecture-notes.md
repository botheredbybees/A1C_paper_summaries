## FXA100: The Arts and Dementia Care - Week 1 Lecture Notes

The lecture notes for the first week of FXA100: The Arts and Dementia Care provide an overview of the course structure, including practical arts-based interventions in dementia care. The unit focuses on music, visual art, drama/theatre, and aims to describe how these creative arts contribute to well-being, mood enhancement, and cognitive processing for individuals with dementia. Students will learn basic research skills, critical evaluation methods, and effective communication strategies through a series of assessments that include research assignments, quizzes, discussion forums, and an essay/video pitch proposal. The lecture also covers different types of dementia such as Alzheimer’s disease, vascular dementia, Lewy body dementia, fronto-temporal dementia, alcohol-related dementia, HIV-associated dementia, Down syndrome related Alzheimer's, and chronic traumatic encephalopathy (CTE). This course aims to equip students with knowledge on the nature and purposes of arts-based interventions in dementia care settings.
