## Week 2 Introduction: Concepts of Creativity

In this lesson from LAW122 Legal Systems for Week 2, the concept of creativity and its neurological underpinnings are introduced. The focus is on understanding how creativity can be developed through experience, challenge, and environment rather than being an innate talent. Key aspects include defining 'big-C' and 'small-c' creativity, recognizing different stages in the creative process (mini-c, Pro-C), and exploring the role of divergent thinking and cognitive abilities like reason and self-reflection. The lesson also touches upon synaesthesia as a complex neural phenomenon linked to creativity and the importance of historical context in assessing levels of creativity.
