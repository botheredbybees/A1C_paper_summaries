## Evaluation of the Music and Memory Program among Nursing Home Residents with Dementia

The report evaluates the impact of the Music and Memory (M&M) program on nursing home residents with dementia. The M&M program uses personalized music playlists to engage residents and improve their quality of life. Four evaluation components were conducted: a crossover study, pre- and post-resident surveys, Minimum Data Set analysis, and key informant surveys. Findings suggest that the M&M program had little effect on cognitive or memory outcomes but showed improvements in behavioral problems and care rejection among Medicaid recipients. The research highlights the need for further rigorous evaluations to validate the effectiveness of non-pharmacological interventions like M&M.
