## Reflection on Creating a Photo Essay About Breast Cancer Diagnosis

Tanya Hughes reflects on her experience of completing a photo essay titled 'You Are the Best Dancer,' which documents her journey with a breast cancer diagnosis. The piece is both challenging and rewarding, as it required her to confront emotions and memories tied to her diagnosis. Hughes discusses her decision-making process in choosing between various topics and ultimately deciding to reflect on her personal experience. She describes how camera manipulation techniques were used to express shock and loneliness felt during treatment, and the absence of human figures was a deliberate choice to emphasize isolation. The photo essay allowed Hughes to explore emotions through confessional-style writing, leading to a sense of flow as she searched for precise language in her captions. Despite the devastating impact of the diagnosis, Hughes finds that it has given her an opportunity to embrace life more fully and love those around her with greater intensity.
