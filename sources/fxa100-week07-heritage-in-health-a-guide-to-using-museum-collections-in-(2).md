## Heritage in Health: A Guide to Using Museum Collections in Hospitals and Other Healthcare Settings

The document 'Heritage in Health' is a comprehensive guide for museums aiming to conduct activities within healthcare environments. It details various benefits of engaging with patients through object-based learning, including improved perception of health and wellbeing among participants. The guide emphasizes the importance of collaboration between museum professionals and healthcare staff to ensure participant welfare and collection safety. It outlines strategies for recruiting participants, planning sessions, developing programs, facilitating collaborative discovery, providing staff support, and evaluating impact. Key areas covered include handling materials in sensitive environments, addressing special needs, and measuring outcomes to provide evidence of the positive effects on health and wellbeing.
