## Evaluating Community Arts & Community Well-Being: An Evaluation Guide for Community Arts Practitioners

The document 'Evaluating Community Arts & Community Well-Being' is an evaluation guide designed to assist community arts practitioners in systematically assessing the social impact of their projects. Developed by Clare Keating for Effective Change Pty Ltd, this guide aims to provide a user-friendly framework and tools that address the lack of rigorous research on the positive social outcomes of community arts activities. The resource is intended to support both basic and comprehensive evaluation practices through its structured stages: preparing for an evaluation, planning it, determining indicators, collecting data, analyzing findings, and reporting results. It emphasizes the importance of continuous improvement in community arts projects by fostering reflection and learning from past experiences. Additionally, the guide highlights the significance of documenting outcomes not only to secure funding but also to contribute to a broader understanding of how arts initiatives benefit communities in terms of health and well-being.
