## The Connection Between Art, Healing, and Public Health: A Review of Current Literature

This review article examines the relationship between engagement with creative arts and health outcomes, focusing on music therapy, visual arts therapy, movement-based expression, and expressive writing. The authors summarize existing literature to establish a foundation for further research into this subject area. They note that while there is evidence suggesting art-based interventions can reduce adverse physiological and psychological effects, the extent of their effectiveness in enhancing overall health remains largely unexplored. The study aims to generate interest in investigating how engagement with the arts contributes to holistic well-being and mental and physical health improvement.
