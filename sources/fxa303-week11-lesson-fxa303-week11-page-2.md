## Creative Arts, Gender Diversity and Mental Wellbeing

This lesson discusses the mental health challenges faced by gender-diverse young people, including higher rates of depression and anxiety due to societal pressures. It explores how creative arts programs can provide supportive environments that foster a sense of community and self-worth, thereby enhancing mental wellbeing. Case studies like 'Mapping Q' and shOUT Youth Chorus illustrate the effectiveness of such initiatives in reducing mental health risks among gender-diverse youth through artistic expression and social engagement.
