## ‘Anything is Possible’: the Arts and Social Inclusion

Pat Rix's article '‘Anything is Possible’: the Arts and Social Inclusion' details the evolution of Tutti Ensemble, an Adelaide-based organization that promotes social inclusion through music. Initiated as a small singing group for individuals with intellectual disabilities in 1997, Tutti has grown into a large-scale performance ensemble renowned for its artistic achievements and inclusive practices. The article highlights how Tutti fosters genuine social inclusion by embracing diversity and creating an environment of mutual acceptance and support. Rix argues that opportunities for creativity can address societal fragmentation and contribute to the empowerment of individuals with disabilities. The organization's success in both social inclusion and artistic excellence demonstrates the potential for transformative impact through arts initiatives.
