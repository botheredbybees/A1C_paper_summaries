## Healthcare and Arts Integration

This chapter discusses the integration of performing and visual arts into healthcare settings as a means to enhance patient and provider experiences. It highlights how art can transform stressful hospital environments into soothing spaces that aid in healing processes. The Center for Performing Arts Medicine (CPAM) at Houston Methodist supports this integration through various activities such as live music, curated visual art displays, and bedside art programs. These initiatives aim to improve health outcomes, patient experience, and reduce healthcare costs. Challenges include curating appropriate arts programming considering diverse audiences, environmental factors, and the need for easy consumption regardless of mental state.
