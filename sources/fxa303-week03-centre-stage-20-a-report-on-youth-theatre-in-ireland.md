## Centre Stage +20: A Report on Youth Theatre in Ireland

The document 'Centre Stage +20' is a comprehensive research report by Rhona Dunnett for Youth Theatre Ireland that details the current state and development of youth theatres across Ireland. The study aims to present an overview of how youth theatre has evolved over three decades, providing insights into various aspects such as models, structures, funding, practices, members, leaders, and outcomes. Key findings highlight the importance of real interaction and active engagement in theatre-making for young people's wellbeing and personal development, emphasizing challenges faced by leaders due to underpayment and increased regulatory compliance. The report advocates for sustained funding and resources that recognize youth theatres' contribution to mental well-being and community engagement.
