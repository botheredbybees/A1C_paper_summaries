## FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care: Final Reflections

This unit concludes the FXA100 course on 'The Arts and Dementia Care,' acknowledging participants' contributions and thanking various personnel involved in its delivery. It highlights the importance of art-based interventions for dementia care and their positive impact on quality of life. The lesson also provides an overview of future units within the Creative Arts & Health program, including specific courses for 2026-27 semesters and trimesters. Additionally, it emphasizes the significance of completing teaching and unit evaluations to enhance the learning experience.
