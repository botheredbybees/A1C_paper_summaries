## 60 Inspiring Examples of Black and White Photography

The document '60 Inspiring Examples of Black and White Photography' by Steph Nelson provides tips for photographers to enhance their black and white images effectively. It emphasizes the importance of clarity in creating a distinct contrast between black and white, which is crucial for impactful visual storytelling. The text also highlights the significance of using light and shadow to create dynamic compositions that stand out visually. Additionally, it encourages focusing on texture and shape as these elements become more pronounced without color to guide the viewer's eye. Nelson offers suggestions for ideal subjects such as scenes with high contrast between dark and light tones or architectural features like buildings against clear skies. Silhouettes are also recommended due to their simplicity in black and white photography. The article concludes by discussing how shooting patterns, particularly those found in nature and architecture, can be more captivating when presented without the distraction of color.
