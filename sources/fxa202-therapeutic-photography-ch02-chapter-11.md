## Voices from Practice: Therapeutic Photography

Chapter 11 of 'Therapeutic Photography: Enhancing Self-Esteem, Self-Efficacy and Resilience' by Neil Gibson compiles narratives from practitioners utilizing therapeutic photography in their work. Antonella Cunsolo discusses a project for breast cancer patients where they used photographs to express emotions, fostering mutual support among participants. Cristina Nunez shares her method 'The Self-Portrait Experience,' transforming personal struggles into art through self-portraits. Sophie Bjorkquist addresses unintentional mindless photography and promotes intentional seeing by engaging volunteers in Trafalgar Square with disposable cameras. Cathy Lander-Goldberg focuses on empowering girls and women through photography, writing, and collage workshops to increase self-awareness.
