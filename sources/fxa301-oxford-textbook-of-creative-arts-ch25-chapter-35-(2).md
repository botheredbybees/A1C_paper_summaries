## The Impact of Cultural Activities on Health and Well-being Across Lifespan

This chapter explores the influence of cultural activities on health and well-being across different stages of life. It discusses how music intervention in schools can enhance cooperation among children, reduce stress levels, and improve classroom climates. Research also highlights the potential benefits of cultural activities at work environments, such as reducing emotional exhaustion and fostering better social cohesion. Additionally, the chapter examines how arts-based programs for healthcare professionals and elderly individuals have positive impacts on mental health and physiological markers like blood pressure control. The studies suggest that interactive cultural experiences yield immediate psychological benefits while passive attendance may provide long-term health benefits.
