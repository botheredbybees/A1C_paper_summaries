## Community Arts, Employment and Poverty: Exploring the Roles of Musical Participation and Professionalisation in Health Equity

This chapter explores how participation in community arts initiatives can impact the socioeconomic status and employment opportunities for individuals living in poverty, with a focus on Vancouver's Downtown Eastside. It discusses the concept of health equity through the lens of socioeconomic status and examines specific cases where musical expression has helped individuals gain skills and improve their socioeconomic standing.
