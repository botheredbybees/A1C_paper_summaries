## Week 10 Overview: Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing

This week's lesson in FXA303 Creative Arts focuses on the importance of cultural identity and connections for mental wellbeing among Aboriginal young people. It highlights how strengthening cultural ties can counteract social isolation and enhance a sense of purpose and belonging. The course discusses cultural differences that influence perceptions of mental distress and appropriate interventions, emphasizing the role of trauma-aware and culturally-specific arts practices in supporting mental health for indigenous children and youth. Additionally, it explores arts-based programs aiding asylum seekers and refugees to build self-confidence and identity amidst their new environment.
