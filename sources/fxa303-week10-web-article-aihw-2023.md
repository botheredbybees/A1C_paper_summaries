## Australia's welfare

Australia's welfare 2025 is the AIHW's 17th biennial welfare report that provides a comprehensive overview of the welfare and wellbeing of Australians, covering topics such as aged care, disability support, employment, education, housing, income, justice and safety, population trends, First Nations people issues, supports and services.
