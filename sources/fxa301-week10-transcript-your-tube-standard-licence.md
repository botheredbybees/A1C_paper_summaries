## Your Tube Standard Licence - Choir of Hard Knocks

This transcript details the story and impact of the choir of hard knocks (CHoH), a television series and real-life ensemble founded by Jonathan Welsh in Melbourne. The show began as an idea to combine choirs with social issues like homelessness, aiming to raise awareness and provide support through music. Initially skeptical, choir members struggled with personal challenges such as addiction and mental health issues but found community and purpose within the group. Despite its success, controversies arose over management of funds, leading to a split between Jonathan and the managing charity Reckling. The story concludes on an optimistic note, reflecting on the personal journey and new responsibilities Jonathan has taken on, including caring for his nephews.
