## Planning Your Photo Essay: Arts and Health Themes

This lesson focuses on the theme of 'Arts and Health' for creating a photo essay. It encourages students to explore various topics related to this theme, such as mindfulness activities in nature, creative self-care, mental health issues, benefits of sports and exercise, emotional support from pets or care dogs, living with chronic illnesses, and community endeavors promoting social connection. The lesson provides examples of past student essays on these themes along with their critical reflections.
