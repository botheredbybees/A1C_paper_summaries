## Medical humanities in medical education and practice

This commentary discusses the role and importance of medical humanities (MH) in contemporary medical education and practice, emphasizing its relevance amid rapid technological advancements in healthcare. The authors argue that MH can foster essential qualities such as professionalism, self-awareness, communication skills, reflective practice, empathy, and resilience among healthcare professionals. They suggest integrating MH into medical curricula to balance the scientific with the humanistic aspects of care, addressing critical issues like stress reduction, burnout mitigation, and overall well-being for both patients and caregivers.
