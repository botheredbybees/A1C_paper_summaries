## Dramatherapy, Dance Therapy and Applied Theatre

The lesson covers the therapeutic uses of dramatherapy, dance therapy, and applied theatre in dementia care. Dramatherapy involves psychological therapy using drama and theatrical techniques to enable clients to explore life experiences indirectly through storytelling, myths, puppetry, masks, movement, dramatisation, and improvisation. Dance therapy focuses on the interconnection between body movements and emotional well-being, promoting physical health and enhancing cognitive function among elderly individuals. Applied theatre employs participatory methods in non-traditional settings to address social issues such as racism, domestic violence, immigration, and ageing.
