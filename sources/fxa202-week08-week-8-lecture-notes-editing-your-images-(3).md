## Week 8 Lecture Notes: Editing Your Images

This lecture note introduces students to the concept of using photography as self-therapy and explores various activities such as mindfulness, nature walks, and the 'photo a day' challenge. The text discusses how these practices can enhance mental health and suggests incorporating them into photo essays. It also touches on the biophilia hypothesis, emphasizing the importance of connecting with nature for well-being. The note provides examples from Australian photographer Hailey Bartholemew's TED Talk to illustrate how consistent photography practice can positively influence oneâ€™s perception of the world. Additionally, it encourages students to explore creative approaches beyond traditional documentary methods, such as digital compositing or mixed media techniques, and advises on editing software options and tips for effective image lighting. The lecture concludes with an invitation for students to engage in a 'before and after' photo editing exercise for discussion.
