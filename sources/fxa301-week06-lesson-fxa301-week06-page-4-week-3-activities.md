## Activities for Week 6: FXA301 Arts in the Community

This week's lesson focuses on understanding the impact of music and dance on health and well-being. It includes a reading assignment from Sheerington et al.'s study on social dancing to reduce falls in older adults. The class also explores case studies such as Glasgow Centre for Population Health’s 'People Change Lives' initiative, which highlights the role of community arts projects in fostering positive change. Additionally, students are encouraged to consider how dance can be used as a therapeutic intervention for neurological disorders and Parkinson's disease, with references to various research articles on these topics.
