## DSM-5-TR Neurocognitive Disorders Supplement

The document is a supplement to the Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders, Fifth Edition, Text Revision (DSM-5-TR), focusing on updates related to neurocognitive disorders. It includes revisions for major and mild neurocognitive disorders (NCDs) with specific medical etiologies such as Alzheimer's disease, frontotemporal degeneration, vascular disease, traumatic brain injury, substance/medication-induced disorder, HIV infection, prion diseases, Parkinson’s disease, Huntington’s disease, and others. The document provides updated diagnostic criteria, coding notes, and procedures for accurately diagnosing these conditions. It emphasizes the importance of clinical judgment in applying the diagnostic criteria rather than using them mechanically. The supplement is intended to complement the DSM-5-TR manual and reflects updates in ICD-10-CM codes effective from October 2022.
