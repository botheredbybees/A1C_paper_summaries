## Creative Approaches to Learning and Leadership

Tony Gillam explores the interplay between creativity, learning, and leadership in mental health practice, emphasizing their mutual influence. He reflects on his personal experiences as a clinical manager and senior lecturer to illustrate how these concepts can be applied in real-world settings. Key themes include resilient leadership, servant leadership, and transformational leadership styles. Gillam also discusses the distinction between management and leadership development, highlighting the importance of fostering a creative organizational climate that encourages role-modelling everyday creativity.
