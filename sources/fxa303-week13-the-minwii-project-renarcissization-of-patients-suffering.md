## The MINWii Project: Renarcissization of Patients Suffering from Alzheimer’s Disease Through Video Game-Based Music Therapy

The document discusses the MINWii project, a serious video game designed as a music therapy tool for Alzheimer's and dementia patients aimed at improving their self-image through interactive gameplay with minimal cognitive and motor requirements. The system uses Nintendo Wiimotes to enable elderly gamers to improvise or play pre-defined songs on a virtual keyboard. Initial qualitative testing showed strong patient interest and potential caregiver benefits, suggesting its use could help reduce behavioral symptoms leading to institutionalization while offering an engaging, low-cost therapeutic option for families.
