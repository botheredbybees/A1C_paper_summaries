## Evaluating benefits to health and wellbeing

This lesson discusses the growing evidence base for the positive impact of creative arts on individual and community health and wellbeing. It contrasts traditional randomized controlled trials with qualitative research methods often used in this field due to their suitability for exploring human and social phenomena. The content emphasizes that qualitative research, including case studies, interviews, and participant feedback, is crucial for evaluating the effectiveness of arts projects aimed at improving health outcomes.
