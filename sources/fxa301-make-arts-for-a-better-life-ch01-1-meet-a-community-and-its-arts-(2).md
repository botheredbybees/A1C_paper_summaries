## Ethnomusicology and Applied Arts: A Guide to Community Engagement

This excerpt introduces a guide to ethnomusicology and applied arts, focusing on community engagement and the use of arts for practical goals. The chapter outlines seven steps to help individuals and communities utilize locally accessible arts for betterment. It emphasizes the importance of building relationships, researching performance contexts ethically, and giving back to communities. Additionally, it discusses concepts such as 'a better life,' creativity, and ethical responsibilities in applied work. The text draws on experiences like Kathleen's and highlights contributions from ethnomusicology, anthropology, folkloristics, and community music. It addresses the need for interdisciplinary approaches and training materials for applied work.
