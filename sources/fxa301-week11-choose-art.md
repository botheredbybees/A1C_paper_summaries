## Choose Art

Choose Art is an online platform designed to promote arts and cultural activities for deaf and disabled individuals, ensuring their inclusion in the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS). The website emphasizes the importance of accessibility and equality in providing arts opportunities. It highlights that arts participation can significantly enhance skills development, social interaction, self-esteem, and creative expression for people with disabilities. Choose Art is a collaborative project supported by various state and federal cultural organizations, emphasizing its commitment to national inclusivity. The platform also acknowledges the traditional custodians of the land on which it operates, showing respect for Indigenous cultures. This initiative plays a crucial role in promoting arts as an essential part of community life and personal development for individuals with disabilities.
