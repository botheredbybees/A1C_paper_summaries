## Making Art with Communities: A Work Guide

The document 'Making Art with Communities: A Work Guide' serves as a comprehensive resource aimed at artists, community leaders, and managers engaged in collaborative arts projects within Victorian communities. Initiated by Arts Victoria and supported by VicHealth and Arts Access, this guide addresses the growing demand for practical advice on community-based arts initiatives. It not only provides logistical guidance but also explores the broader contexts, philosophies, and histories of such practices. The guide emphasizes the transformative power of art in enhancing individual and communal well-being, particularly among marginalized groups. By detailing various models of collaboration and offering checklists, templates, and resource links, it supports project development from conception to execution. This work underscores the significance of ethical considerations, inclusivity, and cultural sensitivity when working with diverse communities.
