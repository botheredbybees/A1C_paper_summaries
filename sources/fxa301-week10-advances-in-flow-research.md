## Advances in Flow Research

The book 'Advances in Flow Research' edited by Corinna Peifer and Stefan Engeser presents a comprehensive overview of research on the concept of flow, originally introduced by Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi. The second edition expands upon the foundational ideas, detailing how the experience of flow—characterized by intense engagement and absorption in an activity—is relevant across various fields such as psychology, sports, leisure activities, and cultural studies. Contributions from multiple disciplines highlight new directions for research, emphasizing empirical methods to study this phenomenon. Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi’s foreword acknowledges the significant progress made since his initial work on flow, expressing optimism about the future of the field due to the advancements presented in the book.
