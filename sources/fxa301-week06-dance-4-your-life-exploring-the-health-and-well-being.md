## dance 4 your life: exploring the health and well-being implications of a contemporary dance intervention for female adolescents

This study evaluated the physiological and psychological impact of contemporary dance classes on female adolescents aged 14 in the UK. The research involved fifty-five participants recruited from secondary schools who underwent a series of assessments before and after a program of contemporary dance focused on building muscular strength. Key findings revealed significant improvements in aerobic capacity, upper body strength, self-esteem, and attitudes towards dance but did not show changes in flexibility or intrinsic motivation levels. The study concluded that participating in dance classes can enhance physical fitness components as well as psychological well-being among adolescent females, fostering positive attitudes toward such activities.
