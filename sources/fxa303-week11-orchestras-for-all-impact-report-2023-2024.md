## Orchestras for All Impact Report 2023-2024

The Orchestras for All Impact Report 2023-2024 documents the organization's mission to make music accessible and inclusive for young people across the UK, focusing on empowering youth and promoting systemic change. Key highlights include the introduction of a new Co-Chair structure with a young trustee joining as Co-Chair, enhancing decision-making processes by actively involving young voices in leadership roles. Programs such as the National Orchestra for All (NOFA), Modulo, and Music Leadership Training (MLT) were emphasized, aiming to foster musical, social, and creative skills among participants while driving inclusive practices within music education sectors. The report also highlights NOFA's most diverse cohort yet and initiatives like 'Sounds of Change,' a piece co-created by the NOFA members addressing contemporary issues through spoken word and orchestral composition. Additionally, Orchestras for All prioritizes youth leadership by incorporating alumni into management roles and establishing a Youth Leadership Coordinator position.
