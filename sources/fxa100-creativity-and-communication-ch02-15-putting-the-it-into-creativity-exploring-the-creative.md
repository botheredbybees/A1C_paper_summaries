## Putting the IT into Creativity: Exploring the Creative Potential of Technology

This chapter discusses how technology can serve as a catalyst for creativity among individuals living with dementia, emphasizing the role of everyday gadgets such as computers, mobile phones, and digital cameras. It highlights that while technology is often perceived merely in terms of functionality, it offers significant potential for engagement, play, exploration, and self-expression. The author presents examples from her own work where people have used technology to create art, write poetry, compile life histories, and share experiences online. She also underscores the importance of considering ethical issues related to sharing creative works publicly via the internet. The chapter concludes by encouraging readers to explore their personal use of technology for creativity and leisure.
