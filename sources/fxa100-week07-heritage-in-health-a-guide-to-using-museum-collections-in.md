## Heritage in Health: A Guide to Using Museum Collections in Hospitals and Other Healthcare Settings

The document 'Heritage in Health' is a comprehensive guide aimed at museum professionals seeking to integrate their collections into healthcare environments. It outlines the benefits of engaging patients with museum objects, which can improve perceptions of health and wellbeing, enhance social interactions, and provide stimulating experiences for those confined to hospital wards or care homes. The guide emphasizes the importance of collaboration between museums and healthcare providers, ensuring that activities are tailored to meet the needs of diverse patient populations, including those with special requirements such as dementia or neurological conditions. It underscores the value of evidence-based practice in measuring the impact on participant wellbeing and encourages further research and implementation of museum-based interventions in health settings.
