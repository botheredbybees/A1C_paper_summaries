## Creative and credible evaluation for arts, health and well-being: opportunities and challenges of co-production

This article presents findings from a UK knowledge exchange project that aimed to enhance evaluation practices in the arts and health sector. The project involved stakeholders such as evaluators, researchers, artists, healthcare professionals, commissioners, and funders, who participated through surveys, interviews, and focus groups. Key challenges identified include a lack of standardized evaluation frameworks and difficulties faced by artists within evaluation discourse. Opportunities were also highlighted, particularly in light of evolving health policies that support arts initiatives. The study underscores the importance of co-production among stakeholders to strengthen evaluation practices and develop the sector further. Despite these opportunities, structural and cultural barriers impede effective collaboration. Recent initiatives aimed at promoting best practice are discussed.
