## FXA303 Week 11: Creative Arts Projects and Therapeutic Programs for Gender Diverse Youth

This document outlines the content of Week 11 in FXA303, a course focused on creative arts and mental wellbeing. It highlights the importance of creative arts programs in improving mental health for gender diverse youth who are at increased risk of depression, anxiety, and other emotional challenges. The text emphasizes the role of these programs in providing safe spaces for young people to build supportive communities, enhance resilience against discrimination, and foster self-esteem. Additionally, it discusses how trauma-informed arts-based projects benefit marginalized youth, including those experiencing homelessness or involvement with the justice system, by promoting mental wellbeing, reducing re-offending rates, and enhancing life orientation.
