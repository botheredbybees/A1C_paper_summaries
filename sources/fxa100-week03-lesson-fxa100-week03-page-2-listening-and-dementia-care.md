## Listening-based Interventions in Dementia Care

This lesson explores the therapeutic value of music for dementia care through case studies like the Music and Memory Project. It discusses how listening to personalized playlists can activate memories and improve well-being in individuals with cognitive decline. Theories on why musical memories remain accessible despite brain atrophy are presented, along with the role of dopamine in triggering emotional responses and memory retrieval.
