## The Schisto Refugee Camp's Colourful Street Art Project, Greece 2018

The article discusses a street art project called 'Coexisting in colour' initiated by AptART (Awareness & Prevention Through Art) and the Danish Refugee Council at Schisto refugee camp in Athens, Greece. This project aims to promote community cohesion among refugees from Afghanistan, Iraq, and Syria who are living in uncertain conditions due to bureaucratic delays and a lack of resources. The intervention includes painting various structures within the camp with vibrant colors and messages that encourage unity and hope. Activities such as workshops on typography and drawing scenes from favorite stories also engage children and youth, fostering a sense of belonging and providing them with creative outlets for self-expression. This initiative not only beautifies the camp but also serves to uplift the spirits of its inhabitants and create a more positive living environment.
