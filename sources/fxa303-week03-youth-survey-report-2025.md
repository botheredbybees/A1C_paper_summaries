## Youth Survey Report 2025

The 2025 Youth Survey report by Mission Australia highlights the challenges and aspirations of young people aged 14 to 19 in Australia. The top issues identified are cost of living (64%), mental health (29%), climate change and the environment (27%), and housing and homelessness (25%). Despite these concerns, many respondents remain optimistic about their futures, expressing hopes for employment, financial stability, relationships, and educational opportunities. Key findings reveal that while most young people are engaged in studying and working to save money or gain experience, 15% have experienced financial hardship. Mental health remains a significant issue, with 39% reporting stress related to mental health and wellbeing, and 19% experiencing high levels of psychological distress. School-related issues were the primary source of stress for many respondents, impacting their ability to achieve work or study goals. The report underscores the need for greater investment in affordable housing, early access to mental health support, and policies that foster inclusive and supportive environments for young people.
