## Brain Development and Cognitive Reserve

This lesson discusses brain development from fetal stages to adolescence, focusing on the growth of grey and white matter, synaptic pruning, and the role of creativity. It highlights how experiences in early childhood can impact synapse development and resilience building against stress. The concept of cognitive reserve is introduced as a protective factor against cognitive decline and dementia, influenced by lifelong learning activities such as education and arts engagement. Studies show that higher levels of cognitive reserve correlate with reduced risk of developing dementia.
