## Evaluating Community Arts & Community Well-Being: An Evaluation Guide for Community Arts Practitioners

The 'Evaluating Community Arts & Community Well-Being' guide by Clare Keating provides a comprehensive framework for community arts practitioners to evaluate their projects systematically. It aims to help practitioners assess the social outcomes of their initiatives and document these findings effectively. The guide covers various stages including preparation, planning, determining evaluation indicators, data collection, analysis, report writing, and continuous improvement. The resource addresses common challenges faced by community arts workers in evaluating their work and highlights its importance for securing funding, improving projects, and fostering partnerships within the community. By offering both an 'Express' version and a more comprehensive guide, it caters to different levels of expertise and time constraints. This document is crucial for demonstrating the positive social impact of community arts activities beyond anecdotal evidence.
