## Activities for Week 11 - FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care

This week's activities in the course 'The Arts and Dementia Care' involve reading a review article by Karkou and Meekums from the Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews (2023) on Dance Movement Therapy for dementia. The module requires students to discuss characteristics needed for studies providing scientific evidence for health department funding and how dance therapy interventions might affect caregivers in residential or community care settings. Students also engage in a quiz and are encouraged to draft their essay proposals, ensuring they refine ideas from research findings.
