## Starting Out: Identifying Purpose and Planning Arts-Based Projects for Persons with Dementia

This chapter discusses the importance of defining the purpose and vision of arts-based projects aimed at improving the quality of life for people with dementia. It emphasizes that the nature of these projects can vary widely, from entertainment-focused activities to community-oriented initiatives fostering social inclusion and active participation. The authors stress the need for careful planning and investment in resources, both internal and external, to ensure sustainability and engagement. They highlight the role of creativity in enhancing self-expression, communication, memory recall, and overall well-being among individuals with dementia. Examples from various arts disciplines such as literature, music, visual arts, and drama are provided to illustrate potential benefits and approaches.
