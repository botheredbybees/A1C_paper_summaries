## Museums as Spaces for Wellbeing: A Second Report from the National Alliance for Museums, Health and Wellbeing

The report 'Museums as Spaces for Wellbeing' by Desmarais et al. (2018) explores how museums can contribute to public health and well-being in the UK. Building upon a previous survey, this second report delves deeper into the innovative activities of museums responding creatively to demographic changes, health inequalities, holistic healthcare approaches, co-creation methods, green wellbeing initiatives, and nuanced understandings of well-being. The authors highlight specific case studies and good practices from regional trainings across England, emphasizing the potential for museums to foster social inclusion, mental and physical health support, and community engagement at various life stages. Additionally, the report outlines the role of the National Alliance for Museums, Health & Wellbeing in advocating for museum involvement in public health initiatives and suggests future directions for research and collaboration between the cultural and healthcare sectors.
