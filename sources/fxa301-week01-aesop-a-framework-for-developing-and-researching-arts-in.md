## Aesop: A Framework for Developing and Researching Arts in Health Programmes

The article introduces Aesop, a framework designed to address the challenges faced by arts in health research. The authors argue that while there is an increasing number of activities and publications in this field, research methods are often misunderstood or incorrectly applied due to the interdisciplinary nature of arts and health. They discuss the need for a standardized approach to guide researchers through the development, design, delivery, and dissemination of arts-in-health programs. Aesop synthesizes methodologies from both art and health research fields, providing clear guidelines that help bridge the gap between artistic practices and scientific rigor. The framework aims to facilitate collaboration among artists, researchers, policy-makers, and funders by offering a comprehensive yet flexible methodological approach. This article is significant for creative arts and health practitioners as it provides a practical tool for enhancing the credibility and effectiveness of their research projects.
