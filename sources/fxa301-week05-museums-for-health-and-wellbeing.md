## Museums for Health and Wellbeing

The document 'Museums for Health and Wellbeing' is a comprehensive report by the National Alliance for Museums, Health and Wellbeing that details current practices and initiatives within UK museums aimed at enhancing public health and wellbeing. The report outlines various activities such as educational programs, vocational training, and support services for marginalized communities. It also highlights research into how museum engagement positively impacts individual and societal health outcomes. Key findings include the breadth of existing projects and a recognition of gaps in knowledge and resources needed to sustain these initiatives. Recommendations focus on fostering collaboration between museums and healthcare sectors, developing best practices, and advocating for increased funding and policy support.
