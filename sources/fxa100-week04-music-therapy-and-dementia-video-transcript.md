## Music Therapy and Dementia video - transcript

This document provides a transcript of a music therapy session in a residential nursing home called Evergreen, which caters to individuals with various levels of care. The therapist emphasizes two primary goals: ensuring that each person leaves the session feeling better than when they arrived and encouraging self-expression through different forms such as speaking, singing, or playing instruments. Music is particularly meaningful for this demographic due to a lifetime of accumulated musical experiences. For instance, songs like 'Blue Skies' can evoke deep personal memories and provide validation of past experiences, enhancing both emotional well-being and engagement in life.
