## FXA300 Music, Mind and Body - Week 3 Activities

The content for FXA300 Music, Mind and Body's Week 3 focuses on music as a study aid. Recommended reading includes an article by Daleiden (2026) discussing the effects of listening to music during studying. The lesson encourages students to engage in discussions about explicit and implicit memory through musical contexts and explore their reminiscence bumps related to music preferences. It also informs about upcoming Quiz 1, which assesses knowledge from weeks one through four, including topics like music and memory, brain anatomy, and processing mechanisms. References are provided for further reading.
