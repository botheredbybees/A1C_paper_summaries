## For family, friends and carers - Dementia Australia

The article discusses the challenges and support available for individuals in a caregiving role for someone with dementia. It highlights that approximately 1.7 million Australians provide care to those living with dementia, often facing significant changes in their daily lives. The website emphasizes the importance of self-care for caregivers and provides various resources such as the National Dementia Helpline, counselling services, peer support programs, and Carer Gateway. A video transcript includes interviews with carers sharing their experiences, insights, and lessons learned from caring for a loved one with dementia. Additionally, it addresses specific issues faced by male caregivers through a webinar presentation.
