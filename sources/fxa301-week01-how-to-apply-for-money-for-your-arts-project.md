## How to apply for money for your arts project

This document, 'How to Apply for Money for Your Arts Project,' provides guidance on applying for the Arts Projects for Individuals and Groups grant offered by Creative Australia. The guide explains that applicants must be Australian-born or have permanent residency in Australia and can apply either as individuals or as part of a group. The application process involves filling out an online form, selecting an assessment panel from various arts categories (such as Dance, Literature, Theatre, etc.), addressing three criteria—Quality, Viability, and Impact—and submitting support materials like work samples and letters of endorsement via the Creative Australia website. This guide is aimed at making the grant application process accessible to a broader audience, including those with limited English proficiency or disabilities.
