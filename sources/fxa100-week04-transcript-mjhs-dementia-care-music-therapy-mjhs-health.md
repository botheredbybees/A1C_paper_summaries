## MJHS Dementia Care: Music Therapy - MJHS Health System

The transcript details a grant-funded program by the New York State Department of Health that explores music therapy's impact on reducing agitation, wandering, and depression in individuals with mid-stage dementia. The program includes six protocols: three group-based (sing-along, tonal music and movement) and three individual-based (range of motion, music-assisted bath or shower, and wound care). Music therapy is noted to have significant positive effects, both statistically and substantively, on the residents' depression levels, agitation, social engagement, and overall quality of life. The program also improves caregivers' productivity and attitudes towards their work, contributing positively to staff retention.
