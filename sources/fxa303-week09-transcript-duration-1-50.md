## Transcript of Flood Experience

The transcript describes an individual's experience during a flood event. The narrator and their husband were inside when the water rapidly rose to nearly two meters high, threatening their escape. They witnessed cars floating in the river and a bridge being washed away. Emotional responses ranged from fear to anxiety about losing control. The narrators reflect on the profound change brought by the disaster, noting that even basic landmarks like the river's flow have shifted.
