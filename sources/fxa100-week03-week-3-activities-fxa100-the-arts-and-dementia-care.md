## Week 3 Activities - FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care

This document outlines the activities for Week 3 of the FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care course at UTAS, focusing on the impact of music in dementia care. It references Levitin's work from his book 'This is Your Brain on Music,' which discusses why we like certain types of music and how it affects our brain. The text emphasizes the importance of understanding cultural nuances when selecting music for individuals with dementia. Additionally, it provides links to supplementary materials such as videos and articles that further explore how music can serve as a therapeutic tool in enhancing cognitive function and emotional well-being among those affected by dementia.
