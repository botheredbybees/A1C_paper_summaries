## Week 1 Introduction and Overview

The lesson provides an introduction to the course FXA302, which explores the connection between arts and health. It emphasizes the growing body of research demonstrating mental and physical health benefits from engaging with creative arts activities. Two examples are highlighted: art therapy programs for trauma recovery among refugees and veterans, and dance initiatives that improve Parkinson's patients' balance and mobility. The lesson introduces Health Humanities as an interdisciplinary field offering new approaches to understanding illness and disability through the lens of creative arts. Students are encouraged to explore intersections between humanities, arts, and medicine, focusing on narrative medicine and various assessment tasks.
