## Engaging in Community Music: An Introduction

The chapter 'Ways Forward' from the book 'Engaging in Community Music: An Introduction,' edited by Higgins, Lee, and Willingham (2017), outlines key principles and practices within community music. It emphasizes the transformative power of participatory music-making to change people's lives, highlighting inclusivity, empathy, and social justice as core values. The chapter explores various contexts where community music can operate, such as educational settings and therapeutic environments. It also addresses the importance of strategic leadership, facilitation skills, and mindfulness in fostering meaningful musical experiences. The authors underscore the role of community musicians in creating spaces for diverse voices to be heard, promoting social cohesion and personal well-being through music.
