## Improvisation and Other Dramas

This chapter discusses the benefits of improvisational drama for individuals with dementia. John Killick, based on his experiences organizing workshops known as 'Funshops,' highlights how these activities engage participants through humor and spontaneity, fostering social connections and emotional relaxation. The text includes various exercises like miming professions, creating statues, and playing Grandmother's Footsteps. It also describes the impact of improvisational drama on individuals' self-perception and relationships with others, such as forming new friendships and breaking down barriers between participants.
