## Personal Storytelling and Memoir Writing

The Personal Storytelling and Memoir Writing course offered by the Writers' Centre is designed to help individuals unlock their creativity through storytelling about personal experiences. The course, led by experienced tutors Nat Newman and Valerie Khoo, spans six weeks with an estimated three to four hours of work per week. It provides a framework for crafting engaging stories from lived experiences, focusing on techniques such as sensory writing, story structure, character development, and the healing power of self-expression. Participants gain skills in transforming personal ideas into meaningful narratives, learn essential storytelling elements, and receive feedback from their tutors each week. The online format offers flexibility with no set class times, allowing students to fit the course around their schedules. This course is ideal for those interested in memoir writing but not suited for fiction writers.
