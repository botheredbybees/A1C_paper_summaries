## Reflection on the Therapeutic and Artistic Process of Creating a Photo Essay about IVF

The reflection discusses the therapeutic and artistic process of creating a photo essay about the author's personal experience with in vitro fertilization (IVF). The author chose to tell her IVF story as a way to explore vulnerability, truth, and emotional healing. Infertility and its treatments often cause significant psychological distress, including stress, depression, and anxiety. By documenting her journey through images and text, the author found a sense of empowerment and self-knowledge, which was both heartbreaking and healing. The photo essay includes seven photographs that capture various stages of the IVF process, from medication injection to embryo transfer, each edited in black and white with strong contrast to convey a sense of reality and drama. The editing choices also include selective use of color for emphasis on specific objects or emotions. The author emphasizes the importance of mindful photography and honest storytelling, noting that sharing personal experiences through art can contribute positively to mental health.
