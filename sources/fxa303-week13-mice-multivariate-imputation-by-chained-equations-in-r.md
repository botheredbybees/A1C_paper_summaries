## mice: Multivariate Imputation by Chained Equations in R

The article introduces the R package mice, which is used for imputing incomplete multivariate data through chained equations. This software extends its predecessor, mice 1.0, with enhanced functionalities such as general analysis of imputed data and an expanded range of models that allow pooling of results. It also includes capabilities like automatic predictor selection, specialized pooling routines, model selection tools, diagnostic graphs, and improvements in handling categorical data to prevent issues caused by perfect prediction. The package supports the creation of complex relationships using passive imputation techniques, providing a comprehensive framework for addressing problems associated with incomplete datasets in applied settings.
