## FXA303 Week 4 Introduction

This document is an introduction to Week 4 of FXA303: Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing. The text highlights the focus on neurodiversity, particularly autism, as a key theme for understanding brain development differences in children and adolescents. It underscores the importance of early intervention through creative arts and play therapies, noting that intensive work with autistic children can be most effective due to increased neural plasticity during their formative years. The document mentions the role of music and play-based interventions in supporting mental health, communication, motor skills, and social development for those on the autism spectrum. It also provides guidance on preparing for Quiz 1 by revising module materials through various study methods such as summarizing notes or creating flashcards. Additionally, it encourages engagement with web lectures and recordings available to support understanding of upcoming assessment tasks.
