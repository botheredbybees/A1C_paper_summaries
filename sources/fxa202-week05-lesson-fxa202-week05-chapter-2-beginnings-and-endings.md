## Structuring Your Visual Story

This lesson focuses on structuring visual stories through photo essays. It covers linear and non-linear story structures, the importance of beginnings and endings in storytelling, lead photos to engage readers, establishing shots to provide context, and closing shots for narrative resolution. Examples are provided from news outlets like The Guardian and ABC News to illustrate effective use of these techniques.
