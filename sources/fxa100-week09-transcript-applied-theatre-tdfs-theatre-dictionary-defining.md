## Transcript: Applied Theatre -- TDF's Theatre Dictionary -- Defining Theatre Terms

The transcript defines and discusses the concept of applied theatre, where drama techniques are used to address social issues. An example given is 'Choices,' an interactive play by Looking for Lilith Theater Company that tackles cyberbullying through audience interaction. The piece highlights how individuals can advocate for changes within a narrative and emphasizes the role of active participation from those vested in the issue. Applied theatre transcends traditional performance spaces, occurring in classrooms, community centers, parks, and other settings. It encompasses various forms such as drama therapy, educational programs, conflict resolution initiatives, and political theatre.
