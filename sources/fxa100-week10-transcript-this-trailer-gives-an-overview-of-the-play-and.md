## Transcript of Play Trailer Discussing Dementia Care

The transcript is an overview of a theater project aimed at raising awareness about dementia care in the UK. Professor Justine Schneider describes her research which involved field notes from NHS hospital wards treating individuals with severe dementia. A playwright was commissioned to create a play based on these detailed and poetic field notes. The play, titled 'No Agenda,' serves as an educational tool for healthcare assistants (HCAs) and aims to improve the understanding of dementia care among various stakeholders. Post-show workshops allow HCAs to discuss their feelings and learn from each other's experiences. Audience reactions highlight the emotional impact of the play, with many participants indicating a desire to return or recommend it to others due to its educational value.
