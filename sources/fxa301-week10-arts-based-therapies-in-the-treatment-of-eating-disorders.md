## Arts-Based Therapies in the Treatment of Eating Disorders

The article explores the incorporation of arts-based therapies, such as music therapy, dance/movement therapy, and creative-arts therapy, in the treatment of eating disorders like anorexia nervosa (AN), bulimia nervosa (BN), and binge eating disorder (BED). Despite their growing use in residential treatment programs across North America, there is a lack of empirical evidence supporting their efficacy. The authors conducted a survey among 22 such programs, finding that all but three incorporate arts-based therapies weekly into patient care. However, they noted the absence of robust research to substantiate the benefits of these therapies specifically for eating disorder patients. This gap in knowledge highlights the need for further investigation into how arts-based approaches can positively impact treatment outcomes within this specialized context.
