## Week 6 Activities - FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care

The lesson for Week 6 of FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care focuses on the impact of art-making activities for individuals living with dementia. It emphasizes a case study by Mell et al., (2003) which highlights how an accomplished artist continued to paint despite frontotemporal dementia, thereby preserving creative abilities even as language and decision-making declined. This module encourages students to discuss how artwork from people with dementia can provide insights into their perceptions of the world and its implications for care practices. The course materials also include supplementary articles detailing various studies on art therapy and cognitive neuroscience in relation to dementia.
