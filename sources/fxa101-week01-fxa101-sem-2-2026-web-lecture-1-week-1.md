## FXA101 Sem 2 2026 - Web Lecture 1 Week 1

This document outlines the structure and requirements of the 'FXA101 Creativity for Life' course, focusing on the first week's content. It provides details about weekly web lectures, discussion forums, tutor drop-ins, and upcoming assessment tasks including a creative project journal and an online quiz. The lecture emphasizes the importance of creative projects that explore specific themes (Air, Cell, Sea) using unconventional materials or methods. Additionally, it discusses the neuroscience behind creativity and journalling, referencing research by Pennebaker on expressive writing's impact on immune system activation and studies on visual journalling for self-reflection and resilience-building. The text also highlights concerns regarding the use of generative artificial intelligence (Gen AI) in academic work due to issues with reliability, skill development, plagiarism, time management, and academic integrity.
