## The Left-Brain/Right-Brain Myth

This transcript discusses the myth of left-brain/right-brain dominance and how it originated from early neuroscientific research. It explains that while different sides of the brain do specialize in specific tasks, there is no evidence for an overall dominant hemisphere in individuals. The video reviews historical studies by Roger Sperry on split-brain patients and more recent fMRI scans showing no lateralization preference in healthy brains.
