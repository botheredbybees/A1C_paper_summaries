## Trauma and Expressive Arts Therapy

Cathy Malchiodi's book 'Trauma and Expressive Arts Therapy' discusses integrating expressive therapies into psychotherapy for trauma recovery. It outlines various chapters covering trauma-informed frameworks, brain-body connection, resilience development, and meaning-making processes. The text emphasizes the role of arts in fostering safe relationships, enhancing self-regulation, and addressing trauma through body awareness and narrative unpacking.
