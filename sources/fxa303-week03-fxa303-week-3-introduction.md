## FXA303 Week 3 Introduction

The document provides an introduction to the third week of FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing. It outlines how the course builds on previous weeks' content and introduces new concepts in adolescent development, particularly focusing on brain changes and cognitive processing during adolescence. Research from neuroscience and developmental psychology highlights that adolescence is a critical period for neural pathway formation, influencing cognitive functioning and mental wellbeing in adulthood. The document emphasizes the role of creative arts engagement as a tool to help adolescents express emotions, develop complex neural pathways, and build supportive social networks, which can enhance their mental health resilience during this transformative phase. Engaging with these activities also prepares students for upcoming assessments and reinforces key learning objectives.
