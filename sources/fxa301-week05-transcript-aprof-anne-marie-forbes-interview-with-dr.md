## Interview with Dr Annalise Rees about her Arts Project in Antarctica

Dr. Annalise Rees discusses her arts project involving a two-month voyage to the sub-Antarctic aboard Australia's Blue Water Research Vessel Investigator as an official voyage artist alongside choreographer James Batsell. The interview highlights field-based research, collaborative work with scientists and crew members, and the creative outcomes from this unique experience including dance installations and public presentations.
