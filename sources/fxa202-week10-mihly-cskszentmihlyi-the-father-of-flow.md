## Mihály Csíkszentmihályi: The Father of Flow

This document explores the work and impact of Mihály Csíkszentmihályi, known as the 'father of flow.' The text discusses his influential theory on optimal experiences, which he termed 'flow,' where an individual is fully immersed in a task to the point of experiencing deep focus and happiness. Key conditions for achieving flow include skill matching challenges, clear goals, and immediate feedback. Csíkszentmihályi's research shows that flow can be cultivated through various activities, not just sports but also work and personal pursuits, enhancing overall well-being and meaning in life. The article emphasizes the lasting influence of his theories on positive psychology and practical applications for improving quality of life.
