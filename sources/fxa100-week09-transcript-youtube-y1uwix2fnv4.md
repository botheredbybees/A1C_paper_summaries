## Interview with Sally Bailey on Drama Therapy

In this interview, Sally Bailey, a professor in the theater department and director of the drama therapy program, discusses her journey from professional theatre to becoming a drama therapist. She emphasizes the importance of creativity and storytelling in her work with residents at Meadowlark who participate in an acting group called 'Acting for Fun.' Bailey describes how she encourages participants to develop their improvisation skills and create a one-act play about how creativity keeps them young. She identifies herself as a facilitator, guiding individuals to express themselves through drama.
