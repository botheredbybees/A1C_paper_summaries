## Lecture Notes for FXA100 - The Arts and Dementia Care

The lecture notes cover the introduction to the unit FXA100 - The Arts and Dementia Care at the University of Tasmania. The course focuses on practical, arts-based interventions for dementia care including music, visual art, drama/theatre. It aims to provide evidence-based research on the benefits of creative arts in dementia care. Students are required to develop basic research skills, analyze case studies, and engage in informed debate. Assessment tasks include a research assignment, quizzes, discussion contributions, and a proposal essay with video pitch. The lecture also introduces various types of dementia such as Alzheimer's disease, vascular dementia, Lewy body dementia, fronto-temporal dementia, alcohol-related dementia, HIV-associated dementia, Down syndrome associated with Alzheimer’s, and chronic traumatic encephalopathy (CTE) dementia.
