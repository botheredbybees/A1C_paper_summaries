## Creative Health Review: How Policy Can Embrace Creative Health

The National Centre for Creative Health and the All-Party Parliamentary Group on Arts, Health and Wellbeing released a review titled 'Creative Health Review: How Policy Can Embrace Creative Health.' The report highlights how creative health can address major challenges in health and social care systems. It emphasizes that creative health should be integrated into 21st-century healthcare frameworks to promote holistic and person-centered care. Key messages include the necessity of cross-sector collaboration, investment by HM Treasury, and involvement of lived experience experts in strategy development. The report also presents recommendations for individual departments and local authorities, aiming to establish sustainable partnerships across various regions.
