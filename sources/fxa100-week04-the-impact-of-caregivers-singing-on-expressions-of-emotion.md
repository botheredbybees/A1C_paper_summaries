## The impact of caregivers' singing on expressions of emotion and resistance during morning care situations in persons with dementia

This study examines the impact of music therapeutic caregiving (MTC), specifically caregivers' singing, on persons with dementia (PWD) during morning care situations. The research involved ten PWDs in a Swedish nursing home and their professional caregivers. Video observations over eight weeks showed that MTC significantly reduced expressions of resistance such as pulling away or grabbing objects from the caregiver. Additionally, positive emotions like pleasure and alertness were more frequently observed when MTC was applied compared to usual care routines. This intervention offers a non-pharmacological approach that enhances the quality of life for PWDs by making morning care experiences more pleasant and less confrontational.
