## Intergenerational Choir Transcript

The intergenerational choir transcript highlights a community singing initiative that brings together adolescent students and seniors with dementia. Initially, participants had concerns about relating to each other due to the different stages of cognitive abilities among members. However, through engaging in music, they found common ground and discovered the therapeutic benefits of singing for those dealing with Alzheimer's. The transcript emphasizes how music can reconnect individuals who have lost their voice due to memory loss, stimulating short-term and long-term memory recall. Participants noted improvements in mood and social interaction, viewing music as a form of medicine that touches one’s soul deeply. Notably, the experience has been transformative for both the young participants learning to care for seniors and the senior members finding joy and community through shared musical experiences.
