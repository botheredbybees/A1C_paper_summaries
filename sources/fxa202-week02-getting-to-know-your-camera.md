## Getting to Know Your Camera

The lecture slides for Week 2 of the FXA202 course, titled 'Getting to Know Your Camera,' provide guidance on using various types of cameras effectively. The document emphasizes experimenting with features like depth of field and night photography settings for camera phones, while also detailing manual functions such as Aperture Priority (Av) and Shutter Priority (Tv or S) for digital SLR cameras. It encourages students to explore their camera's capabilities beyond automatic modes and suggests focusing on composition techniques like the rule of thirds, leading lines, and color contrasts. The importance of understanding color meanings and effectively using different lighting conditions is highlighted. Additionally, the slides mention potential challenges and benefits associated with using film cameras in the unit.
