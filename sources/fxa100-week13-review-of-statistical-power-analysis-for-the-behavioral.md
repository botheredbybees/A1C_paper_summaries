## Review of 'Statistical Power Analysis for the Behavioral Sciences' by Jacob Cohen

The review discusses Jacob Cohen's second edition of 'Statistical Power Analysis for the Behavioral Sciences', which has become a standard reference in the social sciences, particularly for determining appropriate sample sizes. The book covers various statistical tests and provides tables for power analysis and required sample size. Reviewer Peter Lachenbruch praises its clarity but suggests that a software-based approach might be more efficient. He also critiques another work by Helena Chmura Kraemer and Sue Thiemann, which simplifies power calculations using the intraclass correlation coefficient method, noting both its strengths and limitations.
