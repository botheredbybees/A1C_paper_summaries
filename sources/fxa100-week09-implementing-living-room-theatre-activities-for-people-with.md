## Implementing living room theatre activities for people with dementia on nursing home wards: a process evaluation study

This study evaluates the implementation of a new communication method, the 'Veder Method,' in Dutch nursing homes for people with dementia. The Veder Method uses theatrical elements combined with person-centered care techniques to enhance interaction between caregivers and residents. Key findings indicate that positive resident reactions and improved caregiver-resident relationships motivate staff to use this method. Essential factors for successful implementation include executive support, a structured action plan, visibility of the intervention within the organization, and a pioneering group initiating the process. However, high work pressure for care staff was identified as a barrier. The study contributes valuable insights into implementing effective person-centered interventions in dementia care settings.
