## Clown Doctors Australia Strategy 2025-2030

The document outlines the strategic plan and new branding of Clown Doctors Australia for the period 2025-2030. It introduces the organization's updated name, purpose, vision, values, and theory of change, emphasizing their role in enhancing well-being through humor and creativity within healthcare settings. The theory of change highlights how their programs impact patients, families, carers, and health workers across domains such as hope, empowerment, connection, and quality of care. The strategy focuses on five pillars: growing the Clown Doctors program intentionally, creating a sustainable Laughter Care framework, advocating for the benefits of healthcare clowning, scaling sustainably, and doubling fundraising revenue to $10 million by 2030. This strategic document underscores the organization's ambition to deepen their impact and expand their reach while maintaining the core values of authenticity, creativity, trust, and delivering meaningful experiences.
