## PSY114 PSY214 Stress, Self-Care and Mindfulness Wrap Up

This week's lesson in PSY114/PSY214 outlines the current and upcoming assessments for students. It includes details on AT1 quizzes which are a weekly component, accessible via the course platform. Additionally, information regarding AT2 assignments is provided within an Assignment Information document, advising early engagement with required readings to determine focus areas for mindfulness practices. For AT3 workbooks, students are directed to start considering specific self-care activities from the introduction and instructions section of the workbook. The lesson concludes by reminding students that week 3 content will be available on Monday at 9 am.
