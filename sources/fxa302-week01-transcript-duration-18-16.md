## Narrative Medicine: A Doctor's Journey from Reader to Healer

Dr. Rita Charon discusses the field of narrative medicine, emphasizing its role in healthcare by integrating literary theory with clinical practice. She shares personal experiences and transformations in her medical practice through close reading and listening to patient narratives. The concept involves understanding stories holistically, acknowledging silences and unsaid hints, and acting upon them to improve care delivery.
