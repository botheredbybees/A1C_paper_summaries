## Innovations in Aesthetic Spaces for Health and Well-Being

The chapter discusses how aesthetic spaces can promote health and well-being by integrating art, science, and technology to create therapeutic environments. The International Arts + Mind Lab (IAM Lab) collaborated with Google, Reddymade Architecture and Design, and Muuto to explore neuroaesthetics through the exhibition 'A Space for Being.' This exhibit used sensory experiences like lighting, color, sound, and texture to impact visitors' biological responses, monitored via wearable sensors. Three distinct rooms—Essential, Vital, and Transformative—elicited unique atmospheres. The study aims to bridge creative arts therapies with health settings, encouraging community engagement and promoting well-being through interdisciplinary collaboration.
