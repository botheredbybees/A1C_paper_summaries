## FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing - Week 6 Overview

This week's lesson in FXA303 focuses on the use of puppets for enhancing mental wellbeing and therapeutic contexts. It explores how puppetry has been used as a tool in mental health services for nearly a century, allowing clients to project their problems onto a puppet and develop coping strategies. The lesson covers various types of puppets suitable for different ages and circumstances, emphasizing creativity and sustainability. Key literature on puppet therapy is discussed, along with an interview with a dramatherapy-trained puppeteer. Learning objectives include explaining the potential of puppets in supporting mental wellbeing, describing therapeutic contexts where puppets are used, applying principles of expressive puppetry, and designing sustainable puppets.
