## LAW122 Legal Systems Week 2 - Getting to know your camera

This week's lesson in LAW122 focuses on familiarizing students with digital cameras and their functionalities for a photo essay assignment. The content covers essential aspects such as camera phone settings, manual controls, DSLR usage tips, and the importance of obtaining consent when photographing people. Students are encouraged to experiment with different camera functions and share images on discussion boards. Additional resources include tutorials and app recommendations like Snapseed.
