## Is Stress All Bad?

The content discusses the concept of stress in psychological terms, emphasizing that it is neither inherently good nor bad but serves an adaptive purpose. It introduces eustress and distress, where moderate levels of stress can be beneficial while excessive stress leads to performance decline. The lesson also explores how one's perspective on stress can influence its impact on health through metacognitive processes and the tend-and-befriend response. A TED Talk by Kelly McGonigal is referenced, which discusses the positive aspects of stress and suggests that reinterpreting stress as beneficial could lead to healthier physiological responses. The content includes critical thinking exercises about the scientific validity of claims made in the TED Talk.
