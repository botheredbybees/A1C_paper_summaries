## Play-based Interventions for Mental Health: A Systematic Review and Meta-analysis

This systematic review and meta-analysis assesses the impact of play-based interventions on mental health outcomes for children and adolescents with autism spectrum disorder (ASD) and developmental language disorder (DLD). The study, focusing on experimental research adhering to PRISMA guidelines, found that 2,882 papers were identified from which only ten met inclusion criteria. These studies indicated a significant positive effect of play-based interventions on positive mental health outcomes for children with ASD but did not show significant effects on negative mental health outcomes. The review highlights the need for further research to establish the efficacy of such interventions in DLD and emphasizes the importance of considering both positive and negative aspects of mental health when evaluating intervention effectiveness.
