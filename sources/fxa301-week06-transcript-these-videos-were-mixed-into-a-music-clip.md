## Pub Choir Coronavirus Inspired Couch Event Draws 1000 Singers

The content describes a music clip released on March 23, 2020, featuring videos of pub choirs that were inspired by the coronavirus pandemic. This clip drew over 100,000 singers participating in virtual choir performances from their homes within five days. The video highlights how communities are adapting to social distancing measures and finding creative ways to maintain connections through music.
