## FXA303 Week 5 Welcome

This document introduces the fifth week of the FXA303 course, marking a transition into Module 2 which focuses on creative play and its therapeutic benefits for mental wellbeing across different age groups. The module emphasizes hands-on activities leading up to Assessment Task 2, where students create puppets as an empathetic response to trauma narratives. The content provides historical context about the role of puppetry in storytelling and cultural transmission, highlighting its ability to engage with sensitive topics through non-verbal communication. It also outlines the technical aspects of puppet design and mechanisms necessary for expressive movement. Additionally, the document addresses self-care tips for students engaging with traumatic accounts, discussing vicarious trauma and the importance of emotional support during such processes.
