## Guidelines for Arts and Mental Health Projects

The document 'Guidelines for Arts and Mental Health Projects' provides a comprehensive framework to enhance collaboration between artists and mental health practitioners in South Australia. Compiled by Simone Gillam, this initiative aims to improve access and inclusion to arts and cultural activities for individuals with mental health issues, promoting social connection and community engagement. The guidelines detail responsibilities for joint efforts between artists and mental health workers, as well as specific roles for participants, consumers, carers, and the broader community. Emphasizing mutual respect, safety, and inclusivity, these protocols seek to foster a supportive environment where creative expression can lead to positive health outcomes and personal growth. The document underscores the importance of ethical standards and practical considerations such as risk assessment, confidentiality agreements, and the provision of suitable materials and spaces for safe artistic practice.
