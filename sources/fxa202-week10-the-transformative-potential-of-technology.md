## The Transformative Potential of Technology

The chapter explores the potential of emerging technologies to transform aged care services, focusing on artificial intelligence, robotics, and assistive technology during the fourth industrial revolution. It discusses both the benefits and ethical concerns regarding privacy, autonomy, and quality of life for older adults. The text highlights how smart home technologies can enable independent living through monitoring and automation. However, widespread implementation faces challenges due to cost and complexity. Emerging innovations like voice-controlled intelligent assistants, wearables, virtual reality, and robotics are seen as having significant potential to improve care but require careful integration to address ethical issues.
