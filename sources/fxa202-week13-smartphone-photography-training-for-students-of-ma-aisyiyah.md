## Smartphone photography training for students of MA Aisyiyah Sungguminasa Gowa: Creating engaging visual content

This paper reports on a smartphone photography training program conducted for students at MA Aisyiyah Sungguminasa Gowa, Indonesia. Initially, many participants were unfamiliar with basic photography techniques using smartphones. The curriculum was designed to introduce fundamental principles such as lighting, composition, camera angles, visual storytelling, and photo editing through mobile applications. Post-training assessments showed significant improvement in students' knowledge and skills: 85% demonstrated a solid understanding of photography fundamentals, 90% were able to capture high-quality photographs, and 88% successfully shared their photos on social media constructively. The initiative aims to continue fostering creativity and confidence among students in producing meaningful and impactful visual content.
