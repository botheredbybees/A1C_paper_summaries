## Happy Museum Learning Evaluation Report

The Happy Museum Learning Evaluation Report, published in September 2014, details the progress and impact of the Happy Museum project since its inception in 2011. The report outlines how museums can play an active role in promoting sustainability and well-being by reimagining their cultural roles and expertise. Key achievements include funding creative interventions in 22 museums, undertaking evaluation to develop common principles, and sharing learning through events and symposia. However, the project faces challenges such as establishing a firm link between wellbeing and environmental issues and completing learning tools and guidance. The report emphasizes the need for continued resource development to bridge these gaps.
