## Welcome to Week 3 - FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care

This week's introduction in the Arts and Dementia Care course focuses on the therapeutic benefits of listening to music for individuals with dementia. The module aims to explore how music can enhance memory, evoke emotions, and improve overall quality of life and relationships between patients and caregivers. Key topics include an examination of international programs such as the Music & Memory Project, understanding the relationship between music, emotion, and rhythmic entrainment, and analyzing individual musical preferences to derive cognitive stimulation from listening experiences. The course objectives are for students to identify characteristics and findings of two international music-based dementia programs, describe the effects of music on cognition and emotions, and reflect on how these mechanisms can benefit people with dementia through tailored wellbeing initiatives.
