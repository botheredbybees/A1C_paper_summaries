## Play for Health Across the Lifespan: Stories from the Seven Ages of Play

This chapter examines the role of play in fostering health and wellbeing across different life stages, from middle years to old age and end-of-life periods. It discusses how play contributes to work-life balance during midlife, combats loneliness among the elderly, and facilitates a sense of authenticity in later life through super-aging concepts. The text emphasizes the importance of incorporating play into daily routines for individuals across all ages and highlights its significance in enhancing personal development and societal cohesion, especially in times of uncertainty.
