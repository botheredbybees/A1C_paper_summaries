## Therapeutic Photography in Enhancing Self-Esteem and Resilience

This chapter discusses the therapeutic value of using photographs to enhance self-esteem, self-efficacy, and resilience in individuals. It explores how photo elicitation changes traditional dynamics between interviewer and interviewee by providing a tool for exploring cultural, social, economic, and power dynamics that impact an individual's life. Key examples include interviews with Janice about her attachment to her kitten due to moving to a new community and Rebecca’s emotional experiences as a single mother caring for three children. The chapter also delves into the application of narrative therapy through photo elicitation, emphasizing its role in externalizing problems and helping clients reinterpret their past experiences positively. It highlights the importance of visual elements like photography in facilitating communication and fostering resilience among clients.
