## Implementing and Evaluating Interventions

The chapter 'Implementing and Evaluating Interventions' by Daisy Fancourt outlines the process of designing, piloting, and evaluating arts-based health interventions. It emphasizes the importance of active experimentation through pilot projects to assess the feasibility and effectiveness of proposed interventions before full-scale implementation. The author highlights that piloting provides opportunities for detailed testing, stakeholder engagement, and sourcing funding. Key considerations include ensuring the intervention is tailored to its participants, runs smoothly within healthcare settings, and demonstrates potential to achieve its aims. Evaluation data gathered during pilot phases can help refine designs and support future scaling of successful interventions.
