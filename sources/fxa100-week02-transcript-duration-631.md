## Music as Medicine: Healing Power of Music

In this segment from AM Northwest, neuroscientist Daniel Levitin discusses his book 'This Is Your Brain On Music'. He highlights how music can be a form of medicine by activating different parts of the brain to alleviate pain and aid in recovery from conditions like Alzheimer's and Parkinson’s. The conversation includes examples such as Joan Baez's stroke recovery and Glenn Campbell's continued musical prowess despite Alzheimer's disease, illustrating that music deeply embedded in one's youth remains robustly accessible even with significant cognitive decline.
