## Music & Memory: The iPod Project

This transcript documents the impact of the iPod Project at Cobble Hill Alzheimer's unit. It highlights how personalized music therapy enhances connection and quality of life for residents with dementia. Through individualized playlists, the project facilitates emotional engagement and memory recall, fostering person-centered care. Participants like Henry experience significant behavioral improvements when listening to favorite songs, while caregivers are able to better understand patient histories and provide tailored support.
