## Clowning during COVID-19 – A Survey of European Healthcare Clowning Organisations

This paper presents the findings of a Europe-wide survey investigating the impact of COVID-19 on Healthcare Clowning Organisations and their role within healthcare settings. The study, conducted in June 2020, included responses from forty organizations across twenty-one countries. Results indicate that most organizations had to postpone or cancel artistic activities due to pandemic restrictions but managed to adapt through digital and distanced interventions. However, these adapted forms of interaction were perceived as less impactful than face-to-face visits. The authors emphasize the crucial role medical clowns play in providing psychosocial support during challenging times and advocate for more inclusive safety regulations that consider holistic well-being. Additionally, the study highlights the benefits of medical clowning for children's well-being, elderly care, and healthcare staff morale.
