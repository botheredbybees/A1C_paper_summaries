## Celebrate a Community's Arts from the Start

This chapter from 'Make Arts for a Better Life: A Guide for Working with Communities' discusses the importance of valuing and celebrating local arts within communities to promote sustainability, health, well-being, and human rights. It emphasizes that community goals should emerge organically rather than being imposed externally. The authors provide examples such as using traditional instruments in Ethiopian churches and the Waorani women's economic empowerment through handicrafts. They discuss challenges like proving the efficacy of arts programs but advocate for adaptive management to foster resilience. Ethical considerations are highlighted, especially regarding indigenous rights and cultural revitalization.
