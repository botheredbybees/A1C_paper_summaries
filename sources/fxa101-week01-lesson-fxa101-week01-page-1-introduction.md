## FXA101 Creativity and Ageing - Week 1 Introduction

The introduction to FXA101 Creativity and Ageing discusses the importance of creativity in aging by quoting George Bernard Shaw and Sophia Loren. It emphasizes that creative engagement can enhance mental and physical well-being and reduce cognitive decline risks. The course aims to explore individual creative processes through a series of activities, including four weeks dedicated to 'Creativity and the brain through the lifespan.' Students will learn about creating journals for reflective practice and participate in their first creative project based on given themes.
