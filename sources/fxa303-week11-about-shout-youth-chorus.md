## About shOUT Youth Chorus

shOUT is the first LGBTQIA+ youth choir in Australasia, established in 2005 as a branch of Melbourne Pride Chorus. It provides a platform for young people aged 18-29 to express themselves through music and has gained recognition for its dynamic and joyful performances at various events such as the Melbourne Writers' Festival and Gaytimes Festival. The choir is led by Alex Gorbatov, an accomplished lyric tenor, educator, and director who specializes in new and old musical performance traditions. shOUT's repertoire is diverse and surprising, reflecting the creativity of its members. Rehearsals are held weekly on Wednesday evenings in South Yarra, and membership includes access to Melbourne Pride Chorus without additional fees.
