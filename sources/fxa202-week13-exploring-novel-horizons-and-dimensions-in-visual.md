## Exploring Novel Horizons and Dimensions in Visual Representation and Interpretation as Transmedia Storytelling Through the Intersection of Photography and AI-driven Generative Images: A Study

This research explores the integration of photography with AI-generated images in transmedia storytelling, examining how this intersection alters traditional visual representation and storytelling methods. The study employs a qualitative approach involving three case studies, content analysis, and interviews to investigate the impact of AI on narrative creation and audience engagement. Key findings indicate that hybrid visuals combining real photographs and AI-generated imagery enhance creative flexibility and viewer immersion but also raise ethical concerns about authorship and ownership of AI-generated works. The research concludes by highlighting the transformative potential of this integration for visual storytelling in various media formats, while underscoring the need for further exploration into its broader implications for the creative industry.
