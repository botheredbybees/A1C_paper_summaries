## Arts for EveryBody: The One Nation/One Project Impact Report

The document is an impact report detailing the outcomes of 'Arts for EveryBody,' a project initiated by One Nation/One Project in partnership with various organizations including the University of Florida Center for Arts in Medicine. It aims to advance evidence-based engagement between artists and health providers to foster healthier, more equitable communities across the United States.

The report outlines key findings from evaluations conducted at 18 different sites nationwide, highlighting themes such as arts participation, community health worker roles, and the impact of cultural programs on social cohesion. Notable contributions include mixed-method research designs that integrate qualitative and quantitative data to provide a comprehensive view of project impacts.

Additionally, the report serves as an archive documenting the project's journey, emphasizing its importance for policymakers, funders, institutional leaders, and community advocates seeking to leverage arts in health initiatives. The study underscores the potential of artistic interventions to enhance individual wellbeing and promote broader societal health equity.
