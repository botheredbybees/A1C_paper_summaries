## Welcome to Week 6 - FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care

Dr Brendan Lamb introduces Week 6 of the FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care course, focusing on how visual arts such as painting and drawing are perceived and processed by the brain. He explains that these functions can be affected by various forms of dementia and outlines an overview of Art Therapy principles and their applications in dementia care settings. The module also explores ways in which professional artists and caregivers can engage persons living with dementia through visual art activities, including visits to galleries and museums, as well as the use of technology such as tablet devices for viewing and creating art. Dr Lamb highlights key questions related to research on brain reactions to art, embodied cognition, and principles of Art Therapy that improve quality of life for those with dementia.
