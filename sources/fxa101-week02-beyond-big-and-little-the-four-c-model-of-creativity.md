## Beyond Big and Little: The Four C Model of Creativity

James C. Kaufman and Ronald A. Beghetto propose the Four C Model of Creativity to address limitations in existing research that typically dichotomizes creativity into 'Big-C' (eminent) and 'little-c' (everyday) categories. The model introduces two additional dimensions: 'mini-c', which encapsulates personal and developmental aspects of creativity, such as a child's novel use of an art technique; and 'Pro-c', representing professional-level expertise beyond everyday creativity but not yet reaching the level of eminent creativity. This expanded framework aims to clarify distinctions between different levels of creative magnitude and foster more nuanced research into the nature of creativity across various disciplines.
