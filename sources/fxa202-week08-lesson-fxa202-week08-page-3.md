## Editing Techniques for Improved Photography

This lesson on photo editing emphasizes the importance of basic adjustments to enhance visual storytelling. Key techniques include straightening horizons, converting images to black and white for creative effects, warming skin tones, and lightening exposures. The lesson also covers using image editing software like Photoshop and Snapseed to achieve desired outcomes. Additionally, it highlights the significance of lighting quality and offers tips on utilizing natural and artificial lighting conditions effectively.
