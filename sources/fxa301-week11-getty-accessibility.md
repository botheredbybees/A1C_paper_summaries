## Getty Accessibility

The document outlines the Getty's commitment to accessibility and provides detailed information on various aspects of accessibility for visitors, including digital services, physical facilities at both the Getty Center and the Getty Villa, accommodations for visitors with disabilities, and assistance options. It highlights initiatives such as visual descriptions audio tours, assistive listening devices, wheelchair rentals, seating areas, accessible parking spaces, and ASL interpretation. The document emphasizes ongoing efforts to implement W3C Best Practices and Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) for digital accessibility, ensuring that the Getty's website and apps are usable by everyone with disabilities.
