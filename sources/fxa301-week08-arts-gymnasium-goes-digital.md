## Arts Gymnasium Goes Digital

In response to the COVID-19 pandemic and subsequent lockdowns, the Arts Gymnasium at the Belgrade Theatre in Coventry had to transition from in-person poetry workshops to digital sessions via Zoom. Margaret Emerton, an Arts Gym member, reflects on this transformation which required members to adapt to new technologies ranging from basic computing skills to more advanced digital media creation tools. This shift was particularly challenging for older members who were initially unfamiliar with online platforms and technology. Despite these challenges, the group successfully produced a digital poetry book featuring haikus about their experiences during lockdown. The project involved the collaboration of occupational therapy students from Coventry University, aiming to understand how technology can facilitate creativity among older adults while addressing practical barriers such as access to equipment and confidence in using new tools.
