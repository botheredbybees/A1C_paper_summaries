## LAW122 Legal Systems Week 10 Activities

In this LAW122 Legal Systems course module for Week 10, students are encouraged to contribute valuable resources and readings relevant to Assessment Task 4: Critical Reflection on the Discussion Board. The guidance emphasizes the importance of discussing why a particular resource is chosen and its relevance to their learning journey. Additional insights are provided regarding future applications of photography skills learned throughout the course, including exploring larger projects, integrating visuals into writing, communicating effectively through social media platforms, organizing photos for presentation, and embracing personal growth in media literacy.
