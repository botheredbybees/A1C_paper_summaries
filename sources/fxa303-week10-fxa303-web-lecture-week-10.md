## FXA303 Web Lecture Week 10

This document provides guidance and criteria for Assessment Task 4 in the course FXA303, focusing on creative arts and play-based programs designed to enhance mental wellbeing among specific cohorts of children, adolescents, or young people experiencing mental health challenges. The task requires students to choose a specific cohort affected by trauma or risk factors, research their characteristics and challenges, and evaluate recent arts-based or play-based programmes intended to promote better mental health. Students must also critically assess these programs based on scholarly evidence, considering cultural appropriateness and trauma-informed practices. Additionally, the document outlines the potential risks and benefits of the chosen programs and prompts students to propose amendments to enhance outcomes. The assignment emphasizes interdisciplinary knowledge synthesis, contextual understanding, critical analysis, and scholarly research skills.
