## FXA101 Week 3 Introduction

This document provides an introduction to the content covered in Week 3 of the FXA101 course. The week focuses on early childhood brain development and the theory of cognitive reserve, which emphasizes the importance of quality education and enriched learning environments for healthy brain development throughout life. The text also highlights how music and visual arts can be used as tools to enrich learning experiences and promote creativity in children, potentially reducing their risk of dementia later in life. This week's content underscores the significance of social welfare networks and access to high-quality education for all children, with implications for public health initiatives aimed at preventing cognitive decline.
