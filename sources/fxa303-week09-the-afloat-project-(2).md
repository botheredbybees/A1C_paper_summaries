## The Afloat Project

The Afloat Project, initiated by the City of Hobart in response to the May 2018 flood event, aimed at fostering resilience and recovery through creative arts activities. The project utilized a creative pathway involving professional artists working with children, families, and educators using techniques such as drawing, storytelling, model making, music, movement, and play. Activities were designed to help participants deal with change and adversity, promoting personal and community well-being by addressing feelings of loss, isolation, and the need for adaptation in changing environments. The project also emphasized building a sense of place and belonging among children through creative expression and collaboration. A significant event organized as part of this initiative was an open community celebration that included various interactive activities aimed at reconnecting residents with their environment and each other.
