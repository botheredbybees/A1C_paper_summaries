## Brisbane’s Pub Choir movement marks first year

The article discusses the success of Pub Choir, a community-based singing initiative in Brisbane that encourages people to come together once a month to sing songs without the fear of judgment. The concept was initiated by three friends and has grown exponentially over its first year. Participants express feelings of belonging and togetherness, even though they are mostly strangers. One notable event celebrated the movement's one-year anniversary with around 800 participants singing 'My Happiness' by Powderfinger. The article highlights how such community-driven musical events offer a space free from competition or elitism, fostering an inclusive environment where people can enjoy music-making without worrying about their performance skills.
