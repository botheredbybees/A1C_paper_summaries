## Activities for Week 2 - FXA101 Creativity and Ageing

Week 2 of the course focuses on required readings from Stanton's 'Conscious Creativity' and Stuckey & Nobel’s work on art and public health. Students are instructed to engage with these materials and participate in discussions by researching a specific creative figure relevant to their tutorial group, focusing on how one of that individual’s works embodies creativity. Additionally, students will start planning for Creative Project 1, an assessment task due at the end of July, which requires documenting their creative processes.
