## The Role of Music in Enhancing Well-being and Equity for People with Dementia

This chapter explores the role of music in enhancing well-being and equity for people with dementia. It discusses how musical participation can support social inclusion, personal identity, and quality of life among individuals living with dementia. The author emphasizes community arts initiatives as a means to empower these individuals through professional employment in the arts, thereby promoting health equity.
