## Donna: A Photo Essay

The document presents a photo essay about Donna, an autistic adult woman who has faced significant challenges including homelessness and trauma due to rising property prices and social isolation. Through her art, which includes detailed carvings of retro space themes and objects with darkly humorous undertones, Donna expresses herself and copes with past traumas. Her artistic process involves transforming discarded materials into intricate sculptures, such as a 'Death Star,' reflecting on personal healing and the importance of tactile sensory feedback for emotional regulation. The essay highlights Donna's resilience, her journey to self-acceptance as a queer autistic woman, and how every surface in her home becomes an opportunity for creative expression. Her art also serves as a coping mechanism and source of identity affirmation.
