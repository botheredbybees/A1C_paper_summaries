## FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care - Week 3 Activities

This week's module of FXA100 focuses on the use of music in dementia care. The required reading includes Chapter 8 from Daniel Levitin's 'This is Your Brain on Music', which explores why people like certain types of music. Additional resources cover various organizations and articles related to dementia care and music therapy, such as the Alzheimer’s Society UK publications and videos, and a documentary about how music can impact memory in individuals with dementia. The week includes a discussion forum where students explore cultural diversity considerations when choosing music for those with dementia, potential questions to ask patients or family members, and examples of songs that may evoke positive memories based on different birth years. A lecture is scheduled to review the content and discuss assessment task requirements. Students are also reminded about the upcoming due date for Assessment Task 1.
