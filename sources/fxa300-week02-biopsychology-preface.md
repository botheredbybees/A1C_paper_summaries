## Biopsychology (Preface)

The preface of 'Biopsychology' (Global Edition) by John Pinel and Steven Barnes outlines the goals and features of this introductory textbook on biopsychological theory and research. The book is designed for undergraduate courses in Biopsychology, Physiological Psychology, Brain and Behavior, Psychobiology, Behavioral Neuroscience, or Behavioral Neurobiology. Key themes include broad thematic coverage, effective use of case studies to apply theoretical knowledge practically, remarkable illustrations to aid understanding, a strong emphasis on behavior as the central focus, scientific method application both in lab settings and daily life, discussion of personal and social implications, an engaging writing style that addresses students directly with warmth and humor, and new emerging themes identified by Barnes and Pinel for this edition. The text highlights several updates and expansions across chapters, reflecting advancements in biopsychological research.
