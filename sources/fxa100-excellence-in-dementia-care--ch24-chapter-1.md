## Prevalence and Projections of Dementia

This chapter discusses the global prevalence and projections of dementia, emphasizing its increasing burden due to an aging population. It highlights the need for immediate action on a societal and governmental level to address dementia, similar to efforts taken for HIV/AIDS. The text covers learning objectives such as explaining the implications of demographic changes for dementia cases, describing various types of dementia, summarizing regional and global trends in incidence and prevalence, identifying risk factors, and understanding disability and mortality associated with dementia. Key challenges include distinguishing between different subtypes and methodological inconsistencies in research studies.
