## Week 3 Activities: Mindfulness Photo Walk

The LAW122 Legal Systems course introduces the concept of using photography for health and well-being in Week 3. The activities include a Mindfulness Photo Walk where students are encouraged to take photos that reflect mindfulness, either during an outdoor walk or within their immediate environment if mobility is restricted. Instructions on uploading images to the MyLO Discussion Board are provided along with tips on transferring, resizing, and converting photo formats for optimal online viewing.
