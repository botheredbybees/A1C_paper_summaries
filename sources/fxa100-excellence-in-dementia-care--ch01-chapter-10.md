## The Arts in Dementia Care

Chapter 10 of 'Excellence in Dementia Care' explores the use and benefits of artistic interventions for individuals with dementia, including music, visual arts, dance, storytelling, and other creative activities. It highlights how the arts can enhance quality of life by addressing social isolation, improving mood, fostering a sense of identity and purpose, and promoting engagement in meaningful activities. The chapter also discusses challenges in researching the effectiveness of these programs due to their individualized nature and provides examples illustrating the transformative impact of art on dementia care recipients.
