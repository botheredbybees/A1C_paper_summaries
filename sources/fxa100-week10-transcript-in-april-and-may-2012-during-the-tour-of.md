## Sundowner Tour Forums on Dementia in Geelong and Melbourne

The transcript covers two forums held during the Sundowner tour in April and May 2012 in Geelong and Melbourne. The events were facilitated by Alzheimer's Australia Vic and included discussions on dementia care, emotional impacts of Sundowner performances, and personal reflections from family members and carers. Key themes include the importance of understanding dementia as a change rather than a loss of identity, the role of music and dance in providing positive moments, and addressing the challenges of Sundown Syndrome.
