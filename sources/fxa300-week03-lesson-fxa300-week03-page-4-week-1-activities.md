## Activities for Week 1: Discussions and Learning Materials

This week's module in FXA300 Music, Mind and Body focuses on the first of several weekly discussions where students engage in group conversations to introduce themselves and share personal experiences related to music. The content includes instructions for creating threads within designated discussion boards, engaging with peers, and participating in the Virtual Coffee Shop or Zoom Room. Additionally, there are sections on online study help, such as Studiosity services, which offer 24-hour academic support. Study routines are emphasized through an activity involving watching a video about music’s impact on the brain multiple times to enhance comprehension and retention. Reading materials like _Music in the Human Experience_ by Hodges & McPherson (2025) and journal articles related to music psychology, such as those by Juslin & Västfjäll (2008), are also recommended.
