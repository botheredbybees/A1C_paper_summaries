## Process Evaluations and Reporting Results

This chapter discusses the importance of process evaluations in healthcare research beyond mere outcome assessments. It outlines a spectrum for evaluating processes from basic description to detailed ethnographic analysis, emphasizing the context and mechanisms involved. The text also addresses potential issues during research implementation such as unexpected variables or external events affecting study outcomes. Additionally, it introduces scales to assess the depth and reach of health and wellbeing, social, financial, and artistic research findings.
