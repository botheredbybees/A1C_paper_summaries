## Week 5 Introduction - FXA202 The Photo Essay: Storytelling with image and text

Week 5 of the course focuses on visual storytelling, emphasizing how images can convey narratives and metaphors to engage audiences. The lesson outlines learning objectives such as describing how single images can tell stories, giving examples of implied narrative, using visual metaphor in storytelling, and writing captions for photos. It encourages students to participate in Zoom sessions and a discussion board where they share an image along with its caption, fostering a collaborative environment for idea exchange and feedback. Additionally, the text references various articles and videos on narrative tense, stream of consciousness, and other techniques that enhance visual storytelling skills.
