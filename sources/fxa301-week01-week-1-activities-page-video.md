## Week 1 Activities Page Video

This document is a video transcript for explaining weekly activities and expectations for an online course, focusing on student engagement in discussion forums. It outlines the requirement of making at least one post per week and responding to two peer posts as part of the Discussion Contribution assessment task. The role of tutors as discussion moderators is also described, with emphasis on encouraging students to engage directly with each other rather than solely with the tutor. Additionally, it mentions that significant course events such as quizzes and census dates will be posted weekly. This content underscores the importance of regular communication and participation in an online learning environment.
