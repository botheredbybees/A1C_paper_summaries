## Week 3 Activities

The week's activities include reading Gómez-Restrepo et al. (2022) on the role of arts in young people’s mental health in Colombia. Students are encouraged to review web lecture recordings and prepare for a module quiz by engaging with case studies discussed in lectures. A discussion prompt asks students to consider how young participants use arts to regulate emotions, compare organizational engagement to independent participation, and draw parallels to Australian youth wellbeing contexts.
