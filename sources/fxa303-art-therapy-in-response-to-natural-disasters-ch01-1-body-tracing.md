## THRiVE and the Body Map Activity

This chapter describes a five-day arts-based program called THRiVE organized for teachers in Manila in 2016 to support the Tagbanua communities. The body map exercise was pivotal, facilitating emotional release and integration among participants who initially arrived stressed. It aimed to help connect with feelings through their bodies, provide space for expression, connection, and sharing, experience transitions between art modalities, and gain insights from these shifts. The program's impact extended into daily life, fostering a sense of community resilience, compassion, and connectedness. Reflections on the body map exercise highlighted transformative power through openness and vulnerability in collective art-making.
