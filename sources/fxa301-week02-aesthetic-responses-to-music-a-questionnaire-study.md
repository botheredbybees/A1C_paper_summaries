## Aesthetic Responses to Music: A Questionnaire Study

This article explores the cognitive and emotional dimensions underlying aesthetic responses to music through a questionnaire study involving Finnish university students. The researchers aimed to understand the conceptual content and structure of aesthetic responses by asking participants to verbally associate terms related to musical beauty and list appropriate adjectives within a time limit while listening to music. Analysis revealed that 'beautiful' was central, with 'touching' often listed alongside it. Music-specific vocabulary and emotion-related adjectives indicated an affective component in these responses. Differences were observed between music experts and laypeople as well as between genders for certain adjectives, suggesting both commonalities and variability in the conceptual space of aesthetic responses to music.
