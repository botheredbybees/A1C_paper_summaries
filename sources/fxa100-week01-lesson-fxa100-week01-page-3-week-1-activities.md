## FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care Introduction

This course introduction for FXA100 focuses on engaging students in the arts-based approach to dementia care. It outlines the requirements and expectations for discussion forums, including posting and replying within specific time frames. The unit coordinator provides details about accessing weekly lectures via Zoom and emphasizes the importance of active engagement through discussions and quizzes. Students are encouraged to utilize online resources such as Studiosity for academic support. Module 1 begins with an exploration of how music impacts brain function and enhances the quality of life for individuals living with dementia.
