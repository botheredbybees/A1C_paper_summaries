## Getting to Know Your Brain

In this lesson from FXA300 Music, Mind and Body, Dr Brendan Lamb explains that hearing music involves more than just the auditory system; it engages the entire brain. The lesson aims to introduce students to key regions of the brain such as the brainstem, cerebellum, and cerebrum, along with their four lobes. Students will learn how the auditory system processes information from the ear through the brainstem to the auditory cortex, enabling them to hear, recognize, and remember sounds and music. The week's objectives include understanding each section of the brain's role in responding to music.
