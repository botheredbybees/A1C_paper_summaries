## Overview - FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing

This document provides an overview of Week 6 in the FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing course. The week focuses on exploring puppetry as a tool for enhancing mental wellbeing and its use in therapeutic contexts, particularly with children and adults. It discusses how puppets can help individuals identify with their problems and develop coping strategies through indirect expression. The overview mentions the variety of puppet designs suitable for different ages and circumstances, emphasizing creativity and sustainability in puppet-making processes. Videos from Terrapin Puppet Theatre are highlighted as resources to expand understanding of puppetry materials and interactive methods. Key learning objectives include explaining the potential of puppets for mental wellbeing support, describing therapeutic uses of puppets, applying knowledge creatively, and designing sustainable puppets.
