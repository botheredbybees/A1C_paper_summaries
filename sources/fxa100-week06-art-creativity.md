## Art & Creativity

The webpage 'Art & Creativity' on the UCSF Memory and Aging Center website discusses the complex cognitive processes involved in creativity, which encompasses emotional, planning, and sensory perception elements. It highlights that creative expression can be a therapeutic activity for individuals with neurological conditions such as dementia. The page also profiles several patients who engaged in artistic activities despite facing challenges related to health or aging, demonstrating the potential of art in enhancing quality of life and providing a medium for personal expression and cognitive engagement.
