## FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing - Week 2 Overview

The lesson discusses the importance of creative play and the arts for children's healthy brain and social development. It highlights how play supports self-regulation, emotional well-being, cognitive and motor skill development through sensory experiences in various art forms such as music and dance. The content emphasizes the role of creative activities in providing opportunities to understand and manage emotions effectively. Students are encouraged to outline key aspects of early brain development and prominent theories of child development. Additionally, they learn about different types of play and their impacts on brain and social skills, as well as how interactions with arts contribute to physical and emotional growth.
