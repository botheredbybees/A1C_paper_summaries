## Professionalization of the Arts in Health Field

This chapter discusses the professionalization of the arts in health field, emphasizing education and certification efforts to enhance practitioners' competencies. It outlines key practices such as collaboration, ethics, research integration, resource utilization, and leadership. The document mentions the development of a core curriculum by NOAH's Standards Committee, which aims to educate artists and administrators about best practices and ethical standards. Additionally, it addresses plans for establishing a nationally recognized credential, Artist in Healthcare-Certified (AIH-C), through independent testing companies. Various educational pathways are explored, ranging from on-the-job training to formal degree programs, with an emphasis on creating clear pathways similar to those in creative arts therapies.
