## Music Therapy and People with Advanced Dementia

The content discusses the use of music therapy as a therapeutic intervention for individuals suffering from advanced dementia. It emphasizes how personalized music can evoke emotional responses and improve quality of life by reducing agitation and enhancing cognitive functions.
