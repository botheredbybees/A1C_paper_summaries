## Week 11 Overview - FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing

This week's lesson from the FXA303 course focuses on how creative arts can enhance mental wellbeing for vulnerable youth populations such as gender-diverse individuals and those with criminal justice system involvement. The lesson outlines objectives aimed at understanding contributing factors to risks of mental health issues among young people who identify as gender diverse, and describes how engaging in creative arts activities can mitigate these risks by fostering social connections and emotional regulation skills. It also highlights the importance of tailored art programs that recognize individual needs and cultural backgrounds, leading to improved self-efficacy and resilience. The lesson references a successful project from Sandwell Youth Offending Service that demonstrates positive outcomes through arts engagement.
