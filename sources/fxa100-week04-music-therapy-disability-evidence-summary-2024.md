## Music Therapy Disability Evidence Summary 2024

The Australian Music Therapy Association's Disability Evidence Summary 2024 provides an overview of the efficacy and outcomes of music therapy for people with disabilities. The document highlights how music therapy, delivered by Registered Music Therapists (RMTs) who are Bachelor and Masters-level qualified professionals, supports individuals in various aspects such as movement, cognition, communication, social interaction, and self-regulation. RMTs use specialized techniques including singing, songwriting, instrument play, improvisation, receptive listening, and other methods to achieve therapeutic goals. The report emphasizes the importance of evidence-based practices and includes case studies illustrating how music therapy enhances community participation and overall well-being for individuals with disabilities. This resource is particularly relevant for disability and health professionals, policymakers, and community members interested in understanding the benefits and applications of music therapy.
