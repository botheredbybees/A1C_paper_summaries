## The Make Arts for a Better Life Process

This chapter introduces a community arts process that emphasizes collaboration between researchers and communities to create local artistry for societal betterment. It outlines seven steps: meeting the community, specifying goals, connecting these to genres, analyzing genres and events, sparking creativity, improving results, and celebrating integration. The case studies from Uganda and the United States demonstrate how artistic activities can foster peace in post-conflict regions and aid individuals coping with Huntington's disease through creative expression.
