## Creativity, the Arts and Brain Development

In this week's lesson of FXA101 Creativity and Ageing, Dr Brendan Lamb discusses the intersection between brain development and creativity through engagement with the arts. The focus is on how modern brain imaging technologies have revealed insights into neural connections in early life stages and their implications for later cognitive reserve. Key points include the shaping effects of childhood experiences on developing brains during formative periods, which can influence adolescent and adult neuroplasticity. Additionally, the lesson emphasizes enriching sensory learning environments to foster creativity as a crucial foundation for future brain health.
