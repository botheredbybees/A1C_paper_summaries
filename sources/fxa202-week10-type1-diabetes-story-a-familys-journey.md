## Type1 Diabetes Story: A Family's Journey

The document recounts a personal narrative of living with type 1 diabetes through the experience of Kimberley and her mother. The journey began in October 2019 when Kimberley was diagnosed, leading to significant lifestyle changes for both the patient and family members. The diagnosis brings emotional challenges, including fear for Kimberley's safety at night due to hypoglycemic episodes. Despite these difficulties, Kimberley demonstrates remarkable resilience, maintaining a positive outlook on life and recently securing her first part-time job. Her mother also reflects on how this journey has deepened their spiritual faith and reliance on God for support through the uncertainties of managing type 1 diabetes long-term.
