## Week 8 Web Lecture: Editing Your Images

The document provides guidance on editing images for a photo essay, emphasizing the importance of reflecting personal experiences and enhancing visual storytelling. It includes an example where the author, Emma Barratt, discusses her process of adjusting brightness and contrast to capture the freshness she felt while walking at Kingston Beach. The text also covers techniques such as straightening horizons using Photoshop or Snapseed, converting images to black and white for creative effect, and warming skin tones in low-light conditions. These editing practices aim to improve the aesthetic appeal and emotional impact of photographs within a photo essay context.
