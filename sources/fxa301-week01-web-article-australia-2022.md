## Valuing the Arts in Australia and Aotearoa New Zealand

This report, commissioned by the Australia Council and Manatū Taonga Ministry for Culture and Heritage in 2019, explores the contribution of arts to wellbeing, public value, and social inclusion in both Australia and New Zealand. The research was conducted through a literature review, targeted interviews with arts organizations, and thematic analysis. It identifies narratives on capturing and measuring cultural and creative value, emphasizing the importance of integrating First Nations and Indigenous perspectives on arts and culture.
