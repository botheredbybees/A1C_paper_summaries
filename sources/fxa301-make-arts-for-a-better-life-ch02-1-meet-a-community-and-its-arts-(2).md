## Make Arts for a Better Life: A Community-Based Approach

The chapter outlines a community-based approach called 'Make Arts for a Better Life' to engage local communities in improving their lives through artistic activities. It involves seven steps: meeting a community and its arts, specifying goals, connecting these with genres, analyzing genres and events, sparking creativity, improving results, and celebrating integration for continuity. The process emphasizes building relationships and understanding the unique context of each community's artistry to achieve specific social, cultural, or health objectives. Case studies from Uganda and the United States illustrate how communities use arts in conflict resolution, post-conflict healing, and addressing health issues like Huntington’s Disease.
