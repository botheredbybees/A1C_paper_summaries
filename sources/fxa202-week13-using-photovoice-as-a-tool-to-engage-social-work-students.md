## Using Photovoice as a Tool to Engage Social Work Students in Social Justice

This article explores the use of Photovoice, a participatory action research approach, in engaging social work students with the concept of social justice. The author discusses how this method, originally developed for public health projects, can be adapted to educate and empower social work students by fostering critical consciousness and strategic advocacy. By documenting and discussing elements of their community through photography, students gain insight into the transformative power of collaborative creativity and learn to pose problems in ways that highlight social causes, thereby challenging oppressive realities and promoting social change.
