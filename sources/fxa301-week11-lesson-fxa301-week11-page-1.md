## Creative Arts and Social Inclusion

This lesson from the FXA301 Arts in the Community course focuses on using creative arts to enhance social inclusion for individuals with physical disabilities or developmental delays. It highlights the importance of understanding participants' needs and expectations through consultative methods, ensuring projects are socially inclusive and effective. Key elements include summarizing therapeutic uses of creative arts, identifying key components of programs for refugees, analyzing how projects can be tailored to specific needs and abilities, and evaluating benefits such as self-expression and skill development. The lesson emphasizes the role of fostering self-determination in educational and specialized programs for people with disabilities, providing case studies that address barriers through design elements.
