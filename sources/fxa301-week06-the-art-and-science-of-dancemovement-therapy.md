## The Art and Science of Dance/Movement Therapy

The second edition of 'The Art and Science of Dance/Movement Therapy' provides a comprehensive exploration of dance/movement therapy, offering theoretical perspectives alongside practical case studies. Edited by Sharon Chaiklin and Hilda Wengrower, this volume covers the application of DMT in various settings, including new chapters on palliative care interventions. It emphasizes the therapeutic use of movement as a communication tool to address psychological and emotional issues. The book delves into the foundational theories of dance therapy and their practical implications, making it relevant for professionals working within creative arts therapies.
