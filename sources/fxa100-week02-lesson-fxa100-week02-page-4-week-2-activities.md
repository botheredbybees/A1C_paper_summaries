## Activities for Week 2

Week 2 of the course FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care includes required readings on the power of music and lectures discussing assessment tasks. Students are asked to reflect on a song or album from their teenage years that evokes specific memories, linking personal experiences with academic research about musical memory and reminiscence. Weekly discussions guide students through this reflective process, encouraging them to engage with fellow classmates' posts. The module also introduces students to using library resources for scholarly research and APA 7th edition referencing.
