## Is Australia Experiencing an Epidemic of Loneliness?

This report, published by Relationships Australia National in 2018, examines the prevalence of loneliness among Australians using data from 16 waves of the Household Income and Labour Dynamics of Australia (HILDA) Survey. It highlights significant proportions of the population experiencing loneliness and aims to draw attention to this issue.
