## Emotion Work in Caregiving: The Role of Technology to Support Informal Caregivers of Persons Living With Dementia

The paper explores the role of technology in supporting informal caregivers of persons living with dementia, focusing on emotion work and its challenges. Through interviews with 12 caregivers, the study reveals that while technology can help alleviate utilitarian tasks, it should not replace personal connections which are crucial for emotional well-being. Caregivers experience both positive growth and negative distress as the disease progresses, necessitating a balance in their emotion work. The research highlights the need to understand when technology should support or refrain from supporting caregivers' emotional labor to maintain meaningful relationships with dementia patients.
