## Cultural Preservation and Community Arts

This chapter discusses the importance of cultural preservation through community arts activities and publications in regions experiencing rapid urbanization. It outlines steps to establish master-apprentice programs, design training contexts for skill transmission, implement such programs, and plan ongoing development. The focus is on fostering cultural identity by organizing festivals, documenting traditions, publishing journals like Mûɔ láʼ (a periodical focusing on Ngiemboon culture), and forming creators' clubs. It emphasizes the role of community members in sustaining local arts through various activities and publications.
