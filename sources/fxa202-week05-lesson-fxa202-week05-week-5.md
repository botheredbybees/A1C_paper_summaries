## Components of Visual Storytelling

The LAW122 Legal Systems course's Week 7 focuses on visual storytelling, particularly the narrative structures and techniques used in series of images. Key topics include linear and non-linear story structures, establishing shots, ending shots, character depiction, and detail integration. Dr Linda Erceg presents a lecture on these elements, suggesting four hours for completion. Tips for Assessment Task 3 cover basic PowerPoint usage, avoiding distractions like fancy backgrounds, and utilizing PDF format for submissions. The lesson encourages peer feedback in the discussion board and invites students to join the Zoom Common Room for further engagement. Inspirational examples from previous students illustrate diverse storytelling approaches.
