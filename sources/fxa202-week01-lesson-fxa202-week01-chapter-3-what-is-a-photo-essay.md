## The Photo Essay: Storytelling with Image and Text

This chapter introduces the concept of a photo essay as a visual narrative told through a series of photographs often accompanied by text. It discusses the importance and value of photo essays in storytelling, highlighting their ability to expand upon the narrative potential of single images. Examples include Pulitzer Prize-winning work and stories from The Guardian, illustrating how photo essays can cover complex issues with sensitivity and depth. Tips for planning and executing a successful photo essay are also provided.
