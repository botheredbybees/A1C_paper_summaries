## Week 9 Activities: Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing

This week's lesson focuses on resources and networks supporting mental wellbeing after traumatic events. It includes reading a booklet by the Australian Red Cross and Psychological First Aid guide for disaster response in Australia. The discussion task requires students to analyze an arts project from Creative Recovery Network, applying principles of trauma-informed practice. Additional activities include sharing puppets created for Assessment Task 2, viewing module discussions' closure notice, and attending a web lecture where students present their puppet creations.
