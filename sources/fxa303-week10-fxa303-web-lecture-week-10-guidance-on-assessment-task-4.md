## FXA303 Web Lecture Week 10 - Guidance on Assessment Task 4

The document provides guidance for Assessment Task 4 in the course FXA303, which focuses on evaluating arts-based or play-based programs designed to enhance mental wellbeing among children, adolescents, and young people facing challenges such as trauma. The task requires students to choose a specific cohort based on shared experiences like trauma or risk factors, research their characteristics and challenges, and critically evaluate recent programs aimed at improving mental health through artistic or playful activities. Key elements include understanding the chosen group's context, analyzing program designs, assessing cultural appropriateness and effectiveness in addressing trauma, identifying risks and benefits, and proposing amendments to enhance outcomes. The guidance emphasizes scholarly research synthesis and critical reflection, with a due date set for June 5, 2026.
