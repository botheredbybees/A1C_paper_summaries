## Conceptualizing and planning interventions

Chapter 5 of 'Arts in Health: Designing and researching interventions' by Daisy Fancourt outlines a systematic approach to planning arts-based interventions within healthcare settings, drawing on principles from business and management literature. The chapter emphasizes the importance of understanding the environment (including political, economic, social, legal, environmental, and technological factors) before initiating such projects. It introduces a PESTLE analysis framework to assess opportunities and challenges at both macro and micro levels. Fancourt advocates for identifying specific healthcare problems that can be addressed through arts-based solutions, aiming to ensure interventions are effective, sustainable, and garner support from stakeholders.
