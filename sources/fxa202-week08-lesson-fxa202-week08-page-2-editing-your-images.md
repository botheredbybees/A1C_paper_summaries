## Editing Techniques for Photo Essays

This lesson covers the concept of editing in photography, emphasizing its importance in storytelling and audience engagement. It discusses various aspects of editing including selection criteria for images, ethical considerations, and basic photo processing techniques. The focus is on choosing the right photos that resonate with personal meaning and fit into an overarching narrative. Additionally, it provides guidance on minor digital adjustments to enhance image quality without compromising authenticity.
