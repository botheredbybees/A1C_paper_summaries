## Intervention and application of clown care in nursing homes: a scoping review

This scoping review aimed to summarize and describe the literature on clown care interventions in nursing homes for elderly populations, focusing on intervention timing, methods, and outcomes. The authors conducted a systematic search across multiple databases and included 18 studies from 2010 to 2022, revealing that clown care has significant benefits such as reducing negative emotions, cognitive impairments, and physical pain among older adults, while also improving their quality of life and overall satisfaction. Despite these positive findings, the review noted a lack of standardized intervention methods and evaluation criteria within current practices. The study highlights the importance of integrating clown care into elderly care services to enhance holistic well-being in nursing homes.
