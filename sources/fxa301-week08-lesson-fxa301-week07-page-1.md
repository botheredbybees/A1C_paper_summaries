## Engaging with Theatre and Creative Writing

This week's lesson focuses on the impact of theatre and literature projects on community wellbeing and health outcomes. It introduces four case studies: two involving theatrical practices and engagement with drama and literature to enhance mental flexibility and memory skills; another focusing on the benefits of creative writing workshops for wellbeing; and a final one exploring shared reading as an area of growing interest for its potential health benefits. The theoretical framework underlying these projects is discussed, alongside analyses of participant interactions and project leader roles.
