## Week 5 Activities - FXA301 Arts in the Community

The document outlines activities for Week 5 of the course FXA301 Arts in the Community. It includes a reading by Dr Diana Tolmie from Griffith University, discussing how an education in music enhances employability skills and recruiters' perceptions. Additionally, it features a video lecture by Alain de Botton on 'Art as Therapy,' presented at the Rijksmuseum. The course aims to broaden perspectives on the impact of arts for wellbeing and quality of life. It also includes details about a quiz and a discussion forum where students are encouraged to research projects involving visual arts that aim to improve participants' health, well-being, or quality of life.
