## VIRTUAL PUPPET PLAY THERAPY

Karen Fried's chapter on Virtual Puppet Play Therapy explores the adaptation of traditional puppet play therapy for online sessions. The method utilizes an interactive website, onlinepuppets.org, where clients can choose puppets and backdrops to enact scenes that reflect their emotions and experiences. This technique is designed to facilitate emotional expression in a safe and playful manner, enhancing therapeutic assessment and intervention. Through symbolic projection, clients are encouraged to express feelings they might find difficult to verbalize directly. Therapists guide the process by familiarizing themselves with the online platform's features and leading clients through scene creation and interaction with puppets. The chapter emphasizes the importance of therapist enthusiasm in engaging teenage clients and highlights how this approach can be used across different age groups and therapeutic contexts, such as individual, family, or group settings.
