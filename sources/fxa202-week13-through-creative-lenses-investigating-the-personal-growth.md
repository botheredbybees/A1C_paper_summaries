## Through Creative Lenses: Investigating the Personal Growth and Well-being Benefits of Photography and Videography

This study explores the impact of photography and videography on individual well-being, creativity, and personal growth through a qualitative approach involving in-depth interviews with amateur and professional practitioners. Thematic analysis reveals that these art forms contribute significantly to skill development, self-expression, social engagement, mental health improvement, and emotional fulfillment. The research identifies challenges such as financial constraints and time commitments but concludes that the overall impact of photography and videography is overwhelmingly positive for personal development and community involvement. Key theoretical frameworks like Csikszentmihalyi's Flow Theory and Deci & Ryan’s Self-Determination Theory support findings indicating that these hobbies enhance well-being by fostering autonomy, competence, relatedness, mindfulness, and emotional processing.
