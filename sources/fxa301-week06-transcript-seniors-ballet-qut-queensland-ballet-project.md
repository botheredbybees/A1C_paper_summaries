## Seniors Ballet QUT - Queensland Ballet Project

This transcript captures the essence of a seniors ballet program initiated by Queensland Ballet. The program, designed for mature-aged dancers, aims to provide an enjoyable and physically engaging experience. Researchers from QUT have been studying the effects of these classes on participants' physical health, social interactions, and emotional well-being. The initiative not only keeps individuals active but also fosters a sense of community among older adults who share a passion for dance.
