## Creating Our Future: Results of the National Arts Participation Survey

The document 'Creating Our Future' is a comprehensive report published by the Australia Council for the Arts in August 2020, detailing results from the fourth National Arts Participation Survey. This study assesses Australians' engagement with arts and creativity, emphasizing its significance for individual well-being, social cohesion, and national prosperity. Key findings highlight that engagement in artistic activities fosters a sense of community, enhances educational outcomes, supports economic growth, and aids in societal healing during crises such as the COVID-19 pandemic. The report underscores the value of arts and culture in promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion, particularly focusing on First Nations arts and cultural diversity. Additionally, it explores trends in live attendance at arts events, creative participation, digital engagement, and specific demographics including Generation Z, remote communities, culturally diverse populations, and individuals with intellectual disabilities.
