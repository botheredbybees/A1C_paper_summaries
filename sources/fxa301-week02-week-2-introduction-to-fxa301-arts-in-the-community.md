## Week 2 Introduction to FXA301 Arts in the Community

The document provides an introduction to Week 2 of the course FXA301 Arts in the Community. The instructor welcomes students and highlights the focus on understanding brain mechanisms related to perception and engagement with different art forms. This knowledge aims to enhance comprehension of how arts can benefit health and wellbeing, both individually and within communities. Key components include an overview of the main parts of the human brain relevant to these processes and a brief description of neurochemicals and hormones involved in conveying messages between the brain and other body systems. The instructor emphasizes that this foundational knowledge is crucial for engaging with research on the effects of arts engagement on wellbeing, particularly for upcoming assessment tasks such as quizzes. The document concludes by informing students about the availability of lecture recordings should they miss the live sessions.
