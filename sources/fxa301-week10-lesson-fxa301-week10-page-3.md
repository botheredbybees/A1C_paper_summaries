## Case Studies of Therapeutic Creative Arts for Eating Disorders and Trauma

This lesson explores the use of creative arts therapies in treating eating disorders and PTSD. It discusses case studies including 'Sharing the Museum - Sharing the Douglas' which uses art therapy within a museum setting to help individuals with eating disorders gain insights into their disorder and improve body image perception. Another case study, involving veteran David Gillard, illustrates how art therapy can be transformative for those suffering from PTSD, providing an alternative method of expression that reduces intrusive thoughts and enhances coping strategies.
