## Listening to Music - Week 3 Introduction

In this week's module of Listening to Music, Dr Brendan Lamb explores the impact of music on individuals living with dementia. The focus is on two internationally recognized programs that utilize listening to music as a therapeutic approach for people with dementia. Key concepts include the interplay between music, emotion, and memory, alongside rhythmic entrainment. Students are encouraged to consider how music can enhance cognitive stimulation, improve quality of life, and foster emotional connections in dementia care.
