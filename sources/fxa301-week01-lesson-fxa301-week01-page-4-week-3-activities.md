## FXA301 Arts in the Community Week 4 Activities

This lesson for FXA301 focuses on community arts projects and their implications. Key highlights include reminders about the Commonwealth Census Date (March 17, 2026), emphasizing the importance of formal enrollment status. Required reading covers guidelines from Australia Council for Arts grants and successful collaborative community arts projects by Arts Victoria. Assessment involves a quiz that assesses understanding of course material, with one attempt allowed before March 27, 2026. Students engage in discussions on proposed community music and movement project benefits and risks, aiming to identify health improvements and potential challenges.
