## Positive Psychology: The Basics

The text discusses the book 'Positive Psychology: The Basics' by Dr. Rona Hart, providing an accessible and balanced introduction to positive psychology scholarship and its applications. Launched as a discipline over two decades ago, positive psychology aims to balance the traditional focus on disease and dysfunction in applied psychology with an emphasis on promoting happiness, wellbeing, strength, and resilience. The book outlines the theoretical foundations of positive psychology, including key concepts such as happiness, well-being, positive emotions, optimism, hope, goal pursuit, self-regulation, flow, meaning in life, character strengths and virtues, relationships, stress coping, resilience, and posttraumatic growth. It serves as an essential resource for students, practitioners, academics, and anyone interested in understanding how to live a fulfilling life.
