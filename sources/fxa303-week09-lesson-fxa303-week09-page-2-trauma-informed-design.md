## Trauma-Informed Design for Creative Arts and Play Projects

This lesson discusses the importance of trauma-informed design principles in arts-based or play projects that aim to support individuals who have experienced trauma. Key aspects include safety, trust, choice, collaboration, empowerment, and respect for diversity. The text emphasizes the need for these projects to be person-centered, respecting individual backgrounds and circumstances. It highlights how creative arts and play can serve as tools for expression and healing, particularly in communities affected by natural disasters or violence. Examples of successful trauma-informed practices are provided.
