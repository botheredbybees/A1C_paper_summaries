## Step 1: Meet a Community and Its Arts

This chapter introduces the concept of community and guides readers through identifying and engaging with communities to understand their arts. It discusses how communities are defined by shared stories, identities, and patterns of interaction. The chapter emphasizes that communities are dynamic entities characterized by both continuity and change. Key elements include creating a Community Arts Profile to document observations, interviews, and other data related to the community's artistic practices. Additionally, it outlines methods for initial exploration of a community’s arts through surveys and comparisons of various art forms in social contexts.
