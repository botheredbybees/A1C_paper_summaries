## Arts Collaborations with Refugee Communities

This lesson explores how arts and creative projects can support refugees in their integration into new communities. It highlights the role of the arts in preserving cultural identity, facilitating non-verbal communication, promoting intercultural dialogue, and aiding language acquisition. Art therapy initiatives within refugee camps provide psychological support, while community art projects foster social cohesion. Additionally, it discusses the contributions of displaced artists to their host countries' cultural landscape.
