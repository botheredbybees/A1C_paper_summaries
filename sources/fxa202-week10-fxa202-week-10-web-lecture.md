## FXA202 Week 10 Web Lecture

This document is a web lecture for the course 'FXA202 The Photo Essay: Storytelling with Image and Text' at the University of Tasmania. It provides information on preparing for Tasks 3 and 4, which include the creation of a photo essay and a critical reflection. For Task 3, students are required to create a photo essay exploring connections between arts and health or health-related issues, using either linear or non-linear storytelling methods. The lecture includes guidance on structuring the photo essay and writing engaging captions. Task 4 involves submitting a critical reflection with at least three scholarly references, APA-style citations, and the option to include pictures. Students are encouraged to use resources such as Studiosity for feedback and practice quizzes to prepare for their presentations.
