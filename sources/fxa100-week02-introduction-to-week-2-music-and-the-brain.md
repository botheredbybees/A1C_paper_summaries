## Introduction to Week 2: Music and the Brain

This document introduces Week 2 of a course on arts and dementia care, focusing specifically on music's impact on brain function in individuals with dementia. The text highlights how engaging with music can activate different parts of the brain, sometimes enabling bypassing certain cognitive impairments. It also discusses the concept of 'musical memories' and the reminiscence bump, where people tend to remember and emotionally connect with music from their teenage years more vividly than other periods in life. The document encourages students to explore personal musical preferences and associated memories, fostering a deeper understanding of how music interacts with brain function and emotional recall.
