## Analyze Genres and Events

This chapter, part of a guide for working with communities through arts-based initiatives, delves into the analysis of artistic genres and events. It outlines three main parts: describing an event and its genre(s) as a whole; exploring the event's genre(s) through various artistic domains like music and oral verbal arts; and relating the event’s genre(s) to broader cultural contexts. The authors emphasize understanding the intricate relationships between different art forms, community expectations, and cultural meanings within events. They provide detailed methods for analyzing genres through multiple perspectives, including space, materials, performance features, content, and symbolic systems.
