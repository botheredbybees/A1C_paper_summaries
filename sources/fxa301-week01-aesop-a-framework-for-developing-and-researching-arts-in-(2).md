## Aesop: A Framework for Developing and Researching Arts in Health Programmes

The article introduces Aesop, a framework designed to address the complexities of arts in health research by integrating methodologies from both arts and health sectors. It aims to guide researchers through various stages of project development, including intervention creation, study design, implementation, reporting, and dissemination. The framework synthesizes existing approaches such as Medical Research Council guidelines and Participatory Action Research methods while accommodating diverse epistemologies like ethnography and grounded theory. By providing a structured pathway for arts in health projects, Aesop seeks to enhance the rigor and acceptance of such research within both academic and practical contexts, fostering collaboration between artists, healthcare providers, and researchers.
