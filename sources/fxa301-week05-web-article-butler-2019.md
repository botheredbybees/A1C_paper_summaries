## Making a Museum of Happiness

The article discusses Tony Butler's vision for museums as centers that contribute to mental health and well-being in the context of social and environmental challenges. Drawing on research by the New Economics Foundation, Butler highlights how museums can foster community connections through co-production initiatives that engage with local populations, such as programs for learning disabled adults and long-term unemployed individuals. The Happy Museum Project, initiated in 2011, aims to develop a framework for museums to enhance well-being and sustainability through social engagement and environmental stewardship.
