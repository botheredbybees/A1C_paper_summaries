## Review: Albrecht Dürer: Documentary Biography by Jeffrey Ashcroft

This review critically evaluates Jeffrey Ashcroft's 'Albrecht Dürer: Documentary Biography,' a translation and annotation of Hans Rupprich’s foundational work on Albrecht Dürer's documents. The reviewer commends Ashcroft for his accurate and informative notes, which enhance the understanding of Dürer's life through annotated translations of letters, inscriptions, and other records. However, the review points out several shortcomings in the coverage of humanist influences, connections to the Reformation, and the evolution of Dürer’s artistic style. The reviewer suggests that a more comprehensive analysis would have better contextualized these aspects of Dürer's life within his historical and cultural milieu.
