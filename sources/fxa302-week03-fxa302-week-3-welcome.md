## FXA302 Week 3 Welcome

This document introduces Week 3 of the FXA302 course, focusing on intersections between visual arts and health education. It explores historical developments in medical illustration from handwritten manuscripts to modern digital imaging techniques. The text highlights how these images serve as educational tools for both professionals and the public. Additionally, it discusses recent advancements in health humanities, emphasizing the importance of close observation skills derived from visual art study and their application in patient care. The document encourages students to develop reflective skills that are transferable across various healthcare disciplines.
