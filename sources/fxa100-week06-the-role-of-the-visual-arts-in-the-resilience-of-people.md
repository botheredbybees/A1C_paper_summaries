## The Role of the Visual Arts in the Resilience of People Living with Dementia in Care Homes

The study investigates the role of visual arts enrichment activities in fostering resilience among people with dementia living in care homes. Utilizing a theoretical framework grounded in ecopsychosocial principles, the research highlights how creative expression through art can enhance communication, self-esteem, and interpersonal relationships. The findings indicate that even individuals with advanced stages of dementia demonstrate resilience when engaged in visual arts activities. This study contributes to understanding resilience beyond traditional definitions of successful aging by emphasizing its potential for all older adults, including those living with cognitive decline.
