## NSW Health, 2015 NSW Innovation and Health Symposium Transcript

The transcript details the NSW Health's initiative to run a choir for individuals with aphasia or progressive neurological impairments such as Parkinson's disease and dementia, led by a volunteer music therapist in collaboration with speech pathologists. The primary goal was to provide an avenue for social interaction and improved health outcomes for those experiencing severe communication challenges. Patients were engaged across different stages of care from acute settings to community living. Speech pathologists aimed to create opportunities where patients could express themselves through singing, which is accessible and enjoyable despite their impairments.
