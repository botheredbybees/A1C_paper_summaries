## Contemporary Art in Healing Environments

This chapter explores the intersection between contemporary art and healthcare settings, highlighting how artworks can enhance therapeutic environments. Cormack discusses various initiatives where art has been integrated into hospitals and health facilities to improve patient care and staff well-being. Key points include case studies of successful collaborations between artists, medical professionals, and institutions that aim to create more humane spaces for healing. The role of public funding in supporting these projects is also addressed.
