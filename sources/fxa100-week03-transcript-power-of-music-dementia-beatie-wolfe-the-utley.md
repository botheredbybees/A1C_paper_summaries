## Power of Music & Dementia - Beatie Wolfe & The Utley Foundation

This transcript features a musical performance by Beatie Wolfe in collaboration with The Utley Foundation. The song emphasizes the emotional support and companionship needed as one ages or becomes frail due to dementia. It highlights themes of enduring love, understanding, and resilience in the face of life's challenges.
