## Narrative Medicine: Transforming Healthcare Through Storytelling

A physician discusses the concept of 'narrative medicine,' emphasizing the importance of storytelling in clinical practice. The speaker explains how engaging with literature improved their understanding of narratives and patient care. This approach includes meticulous listening to patients' stories without interrupting, recognizing silences and unspoken emotions, and forming enduring connections through shared experiences of illness and mortality.
