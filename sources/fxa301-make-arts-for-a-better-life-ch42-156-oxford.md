## Citations and References in Ethnomusicology and Applied Music Studies

This chapter includes a collection of scholarly references and citations relevant to ethnomusicology and applied music studies. The texts range from foundational works by scholars such as Muriel Saville-Troike, Richard Schechner, and Anthony Seeger, which discuss theoretical frameworks for understanding cultural practices through the lens of performance and discourse analysis. Other contributions explore practical applications in community development, health interventions like dance therapy for Parkinson’s disease, and ethical considerations in fieldwork. Key texts also address the role of music in social justice and sustainability initiatives. The references highlight a diverse range of methodologies including ethnography, participant observation, and collaborative research approaches.
