## A Step-by-Step Approach to the Research Process in Arts in Health

This chapter outlines a structured approach to conducting research in arts and health interventions, breaking down the process into four stages: developing an initial idea for a research study, designing the study, running the research, and deriving outcomes. It details various sub-stages such as identifying a research problem, forming evidence base, developing theories, piloting feasibility studies, selecting a study team, involving patients, choosing quantitative or qualitative strategies, conducting economic evaluations, process evaluations, implementing research, reporting results, disseminating findings, and further implementation of interventions. The chapter emphasizes the importance of understanding existing literature to inform decisions at each stage and provides a spectrum for decision-making within these stages.
