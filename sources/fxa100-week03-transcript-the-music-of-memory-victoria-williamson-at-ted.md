## The Music of Memory: Victoria Williamson at TED MED Live

Victoria Williamson discusses the power and intricacies of musical memory in her TED MED talk. She shares stories illustrating the exceptional capacity for remembering music, such as that of conductor Arturo Toscanini recalling intricate orchestral pieces effortlessly. Another tale focuses on Clive Wearing, who retains his musical abilities despite severe amnesia. Williamson also delves into involuntary musical imagery or 'earworms,' suggesting they serve to keep our minds active and ready. She emphasizes the deep-seated nature of music in memory systems and its potential for therapeutic applications.
