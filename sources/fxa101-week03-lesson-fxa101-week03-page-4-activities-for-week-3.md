## Activities for Week 3

This week’s lesson from FXA101 focuses on activities and readings to enhance creativity in aging. Students are instructed to read chapters from Peter Stanton's 'Conscious Creativity' focusing on structuring creativity, the role of practice, and overcoming obstacles. A creative exercise is also provided where students observe their environment closely for new details over a 5-minute period, documenting these observations in their journals. The week includes tutorial discussions based on the readings and observations. Students are advised to plan and trial the making process for Creative Project 1 within three hours and document this in their journals.
