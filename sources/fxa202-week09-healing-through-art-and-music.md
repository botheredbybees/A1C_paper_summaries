## Healing Through Art and Music

Cassandra Yates reflects on her journey through chronic pain and emotional numbness, emphasizing the slow pace of healing. She describes her body's instinctive survival mode since birth and the struggle to fit together disjointed pieces of her life. Yates found solace in art and music, which helped transform her home from a place of turmoil into one filled with vibrant colors and creative expression. Despite moments of relapse where old pain resurfaces, Yates now embraces the process of healing alongside her inner child. While questioning what her life might have been without past struggles, she finds value in qualities such as compassion and patience that may not have developed otherwise. The narrative culminates with a profound realization: she no longer wishes to die, marking a significant milestone in her personal growth.
