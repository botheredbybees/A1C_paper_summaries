## Selective Attention Test

This YouTube video is a selective attention test by researchers Daniel Simons and Christopher Chabris. Participants are instructed to count the number of times basketball players in white shirts pass the ball while an unexpected event, such as a person in a gorilla suit, passes through the scene unnoticed. The correct answer for the number of passes is 15.
