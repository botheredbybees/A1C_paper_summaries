## Introduction to Anatomy and Physiology

This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts of anatomy and physiology as foundational knowledge for careers in health professions. It emphasizes how understanding these fields can improve personal health decisions and comprehension of medical news. The text outlines key objectives such as distinguishing between anatomy and physiology, identifying body structures and directions, and recognizing the importance of homeostasis. Additionally, it covers basic human organization levels, characteristics of life, survival needs, medical imaging techniques, and provides a preview of subsequent chapters in the textbook.
