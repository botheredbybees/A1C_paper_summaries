## Therapeutic Photography and Related Techniques

This chapter discusses the concept of therapeutic photography as a tool for enhancing self-esteem, self-efficacy, and resilience in individuals. It outlines how photographs can serve as powerful therapeutic tools by allowing clients to explore their emotions, thoughts, and experiences visually. The text explores various methods of incorporating photography into therapy sessions, including phototherapy techniques that use personal snapshots and family albums to facilitate communication between therapist and client. Additionally, the chapter delves into the role of photography in building self-awareness and fostering healing processes through visual expression.
