## Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing: Case Studies on Trauma Recovery

This lesson focuses on the role of creative arts and play in aiding mental wellbeing after natural disasters. It discusses case studies from Japan's Great East Earthquake and Tsunami (2011) where adventure playgrounds were set up to help children process their trauma through play, and a similar initiative called 'Afloat' in Hobart (Tasmania) following severe flooding in 2018. These projects highlight the importance of age-appropriate creative expression for emotional processing, social bonding, and resilience building among affected communities.
