## Submission to the NSW Health and Wellbeing Strategy

This submission by Museums & Galleries of NSW (M&G NSW) advocates for the inclusion of arts and cultural initiatives in the upcoming NSW Mental Health and Wellbeing Strategy. The organization highlights the significant mental health benefits derived from programs such as Culture Dose for Kids and Arts on Prescription, which have shown measurable improvements in anxiety reduction, social connectedness, and overall well-being among participants. However, M&G NSW also expresses concern over the current funding challenges faced by cultural organizations, emphasizing that cuts to community-focused programs will undermine their ability to deliver essential health services. The submission proposes several recommendations including policy recognition of arts-based mental health initiatives, targeted research on diverse populations, and sustainable co-funding models between government agencies. The document underscores the alignment of these cultural programs with broader NSW Health policies and strategic goals, advocating for a more integrated approach that leverages the strengths of both healthcare and cultural sectors to promote better mental health outcomes.
