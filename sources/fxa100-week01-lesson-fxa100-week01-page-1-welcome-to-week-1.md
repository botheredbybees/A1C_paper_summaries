## Week 1: Introduction to FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care

This week's lesson in the FXA100 The Arts and Dementia Care course introduces students to dementia care, focusing on understanding how dementia affects individuals and their daily lives. It covers common types of dementia and their impact on brain function. Students are expected to complete an orientation quiz to access further materials and participate actively in weekly discussions as part of the assessment tasks.
