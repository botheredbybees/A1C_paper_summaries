## FXA302 Perspectives of the Arts on Health and Wellbeing - Week 1 Activities

This lesson introduces students to the topic of Health Humanities within the context of Creative Arts. It includes a reading assignment on the relationship between health humanities and creative disciplines as well as an introduction to characteristics and trends in the field through another scholarly work. Students are encouraged to participate in discussion forums where they can interact with peers, exchange ideas, and engage in dialogue about their readings. The module also outlines weekly web lectures via Zoom, providing access details for participants. Additional resources such as supplementary articles and guidance on using discussion boards and Studiosity support service are provided.
