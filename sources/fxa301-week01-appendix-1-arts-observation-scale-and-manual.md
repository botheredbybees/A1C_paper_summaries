## Appendix 1: Arts Observation scale and manual

The document contains an observation scale and manual for assessing the impact of arts activities, particularly music sessions, on residents in care homes. It includes a questionnaire to evaluate resident mood before, during, and after these sessions using a seven-point scale (1-7). The assessment covers various emotional states such as happiness, calmness, satisfaction, excitement, and others. Additional survey questions ask about the observer's confidence in supporting music activities and note any changes or increases in informal music-making activities since the last session. This tool aims to document residents' emotional responses and engagement levels through structured observations, providing valuable insights into how arts interventions can positively influence well-being in care settings.
