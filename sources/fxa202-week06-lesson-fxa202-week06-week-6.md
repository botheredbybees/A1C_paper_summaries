## Visual Storytelling: Crafting Narratives Through Single Images

This week's lesson in LAW122 focuses on the craft of visual storytelling through single images. It highlights the importance of telling good stories and emphasizes how a single image can convey a narrative, use implied narratives, and employ visual metaphors. The session includes learning objectives such as describing how an image tells a story and using it as a writing prompt. Students are encouraged to engage in Zoom sessions for discussions and participate in the Week 5 Discussion Board by sharing an image with a caption. Inspirational examples from previous students' photo essays are provided, along with tips on effective caption writing, including narrative tense and stream of consciousness techniques.
