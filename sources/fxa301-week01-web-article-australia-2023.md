## Creating Value: Results of the National Arts Participation Survey

The National Arts Participation Survey, conducted by Creative Australia and Lonergan Research in 2022, explores Australians' engagement with arts and creativity post-COVID-19. The survey highlights a significant rise in people recognizing the benefits of arts to personal wellbeing and acting on that belief more actively than before the pandemic. Over half of respondents acknowledged the positive impacts of arts on their lives, with many indicating that creative activities helped them navigate challenging times brought about by the pandemic.
