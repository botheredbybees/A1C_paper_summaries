## Applying Creativity in Practice

This chapter explores the application of creativity to mental health practice, focusing on two key aspects: applying creative thinking within therapeutic contexts and integrating novel approaches into clinical work. Gillam discusses how creativity complements evidence-based practice while emphasizing ethicality in interventions. The importance of risk-taking, improvisation, and fostering a playful yet disciplined approach are highlighted. Drawing from studies in family therapy, the chapter illustrates the role of innovative strategies in achieving breakthroughs with clients.
