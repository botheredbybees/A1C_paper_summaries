## Handbook of Autism and Pervasive Developmental Disorder

The 'Handbook of Autism and Pervasive Developmental Disorder' is an edited volume that focuses on the comprehensive assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of autism spectrum disorder (ASD) and other pervasive developmental disorders. The book addresses various dimensions of ASD, including psychopathologies such as anxiety, OCD, depression, ADHD, challenging behaviors, sensory processing issues, cognitive function, and quality of life for individuals with these conditions. Additionally, it covers general assessment methods, diagnostic systems, educational assessments, monitoring progress in treatment, and ethical considerations in the application of evidence-based practices. The handbook provides a detailed exploration into the multidisciplinary aspects of ASD and related disorders, making it an essential reference for researchers, clinicians, educators, and caregivers involved in the field.
