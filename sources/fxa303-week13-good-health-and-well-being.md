## Good Health and Well-Being

The document discusses the importance of sustainable development goals (SDGs) for achieving global health and well-being, particularly focusing on SDG 3. It emphasizes that health is a fundamental human right and serves as an indicator of a country's progress towards sustainable development. The text highlights current health trends such as insufficient access to healthcare, medicines, clean water, and nutrition, which exacerbate poverty and inequality. The volume aims to address these issues through interdisciplinary approaches, advocating for better governance and systems in health care that focus on disease prevention, improved sanitation, and reduced risks of diseases. Contributions from various experts aim to support the implementation of SDG 3 by providing insights into numerous factors affecting global health discourse.
