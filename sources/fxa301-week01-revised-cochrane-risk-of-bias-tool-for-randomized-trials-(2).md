## Revised Cochrane risk of bias tool for randomized trials (RoB 2) — Additional considerations for crossover trials

The document discusses the revised Cochrane Risk of Bias (RoB) tool version 2, specifically tailored for crossover trial designs. It highlights key aspects such as the advantages and limitations of crossover trials compared to parallel group trials, emphasizing the need for appropriate analysis methods that account for within-participant design. The document outlines scenarios where crossover trials might be unsuitable due to conditions like rapid disease progression or long drug half-lives leading to carryover effects. Additionally, it provides recommendations on how to choose between using RoB 2 tools for crossover trials versus parallel group trials based on the specific analysis methods used in individual studies. This is crucial for ensuring accurate and unbiased systematic reviews involving crossover trial data.
