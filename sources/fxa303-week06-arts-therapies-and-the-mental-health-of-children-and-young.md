## Arts Therapies and the Mental Health of Children and Young People

The book 'Arts Therapies and the Mental Health of Children and Young People' presents a collection of international research, theories, and practices in arts therapy for children and adolescents. The volume addresses high rates of mental disorders among young people by exploring various art forms and their therapeutic applications across different social, cultural, and developmental contexts. Contributions highlight how expressive arts within therapeutic settings can foster hope, trust, and resilience. Chapters discuss innovative methods such as puppetry, music therapy, dance movement therapy, and participatory ethnography in diverse locations like schools, centers for young offenders, and communities affected by violence. The book is essential reading for arts therapists, academics, postgraduate students, and professionals in related fields.
