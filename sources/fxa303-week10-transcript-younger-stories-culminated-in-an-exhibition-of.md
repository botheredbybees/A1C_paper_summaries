## 'Younger Stories' Multicultural Arts Project Exhibition

The transcript discusses the 'Younger Stories' multicultural arts project culminating in an exhibition at the new health facilities of Coffs Harbor Health Campus. The project was initiated by Jay Black (artist) and Jude Dardel (social worker), involving multi-cultural elders to ensure community investment, with trauma-informed practices guiding workshops for young refugees from three local schools. Artworks, stories, and poems created through these sessions are now on display in the health facilities, fostering a welcoming environment for recent arrivals.
