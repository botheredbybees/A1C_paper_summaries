## Projects Using Music for Social, Health and Economic Issues

This chapter discusses various projects that utilize music to address social, health, and economic issues globally. Examples include the Berlin Center's use of music therapy for trauma survivors from natural disasters or conflict zones, Commuon The Big Band in Japan as a community rehabilitation project after the 2011 earthquake, and Musicians Without Borders (MwB) initiatives in post-conflict regions like Palestine and Kosovo. These projects aim to provide healing, social cohesion, and economic empowerment through music.
