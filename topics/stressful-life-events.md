## Impact of Stressful Life Events

The sources highlight the significant impact that stressful and traumatic life events can have on individuals with autism spectrum disorder (ASD), leading to psychological comorbidities. Despite this, there is a need for trauma-focused strategies in clinical practice to address these issues effectively. The integration of such interventions underscores the importance of understanding how external stressors exacerbate mental health challenges in people with ASD.

## Key Findings

- Stressful life events significantly impact individuals on the autism spectrum by contributing to psychological comorbidities.
- There is a growing recognition for trauma-focused strategies in clinical settings to mitigate the adverse effects of stressful events on individuals with ASD.
