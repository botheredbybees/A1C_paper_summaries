## Visual Literacy

Both sources emphasize the importance of visual literacy in understanding and interpreting symbolic systems within cultural contexts. They stress that analyzing an image is not just about decoding its immediate content but also about recognizing how it functions within a community's broader symbolic landscape. The concept of visual literacy encompasses skills such as identifying, understanding, evaluating, and creating visual messages within various cultural frameworks. This involves looking beyond the surface elements to consider deeper layers of meaning embedded in images that reflect societal norms, values, and beliefs.

## Key Findings

- Visual literacy is crucial for interpreting symbolic systems effectively.
- Analyzing an image requires understanding its place within broader cultural contexts.
- Skills related to visual literacy contribute to the ability to decode and produce meaningful visual messages.
