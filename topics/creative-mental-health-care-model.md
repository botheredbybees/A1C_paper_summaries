## Creative Mental Health Care Model

The exploration of a creative mental health care model highlights the shift towards incorporating arts-based therapeutic practices in mental health care. This approach is advocated as an effective way to enhance patient engagement, improve treatment outcomes, and foster holistic wellbeing. Key findings across studies indicate that integrating creativity into mental health interventions can lead to better psychological resilience and recovery. However, challenges remain in terms of standardizing these practices within mainstream healthcare systems, leading to questions about the scalability and implementation of such models.

## Key Findings

- Integrating creative practices enhances patient engagement.
- Creative mental health care improves treatment outcomes.
- Challenges exist in implementing arts-based therapies widely in healthcare settings.

## Open Questions

- What are the barriers to adopting a creative mental health care model across different cultural contexts?
