## Workforce Development in Healthcare and Arts Integration

The topic of workforce development in healthcare emphasizes the importance of developing competencies and certification for arts practitioners within health settings (Source 1). This focus on foundational skills and career architecture underscores a structured approach to nurturing talent. In parallel, Source 2 discusses organizational change through trauma-informed care principles, which aims at creating a sustainable culture within organizations. The integration of these two approaches highlights the necessity for healthcare systems to incorporate both arts-based interventions and trauma-sensitive practices in their workforce development strategies.

## Key Findings

- Development of foundational competencies is crucial for practitioners integrating arts into health services
- Certification programs are instrumental in standardizing practice and recognizing the role of arts in health
- Organizational culture must support sustainable change, including trauma-informed practices

## Open Questions

- How can certification programs effectively integrate trauma-informed care principles?
