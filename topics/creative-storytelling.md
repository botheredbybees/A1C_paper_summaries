## Creative Storytelling

The concept of creative storytelling emerges as a versatile and potent intervention in various contexts. In the legal realm, creative storytelling is used to enhance free-writing exercises, suggesting its utility beyond traditional narrative forms to include legal and ethical discussions (Source 1). This practice aligns with broader applications in community settings, where it fosters social engagement through cultural asset mapping (Source 2). Both sources highlight creative storytelling's adaptability, whether as a tool for individual expression or communal interaction. However, the specific mechanisms by which creative storytelling translates to tangible benefits in diverse populations remain underexplored.

## Key Findings

- Creative storytelling is utilized both in personal and community contexts.
- The practice enhances social engagement within communities.
- Adaptability of creative storytelling allows its application across various domains.

## Open Questions

- What are the specific mechanisms through which creative storytelling benefits different populations?
