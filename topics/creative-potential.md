## Creative Potential in Dementia Care

The exploration of creative interventions for dementia care highlights the importance of artistic expression and social engagement. Source 1, 'How creating sculpture benefits people with dementia,' emphasizes how engaging in sculptural activities not only fosters self-expression but also enhances social interaction among participants. Co-creation processes are seen as a means to activate the dormant creative potential within individuals living with dementia, contributing positively to their quality of life and sense of identity.

## Key Findings

- Artistic interventions such as sculpting can significantly enhance self-expression in people with dementia.
- Co-creative processes facilitate social interaction and community building among dementia patients.
- Engaging in creative activities helps activate the hidden creative potential within individuals living with dementia.
