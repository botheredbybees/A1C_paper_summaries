## Mental Health Assessment

The THRivE Toolkit emphasizes the importance of comprehensive mental health assessment tools within the context of arts and heritage. It highlights the need for methods that go beyond traditional medical approaches to consider quality-of-life factors, which are critical in understanding the holistic well-being of individuals engaged with cultural activities. The toolkit's focus on integrating qualitative assessments alongside quantitative measures underscores a broader approach to evaluating mental health.

## Key Findings

- Comprehensive mental health assessment includes both qualitative and quantitative methods.
- Quality-of-life measurement is essential for a thorough understanding of mental health status.
