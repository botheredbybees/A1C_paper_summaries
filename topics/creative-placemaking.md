## Creative Placemaking

The concept of creative placemaking as an intervention aims to integrate arts and cultural strategies into community development initiatives. This approach seeks to enhance the quality of life, social cohesion, and economic vitality in specific neighborhoods or regions. Across various sources, it is evident that creative placemaking fosters community engagement through artistic activities and encourages residents to participate actively in urban planning processes. However, there are tensions regarding its efficacy in addressing underlying socio-economic disparities within communities. Some studies highlight the transformative potential of such interventions, while others question their scalability and sustainability in diverse contexts.

## Key Findings

- Creative placemaking enhances community cultural development by integrating arts into local initiatives.
- Such interventions aim to improve social cohesion and economic health of neighborhoods.
- Participation in creative placemaking activities fosters a sense of belonging among residents.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of creative placemaking on socio-economic disparities within communities?
