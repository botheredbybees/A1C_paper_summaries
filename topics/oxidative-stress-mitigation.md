## Oxidative Stress Mitigation

Natural products hold therapeutic potential in managing Alzheimer's disease by mitigating oxidative stress and suppressing amyloid beta aggregation. This intervention addresses the underlying pathophysiology of neurodegeneration, offering a promising pathway for neuroprotection. Across various studies, these natural compounds have been shown to exhibit antioxidative properties that combat the damaging effects of reactive oxygen species, thereby reducing inflammation and cellular damage associated with Alzheimer's disease. However, while in vitro and in vivo models demonstrate positive outcomes, translating these benefits into clinical efficacy remains challenging due to factors such as bioavailability and patient-specific variability.

## Key Findings

- Natural products have antioxidative properties that mitigate oxidative stress.
- These compounds can suppress amyloid beta aggregation.
- Potential exists for neuroprotection through intervention with natural products.
