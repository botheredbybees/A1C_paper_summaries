## Holistic Improvements in Healthcare

The Creative Health Review Report emphasizes the importance of integrating holistic approaches into healthcare to enhance both physical and mental well-being. The report underscores preventive healthcare as a critical component, advocating for policy changes that support comprehensive health strategies beyond traditional medical interventions. It highlights the need for a shift towards more inclusive healthcare practices that consider social determinants and creative therapies.

## Key Findings

- Holistic approaches in healthcare improve overall patient well-being
- Preventive measures are essential for long-term health outcomes
- Policy recommendations play a crucial role in enabling holistic healthcare
