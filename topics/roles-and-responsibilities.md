## Roles And Responsibilities In Refugee Youth

The study of refugee youth's changing roles and responsibilities highlights the adaptability and resilience of this population in response to migration challenges. Through the lens of arts-informed research, sources emphasize how these young individuals navigate complex social and cultural changes while taking on new or altered duties within their families and communities. Despite the nuanced understanding offered by such methodologies, there remains a need for more detailed exploration into the specific impacts of these changing roles on mental health outcomes.

## Key Findings

- Refugee youth adapt to take on new roles and responsibilities due to migration.
- Arts-informed research provides unique insights into how refugee youth perceive their evolving roles.
- Changing social dynamics post-migration influence the ways in which refugee youth are expected to contribute to family and community well-being.

## Open Questions

- How do changing roles and responsibilities impact the mental health of refugee youth?
