## Servant Leadership

The concept of servant leadership emerges as a pivotal framework within the broader spectrum of leadership theories. It emphasizes serving and supporting followers to achieve their full potential rather than traditional hierarchical control structures. This approach aligns with resilient and transformational leadership, fostering environments where individuals are encouraged to grow and contribute positively. Gillam highlights how such an ethos not only enhances individual wellbeing but also drives collective success by prioritizing the needs of employees over those of the leader or organization.

## Key Findings

- Servant leadership promotes a supportive environment that encourages employee growth
- The approach aligns with resilient and transformational leadership principles
- Fosters positive organizational culture and individual wellbeing
