## Quality Improvement Measures

The sources emphasize the importance of quality improvement measures in healthcare settings to enhance patient outcomes and implement patient-centered care. The integration of PESTLE (Political, Economic, Sociocultural, Technological, Legal, Environmental) analysis in planning interventions highlights a comprehensive approach to identify external factors affecting health systems and strategies for their optimization. Quality improvement measures are discussed as critical tools in enhancing the efficiency, effectiveness, and accessibility of healthcare services while ensuring that these enhancements are aligned with patient needs and expectations.

## Key Findings

- Quality improvement measures are vital for optimizing healthcare delivery
- Patient-centered care is a cornerstone of effective quality improvement initiatives
- PESTLE analysis aids in strategic planning by identifying external factors influencing healthcare interventions
