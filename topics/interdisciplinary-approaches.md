## Interdisciplinary Approaches to Museum Spaces

The integration of health and well-being principles into museum design showcases an evolving trend towards interdisciplinary collaboration between arts professionals and healthcare practitioners. This approach emphasizes the creation of sensory-friendly spaces within museums, reflecting a growing recognition of the therapeutic value associated with such environments. These initiatives aim to enhance visitor experiences by addressing diverse needs through thoughtful spatial practices that transcend traditional exhibition strategies.

## Key Findings

- Sensory-friendly museum spaces can positively influence visitor well-being and engagement
- Interdisciplinary collaboration between arts and healthcare enhances the effectiveness of spatial interventions in museums
- Emerging spatial practices in museums are increasingly focused on health promotion

## Open Questions

- How can interdisciplinary approaches be scaled up to impact broader public health initiatives?
