## Arts On Prescription During The Lockdown History Project

The Arts On Prescription (AOP) initiative during the Lockdown History Project leverages creative arts therapy as a means to support mental health and well-being. Sir Quentin Blake's involvement adds an artistic dimension, enhancing engagement and emotional expression among participants. The project highlights the unique challenges posed by prolonged isolation and how AOP can provide therapeutic benefits through structured art-related activities in response to these conditions.

## Key Findings

- Arts On Prescription is a recognized intervention for mental health support.
- Sir Quentin Blake's contribution enriches the engagement and emotional expression in the program.
- The Lockdown History Project specifically addresses isolation-induced stress and anxiety through AOP.
