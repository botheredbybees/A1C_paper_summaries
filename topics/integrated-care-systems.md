## Integrated Care Systems

The provided sources highlight the role of creative interventions and arts-based approaches within integrated care systems. These systems aim to coordinate services across different health providers and sectors to improve patient outcomes and enhance quality of life. The Executive Summary from the Creative Health Review underscores how creativity, including arts in healthcare initiatives, acts as a determinant for health improvement by fostering social connections, personal resilience, and mental wellbeing. This suggests an emerging trend towards incorporating non-traditional therapeutic modalities into standard care pathways.

## Key Findings

- Integrated care systems are increasingly adopting creative interventions to enhance patient outcomes.
- Arts-based approaches contribute significantly to the health benefits derived from integrated care models.
- Creativity is recognized as a crucial determinant of overall public health and wellbeing.
