## Mixed-Methods Research Applications

The integration of qualitative and quantitative research methodologies in mixed-methods studies is demonstrated through the examination of two diverse applications. Source 1 discusses the impact of AI tools on cognitive offloading and critical thinking skills, highlighting how technology can alter human cognition and educational outcomes. In contrast, Source 2 focuses on a protocol for arts-based interventions aimed at improving quality of life and social engagement among individuals with dementia. Both sources underscore the versatility and depth mixed-methods approaches offer in understanding complex phenomena by combining quantitative data analysis with qualitative insights into individual experiences and societal impacts.

## Key Findings

- Mixed-methods research enhances understanding of multifaceted issues through comprehensive data integration
- Art-based interventions positively influence social engagement and quality-of-life for dementia patients
- Cognitive offloading via AI can affect educational attainment and critical thinking skills

## Open Questions

- How does the combination of quantitative and qualitative methods optimally enhance research on cognitive impacts of technology?
