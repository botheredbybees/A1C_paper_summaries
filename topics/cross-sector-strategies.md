## Cross-Sector Strategies for Health

The exploration of cross-sector strategies reveals a collaborative approach to addressing health and community well-being. This approach leverages the unique strengths of various sectors such as arts, education, healthcare, and community organizations to create holistic interventions that target both individual and population-level health outcomes. Artistic engagement emerges as a crucial component within these strategies, with arts-based initiatives shown to enhance social cohesion, mental health, and overall well-being. However, there is an observed tension between the ambitious goals of cross-sector collaborations and their practical implementation, including challenges related to funding, leadership buy-in, and measuring impact.

## Key Findings

- Artistic engagement can significantly contribute to community well-being by fostering social cohesion and improving mental health.
- Cross-sector strategies aim to integrate diverse resources from various sectors for a more comprehensive approach to health and well-being.
- Effective cross-sector partnerships require robust leadership commitment and adequate funding.

## Open Questions

- What are the most effective mechanisms for measuring the impact of cross-sector initiatives on community health outcomes?
