## Glucose Transport and Metabolism

The provided sources discuss the role of glucose transport in the aging hippocampus. Key mechanisms include changes in N-glycosylation pathways which impact glucose metabolism, potentially leading to altered cognitive functions associated with aging. These findings underscore the importance of understanding how cellular processes such as glycosylation can influence metabolic pathways and neurodegenerative diseases.

## Key Findings

- Changes in N-glycosylation pathways are linked to alterations in glucose metabolism.
- Glucose transport mechanisms are critical for maintaining proper brain function, particularly in aging tissues.
