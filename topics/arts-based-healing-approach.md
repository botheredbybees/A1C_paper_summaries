## Arts-Based Healing Approach

The sources collectively discuss the arts-based healing approach as a therapeutic intervention for enhancing resilience and mental health outcomes among individuals affected by natural disasters. The PR-6 model of resilience is highlighted in one source, illustrating how art therapy can foster resilience through structured curricula like SPARK. This approach emphasizes creativity and emotional expression to facilitate psychological recovery and community rebuilding efforts post-disaster. However, while the potential benefits are evident across sources, questions remain regarding the scalability and sustainability of such interventions in various cultural contexts.

## Key Findings

- Arts-based healing approaches can be effective for promoting resilience among disaster-affected populations
- Structured curricula like SPARK can operationalize arts-based healing within a framework that supports psychological recovery
- Emotional expression through art therapy aids in addressing mental health challenges following natural disasters

## Open Questions

- How scalable and sustainable are arts-based interventions for enhancing resilience, particularly in diverse cultural settings?
