## Self-expression Through Art

The sources collectively emphasize the therapeutic and developmental benefits of self-expression through art. The intervention is positioned as a powerful tool for personal growth, emotional release, and creative problem-solving. It encourages individuals to explore their inner worlds and express complex feelings that may be difficult to articulate verbally. This process not only fosters individual creativity but also enhances mental health by providing a medium for processing trauma and building resilience.

## Key Findings

- Art as an intervention facilitates emotional expression and healing
- Self-expression through art promotes personal growth and resilience
- Creative activities can be therapeutic, aiding in the resolution of psychological issues
