## Hidden Puppet Technique

The Hidden Puppet Technique is explored in the context of addressing emotional dysregulation and projection-in-therapy. The method involves using a puppet to externalize feelings, facilitating a cultural-sensitive therapeutic approach. This technique helps clients express and understand their emotions more effectively by providing an intermediary through which they can project and process complex internal states. Across sources, there is agreement on the value of such a tool in helping individuals navigate and articulate difficult emotional experiences. However, tensions arise regarding its applicability across different cultural contexts, highlighting the need for careful consideration when integrating this technique into diverse therapeutic settings.

## Key Findings

- The Hidden Puppet Technique aids in addressing emotional dysregulation.
- It serves as an effective intermediary for projection-in-therapy.
- Cultural sensitivity is a critical aspect of implementing the hidden puppet technique effectively.

## Open Questions

- How can cultural nuances be accommodated to ensure the universal efficacy of the hidden puppet technique?
