## Risk Analysis

The concept of risk analysis across the reviewed sources highlights its integral role in project design and management. Risk analysis serves as a critical tool for understanding potential threats or challenges that could impact project outcomes, such as cultural misalignment or failure to adopt inclusive practices (Source 1). It aids stakeholders in making informed decisions by identifying, assessing, and prioritizing risks through systematic methods.

## Key Findings

- Risk analysis is essential for effective project management and decision-making.
- Cultural awareness and inclusive practices are critical components of comprehensive risk analysis.
