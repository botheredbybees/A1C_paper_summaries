## Demographic Disparities in Educational Outcomes

The examination of educational outcomes across different demographics reveals persistent disparities influenced by factors such as socioeconomic status and access to extracurricular activities. Sources highlight the significant impact these factors have on academic performance, attendance rates, and standardized test scores like the PARCC tests. However, there is a notable gap in understanding how to effectively close these gaps through targeted interventions that address underlying issues of equity and accessibility.

## Key Findings

- Demographic disparities significantly influence educational outcomes.
- Participation in extracurricular activities positively correlates with academic performance.
- Access to resources like free and reduced lunch programs does not fully mitigate educational inequalities.

## Open Questions

- What are the most effective interventions for reducing demographic-based disparities in education?
