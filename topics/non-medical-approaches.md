## Non-Medical Approaches to Mental Health

The provided sources emphasize the importance of non-medical interventions in enhancing mental health and wellbeing. A common theme across these texts is the recognition that holistic care, which incorporates creative health strategies, can be more effective than traditional medical approaches alone. These strategies aim to address emotional, social, and psychological needs through means such as art therapy or community engagement rather than solely relying on pharmacological treatments. However, while there is consensus on the benefits of these non-medical methods, gaps remain in understanding their long-term efficacy and how they can be integrated into existing healthcare systems without medical oversight.

## Key Findings

- Non-medical approaches to mental health emphasize holistic care over traditional medical interventions.
- Creative health strategies such as art therapy are seen as beneficial components of non-medical mental health interventions.
- Holistic care aims to address multiple aspects of wellbeing beyond just treating symptoms.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term outcomes and sustainability of non-medical approaches compared to medical ones?
