## Integration In Social Commentary

The integration of social commentary into Punch and Judy shows has a long-standing tradition of using humor to critique societal norms. Historically, Punch's subversive nature served as a vehicle for expressing dissenting views and challenging authority figures. Over time, the character design evolved to reflect changing cultural contexts while maintaining its core role in offering social critique. The use of Punch and Judy performances has allowed for nuanced discussions on contemporary issues through their traditional framework.

## Key Findings

- Punch's subversive nature allows for social critique
- Costume evolution reflects societal changes
- Traditional format facilitates modern commentary
