## Montgomery-aasberg-depression-rating-scale-madrs

The Montgomery-Aasberg Depression Rating Scale (MADRS) is a widely used clinical tool for assessing the severity of depressive symptoms. It is particularly noted in studies involving music therapy as an intervention, providing quantifiable measures that complement qualitative assessments of patient improvement through musical engagement.

## Key Findings

- MADRS is integral to assessing changes in depression symptomatology.
- Music therapy has shown effectiveness when MADRS scores are used for evaluation.
- The integration of MADRS and music therapy provides a comprehensive approach to treating depression.

## Open Questions

- How does the use of MADRS with other interventions besides music therapy compare in efficacy?
