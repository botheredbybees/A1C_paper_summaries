## Occupational Therapy Practice Framework

The Occupational Therapy Practice Framework (OTPF) is a guiding document for occupational therapists that outlines the profession's unique domain of concern. The review on art interventions for children with autism spectrum disorder highlights how creative arts can serve as effective tools within this framework to enhance performance skills and social interaction. This approach underscores the OTPF’s emphasis on using evidence-based, client-centered strategies that promote engagement in meaningful activities for improved functioning.

## Key Findings

- The Occupational Therapy Practice Framework (OTPF) provides a comprehensive guide for practitioners focusing on enhancing clients' occupational performance.
- Creative arts interventions are effective within the framework for improving social interaction and performance skills among children with autism spectrum disorder.
