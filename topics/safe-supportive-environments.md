## Safe Supportive Environments

The concept of creating a safe and supportive environment is integral to various mental health interventions, particularly those involving the arts. Across sources, it emerges as essential for fostering personal growth through creative expression, aiding individuals in exploring their identities and emotions in a non-judgmental space. However, there are varying degrees of emphasis on how these environments should be structured and what specific elements contribute most effectively to their safety and supportiveness.

## Key Findings

- Safe supportive environments promote personal growth through creative expression
- Cultural expression within these spaces helps individuals explore identity and emotions

## Open Questions

- What are the key structural elements that define a safe and supportive environment in arts-based mental health interventions?
