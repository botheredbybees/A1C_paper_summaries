## Funding Justification for Community Arts Programs

The evaluation guide emphasizes the importance of demonstrating social outcomes as a means to secure and justify funding for community arts programs. It underscores that continuous improvement is essential, not just to meet current needs but also to adapt to changing societal contexts. The guide offers strategies for evaluating these programs rigorously to substantiate their contributions to community well-being.

## Key Findings

- Social outcomes are crucial in securing funding
- Continuous improvement enhances program effectiveness and relevance
