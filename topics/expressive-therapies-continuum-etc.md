## Expressive Therapies Continuum

The Expressive Therapies Continuum (ETC) is a framework used in art therapy and other expressive therapies that integrates sensory experiences with symbolic communication to promote emotional expression and empathy. Source 1 highlights the use of puppet making as an intervention within ETC for enhancing both cognitive and affective empathy, particularly in adolescents with autism. This approach underscores the versatility of ETC in facilitating therapeutic outcomes through creative activities.

## Key Findings

- Expressive Therapies Continuum (ETC) integrates sensory experiences with symbolic communication to foster emotional expression and empathy
- Puppet making within ETC can enhance both cognitive and affective empathy, especially for adolescents with autism
