## Anxiety Management

The exploration of anxiety management interventions reveals a variety of approaches aimed at improving emotional regulation and reducing symptoms in individuals. Puppetry has emerged as an innovative tool for mental health promotion, offering unique opportunities for social interaction and engagement that can contribute to the alleviation of anxiety symptoms. However, while puppetry demonstrates potential efficacy in certain populations, its broader application and comparative effectiveness with more traditional methods like cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) remain areas needing further investigation.

## Key Findings

- Puppetry as an intervention may facilitate emotional regulation and social interaction in individuals managing anxiety.
- Anxiety management interventions vary widely in their approaches, with puppetry being a less conventional but promising method.
- There is a need for more comparative studies between unconventional therapeutic methods like puppetry and traditional psychological interventions.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term outcomes of using puppetry as an anxiety management tool compared to established practices such as CBT?
