## Data Enrichment Through Art

The sources collectively explore the benefits of incorporating artistic activities into various health and social programs. Each source discusses unique ways art can be used to enhance data collection, patient engagement, and public policy reform. For instance, one approach uses creative writing workshops to gather qualitative insights from participants which can enrich databases with personal stories and perspectives, offering a richer understanding of community needs beyond quantitative data alone. Another method focuses on the implementation of social policies that support arts-based interventions in healthcare settings, aiming to improve health outcomes through enhanced patient engagement facilitated by art therapy and other artistic activities.

## Key Findings

- Artistic interventions can enrich qualitative data collection methods in health research and policy making.
- Creative writing workshops are effective tools for collecting personal stories which enhance public understanding of social issues.
- Social policies that support arts-based interventions improve patient engagement and overall health outcomes.

## Open Questions

- How can the integration of art into health data collection be standardized to ensure consistency across different settings?
