## Emotional Skills Development

The development of emotional skills is an intervention that can be integrated into various creative arts and play activities aimed at enhancing mental wellbeing. The sources highlight the importance of cross-disciplinary frameworks in facilitating the acquisition of these essential life skills, emphasizing their role in overall mental health and resilience. While there is a consensus on the benefits of such interventions, questions remain about how to effectively measure emotional skill development and tailor it to diverse populations.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts and play-based activities can be instrumental in developing emotional skills.
- Cross-disciplinary frameworks are crucial for enhancing emotional competencies in educational settings.
- Emotional skills contribute significantly to mental wellbeing and resilience.

## Open Questions

- How can the effectiveness of interventions aimed at developing emotional skills be measured accurately?
