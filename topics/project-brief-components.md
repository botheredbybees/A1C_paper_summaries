## Project Brief Components

The development of effective arts and health projects hinges on establishing clear partnerships and comprehensive project briefs. Sources emphasize the importance of a well-documented 'case for support' to articulate the rationale, goals, and impact of such initiatives. The inclusion of specific components within a project brief is crucial, ensuring clarity regarding project aims, roles, responsibilities, timelines, and evaluation criteria. These elements facilitate effective communication among stakeholders and enhance project feasibility and success.

## Key Findings

- Effective arts and health projects require clear partnerships
- Project briefs should include detailed case for support documentation
