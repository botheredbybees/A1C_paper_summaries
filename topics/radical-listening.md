## Radical Listening

The concept of 'radical listening' emerges as a transformative approach in various therapeutic and social contexts, emphasizing deep engagement and empathy towards unheard narratives. Puppet therapy, specifically mentioned in one source, serves as an innovative method to facilitate radical listening by providing participants with symbolic tools that help articulate and process complex emotions and traumatic experiences. This technique not only aids individuals in expressing their internal struggles but also fosters intergenerational dialogue, bridging gaps between different age groups and cultures through shared storytelling sessions using puppets. The integration of puppetry as a medium underscores the intervention's capacity to transcend verbal limitations, creating a safe space for vulnerable disclosures and mutual understanding.

## Key Findings

- Radical listening involves deep empathetic engagement with participants' unspoken narratives
- Puppet therapy can facilitate expression of complex emotions and traumatic experiences through symbolic representation
- Intergenerational dialogue is supported by puppetry, enhancing communication across different age groups
