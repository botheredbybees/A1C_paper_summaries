## Story Structure

The sources emphasize the foundational role of story structure in visual storytelling and memoir writing. Both stress the importance of a clear beginning that sets the stage for the narrative through elements like lead photos or establishing shots, as well as a compelling end to tie back to these initial points. The concept of 'story structure' is central, reflecting an organized approach to arranging events within a story, which can evoke emotional responses and convey deeper themes effectively. Additionally, sensory writing plays a key role in memoir writing by engaging the reader's senses to create vivid mental images, enhancing connection with the narrative. This methodological approach highlights how visual cues and language can work together to enhance storytelling efficacy.

## Key Findings

- Story structure is crucial for effective personal storytelling and memoir writing, providing a framework that enhances engagement through carefully chosen beginnings and endings.
- Visual elements like lead photos in visual stories serve the same purpose as sensory descriptions in memoirs: they immerse readers or viewers into the narrative context.
