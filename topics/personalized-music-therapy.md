## Personalized Music Therapy

The provided sources emphasize the effectiveness and broad applicability of personalized music therapy in enhancing well-being for dementia patients. The Wisconsin Music & Memory Program exemplifies this intervention, which customizes playlists to evoke positive emotional responses and memory recall among participants. Across all sources, personalized music therapy is highlighted as a beneficial practice that can improve resident well-being by increasing quality of life through tailored music interventions. Intergenerational programming also plays a role in the implementation and success of such programs, suggesting community involvement enhances its impact.

## Key Findings

- Personalized music therapy positively affects dementia patients' memory recall and emotional engagement.
- Customized playlists can enhance resident well-being in care settings through tailored interventions.
- Intergenerational programming supports the effectiveness of personalized music therapy.

## Open Questions

- How does community involvement influence the long-term success and sustainability of personalized music therapy programs?
