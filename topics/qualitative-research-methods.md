## Qualitative Research Methods

The provided sources offer insights into the evaluation of health and wellbeing benefits through various methodologies. Source 1 highlights the importance of qualitative research methods as a complement to randomized controlled trials (RCTs) in understanding complex phenomena within healthcare settings. Qualitative approaches are essential for capturing nuanced experiences, perceptions, and subjective understandings that quantitative RCTs may overlook due to their structured nature. These methods allow researchers to explore areas such as patient satisfaction, quality of life improvements, and the psychological impact of treatments, thereby providing a holistic view alongside empirical data.

## Key Findings

- Qualitative research is crucial for understanding subjective experiences in healthcare settings.
- Randomized controlled trials provide essential empirical evidence but may lack context-specific insights.
- Combining qualitative and quantitative methods enhances comprehensive evaluation of health interventions.

## Open Questions

- How can the integration of qualitative research methods be optimized to enhance the credibility and applicability of RCT findings?
