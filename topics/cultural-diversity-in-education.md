## Cultural Diversity in Education

The integration of cultural diversity into educational settings is a critical issue highlighted across various sources. Interactive theatre and drama-based strategies have been proposed as effective methods to address this need, particularly for sexual health promotion among multicultural youth (Source 1). These strategies not only aim at enhancing the knowledge base but also foster a more inclusive learning environment that respects and values diverse backgrounds. However, while interactive approaches are shown to be beneficial in promoting cultural awareness and understanding, there remains a challenge in ensuring their widespread implementation due to logistical and resource constraints.

## Key Findings

- Interactive theatre and drama-based strategies can effectively promote sexual health knowledge among multicultural youth
- Cultural diversity needs to be integrated into educational settings as an essential component of education
- There is a need for more inclusive learning environments that value diverse backgrounds

## Open Questions

- What are the main barriers to implementing interactive theatre and drama-based strategies in schools?
