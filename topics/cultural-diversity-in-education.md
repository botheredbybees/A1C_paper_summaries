## Cultural Diversity in Education

The integration of cultural diversity into educational practices is a critical component for effective learning environments that cater to the needs and backgrounds of all students. The 'Sharing Stories' youth theatre program highlights an innovative approach to integrating cultural diversity through interactive theatre, aimed at promoting sexual health awareness among multicultural youths. This method not only enhances sexual health knowledge but also fosters intercultural dialogue and understanding within a structured educational framework. While there is agreement on the benefits of such culturally responsive interventions for improving health outcomes and social cohesion among diverse populations, gaps remain in fully capturing their long-term impacts or potential scalability across different cultural contexts.

## Key Findings

- Interactive theatre programs can significantly enhance sexual health knowledge among multicultural youth.
- Educational interventions that embrace cultural diversity are crucial for promoting inclusivity and understanding in schools.
- Intercultural dialogue within educational settings improves social cohesion and health literacy.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of culturally responsive education on student well-being and academic success?
