## Social Impact Measurement Framework

The sources collectively discuss the application and significance of a social impact measurement (SIM) framework in assessing the effectiveness and value of interventions like Clown Doctors Australia. They emphasize the importance of quantifying not just financial outcomes but also qualitative aspects such as quality of care and overall societal benefit, using methodologies like SROI to provide comprehensive evaluations that can inform better resource allocation and policy-making.

## Key Findings

- Social impact measurement frameworks enable a holistic assessment of intervention effectiveness beyond traditional financial metrics.
- SROI is instrumental in quantifying the social value created by non-profit organizations such as Clown Doctors Australia, offering insights into quality-of-care improvements and patient well-being.
- The SIM framework facilitates more informed decision-making processes in healthcare resource allocation.
