## Social Distancing

The concept of social distancing as an intervention is explored in the context of public health responses to infectious diseases. In one example provided, a pub choir adapted their gatherings during coronavirus times by engaging in a virtual event that attracted over 1000 singers, demonstrating how traditional community activities can be reimagined for online platforms to maintain engagement and support under social distancing guidelines.

## Key Findings

- Social distancing measures have facilitated the rise of virtual communal activities.
- Communities find innovative ways to stay connected while adhering to public health guidelines.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts on community engagement and mental well-being due to prolonged social distancing?
