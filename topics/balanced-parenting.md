## Balanced Parenting

The concept of balanced parenting emerges as a critical intervention to support the mental health and well-being of teenagers. Across sources, there is agreement on the importance of fostering emotional resilience in teens through effective stress management techniques. Balanced parenting involves a careful approach where parents neither overprotect nor neglect their children, allowing them space for growth while still providing necessary support. This balance helps teenagers develop healthy coping mechanisms and stronger mental health resilience. However, there are tensions regarding the exact methods and boundaries within this balanced approach, especially in navigating the digital age's unique challenges. The sources highlight the need for parents to engage actively yet responsibly with their teens' lives, ensuring they remain supportive without being overly intrusive or detached.

## Key Findings

- Balanced parenting is crucial for promoting emotional resilience in teenagers.
- Effective stress management techniques are essential components of balanced parenting strategies.
- Parents should avoid both overprotectiveness and neglect to foster healthy development.
