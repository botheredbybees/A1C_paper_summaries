## Psychological Benefits

The reviewed literature on dance therapy highlights significant psychological benefits such as reduced anxiety and improved self-esteem in children and adolescents. Sources converge on the positive impact of rhythmic activities on mental health, though physiological effects are also noted. However, there is a lack of focus on long-term therapeutic outcomes and the mechanisms behind these psychological improvements.

## Key Findings

- Dance therapy enhances psychological well-being
- Reduced anxiety in young participants
- Improved self-esteem among children and adolescents

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term psychological impacts of dance therapy?
