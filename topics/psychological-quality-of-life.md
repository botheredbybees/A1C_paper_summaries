## Psychological Quality of Life

The concept of enhancing psychological quality of life through therapeutic interventions is a focal point across studies. The 'Song of Life' (SOL) study, for instance, emphasizes the role of music therapy in palliative care settings to improve emotional support and spiritual integration among patients. This approach underscores how tailored therapies can address both the tangible and intangible aspects of well-being in critical medical conditions. Despite the promising nature of such interventions, there is a consistent recognition that psychological quality of life involves complex interplays between physiological, emotional, and spiritual dimensions. However, current research often lacks comprehensive methodologies to capture these multifaceted experiences effectively.

## Key Findings

- Music therapy can enhance psychological quality of life in palliative care settings through emotional and spiritual support.
- Interventions aimed at improving psychological quality of life require a holistic approach addressing multiple dimensions of well-being.

## Open Questions

- What are the most effective methodologies for measuring and enhancing multi-dimensional aspects of psychological quality of life?
