## Contractual Agreements in Arts and Health

The integration of arts into health projects requires robust contractual frameworks to ensure artists are adequately supported. Sources highlight the importance of clear induction processes that provide necessary training and support, and funding strategies that sustainably finance these initiatives. However, there is a tension between ensuring artists have creative freedom while also adhering to project constraints.

## Key Findings

- Contractual agreements in arts-health projects need clarity.
- Induction processes are crucial for successful artist integration into health settings.
- Sustainable funding models are essential for long-term success of arts-integrated interventions.

## Open Questions

- How can artists maintain creative freedom while adhering to project constraints?
