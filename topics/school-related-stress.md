## School-Related Stress Among Youth

The Youth Survey Report 2025 highlights the pervasive issue of school-related stress among youth today. This report underscores how contemporary challenges such as high cost of living and mental health issues significantly exacerbate students' academic pressures, leading to increased levels of anxiety and depression. The findings indicate a direct correlation between heightened financial burdens on families and greater psychological distress experienced by children attending schools under these economic constraints.

## Key Findings

- High cost of living is identified as a contributing factor to school-related stress among youth.
- Mental health issues are prevalent and intertwined with the pressures associated with schooling, indicating a complex interplay between personal well-being and educational challenges.
