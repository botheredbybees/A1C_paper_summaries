## Rhythmic Auditory Stimulation

The provided sources highlight the therapeutic benefits of rhythmic auditory stimulation (RAS) as an intervention for improving various aspects of health and wellbeing, particularly in neurological recovery contexts. Neural plasticity is noted as a key mechanism through which RAS can facilitate improvements in executive functioning by engaging specific brain regions involved in motor control and cognitive processing. Case studies illustrate how RAS integrates with music therapy to provide significant therapeutic benefits, underscoring its role in rehabilitation strategies for patients recovering from injuries or diseases affecting the nervous system.

## Key Findings

- Rhythmic auditory stimulation (RAS) enhances neural plasticity.
- RAS is effective in improving executive functioning.
- Case studies support RAS as a beneficial component of music therapy.
