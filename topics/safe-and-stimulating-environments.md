## Safe And Stimulating Environments

The concept of providing infants with 'safe and stimulating environments' is central to supporting their early development. Across the sources, tummy-time emerges as a key intervention that not only aids in physical strength but also stimulates cognitive growth through exploration. Foundational learning abilities are emphasized as essential for children's overall developmental progress, indicating that safe spaces designed to encourage movement and curiosity can significantly enhance these skills. However, there is a noted tension between ensuring safety and allowing adequate freedom for the necessary risk-taking inherent in exploratory play.

## Key Findings

- Tummy-time supports physical strength and cognitive development
- Safe environments are crucial but must balance safety with opportunities for exploration
- Foundational learning abilities are enhanced through engaging and secure settings

## Open Questions

- How to optimally design spaces that balance safety concerns with the need for exploratory play
