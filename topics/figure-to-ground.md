## Figure-To-Ground in Visual Composition

The concept of 'figure-to-ground' is a fundamental principle in visual composition that distinguishes the main subject or figure from its background. This principle helps photographers and artists create clarity, balance, and visual interest in their work by ensuring that the primary focus stands out distinctly against the surrounding elements. Sources highlight how this method enhances readability and aesthetic appeal by managing contrast between figures and grounds effectively.

## Key Findings

- Figure-to-ground is crucial for enhancing visual communication.
- It involves creating clear contrasts to distinguish subjects from backgrounds.
- Effective use of figure-to-ground principles improves image clarity and viewer engagement.
