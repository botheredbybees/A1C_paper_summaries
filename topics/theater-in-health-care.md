## Theater in Health Care

The application of theatrical methods and playful engagement strategies within healthcare settings offers a unique approach to enhancing patient experiences. Sources illustrate the use of theatre techniques as interventions for therapeutic purposes, particularly beneficial in dementia care where such creative activities can improve social interaction and emotional well-being among patients. These interventions aim to provide psychological and physical benefits, encouraging expression through playfulness which can alleviate symptoms associated with various health conditions.

## Key Findings

- Theater-based interventions are increasingly recognized for their potential therapeutic value in healthcare settings.
- Playful engagement strategies, such as those used in dementia care, significantly enhance social interaction and emotional well-being among patients.
- Creative activities like theater can provide psychological benefits by encouraging self-expression through play.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of sustained theatrical interventions on patient health outcomes?
