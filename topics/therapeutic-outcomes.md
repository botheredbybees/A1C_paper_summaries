## Therapeutic Outcomes of Arts Therapies for Eating Disorders

This synthesis focuses on the efficacy and outcomes of arts therapies in treating eating disorders. Both sources present a scoping review protocol that aims to explore the therapeutic benefits of various art-based interventions among individuals suffering from eating disorders. The studies highlight the potential for arts therapies to provide unique avenues for emotional expression, psychological insight, and behavioral change crucial for recovery. However, there is a noted gap in standardized methodologies and outcome measures across different artistic modalities within this field, which hinders comprehensive evaluation of their therapeutic effectiveness.

## Key Findings

- Arts therapies offer potential benefits for individuals with eating disorders
- Emotional expression and psychological insight are key components in arts-based interventions
- Lack of standardization in evaluating therapeutic outcomes across arts therapy methods

## Open Questions

- How can standardized outcome measures be developed to better assess the effectiveness of arts therapies in treating eating disorders?
