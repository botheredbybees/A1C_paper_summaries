## Digital Arts in Community Engagement and Education

The integration of digital arts into community engagement and education is a growing trend that reflects the evolving nature of creative expression and learning. The National Arts Participation Survey highlights how digital platforms are being leveraged to increase participation, enhance educational outcomes, and foster community engagement in artistic activities. Digital tools provide an accessible means for both professional artists and amateurs to create, share, and critique works online, potentially democratizing access to the arts. However, this shift also raises questions about the quality of education and engagement when compared to traditional methods, indicating a need for research into the most effective ways digital platforms can support artistic development.

## Key Findings

- Digital arts offer increased accessibility through community engagement
- Enhanced educational outcomes are linked with participation in digital arts
- There is a growing use of digital tools in creative expression

## Open Questions

- How does the quality and effectiveness of education compare between traditional and digital arts?
