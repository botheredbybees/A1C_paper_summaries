## Reflective Journaling

Reflective journaling is a therapeutic practice emphasizing the act of writing down thoughts and feelings to foster self-understanding. Across various studies and reviews, it emerges as an intervention that encourages emotional introspection, helping individuals explore their emotions and experiences in depth. Sources highlight its effectiveness in promoting mental health by providing a platform for personal expression and reflection. However, there is variability in the methodologies used across different studies and contexts, affecting the consistency of outcomes observed.

## Key Findings

- Reflective journaling aids emotional introspection.
- It serves as an intervention to promote mental health through self-expression.
- Variability in implementation methods influences effectiveness.

## Open Questions

- How does variability in reflective journaling methodologies impact its therapeutic efficacy?
