## Emotion Regulation Checklist (Erc)

The Emotion Regulation Checklist (ERC) is a tool that assesses emotion regulation skills in children and has been utilized to measure the efficacy of interventions aimed at improving these skills. Specifically, studies involving Child-Centred Play Therapy (CCPT) and Rhythmic Relating have shown positive outcomes on ERC scores for children with autism. These findings highlight the importance of play therapy and rhythmic engagement as effective strategies in enhancing emotion regulation abilities.

## Key Findings

- Emotion Regulation Checklist is a reliable tool to measure improvement in emotion regulation skills.
- Child-Centred Play Therapy (CCPT) combined with Rhythmic Relating shows promise in improving emotion regulation for children with autism.
- Interventions that focus on play and rhythmic engagement can be effective in enhancing emotion regulation

## Open Questions
