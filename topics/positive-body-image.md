## Positive Body Image

The sources collectively highlight the multifaceted approach to fostering a positive body image through therapeutic interventions. Art therapy and collaborative programs are found to be particularly effective in addressing negative body perceptions, especially among individuals dealing with eating disorders. These methods emphasize empowerment and self-expression, aiming to shift focus from external criticism to internal acceptance. Despite these promising strategies, challenges remain in tailoring approaches for diverse populations and ensuring long-term maintenance of positive body image.

## Key Findings

- Art therapy is an effective intervention for promoting a positive body image among individuals with eating disorders.
- Collaborative programs that involve multiple visits can enhance the effectiveness of interventions aimed at fostering positive body image.
- Empowerment through self-expression and internal acceptance are critical components in therapeutic approaches to improve body image.

## Open Questions

- How can existing interventions for positive body image be adapted or tailored to meet the needs of diverse populations?
