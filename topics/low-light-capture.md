## Low-Light Capture Techniques

The integration of multiple sources reveals that low-light capture techniques in photography and videography involve adjusting image parameters to improve visibility and detail under insufficient lighting conditions. Sources highlight the importance of brightness adjustment, contrast enhancement, and other specific adjustments tailored for low light scenarios. These methods aim to preserve details while maintaining a natural look without excessive noise or graininess, which can detract from the quality of the final output.

## Key Findings

- Brightness adjustment is critical in enhancing images captured in low-light conditions.
- Contrast enhancement helps improve visual clarity and definition in dimly lit scenes.
- Preserving detail without introducing significant digital noise is a key challenge in low-light capture.
