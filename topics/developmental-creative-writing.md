## Developmental Creative Writing

The synthesis examines the evolution from expressive writing to developmental creative writing, highlighting its increasing focus on personal growth and psychological development. While traditional expressive writing emphasizes emotional release through self-disclosure, newer models in developmental creative writing aim at fostering long-term therapeutic benefits through a more structured approach that includes reflection and integration of experiences. This shift recognizes the potential for narrative creation to not only serve as an outlet for emotions but also to facilitate cognitive restructuring and resilience building.

## Key Findings

- Developmental creative writing builds upon expressive writing by integrating elements such as reflective practice and personal growth into the process
- The therapeutic value of developmental creative writing extends beyond emotional catharsis, offering tools for cognitive development and resilience

## Open Questions

- What are the optimal methods to integrate reflection and narrative creation in a way that maximizes psychological benefits?
