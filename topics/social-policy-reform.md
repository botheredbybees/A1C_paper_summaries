## Social Policy Reform Through Arts Integration

The integration of arts into social policy reform is explored through various mechanisms such as art therapy and creative writing workshops. These interventions aim to improve health outcomes and enrich data collection in ways that traditional methods might not achieve. While the benefits of these practices are acknowledged, challenges remain in terms of implementing comprehensive policies at a societal level.

## Key Findings

- Arts-based interventions like art therapy can positively impact mental health outcomes.
- Creative writing workshops contribute to personal expression and social engagement among participants.
- Data enrichment through arts initiatives provides novel insights into community needs and resources.

## Open Questions

- What are the barriers to incorporating arts-based approaches in widespread social policy reforms?
