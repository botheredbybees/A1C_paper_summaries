## Psychological Distress

The provided sources explore the concept of psychological distress from a therapeutic perspective, primarily focusing on interventions and creative processes that individuals can employ to cope with stress. Source 1 delves into the therapeutic and artistic process involved in creating a photo essay about IVF treatments, highlighting how engaging in mindful photography can be beneficial for those experiencing high levels of psychological distress due to fertility challenges. This approach not only allows for emotional expression but also promotes mindfulness and self-reflection.

## Key Findings

- Mindful photography can serve as a therapeutic intervention for individuals facing psychological distress from fertility issues.
- Creative processes such as photo essays provide an outlet for emotional expression and promote mental health.
- Self-reflection through creative activities like mindful photography is beneficial in managing psychological stress.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects of using creative interventions, such as photography, to manage psychological distress?
