## Sexual Health Education

The sources emphasize the critical role of comprehensive sexual health education in addressing complex issues like premarital sex, marriage expectations, and virginity pledges. They highlight how cultural norms around relationships and sexuality can influence individual choices and societal attitudes towards sexual behavior. However, there is a tension between traditional values that discourage premarital sex and educational programs aimed at promoting safe practices.

## Key Findings

- Sexual health education is crucial for addressing issues related to marriage expectations and premarital sex.
- Cultural norms significantly impact individual decisions regarding sexual activity and relationship commitments.
- Educational interventions face challenges in balancing traditional values with the need for informed decision-making.

## Open Questions

- How can educational programs effectively bridge cultural expectations and the promotion of safe sexual practices?
