## Arts and Refugees

The integration of arts into refugee support programs highlights a multidisciplinary approach to addressing the complex needs of displaced populations. Both sources emphasize the therapeutic benefits of artistic practices for refugees, fostering not only individual healing but also community building through shared cultural expressions and intercultural understanding. Vol. 2 of 'Arts and Refugees: Multidisciplinary Perspectives' delves into gender dynamics within these arts-based interventions, suggesting that women often play a crucial role in maintaining cultural traditions and creating safe spaces for expression. Meanwhile, the lesson on 'Arts Collaborations with Refugee Communities' underscores the importance of preserving cultural identity and facilitating intercultural understanding as key goals of art-for-healing initiatives.

## Key Findings

- Artistic practices offer therapeutic benefits to refugees, aiding in individual healing and community cohesion
- Women are often pivotal in maintaining cultural traditions within refugee communities through arts-based interventions
- Intercultural understanding is a vital outcome of arts collaborations with refugee populations

## Open Questions

- How can gender dynamics be further integrated into the design and evaluation of arts-based refugee support programs?
