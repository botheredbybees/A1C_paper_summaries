## Individualized Music-Based Intervention for Acute Neuropsychiatric Symptoms

The study examines the efficacy of an individualized music-based intervention to manage acute neuropsychiatric symptoms in hospitalized older adults with cognitive impairment. It highlights that nonpharmacological treatments such as personalized music therapy can be effective alternatives or complements to traditional pharmacological approaches, providing relief from symptoms without side effects common with medication use.

## Key Findings

- Individualized music-based interventions are beneficial for managing acute neuropsychiatric symptoms in cognitively impaired older adults.
- Nonpharmacological treatments offer viable alternatives to drug therapy, reducing potential adverse reactions and improving quality of life.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects of individualized music-based interventions on cognitive health and mental well-being?
