## Respiratory Function Assessment

The sources indicate that respiratory function assessment is a critical aspect of managing conditions like COPD. Singing for Better Breathing, as explored in the first source, provides a method to improve breathing patterns and enhance respiratory muscle strength among patients with chronic obstructive pulmonary disease. This approach suggests a shift from traditional medical assessments towards integrative methods involving music therapy or singing techniques which can be used alongside conventional clinical evaluations.

## Key Findings

- Respiratory function assessment is crucial for managing COPD.
- Singing-based interventions enhance respiratory health and quality of life in patients with COPD.
