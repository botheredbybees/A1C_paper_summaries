## Youth Cost-of-Living Concerns

The Youth Survey Reports from 2024 and 2025 highlight consistent concerns among young people regarding the cost of living. Both reports emphasize that high expenses are not only straining their financial resources but also impacting their mental health, creating a cycle of stress and economic anxiety. The 2024 report adds nuance by including discrimination experiences as a factor affecting youth finances and overall well-being. Meanwhile, the 2025 report further elucidates school-related stress contributing to increased cost-of-living concerns. Both years indicate that rising expenses are leading to significant mental health issues among young people, suggesting a pressing need for support systems tailored to address both financial stability and psychological resilience.

## Key Findings

- Youth face increasing cost-of-living pressures that impact their financial stability.
- Mental health issues correlate with the economic strain faced by youth.
- School-related stress compounds existing financial difficulties among young people.

## Open Questions

- How can educational institutions better support students financially to alleviate school-related stress and its associated cost-of-living concerns?
