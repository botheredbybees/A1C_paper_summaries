## Cyberbullying Intervention

The discussion on cyberbullying intervention reveals a multi-faceted approach that integrates traditional methods of theatre and audience engagement to combat digital harassment. Sources highlight the importance of interactive interventions that educate both victims and perpetrators about the harmful effects of cyberbullying while fostering empathy among participants. Theatre-based activities, specifically designed for online platforms, serve as powerful tools in creating awareness and promoting anti-bullying behaviors within youth communities. However, there is a need to explore more technologically advanced methods alongside traditional theatrical techniques to ensure interventions remain relevant and effective.

## Key Findings

- Theatre-based cyberbullying interventions can effectively educate participants about the consequences of online harassment.
- Interactive audience engagement plays a crucial role in fostering empathy among youth involved in or affected by cyberbullying.
- Combining technological advancements with theatre methods enhances intervention efficacy in addressing cyberbullying

## Open Questions

- How can traditional theatre-based interventions be adapted to fully leverage digital technologies for better reach and effectiveness against cyberbullying?
