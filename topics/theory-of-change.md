## Theory Of Change

The Theory of Change is a conceptual framework that outlines the steps needed to achieve desired goals. It provides a detailed pathway from the current situation to the intended outcomes through specific actions and conditions. In the context of Clown Doctors Australia's strategy, this theory emphasizes how laughter and clowning activities contribute to enhancing patients' wellbeing in healthcare settings. The key concepts like 'Clown Doctors Program' and 'Laughter Care Framework' underscore an intentional design focused on delivering therapeutic benefits such as reduced anxiety and pain through humor and play. Though the sources focus heavily on application within Clown Doctors Australia, there is a clear theoretical underpinning supporting its effectiveness.

## Key Findings

- The Theory of Change framework outlines sequential steps towards achieving positive outcomes in healthcare settings.
- Clowning activities are purposefully designed to enhance patient wellbeing through reduced anxiety and pain via humor and play.
- Laughter Care Framework provides a structured approach for implementing clown doctors programs effectively.
