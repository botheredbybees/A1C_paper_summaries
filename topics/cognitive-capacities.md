## Cognitive Capacities in Dementia Care

The integration of art-based interventions in dementia care settings enhances the quality of life for both individuals with dementia and their caregivers. Through multi-session art-gallery visits, participants engage in activities that not only provide a break from the daily stressors but also help maintain or improve cognitive capacities. However, there are discrepancies regarding the extent to which such interventions can sustainably influence caregiver burden over prolonged periods. While some studies indicate significant positive impacts on reducing anxiety and enhancing social interaction among participants, others highlight the need for more personalized approaches considering individual differences in cognitive decline.

## Key Findings

- Art-based interventions positively impact quality of life for individuals with dementia and their caregivers.
- Activities such as visiting art galleries can enhance or maintain cognitive capacities.
- Interventions may vary in effectiveness based on personal needs and progression of dementia.

## Open Questions

- How sustainable are the benefits of art-based interventions over time, considering varying degrees of dementia progression?
