## Physiological and Psychological Benefits of Dance

The systematic review 'The Physiological and Psychological Benefits of Dance' investigates the advantages of dance for children and adolescents. It highlights that regular participation in dance can significantly enhance physical health, including improvements in cardiovascular fitness, flexibility, and strength. Additionally, the psychological benefits are profound: reduced stress levels, enhanced mood, and better social skills. The review also notes dance as an effective alternative therapy that complements traditional medical treatments. However, it points out variability across studies regarding the intensity of effects, suggesting a need for further research to standardize measurements and enhance intervention efficacy.

## Key Findings

- Dance provides both physiological benefits like improved cardiovascular health and flexibility.
- Psychological benefits include reduced stress and enhanced mood among participants.
- Dance can serve as an alternative therapy complementing traditional medical approaches.

## Open Questions

- How to standardize measurement of dance intervention effects across diverse study populations?
