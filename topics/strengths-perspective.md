## Strengths Perspective in Therapeutic Interventions

The concept of the strengths perspective is well-integrated across therapeutic interventions for enhancing creativity and conflict resolution among children. This approach emphasizes fostering positive attributes and capabilities within clients rather than focusing on deficits or problems. Source 1 highlights the use of puppet-assisted play therapy to encourage children's imagination and creativity, aligning with a strengths-based approach that leverages their unique abilities and interests. The integration of mindfulness in this therapeutic context also underscores an appreciation for the inherent resources and resilience of children, promoting self-awareness and emotional regulation as key strengths.

## Key Findings

- Puppet-assisted play therapy is used to enhance creativity and conflict resolution among children.
- The strengths perspective encourages a focus on positive attributes within clients rather than deficits.
- Mindfulness practices in therapeutic settings support the cultivation of self-awareness and emotional regulation as key strengths.

## Open Questions
