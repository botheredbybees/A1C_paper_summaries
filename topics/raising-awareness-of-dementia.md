## Raising Awareness of Dementia

The sources highlight the role of creative interventions like physical theatre in raising awareness about dementia. Sundowner, a play by KAGE Physical Theatre, leverages lived-experience narratives to provide audiences with insights into the challenges faced by individuals living with dementia. This approach not only educates but also fosters empathy and understanding among viewers towards those affected by this condition. However, while such interventions are valuable in reaching public consciousness, their impact on long-term societal attitudes and practices remains uncertain.

## Key Findings

- Creative interventions like physical theatre plays can effectively raise awareness about dementia.
- Lived-experience narratives incorporated into creative works provide a deeper understanding of the lived realities of individuals with dementia.
- Public education through artistic means contributes to fostering empathy towards those affected by dementia.

## Open Questions

- What is the long-term impact of using creative interventions on societal attitudes and practices regarding dementia?
