## Creative Economy

The creative economy encompasses various sectors that drive economic growth through the production and distribution of culture. The Australian government's 'Revive' policy highlights the importance of First Nations stories in revitalizing cultural infrastructure, aiming to support a thriving creative sector that includes arts, media, and design industries. This policy recognizes storytelling as central to community identity and economic resilience, emphasizing the need for inclusive narratives and equitable opportunities within the creative economy.

## Key Findings

- First Nations stories are crucial for fostering cultural revitalization in Australia's creative economy
- Cultural infrastructure is essential for supporting the growth of the creative sector
- Inclusive narratives contribute to economic resilience by engaging diverse communities

## Open Questions

- How can policies effectively bridge the gap between First Nations storytelling and broader economic benefits?
