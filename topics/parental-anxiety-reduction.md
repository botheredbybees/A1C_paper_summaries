## Parental Anxiety Reduction

The synthesis of the provided sources highlights interventions aimed at reducing anxiety in parents whose children are undergoing medical procedures. Therapeutic clowning stands out as an innovative approach, with studies indicating its effectiveness in providing distraction therapy and facilitating anesthesia induction, thereby indirectly contributing to parental anxiety reduction through improved child cooperation and reduced stress levels during medical procedures. While therapeutic clowning shows promise in enhancing the overall patient experience, it is essential to consider additional strategies that address parents' psychological needs directly without relying solely on indirect benefits from pediatric interventions.

## Key Findings

- Therapeutic clowning serves as a distraction therapy for children undergoing medical procedures.
- The intervention indirectly aids parental anxiety reduction by supporting anesthesia induction and improving child cooperation during stressful events.

## Open Questions

- What are the direct psychological benefits of therapeutic clowning on parents, independent of their children's experience?
