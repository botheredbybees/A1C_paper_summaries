## Creativity Stimulation

The provided sources highlight the use of creative interventions to stimulate creativity and enhance cognitive functions such as memory and storytelling. Both texts advocate for the use of external stimuli like images or reminiscence-based techniques to facilitate these benefits, emphasizing their effectiveness in diverse contexts. The overlap between these approaches underscores a shared understanding that targeted prompts can serve as catalysts for enhanced creativity and mental engagement.

## Key Findings

- Reminiscence-based activities and the use of visual stimuli are effective interventions for stimulating creativity.
- Both methods enhance cognitive functions such as memory association and storytelling skills.
- Targeted prompts (visual or through reminiscence) serve as catalysts to unlock creative potential in individuals.

## Open Questions

- How do individual differences influence the effectiveness of these creativity stimulation interventions?
