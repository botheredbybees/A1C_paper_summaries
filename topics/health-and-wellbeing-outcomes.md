## Health and Wellbeing Outcomes

The integration of creative arts into community practices has emerged as a promising intervention for enhancing both physical and mental health outcomes. Sources highlight the benefits of arts-on-prescription initiatives, which encourage individuals to engage with various forms of art as part of their therapeutic regimen. These programs aim to foster social cohesion by bringing people together through shared artistic experiences, thus addressing feelings of isolation and loneliness often linked to poor health. However, while there is consensus on the positive outcomes associated with these interventions, questions remain about how to scale such initiatives effectively and ensure they are accessible to diverse populations.

## Key Findings

- Arts-on-prescription programs show promise in improving health and wellbeing.
- Engagement in community arts activities fosters social cohesion.
- Creative arts can help address loneliness and isolation

## Open Questions

- How can creative arts interventions be scaled up for broader impact?
