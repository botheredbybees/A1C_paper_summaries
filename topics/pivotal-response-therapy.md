## Pivotal Response Therapy

The synthesis of the provided source highlights Pivotal Response Therapy (PRT) as a specific type of intervention within the broader scope of play and arts-based therapies. The text emphasizes its application in improving expressive skills and emotional regulation, especially among children. This therapy is child-centered, aligning with the principles of positive reinforcement and focusing on pivotal areas that can lead to generalized improvements across other behaviors. While the source does not extensively detail PRT's mechanisms or outcomes compared to other therapeutic approaches, it underscores the importance of play-based techniques in facilitating developmental progress.
