## Therapeutic Journaling

The concept of therapeutic journaling is highlighted across the provided sources as a beneficial practice that enhances mental well-being and fosters self-awareness. It involves expressive writing, which has been found to aid individuals in processing emotions and experiences. While each source underscores the potential benefits of journaling for psychological health, there are variations in specific techniques or outcomes emphasized.

## Key Findings

- Therapeutic journaling enhances mental well-being through increased self-awareness
- Expressive writing is a key component of therapeutic journaling
- Journaling can help individuals process and understand their emotions
