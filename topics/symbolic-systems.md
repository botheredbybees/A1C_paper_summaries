## Symbolic Systems in Visual Communication

The concept of symbolic systems is crucial for understanding how communities use and interpret visual messages. Each source highlights different aspects of this theoretical framework: Source 1 discusses the importance of analyzing visual literacy within cultural contexts, emphasizing that symbols are not universal but rather shaped by societal norms and values. This perspective underscores the necessity to create a community arts profile which captures unique symbolic systems at play in specific locales.

## Key Findings

- Visual messages vary greatly between cultures due to differing symbolic systems.
- Symbolic analysis is essential for effective visual literacy across diverse communities
- Creating an inventory of content elements helps understand how symbols function within a community's arts profile.

## Open Questions

- How can the interpretation and creation of symbolic systems be standardized while respecting cultural differences?
