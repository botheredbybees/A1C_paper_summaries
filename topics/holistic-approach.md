## Holistic Approach in Community Health Interventions

The holistic approach as a framework for community health interventions emphasizes the interconnectedness of social determinants and individual needs within communities. Both sources highlight the importance of creative health initiatives, which encompass arts-based methods to address broader societal issues such as inequalities in resource distribution and access. The concept underscores that effective interventions must be tailored to meet community-specific goals and foster ownership among residents for sustainable impact. However, there is an acknowledged gap in ensuring equitable access to these resources across diverse communities, necessitating further research on scalable models.

## Key Findings

- The holistic approach integrates social determinants of health with individual needs.
- Creative health interventions play a crucial role in addressing societal inequalities.
- Community ownership and engagement are vital for the success of such initiatives.

## Open Questions

- How can equitable access to creative health resources be ensured across diverse communities?
