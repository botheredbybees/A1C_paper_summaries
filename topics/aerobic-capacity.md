## Aerobic Capacity in Female Adolescents

The sources collectively emphasize the role of dance and exercise interventions in enhancing the aerobic capacity of female adolescents. While 'Dance 4 Your Life' focuses on health benefits specific to contemporary dance, including self-esteem and upper-body strength, other unspecified sources suggest similar improvements in cardiovascular fitness through various physical activities. There is a consensus that these interventions positively impact physical health outcomes. However, there remains a gap regarding the long-term sustainability of aerobic capacity gains post-intervention.

## Key Findings

- Physical activity interventions improve aerobic capacity
- Contemporary dance enhances self-esteem and upper body strength

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects on aerobic capacity after intervention discontinuation?
