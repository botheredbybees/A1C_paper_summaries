## Resilience Development in Youth

Both sources discuss strategies for enhancing resilience and mental well-being among youth. Source 1 highlights the role of arts activities in fostering self-efficacy and resilience, emphasizing their contribution to overall mental health. In contrast, Source 2 outlines specific stress management techniques aimed at developing resilience, focusing on practical skills and wellness practices that can mitigate stress and build long-term resilience. While both sources advocate for interventions targeting youth's psychological development, there is a distinction in the methods proposed: arts-based activities versus direct stress management strategies.

## Key Findings

- Arts activities contribute significantly to self-efficacy and resilience among young people.
- Stress management techniques are effective tools for developing personal resilience.
- Both arts engagement and stress reduction practices enhance mental well-being.

## Open Questions

- How do the long-term effects of arts-based interventions compare with those of stress management techniques in building resilience?
