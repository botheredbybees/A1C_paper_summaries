## Gerotranscendence

The concept of gerotranscendence refers to the transformation and transcendence experienced by older adults as they age. The reciprocal influence between art and aging highlights how creativity can expand with age, aligning with the theory that later life stages involve a shift from materialistic values towards more spiritual or existential concerns. However, there is a tension in defining exactly what constitutes 'creative expansion' within the context of gerotranscendence, and whether this process is uniform across different cultural and individual experiences.

## Key Findings

- Gerotranscendence involves a shift from materialistic to more existential or spiritual values with aging
- Creative expression can expand and evolve in later life stages, contributing to gerotranscendence
- The relationship between art and aging suggests that creativity may play an integral role in the process of gerotranscendence

## Open Questions

- What are the cultural variations in how creative expansion manifests with age and contributes to gerotranscendence?
