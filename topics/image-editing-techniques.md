## Image Editing Techniques

The provided sources emphasize the multifaceted nature of image editing techniques as both a practical skill and a therapeutic practice. Source one underscores the importance of software tools in executing specific image editing tasks, highlighting technical proficiency as key to effective manipulation. In contrast, source two introduces a broader context for image editing by discussing its potential as a form of therapeutic photography and mindfulness practice. This suggests that beyond mere technical application, image editing can serve as a medium for emotional expression and mental well-being. Both sources converge on the central role of specific techniques in achieving desired outcomes, whether those outcomes are aesthetic adjustments or psychological benefits.

## Key Findings

- Image editing techniques encompass both practical skills and therapeutic applications.
- Software tools are essential for executing precise image modifications.
- Therapeutic photography can utilize image editing as a mindfulness practice.
