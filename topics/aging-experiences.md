## Aging Experiences

The concept of aging experiences encompasses the unique challenges and enrichments that come with growing older. Creative arts-based research in aged care, specifically through methodologies like photovoice, photography, and poetry, allows for a more nuanced understanding of these experiences. This approach empowers elderly individuals to express their perspectives on life stages, health issues, social isolation, and community engagement. While each medium offers distinct insights into aging emotions and challenges, there is a consistent thread highlighting the importance of emotional expression in enhancing well-being among older adults.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts-based research methods like photovoice are effective in capturing diverse aspects of aging experiences.
- Photography and poetry allow elderly individuals to articulate their feelings regarding health issues and social isolation.
- Emotional expression through creative mediums is crucial for improving the quality of life for seniors.
