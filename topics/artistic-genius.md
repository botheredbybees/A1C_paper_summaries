## Artistic Genius

The concept of artistic genius is explored across various sources, focusing on how engagement with the arts can impact mental and emotional well-being. Sources discuss the benefits of community-based art projects in fostering a sense of belonging and self-expression among participants. Artistic expression serves not only as a therapeutic tool but also as an avenue for personal growth and social interaction. While there is agreement that artistic activities can improve wellbeing, tensions arise regarding who qualifies as having 'genius' and whether such labels are necessary or detrimental to the creative process.

## Key Findings

- Engagement in visual arts improves mental health and emotional well-being
- Community-based art projects encourage social interaction and personal growth

## Open Questions

- Does labeling individuals as artistic geniuses enhance or detract from their creative expression?
