## Artistic Engagement

The role of artistic engagement as a cross-sector strategy for enhancing individual and communal well-being is explored in the provided sources. Source 1 highlights how engaging with arts across various sectors can foster health and community welfare, emphasizing its multifaceted benefits. In contrast, Source 2 delves into a qualitative study that investigates the mental health outcomes of young people who participate in artistic organizations in Colombia. This source underscores the critical importance of supportive community environments for fostering positive mental health through artistic engagement. Both sources converge on the notion that active participation in arts can yield significant psychological and social benefits.

## Key Findings

- Artistic engagement is a cross-sector strategy with broad applications in promoting health and well-being.
- Supportive community environments are crucial for enhancing mental health outcomes among youth through artistic involvement.
