## Community Value of Experience

The concept of the 'community value of experience' highlights how older adults can contribute to and benefit from community-based initiatives that recognize their life experiences. The Active Energy Project, as an example, underscores the importance of creative arts in enhancing wellbeing among seniors. This intervention integrates a quality-of-life model that places emphasis on holistic health outcomes beyond mere physical or mental wellness, including social and emotional aspects. Key themes across sources include the role of creativity, intergenerational learning, and the therapeutic benefits derived from sharing one's life experiences within a supportive community framework.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts interventions can enhance the wellbeing of older adults by recognizing their community value of experience.
- Intergenerational programs that leverage the life experiences of older individuals promote mutual enrichment between generations.
- A quality-of-life model encompasses various dimensions such as social, emotional, and physical health.

## Open Questions

- How can community initiatives be designed to effectively incorporate the life experiences of participants for maximum benefit?
