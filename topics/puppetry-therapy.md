## Puppetry Therapy

Puppetry therapy is a specialized intervention that utilizes the medium of puppets to engage children in creative and imaginative play. Both sources highlight its ability to enhance creativity, conflict resolution skills, and therapeutic benefits through non-verbal communication. The Torrance Test of Creative Thinking is mentioned as an assessment tool for evaluating the impact on creativity, while mindfulness and strengths-based approaches are discussed as integral components of puppetry therapy's effectiveness. However, there is a gap in understanding how puppetry therapy specifically addresses vicarious trauma, suggesting a need for further research.

## Key Findings

- Puppetry therapy enhances children's creativity through imaginative play.
- Non-verbal communication in puppetry can be a significant therapeutic tool.
- Mindfulness and strengths-based approaches are fundamental to the effectiveness of puppetry therapy.

## Open Questions
