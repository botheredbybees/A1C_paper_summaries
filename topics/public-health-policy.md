## Public Health Policy Frameworks

The provided sources explore various frameworks and strategies within the realm of public health policy. Source 1 examines the National Dementia Action Plan's indicators dashboard, focusing on evaluating a specific plan for dementia care policies. In contrast, Sources 2 and 3 delve into the National Arts and Health Framework, emphasizing the role of artistic practices in fostering holistic approaches to healthcare. This framework promotes collaborative relationships between health professionals and artists to integrate arts into public health initiatives. Source 4 shifts attention towards disaster management strategies, highlighting the crucial role of general practitioners in post-disaster mental health care through a case study involving post-traumatic stress disorder responses after fires.

## Key Findings

- Public health policies increasingly incorporate multidisciplinary approaches integrating arts and healthcare to promote holistic well-being.
- Effective public health policy evaluation requires comprehensive indicators, such as those outlined in the National Dementia Action Plan's dashboard.
- Health professionals play a pivotal role in disaster response strategies, particularly in addressing mental health concerns like post-traumatic stress disorder.

## Open Questions

- How can public health policies effectively integrate arts-based practices across various demographics and healthcare settings?
