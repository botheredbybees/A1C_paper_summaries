## Behavioral And Psychological Symptoms Of Dementia

The synthesis integrates the findings from studies focusing on Behavioral and Psychological Symptoms of Dementia (BPSD), highlighting interventions such as elder-clowning aimed at alleviating these symptoms. Key instruments like the Neuropsychiatric Inventory-Nursing Home version (NPI-NH) are used to measure BPSD, providing valuable insights into patient outcomes but also presenting challenges in standardizing and validating these measures across diverse settings.

## Key Findings

- Elder-clowning shows promise as an intervention for managing BPSD.
- The NPI-NH is a widely recognized tool for assessing the severity of BPSD.
- Standardized measurement tools are crucial for comparing effectiveness of various interventions.

## Open Questions

- What additional non-pharmacological methods can be integrated alongside clown therapy to enhance treatment efficacy in dementia care?
