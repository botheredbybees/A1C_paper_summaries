## Adolescent Brain Development

Sources highlight the critical period of adolescent brain development, emphasizing its malleability through various environmental influences and interventions. Neuroplasticity is a recurring theme, underscoring the brain's capacity to adapt and change during this developmental stage. Environmental factors such as music instruction play a significant role in shaping auditory neural functions and phonological awareness, contributing to overall cognitive development. The importance of creative arts and play in mental wellbeing further supports the notion that diverse experiences can positively impact adolescent neurodevelopment.

## Key Findings

- Adolescent brain development is highly influenced by environmental factors and experiences.
- Neuroplasticity allows for significant changes in brain structure and function during adolescence.
- Creative arts, play, and music instruction contribute to the enhancement of auditory neural functions.
