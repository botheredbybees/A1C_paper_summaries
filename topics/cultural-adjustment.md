## Cultural Adjustment

The topic of cultural adjustment focuses on the process individuals undergo when integrating into a new cultural environment. Across sources, this theme is addressed through various therapeutic and supportive interventions aimed at enhancing mental wellbeing in contexts where significant cultural change has occurred. Creative therapy emerges as a particularly effective method for aiding individuals undergoing such transitions by providing an outlet for self-expression that transcends traditional language barriers. However, there are tensions between the universality of creative arts as a therapeutic tool versus its ability to address specific cultural nuances and challenges unique to each individual's experience of adjustment.

## Key Findings

- Creative therapy can enhance mental wellbeing during cultural transitions.
- Cultural adjustment involves significant psychological challenges that require tailored interventions.
- Language barriers are effectively mitigated by the use of creative arts in therapeutic settings.

## Open Questions

- How can creative therapies be adapted to more effectively address specific cultural nuances and individual experiences?
