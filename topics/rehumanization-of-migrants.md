## Rehumanization Of Migrants

The concept of rehumanizing migrants is explored through a multi/transdisciplinary lens that integrates arts and culture. This approach aims to broaden the understanding of migrants beyond narrow, often dehumanizing definitions by engaging with cultural practices. Research indicates that collaboration across multiple disciplines can offer fresh perspectives on migration issues, enabling a more humane representation of migrant populations in societal narratives. However, this synthesis also reveals potential tensions between academic rigour and practical application, highlighting challenges such as the operationalization of arts-based interventions in rigorous research frameworks.

## Key Findings

- Rehumanizing migrants requires broadening cultural definitions beyond traditional disciplinary boundaries.
- Collaborative multi/transdisciplinary approaches can offer innovative solutions to rehumanize migrant populations.
- Arts-based interventions have potential for enhancing societal perceptions of migrants, but face challenges in implementation and measurement.

## Open Questions

- How can arts-based interventions be effectively integrated into mainstream research methodologies without losing their transformative qualities?
