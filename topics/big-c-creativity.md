## Big-C Creativity

The concept of Big-C Creativity is pivotal in understanding exceptional creative achievements that significantly impact society and culture. This model distinguishes itself from smaller-scale creative behaviors through its emphasis on originality, innovation, and cultural significance. Sources highlight the Four C Model of Creativity, which includes big-c creativity alongside little-c (everyday), mini-c (personal growth), and Pro-C (professional) creativity. The distinction between Big-C and Little-c is evident in their respective impacts: Big-C leads to enduring contributions while Little-c involves personal and everyday creative acts. Both sources discuss divergent thinking as a key component of broader creativity paradigms, underscoring its role in generating novel ideas essential for all levels of creative behavior.

## Key Findings

- Big-C Creativity involves significant societal or cultural impact through original and innovative contributions.
- The Four C Model provides a comprehensive framework distinguishing various types of creativity based on context and scale.
- Divergent thinking is crucial across all levels of creativity, from personal to professional.
