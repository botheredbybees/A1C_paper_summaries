## Employment Rates Among Program Participants

The synthesis of the provided sources focuses on the impact of the Big Noise Raploch program on educational and employment outcomes. Sources indicate a positive correlation between participation in this program and improved post-school education results, including higher employment rates. The statistical analysis emphasizes the significance of adjusted analyses to account for confounding variables, highlighting that the programme's influence on employment is substantial even after controlling for these factors. This suggests that Big Noise Raploch not only enhances educational achievements but also positively impacts job opportunities for its participants.

## Key Findings

- Participation in the Big Noise Raploch program correlates with improved post-school education outcomes.
- The positive impact of the program on employment rates is consistent even after adjusting for confounding variables.
- Statistical analysis reveals a significant association between educational achievement and increased likelihood of securing employment.
