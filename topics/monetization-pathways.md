## Monetization Pathways in Health Interventions

The sources collectively explore monetization pathways within health interventions, focusing on the economic benefits derived from activities such as music engagement for patients with Alzheimer's disease. The analysis reveals significant impacts on quality of life and cost-effectiveness measures through enhanced activities of daily living (ADLs). However, there is a noted gap in how these interventions are scaled and monetized across broader healthcare systems.

## Key Findings

- Enhanced ADLs improve the economic benefits of health interventions.
- Monetization pathways can be pivotal in scaling interventions for wider patient benefit.
- Quality of life improvements through music engagement correlate with reduced healthcare costs.
