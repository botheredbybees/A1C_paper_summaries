## Street Dance Survey

The Street Dance Survey highlighted in the Creative Australia initiative underscores a significant methodological approach to understanding and promoting street dance culture within Australia. This survey aims to gather comprehensive data on street dancers, including their demographics, motivations for participation, and perceptions of the wider community's acceptance towards street dance. The inclusion of the Street Dance Survey under the Creative Futures Fund signifies an official recognition of street dance as a valuable cultural practice warranting research attention. However, there is a noticeable absence in detailing specific methodologies employed or outcomes achieved from such surveys across different sources provided, indicating a need for more detailed reporting on data collection and analysis techniques.
