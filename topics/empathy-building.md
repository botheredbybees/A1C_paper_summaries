## Empathy Building

The concept of empathy building is a central theme in both community music and dance therapy interventions. Community music emphasizes the role of life-long learning in fostering social well-being, with empathy-building activities playing a significant part in these efforts. Similarly, dance therapy focuses on integrating personality and preventing violence through movement, where empathy-building exercises are crucial for personal development and social cohesion. Both sources highlight the transformative power of arts-based interventions in enhancing emotional understanding and connection among participants.

## Key Findings

- Empathy building is a core component of both community music and dance therapy practices.
- Activities that foster empathy contribute to the overall goal of improving social well-being.
- Interventions involving movement and music can effectively integrate personality and prevent violence.

## Open Questions

- How do different forms of artistic expression uniquely impact the development of empathy in individuals?
