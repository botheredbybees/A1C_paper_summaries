## Positive Psychology and Resilience

Positive psychology focuses on fostering resilience and well-being through the development of personal strengths. Source 1 highlights the role of music in providing pleasure, engagement, and meaning to university students, thereby contributing positively to their mental health and well-being. Meanwhile, Source 2 introduces humor as a critical component for coping and growth within the framework of positive psychology, emphasizing its importance in enhancing resilience through character strengths and emotion regulation. Both sources underscore the significance of engaging activities and emotional processes in promoting psychological resilience and overall happiness.

## Key Findings

- Humor is vital in fostering resiliency and emotional well-being through the use of character strengths and improved coping strategies
- Engagement with pleasurable activities like music contributes significantly to personal meaning, pleasure, and mental health among young adults
- Positive psychology integrates diverse elements such as humor and meaningful engagement to promote resilience and overall happiness
