## Academic Skills Workshop

The sources collectively highlight the importance of academic skills workshops in enhancing students' understanding and application of critical reflection models. Through activities such as creating a photo essay, these sessions aim to facilitate deeper engagement with course material and promote reflective practice. However, there is a need for further exploration into how different pedagogical methods can be effectively integrated within these workshops to maximize their educational benefits.
