## Artz Program

The ARTZ program is highlighted as a pioneering approach in the realm of Alzheimer's care and support. It emphasizes non-pharmacological interventions that focus on artistic expression and engagement to enhance the quality of life for individuals with Alzheimer's disease. Sources indicate that by participating in art-making activities, patients can experience increased communication, social interaction, and self-expression, which are often diminished due to cognitive decline. The program’s success underscores the importance of holistic care strategies that extend beyond traditional medical treatments.

## Key Findings

- The ARTZ program utilizes non-pharmacological methods for Alzheimer's support.
- Artistic activities can improve communication and social interaction in individuals with dementia.
- Holistic care approaches are essential for enhancing quality of life in patients with Alzheimer’s.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of artistic interventions on cognitive decline?
