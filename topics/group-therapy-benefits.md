## Group Therapy Benefits

The provided sources highlight the multifaceted benefits of group therapy, particularly when incorporated with creative arts for addressing conditions such as eating disorders and trauma. Symbolic language within therapeutic settings is recognized as a potent communication tool, facilitating deeper emotional expression among participants. Creative arts therapies are noted to bolster resilience in individuals by offering alternative modes of self-expression that traditional talk therapies might not reach. Consistently across sources, group therapy emerges as an intervention that amplifies these benefits through social interaction and shared experiences, providing a sense of community and mutual understanding.

## Key Findings

- Symbolic language enhances emotional expression in therapeutic settings.
- Creative arts therapies are effective resilience-building tools for individuals with eating disorders or trauma.
- Group therapy provides additional support through collective experience and mutual understanding.
