## Occupational Therapy Intervention

The provided sources explore the theme of occupational therapy intervention, particularly focusing on how digital and technological advancements impact traditional therapeutic practices. Source one discusses the transition from in-person arts-based occupational therapy to a digital format, highlighting challenges such as technological barriers that may hinder effective implementation and client engagement. Despite these obstacles, there is an acknowledgment of the potential for digital platforms to expand access to therapy services for those who might otherwise face significant barriers due to geographical or mobility constraints.

## Key Findings

- Digital platforms can potentially broaden accessibility to occupational therapy services.
- Technological barriers are a challenge in implementing digital forms of occupational therapy intervention.
- Arts-based interventions remain relevant and beneficial even when delivered through digital means.

## Open Questions

- How do we effectively address the technological challenges faced by both therapists and clients in adapting to digital occupational therapy?
