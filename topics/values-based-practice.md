## Values-Based Practice in Dementia Care

The concept of values-based practice is highlighted across the sources as a holistic approach to dementia care that emphasizes individualization and considers the social environmental factors affecting patients. It advocates for personalized care strategies, focusing on understanding the unique needs, preferences, and circumstances of each person living with dementia. However, while there's consensus on its importance, practical implementation remains challenging due to varying levels of healthcare providers' commitment to this approach.

## Key Findings

- Values-based practice emphasizes individualization in dementia care
- Social environmental factors significantly impact the efficacy of values-based interventions
- Implementation challenges exist due to provider attitudes and resources

## Open Questions

- How can healthcare systems better support the adoption of values-based practices by practitioners?
