## Therapeutic Value of Museums

Museums have emerged as potential sites for promoting health and wellbeing through various interventions. The Beaney Health and Wellbeing Toolkit, a significant resource in this field, highlights the therapeutic benefits that museums can offer to individuals by providing an engaging environment rich with cultural and educational experiences. Museums are recognized not only for their traditional roles but also for their capacity to support mental and physical health initiatives. However, while there is recognition of these potential benefits, specific interventions and frameworks for integrating such programs need further development.

## Key Findings

- Museums can serve as therapeutic environments promoting health and wellbeing
- The Beaney Health and Wellbeing Toolkit outlines strategies to enhance museum experiences for health benefits

## Open Questions

- What specific interventions are most effective in leveraging the therapeutic potential of museums?
