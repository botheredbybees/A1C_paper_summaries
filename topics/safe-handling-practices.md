## Safe Handling Practices

The sources collectively emphasize the importance of safe handling practices for artwork and objects. They highlight the need for proper documentation and use of appropriate materials such as consistent light sources to ensure long-term preservation. The integration of these elements underscores a holistic approach to safeguarding cultural heritage, involving both preventive measures and practical guidelines.

## Key Findings

- Safe handling practices are crucial in preserving artwork and objects.
- Documentation plays a key role alongside physical handling techniques.
- Use of consistent light sources is an important aspect of safe handling.
