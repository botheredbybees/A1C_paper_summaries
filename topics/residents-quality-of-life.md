## Residents' Quality Of Life

The Music & Memory Program demonstrates a positive impact on the quality of life for older adults in care facilities. Across multiple sources, this program is highlighted as an effective intervention that enhances residents' emotional and cognitive well-being through personalized music experiences. However, variations exist in the extent to which different studies emphasize the social engagement benefits versus the individual therapeutic effects of tailored musical interventions.

## Key Findings

- The Music & Memory Program significantly improves residents' quality of life.
- Personalized music therapy can enhance both emotional and cognitive functions.
- Social interaction among residents is positively influenced by shared music experiences.

## Open Questions

- How do the long-term impacts of the Music & Memory Program compare with other interventions in enhancing resident well-being?
