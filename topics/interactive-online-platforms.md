## Interactive Online Platforms

The integration of various sources highlights the potential of interactive online platforms in enhancing therapy practices such as virtual puppet play therapy. This intervention allows for symbolic projection and therapeutic assessment, thereby broadening access to mental health services through digital means. However, there is a noted tension between the effectiveness of traditional face-to-face therapy versus online interventions, with concerns over technological barriers and privacy issues.

## Key Findings

- Interactive online platforms offer new avenues for conducting effective therapeutic assessments.
- Symbolic projection remains an integral part of therapeutic practices in virtual environments.
- There are accessibility benefits to utilizing interactive online platforms in mental health care.

## Open Questions

- How do traditional therapy methods compare with those delivered via interactive online platforms?
