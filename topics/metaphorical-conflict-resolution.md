## Metaphorical Conflict Resolution

Metaphorical conflict resolution is an intervention that utilizes the power of metaphors to aid individuals in resolving conflicts. This technique involves presenting a metaphorical scenario or story that mirrors the individual's real-life conflict, thus allowing them to explore and understand their situation from a different perspective. The use of puppet-assisted play therapy (as detailed in one source) exemplifies this approach by providing a safe medium for children to express emotions and conflicts through storytelling with puppets. This method encourages divergent thinking, enabling individuals to find creative solutions that they might not have considered otherwise.

## Key Findings

- Metaphorical conflict resolution aids individuals in understanding complex emotional issues through storytelling
- Puppet-assisted play therapy is an effective medium for children to express and resolve conflicts
- The use of metaphors promotes divergent thinking, leading to innovative problem-solving
