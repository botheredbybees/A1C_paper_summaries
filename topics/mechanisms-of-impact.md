## Mechanisms of Impact

The concept of 'mechanisms of impact' is central to understanding how interventions or treatments affect their intended outcomes. This theory involves dissecting the underlying processes and pathways that connect a given intervention with its observed effects, distinguishing it from broader evaluation practices which often focus on assessing effectiveness without delving into causative mechanisms. Rigorous scientific investigation highlights the necessity of identifying these specific mechanisms for enhancing our understanding and improving interventions.

## Key Findings

- Mechanisms of impact are essential for comprehending how interventions produce outcomes.
- Distinguishing evaluation from research is critical when aiming to identify causal pathways in intervention effects.

## Open Questions

- What methods effectively uncover the specific mechanisms linking interventions with their impacts?
