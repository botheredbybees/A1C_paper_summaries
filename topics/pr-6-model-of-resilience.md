## Pr-6 Model Of Resilience

The PR-6 model of resilience is a theoretical framework used to understand and promote resilience in individuals affected by natural disasters. This theory emphasizes the use of arts-based healing approaches, specifically through art therapy, as a means to foster personal growth and adaptability. The SPARK Curriculum, an application of this model, aims to help people navigate the challenges posed by environmental catastrophes by engaging them in creative expression activities that enhance psychological resilience.

## Key Findings

- PR-6 model highlights arts-based healing through art therapy
- SPARK Curriculum is a practical application of PR-6 theory
