## The Healing Power Of Self-Expression

Personal storytelling and memoir writing are discussed in the context of their therapeutic benefits. Sensory writing is highlighted as a technique that can evoke emotions, enhance memory recall, and foster personal growth through vivid descriptions. Story structure is another critical aspect, guiding participants to organize their thoughts and experiences into coherent narratives, which helps them gain insights and find meaning in their lives. The overall narrative suggests that the act of self-expression through storytelling has significant healing potential, offering emotional release and psychological well-being.

## Key Findings

- Sensory writing techniques aid in evoking deep emotions.
- Story structure contributes to personal insight and narrative coherence.
- Self-expression through memoir writing serves as a therapeutic intervention.
