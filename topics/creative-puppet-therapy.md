## Creative Puppet Therapy

The synthesis integrates findings from a single source detailing preliminary results of Creative Puppet Therapy on patients diagnosed with schizophrenia. This therapy shows promise in reducing hallucinations, a critical symptom often challenging to manage through conventional treatments. The innovative use of puppetry as an intervention points towards the potential of integrating creative arts into therapeutic practices for mental health disorders.

## Key Findings

- Creative Puppet Therapy reduces hallucinations in patients with schizophrenia.
- Puppet therapy represents a novel approach in treating symptoms unresponsive to traditional methods.

## Open Questions

- Further research is needed to explore long-term efficacy and broader applicability of Creative Puppet Therapy.
