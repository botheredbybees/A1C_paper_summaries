## Photography for Wellbeing

The integration of photography into one's daily routine can serve as a potent intervention for enhancing wellbeing. This intervention leverages the medium of photography to cultivate mindfulness and foster community interaction. Across different contexts, it has been observed that engaging with photography regularly can act as a therapeutic tool, promoting mental health by encouraging individuals to slow down and be present in their environments. However, there is also an acknowledgement of variability in how effective these practices are across diverse populations, suggesting that further exploration into the nuances of community interaction through photography would be beneficial.

## Key Findings

- Photography can serve as a daily mindfulness practice.
- Community interaction facilitated by shared photographic activities enhances social bonds.
- There is a need for tailored approaches to accommodate different cultural and individual preferences in photography-based wellbeing interventions.

## Open Questions

- How do varying cultural contexts influence the effectiveness of photography as a tool for community interaction and mental health improvement?
