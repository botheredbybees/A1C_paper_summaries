## Poetic Healing

The concept of Poetic Healing is explored as an intervention that leverages the inherent power and universality of poetry to address cultural wisdom and healing needs. Sources highlight poetry's unique ability to transcend linguistic and cultural barriers, promoting a shared human experience and understanding of complex emotions and wisdom. This therapeutic approach emphasizes the role of literature in fostering emotional resilience and intercultural dialogue, bridging gaps between diverse communities through its universal appeal.

## Key Findings

- Poetic Healing is recognized as an intervention that utilizes poetry to promote cultural understanding and healing across different societies.
- Wisdom Poetry acts as a bridge for communication, facilitating the expression of complex emotions in a universally accessible manner.
- The transcendental nature of poetic language supports emotional resilience and intercultural dialogue.
