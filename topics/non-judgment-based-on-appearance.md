## Non-Judgment Based On Appearance

The sources collectively emphasize the importance of avoiding judgments based on physical appearance and promoting self-confidence. They highlight how societal norms often lead to superficial evaluations that can harm individual psychological well-being. Miss Lily's approach in 'Miss Lily's Fabulous Feather Boa' underscores the need for interventions that build personal confidence independent of external perceptions, thereby fostering a non-judgmental attitude towards others based on appearance.

## Key Findings

- Societal norms often lead to superficial evaluations.
- Interventions like Miss Lily's feather boa encourage self-confidence independent of external judgments.
- Non-judgment based on appearance promotes psychological well-being.

## Open Questions

- How effective are confidence-building interventions in changing societal attitudes towards judging others by their appearance?
