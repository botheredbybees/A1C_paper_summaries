## Intergenerational Dialogue

The concept of intergenerational dialogue is explored through the lens of puppet theatre as an innovative intervention method. Puppet therapy serves not only as a means to communicate experiences that are challenging to verbalize but also as a therapeutic tool for fostering radical listening across different age groups. This approach facilitates deep emotional engagement and provides a safe space for participants to explore pain, trauma, and shared histories in a less intimidating manner.

## Key Findings

- Puppet theatre is an effective medium for intergenerational dialogue
- Radical listening enhances the therapeutic value of puppet therapy

## Open Questions

- How can puppet therapy be adapted to address specific cultural contexts?
