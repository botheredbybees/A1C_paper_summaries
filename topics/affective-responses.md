## Affective Responses in Music Psychology

The synthesis integrates findings from the study of current emotion research within music psychology, highlighting the role of emotional expressiveness and affective responses. Sources emphasize that culturally specific musical expectations significantly shape listeners' affective experiences. Emotional expressiveness is discussed as a key aspect influencing how individuals perceive and respond emotionally to music across different cultural contexts. Theories suggest that while there are universal elements in perceiving emotion through music, individual and cultural variations play crucial roles in shaping the spectrum of emotional responses.

## Key Findings

- Emotional expressiveness is fundamental in interpreting affective responses to music.
- Culturally specific musical expectations significantly influence how individuals emotionally engage with music.
- There are both universal and culturally contingent aspects to perceiving emotion through music.

## Open Questions

- How do individual differences interact with cultural influences on emotional expressiveness in music?
