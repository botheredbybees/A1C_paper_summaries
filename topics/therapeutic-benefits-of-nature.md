## Therapeutic Benefits of Nature

The integration of nature-based interventions in therapy highlights their potential for enhancing psychological resilience and overall well-being. Sources suggest that engaging with natural environments during challenging times, such as lockdowns due to a pandemic, can serve as a therapeutic tool to rediscover joy and manage stress effectively. The role of creative hobbies inspired by or carried out in nature is also emphasized, contributing further to mental health benefits.

## Key Findings

- Nature-based interventions are effective for improving psychological resilience.
- Creative activities inspired by nature support overall well-being.
- Engaging with natural environments can help individuals cope during crises like pandemics.

## Open Questions

- How can the therapeutic use of nature be standardized and integrated into mainstream mental health practices?
