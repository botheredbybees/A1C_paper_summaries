## Policy Evaluation Indicators

The synthesis of the provided sources reveals a focus on developing and utilizing specific indicators for evaluating policies aimed at addressing dementia. The sources emphasize the importance of clear metrics to assess the effectiveness of national action plans, such as the National Dementia Action Plan in the context of managing dementia care and support. These indicators are crucial for policymakers and stakeholders to track progress, identify gaps, and refine strategies over time. However, there is a noted challenge in creating comprehensive and universally applicable indicators that can capture the complexity of policy implementation across diverse populations.

## Key Findings

- Policy evaluation indicators are essential tools for assessing the impact of national dementia action plans.
- Clear metrics help stakeholders track progress and identify areas needing improvement in dementia care policies.

## Open Questions
