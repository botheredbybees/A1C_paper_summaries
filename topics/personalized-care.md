## Personalized Care in Health and Wellbeing

The concept of personalized care emerges as a critical approach across both sources, emphasizing tailored interventions to meet individual needs. Source one discusses the therapeutic benefits of art in enhancing health and wellbeing, suggesting that creative activities like painting or sculpture can serve as effective personalized care methods for patients using dopaminergic medications. This source highlights how engaging with art can offer unique advantages beyond conventional medical treatments by providing emotional release and cognitive stimulation. In contrast, source two delves into the challenges faced at end-of-life stages and during bereavement periods. It underscores the importance of an integrated care model that includes creative arts therapy as part of personalized approaches to ensure holistic support for patients and their families. This integration aims to address not only physical health but also psychological and emotional aspects through customized therapeutic strategies.

## Key Findings

- Art-based activities provide significant dopaminergic benefits and enhance overall wellbeing
- Personalized care models incorporating creative arts therapy are effective in end-of-life scenarios

## Open Questions

- How can the integration of art into personalized care plans be optimized for diverse patient populations?
