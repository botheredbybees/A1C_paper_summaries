## Commercial Use of Photography

The sources highlight the complexities surrounding the use of photography for commercial purposes. Privacy laws play a significant role in regulating how photographs can be taken and used commercially, particularly concerning street photographers who must navigate legal frameworks that vary widely between jurisdictions. Commercial use raises ethical questions about consent and exploitation, especially when images are captured without explicit permission from subjects or in public spaces where individuals expect some level of privacy.

## Key Findings

- Privacy laws significantly impact the legality and ethics of commercial photography.
- Street photographers face unique legal challenges in balancing artistic freedom with respect for individual privacy rights.

## Open Questions

- How can street photographers ethically capture images intended for commercial use without infringing on individuals' privacy?
