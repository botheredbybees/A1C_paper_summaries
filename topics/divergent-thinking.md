## Divergent Thinking

The sources provided explore the concept of divergent thinking in different contexts, highlighting its importance in creative problem-solving and critical evaluation. Source 1 emphasizes journaling as a tool for enhancing both critical and divergent thinking skills. This practice allows individuals to reflect deeply on their thoughts, fostering innovative solutions through non-linear thought processes. In contrast, Source 2 examines the use of puppet-assisted play therapy to resolve metaphorical conflicts, an approach that heavily relies on divergent thinking to create new narratives and scenarios for emotional expression and resolution. Both sources underscore the versatility and adaptability of divergent thinking across various therapeutic and educational interventions.

## Key Findings

- Divergent thinking is essential in both journaling and puppet-assisted play therapy as it facilitates creativity and innovation.
- Journaling enhances critical-thinking alongside divergent thinking, supporting deeper reflection and analysis.
- Puppet-assisted play therapy uses metaphors to promote emotional expression through the lens of divergent thinking.
