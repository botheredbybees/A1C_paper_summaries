## Puppet Play Therapy

Puppet play therapy emerges as a versatile and engaging intervention that leverages the symbolism of puppets to facilitate communication and emotional expression. Both sources highlight its use in traditional settings (Chapter on Adlerian Theory) and adaptability to virtual platforms, which extends reach and inclusivity (VIRTUAL PUPPET PLAY THERAPY). The method allows for symbolic projection, enabling individuals to explore complex emotions and relationships through the medium of puppets. Interactive online platforms enhance engagement by providing a safe space for therapeutic assessment without physical presence constraints.

## Key Findings

- Puppet play therapy facilitates emotional expression and communication through symbolic projection
- Therapeutic assessment can be conducted via interactive online platforms, enhancing accessibility
- Adlerian theory underpins the practical application of puppet play in therapy
