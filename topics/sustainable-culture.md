## Trauma-Informed Care and Practice Organizational Toolkit

The Trauma-Informed Care and Practice Organisational Toolkit (TICPOT) provides a framework for organizational change, emphasizing the importance of creating a sustainable culture that supports trauma-informed principles. This toolkit outlines specific steps and strategies aimed at fostering an environment where staff are well-equipped to understand and respond to individuals affected by trauma. It highlights the necessity of a quality improvement plan to sustain these practices over time, ensuring that the organizational culture remains supportive and responsive to the needs of all stakeholders involved.

## Key Findings

- TICPOT outlines steps for fostering an environment that supports individuals affected by trauma.
- The toolkit emphasizes the need for a sustainable culture within organizations to maintain trauma-informed practices over time.
- A quality improvement plan is crucial for sustaining organizational change in line with trauma-informed principles.
