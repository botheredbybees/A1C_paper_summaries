## Prevalence Rates of Dementia

The sources highlight the significant and growing issue of dementia across various populations. Prevalence rates are noted as increasing due to an aging global population, with specific attention given to the often overlooked category of young onset dementia. The data underscores that while overall prevalence is rising, there remains a need for detailed demographic breakdowns to better understand risk factors and develop targeted interventions.

## Key Findings

- Dementia affects a growing number of individuals due to global population aging.
- Prevalence rates vary significantly across different demographics.
- Young onset dementia is an under-researched and often overlooked subset of the broader condition.
