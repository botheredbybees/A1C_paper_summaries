## Empathy in Therapy

The role of empathy in therapeutic interventions is crucial for building rapport and understanding the client's experiences. The source highlights the AutPlay® framework as a tool to promote growth, empowerment, and affirming practices specifically tailored for neurodivergent children and adolescents. This underscores the importance of empathetic approaches that recognize and respect individual differences within this population. However, while empathy is often acknowledged in therapeutic contexts, there remains a need for more detailed research on how to effectively integrate empathy into various therapy frameworks.

## Key Findings

- Empathy-in-therapy promotes rapport and understanding
- AutPlay® framework supports growth and empowerment in neurodivergent children
