## Course Resources

The lecture transcript emphasizes the importance of various course resources in enhancing student learning and engagement. This approach encourages deeper understanding through the juxtaposition of personal reflection and photographic evidence, which can be particularly effective in online or hybrid courses where traditional methods might not suffice.
