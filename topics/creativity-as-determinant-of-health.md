## Creativity As A Determinant Of Health

The concept of 'creativity as a determinant of health' is explored across several sources, emphasizing the interplay between creative activities and overall well-being. One key source suggests that creativity can serve not only as an intrinsic value but also as a significant contributor to public health through integrated care systems and arts-based initiatives in healthcare settings. This perspective shifts the traditional view of health determinants towards a more holistic model inclusive of cultural and social factors, such as engagement with creative arts, which can lead to improved mental and physical health outcomes.

## Key Findings

- Creativity is increasingly recognized as an important determinant of public health
- Integrated care systems incorporating the arts have potential benefits for patient outcomes
- Creative activities contribute positively to both mental and physical well-being

## Open Questions

- How can the relationship between creativity and health be operationalized in policy frameworks?
