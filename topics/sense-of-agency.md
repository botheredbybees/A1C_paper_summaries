## Sense of Agency

The concept of 'sense of agency' is a central theme in psychological well-being, influencing how individuals perceive their ability to influence outcomes and navigate emotional states. Across different contexts such as adolescent music listening (Source 1), immigrant and refugee children's puppet integration (Source 2), and refugees’ wellbeing through protracted displacement (Source 3), the sense of agency is shown to be a pivotal factor in enhancing self-efficacy, emotional well-being, coping mechanisms, and overall resilience. Music plays a crucial role as both an expressive tool and therapeutic intervention for fostering a positive sense of agency among adolescents and displaced populations. While music provides a medium through which individuals can explore their identities and strengthen emotional connections (Sources 1 & 3), the use of creative expression tools like puppets in storytelling programs aids children in transitioning from helplessness to renewed empowerment by providing safe spaces for exploration and coping strategies (Source 2). This interplay underscores the importance of culturally relevant interventions that cater to diverse populations, recognizing the significant role emotional well-being plays in fostering a robust sense of agency. However, there remains a gap in understanding how these varied methodologies can be effectively integrated into broader support frameworks.

## Key Findings

- The sense of agency is crucial for emotional well-being and resilience in various contexts, including adolescent music listening and refugee support programs.
- Music serves as an effective therapeutic tool that enhances self-efficacy and emotional expression among different populations.
- Creative expression methods like puppet integration can empower children to cope with trauma by providing a platform for storytelling and emotional processing.

## Open Questions

- How can diverse interventions, such as music therapy and creative arts programs, be effectively integrated into comprehensive support frameworks for promoting sense of agency in marginalized populations?
