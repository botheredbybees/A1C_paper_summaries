## Student Engagement with Music

The sources collectively highlight the multifaceted nature of music as an engaging activity for university students, emphasizing its role in providing both pleasure and deeper personal significance. Music's capacity to enhance engagement is underscored by how it offers a pleasurable experience that can be deeply meaningful to individuals. This suggests that beyond mere enjoyment, music serves as a vehicle through which students find meaning and purpose, contributing significantly to their overall university experience.

## Key Findings

- Music plays a significant role in the lives of university students, providing both pleasure and deeper personal significance.
- Engagement with music transcends simple enjoyment and includes meaningful experiences that enhance personal growth.
- The interplay between pleasure, engagement, and meaning underscores the profound impact of music on student life.
