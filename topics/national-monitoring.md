## National Dementia Data Improvement Plan

The National Dementia Data Improvement Plan (2023-2034) aims to enhance the collection and use of data on dementia prevalence and incidence. The plan underscores the importance of accurate national monitoring for guiding policy decisions, research efforts, and service provision. Across all sources, there is a consensus that improving data quality is crucial for effective public health responses. However, tensions arise regarding the methods and resources required to achieve comprehensive monitoring.

## Key Findings

- Improving data on dementia prevalence and incidence is critical
- National monitoring guides policy decisions and service provision

## Open Questions

- What are the most effective methods for national monitoring of dementia?
