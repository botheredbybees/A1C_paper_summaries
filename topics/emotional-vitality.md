## Emotional Vitality

The concept of emotional vitality is explored through the lens of dance therapy and expressive arts as a therapeutic intervention. Sources highlight that these practices enhance emotional well-being by providing an avenue for self-expression, particularly beneficial in populations with cognitive decline or memory loss. Memory stimulation through movement-based activities not only aids in preserving past memories but also fosters new connections essential to maintaining cognitive health. However, the integration of such therapies into broader mental health frameworks presents challenges due to varying perceptions of their efficacy compared to conventional therapeutic approaches.

## Key Findings

- Dance therapy and expressive arts enhance emotional well-being through self-expression.
- Movement-based activities aid in memory preservation and new cognitive connections.
- Integrating dance therapy into mainstream mental health practices faces hurdles.

## Open Questions

- How can the perceived efficacy of dance therapy be improved to match that of conventional therapies?
