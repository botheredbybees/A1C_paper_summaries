## Artful Thinking

The concept of 'artful thinking' emerges as a critical intervention aimed at enhancing observational skills and fostering deeper engagement with the visual arts. Both sources emphasize the importance of Visual Thinking Strategies (VTS) in facilitating this process, suggesting that VTS not only aids in developing perceptual acuity but also encourages reflective practices and empathetic understanding. The Health Humanities domain is highlighted as a key area where artful thinking can be effectively applied, particularly within medical education contexts to improve clinical skills through artistic engagement.

## Key Findings

- Artful Thinking significantly enhances observational and analytical skills in participants
- Visual Thinking Strategies (VTS) are central to the practice of Artful Thinking
- The application of artful thinking principles can be beneficial across various educational domains, including medical education

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of incorporating Artful Thinking and VTS in medical education?
