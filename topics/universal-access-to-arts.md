## Universal Access To Arts

The integration of creative activities and the arts into educational systems has been advocated as a means to improve the mental health and overall wellbeing of students. Sources emphasize the role of these interventions in providing accessible therapeutic benefits, reducing stress, enhancing cognitive abilities, and fostering social connections among young people. However, while there is agreement on the potential for such programs to positively impact mental health disorders and educational outcomes, gaps remain regarding how best to implement these initiatives universally across diverse populations and settings.

## Key Findings

- Creative activities can significantly improve mental health and wellbeing in students
- Incorporating arts into education systems has therapeutic benefits and enhances cognitive abilities
- Universal access to the arts is seen as a valuable intervention for reducing stress among youth

## Open Questions

- What are the most effective methods for integrating universal access to arts programs across diverse educational settings?
