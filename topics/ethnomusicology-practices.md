## Ethnomusicology Practices

The synthesis of the provided sources highlights the interdisciplinary nature of ethnomusicology as a research method that integrates cultural studies with musical practice. Ethnomusicologists not only study music in its sociocultural context but also engage in practices to sustain and promote creative traditions within communities. This approach involves understanding how music functions within societal structures, preserving endangered music cultures, and fostering innovative expressions through education and community engagement. The sources emphasize the importance of ethnomusicology's role in documenting, revitalizing, and innovating musical practices that contribute to cultural identity and sustainability.

## Key Findings

- Ethnomusicologists study music within its sociocultural context to understand its significance and function.
- The practice includes efforts to preserve endangered music cultures and promote sustainable music traditions.
- Engagement in community-based activities is a crucial aspect of ethnomusicology, fostering creativity and cultural identity.
