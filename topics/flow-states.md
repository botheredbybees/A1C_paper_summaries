## Flow States

The concept of 'flow states' is explored across the sources provided, with a focus on its impact on emotional well-being and personal growth. Visual storytelling in Source 1 highlights how individuals navigate challenging periods like lockdowns by engaging deeply in activities that induce flow states, enhancing their emotional health through immersive engagement. The interplay between activity immersion, skill enhancement, and emotional satisfaction is evident, reflecting the broader therapeutic potential of flow states as an intervention for mental well-being.

## Key Findings

- Flow states contribute to enhanced emotional well-being during challenging times.
- Activities that induce flow states can serve as a therapeutic tool for managing stress.
- Visual storytelling effectively captures personal journeys through immersive activities.

## Open Questions
