## Individualized Care

The concept of individualized care is pivotal in enhancing the quality of life for individuals with dementia. Sources emphasize the importance of understanding each person's unique history and preferences to tailor interventions effectively, such as through reminiscence therapy which leverages personal memories to reinforce identity and foster emotional connections. These personalized approaches aim to ensure that healthcare aligns closely with individual needs, thereby promoting a sense of dignity and self-worth.

## Key Findings

- Individualized care is crucial for dementia patients
- Reminiscence therapy supports identity reinforcement in dementia care
- Personal history integration enhances the effectiveness of person-centered interventions
