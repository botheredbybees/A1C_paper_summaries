## Arts-Based Strategies for Global Youth Mental Health

The integration of arts-based strategies to support youth mental health from a global perspective highlights the importance of culturally sensitive interventions that are grounded in local practices. Source 1 underscores the utility of these strategies in addressing the specific needs and challenges faced by young people around the world, emphasizing the role of cultural context in shaping effective approaches. In contrast, Source 2 focuses on broader frameworks such as sustainable development goals (SDGs) and the universal right to health, which sets a wider stage for understanding how arts-based mental health interventions can be integrated into larger global initiatives aimed at improving overall well-being and reducing disease burdens.

## Key Findings

- Arts-based strategies are effective in supporting youth mental health globally when adapted to local cultural contexts.
- The integration of these strategies within broader frameworks like the SDGs could enhance their reach and impact.

## Open Questions

- How can arts-based interventions be scaled up while maintaining cultural sensitivity?
