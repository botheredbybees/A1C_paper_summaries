## Healing Through Art

The use of art as a therapeutic intervention for mental health is highlighted across sources. Creative expression through art serves not only as an outlet for emotional distress but also enhances non-verbal communication, offering individuals ways to articulate experiences that may be difficult or impossible to express verbally. Sources emphasize the transformative power of engaging with artistic activities in various forms such as photography and painting, indicating a broad applicability of healing-through-art across different contexts.

## Key Findings

- Art therapy serves as an effective intervention for mental health issues by providing non-verbal communication channels.
- Creative expression through art can act as a powerful medium for emotional release and self-discovery.
