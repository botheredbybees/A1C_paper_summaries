## Health Policy Frameworks

The exploration of arts in the community for health and wellbeing highlights the interplay between cultural practices and public health policy. The integration of creative therapies within healthcare frameworks underscores a holistic approach to wellness, emphasizing preventive measures over reactive treatments. This theoretical framework advocates for policies that support artistic endeavors as vital components of community health initiatives. However, there is a tension between the recognized benefits of arts-based interventions and the practical challenges in implementing such strategies on a broad scale due to funding constraints and policy barriers.

## Key Findings

- Arts-based interventions are increasingly recognized within public health discourse
- Creative therapies offer preventive healthcare benefits
- Policy frameworks need to incorporate support for community arts

## Open Questions

- How can existing policy frameworks be adapted to better integrate arts-based initiatives?
