## Recovery-Oriented Language

The Recovery Oriented Language Guide emphasizes the importance of using supportive and empowering language in mental health settings to promote recovery. The guide highlights that the choice of words can significantly impact a person's perception of their ability to recover from mental illness, reducing stigma and fostering hope. By adopting a recovery-focused vocabulary, professionals and communities contribute to an environment where individuals feel encouraged and supported on their path toward recovery.

## Key Findings

- Language plays a crucial role in shaping perceptions about mental health recovery.
- Using recovery-oriented language can reduce the stigma associated with mental illness.
- Adopting supportive vocabulary is instrumental in promoting positive outcomes for those seeking to recover from mental health issues.
