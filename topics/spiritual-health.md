## Spiritual Health

The concept of spiritual health is explored within the socio-cultural context of dementia care for Indigenous communities. A key theme across sources is the emphasis on a holistic care approach that integrates physical, emotional, social, and spiritual well-being. Sources highlight how attending to spiritual needs can significantly impact overall quality of life and sense of identity in individuals with dementia. Despite this recognition, practical applications vary widely among different cultural groups, suggesting a need for culturally tailored interventions.

## Key Findings

- Spiritual health is an integral part of holistic care.
- Cultural context shapes the understanding and application of spiritual health concepts.
- Attention to spiritual needs can enhance quality of life for individuals with dementia.

## Open Questions

- How can spiritual health be effectively integrated into standard healthcare practices across diverse cultural contexts?
