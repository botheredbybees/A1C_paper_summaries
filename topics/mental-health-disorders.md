## Mental Health Disorders in Adolescents and Young Adults

The synthesis of sources on mental health disorders highlights the multifaceted impact these conditions have on young people. Both sources emphasize that mental health disorders are prevalent among adolescents and young adults, but they approach this issue from different angles. The first source focuses on how creative activities like arts can be utilized to promote mental health within educational settings, advocating for universal access to such resources as a means of support. In contrast, the second source discusses adolescent and young adult health more broadly, touching upon various aspects including communicable diseases and environmental factors that influence mental health, indicating an interconnectedness between these elements and mental health disorders.

## Key Findings

- Mental health disorders are significant among adolescents and young adults
- Creative activities can serve as a supportive intervention in educational settings
- A broader view of adolescent health must consider environmental factors

## Open Questions

- How do creative interventions effectively address the diverse array of mental health issues faced by youth?
