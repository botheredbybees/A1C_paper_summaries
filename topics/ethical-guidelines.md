## Ethical Guidelines In Arts-In-Health Projects

The provided sources emphasize the importance of ethical guidelines in arts-in-health projects. Source 1 underscores the need for clear project contracts that address intellectual property rights and ethical considerations to support artists working within healthcare settings. This highlights the necessity of establishing a framework that protects both the creative integrity of the artist and the needs and rights of patients or participants involved in such projects.

## Key Findings

- Project contracts are essential for arts-in-health initiatives.
- Ethical guidelines must cover intellectual property rights.
- Supporting frameworks protect artists' work and participant rights.
