## Cohesive Narrative in Photography

The sources collectively emphasize the importance of a cohesive narrative in photography. The 3-picture technique is presented as an effective method to enhance viewer engagement and maintain a consistent story across visual media. Each picture plays a crucial role, with the first setting the stage, the second developing intrigue or tension, and the third providing closure or resolution. This approach not only simplifies storytelling but also ensures that each component of the narrative contributes meaningfully to the overall message.

## Key Findings

- The 3-picture technique is a method to enhance viewer engagement in photography by ensuring a cohesive narrative
- Each picture in the series serves distinct functions: setting up the story, developing tension or intrigue, and providing closure
- A consistent approach across visual media contributes to stronger storytelling
