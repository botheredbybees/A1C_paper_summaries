## Creative Therapy Outcomes

The integration of creative therapies for care-experienced children and young people highlights the significance of trauma-informed care. Across various sources, there is a clear emphasis on how such interventions can positively impact social care pathways. However, while all studies acknowledge the potential benefits of creative therapy in addressing emotional and psychological needs, there remains inconsistency regarding specific therapeutic techniques that are most effective for diverse populations within this demographic.

## Key Findings

- Creative therapies provide significant support to care-experienced children and young people by fostering healing through non-verbal expression.
- Trauma-informed care is a critical framework when implementing creative therapy interventions among vulnerable youth groups.
- Creative therapeutic methods have the potential to improve social care pathways for individuals who have experienced trauma.

## Open Questions

- What specific types of creative therapies are most effective in addressing diverse needs within the population of care-experienced children and young people?
