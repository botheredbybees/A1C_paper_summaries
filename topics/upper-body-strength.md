## Upper Body Strength

The provided sources underscore the multifaceted benefits of interventions focused on enhancing upper body strength. One intervention highlighted is 'dance 4 your life,' a contemporary dance program for female adolescents, which shows positive impacts on aerobic capacity and self-esteem alongside improvements in upper body strength. The focus here indicates that while programs can be designed primarily to improve physical health aspects like upper body strength, they also tangibly contribute to psychological well-being metrics such as self-esteem. This suggests a holistic approach where interventions aimed at specific physiological outcomes may naturally address broader health indicators.

## Key Findings

- Interventions targeting upper body strength can enhance aerobic capacity in participants
- Improvements in upper body strength are associated with increased self-esteem among adolescents
- Contemporary dance is an effective intervention for enhancing both physical and psychological well-being
