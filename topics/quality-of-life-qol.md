## Quality of Life in Older People with Dementia

The topic explores the impact of drama therapy on the quality of life (QoL) for older individuals living with dementia. Sources emphasize the potential benefits of therapeutic interventions, particularly those that foster self-awareness and creative expression. A mixed-method approach is highlighted as a robust way to measure QoL improvements. Despite encouraging findings suggesting enhanced self-awareness and engagement, questions remain about the long-term efficacy and generalizability of such therapies.

## Key Findings

- Drama therapy can enhance quality of life for older people with dementia
- Self-awareness is a key outcome in therapeutic interventions targeting QoL
- A mixed-method approach provides comprehensive insights into patient experiences

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects of drama therapy on QoL for individuals with dementia?
