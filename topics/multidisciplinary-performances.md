## Multidisciplinary Performances

The concept of multidisciplinary performances is highlighted as a means to recontextualize music for social change. These performances integrate various artistic disciplines and foster collective engagement, emphasizing the ritualistic significance of music in fostering social cohesion and transformation. Multiple sources converge on the idea that multidisciplinary approaches can amplify the impact of music in addressing societal issues by leveraging diverse cultural expressions and collaborative creativity.

## Key Findings

- Multidisciplinary performances incorporate various artistic disciplines to enhance the impact of music for social change.
- Music's ritualistic significance plays a crucial role in these multidisciplinary settings, facilitating social cohesion and transformation.

## Open Questions

- How can multidisciplinary performances be effectively integrated into existing cultural practices to maximize their potential for social impact?
