## Neural Interactions

The topic of neural interactions encompasses the intricate ways in which neural networks and structures within the brain communicate and influence behavior, perception, and cognitive functions. The concept is explored as a theoretical framework in understanding how various stimuli and activities affect neural pathways and contribute to healing practices and engagement modes. Despite varying contexts across sources, there is an agreement on the significance of neural interactions for enhancing therapeutic outcomes and personal development through arts-based interventions.

## Key Findings

- Neural interactions play a crucial role in healing processes.
- Engagement modes vary widely but consistently influence brain function.
- Arts-based activities can positively alter neural pathways.

## Open Questions

- How do different engagement modes specifically impact neural plasticity?
