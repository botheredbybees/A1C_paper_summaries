## Enhancing Communication

The source highlights the intervention of Clown Doctor rounds to enhance communication among children, families, and staff. These rounds are aimed at reducing anxiety in clinical settings, thereby improving communication by creating a more relaxed environment where participants feel more comfortable expressing themselves. The key concept of 'enhancing-communication' is central to this intervention, which involves not only verbal exchanges but also non-verbal interactions that foster mutual understanding between patients and healthcare providers.

## Key Findings

- Clown Doctor rounds help in reducing anxiety among children and their families in clinical settings.
- Enhanced communication leads to better patient-provider interaction, improving overall care quality.
- Non-verbal communication plays a significant role in the success of Clown Doctor interventions.
