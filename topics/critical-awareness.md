## Critical Awareness in Young Black Men

The provided sources highlight the use of community-based drama as an intervention to promote health and wellbeing among young Black men. This method aims at fostering critical awareness, which involves enabling participants to critically evaluate their social environments and personal circumstances. The creation of a safe-space within these communities is essential for building trust and encouraging openness about issues affecting them. Additionally, the approach focuses on enhancing self-esteem and individual agency, empowering these young men to make informed decisions about their health and futures.

## Key Findings

- Community-based drama interventions are effective in promoting critical awareness among young Black men.
- The establishment of safe-spaces is crucial for fostering trust and open dialogue within communities.
- Enhancing self-esteem and agency through community engagement can lead to better mental and physical health outcomes.

## Open Questions

- How can the impact of community-based drama interventions on critical awareness be measured over longer periods?
