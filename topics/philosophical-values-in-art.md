## Philosophical Values in Art

The exploration of philosophical values within art reveals a rich tapestry of beliefs and meanings that transcend time and culture. The summary from the source on Ancient Weiyang Storytelling of Indonesia highlights how philosophical values deeply influence artistic expression, particularly through cultural myths and legends. These narratives not only entertain but also impart moral lessons and reflect societal norms and ideals, illustrating the significant role art plays in cultural transmission and identity formation.

## Key Findings

- Philosophical values are integral to the creation and interpretation of art.
- Artistic expressions often encapsulate cultural myths and legends.
- Narratives within art serve educational and social functions.
