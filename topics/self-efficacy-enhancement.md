## Self-Efficacy Enhancement

Both sources emphasize the role of creative arts interventions in enhancing self-efficacy. Source 1 delves into therapeutic photography as a technique that aids individuals in expressing themselves and building confidence through visual storytelling. This method is part of broader phototherapy techniques which aim to improve psychological well-being by engaging participants with photography-based activities. Similarly, Source 2 highlights the importance of cathartic emotional expression and community-building in mental wellbeing programs, suggesting that creative arts practices such as those involving photography can foster a sense of belonging and competence among participants. Both sources underscore how interventions like therapeutic photography not only serve to heal individuals psychologically but also empower them through practical engagement with art media.

## Key Findings

- Therapeutic photography is an intervention aimed at enhancing self-efficacy by allowing individuals to express themselves visually
- Creative arts practices, including photography, contribute to cathartic emotional expression and community-building
- Phototherapy techniques are part of a wider set of methods used in mental health support
