## Health Promotion Through Cultural Activities

Cultural activities have emerged as a significant health-promotion tool across various life stages. Source 1 highlights the positive impact of cultural participation on mental health and social cohesion, indicating that these activities foster community engagement and mutual support networks. In contrast, Source 2 delves into museum-specific initiatives aimed at enhancing health outcomes through sensory-friendly spaces designed for diverse audiences. Both sources underscore the importance of interdisciplinary approaches in creating inclusive environments that cater to a wide range of needs. However, while Source 1 focuses on broad cultural activities, Source 2 provides specific examples within the context of museums, highlighting their unique role in promoting physical and mental health through tailored spatial designs.

## Key Findings

- Cultural participation positively influences mental health and social cohesion across all life stages.
- Museums can serve as key venues for implementing health-promotion initiatives by creating sensory-friendly spaces.
- Interdisciplinary approaches are crucial for designing inclusive environments that cater to diverse needs.
