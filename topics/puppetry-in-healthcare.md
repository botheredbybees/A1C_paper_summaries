## Puppetry In Healthcare

The integration of puppetry into healthcare settings through applied theatre serves as an innovative intervention that fosters emotional resilience and active listening among healthcare professionals and patients. Both sources emphasize the development of a cross-disciplinary pedagogical framework designed to enhance emotional skills within the healthcare context, leveraging creative arts such as puppetry to build capacity for empathy and communication. While one source delves more into the theoretical underpinnings of using puppetry as part of an applied theatre practice, the other focuses on practical activities that employ this approach to improve mental wellbeing. The synergy between these perspectives underscores the versatility and potential impact of puppetry in healthcare.

## Key Findings

- Puppetry is a valuable intervention tool for enhancing emotional skills in healthcare.
- A cross-disciplinary pedagogical framework supports the integration of puppetry into healthcare education and practice.
- Active listening and empathy are key benefits derived from using puppetry in healthcare contexts.
