## Systemic Capacity Building

The topic of systemic capacity building focuses on enhancing the professionalization and ethical standards within arts-based health interventions. The key source discusses the importance of a core curriculum for professionals in this field, alongside the development of a code of ethics to ensure professionalism and high-quality practice. These elements are crucial for systemic capacity building, as they not only establish a framework for individual growth but also foster collaborative efforts among different stakeholders within the arts-in-health community. The integration of these frameworks supports the creation of sustainable systems capable of addressing the unique challenges faced by arts-based health practices.

## Key Findings

- Professionalization through core curricula and ethical guidelines is essential for systemic capacity building in arts-in-health fields.
- Collaboration among diverse stakeholders enhances systemic capacity within the field.
