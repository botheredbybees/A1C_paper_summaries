## Dance/Movement Therapy

The provided sources indicate that dance/movement therapy is a form of psychological intervention utilizing the process of dancing or moving as a mode to facilitate therapeutic change in individuals. Sources highlight how this modality serves not only as an artistic expression but also as a scientifically validated therapeutic approach aimed at addressing emotional and mental health issues through physical activity. This therapy is seen as beneficial for various populations, including those with psychological disorders, neurological conditions, and developmental delays.

## Key Findings

- Dance/Movement Therapy is recognized as a legitimate form of psychological intervention.
- It utilizes the therapeutic use of movement to address emotional and mental health issues.
- It can benefit individuals with diverse psychological and physical conditions.
