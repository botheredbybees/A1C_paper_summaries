## Self-Confidence

The sources collectively explore the impact of arts-based programs on participants' self-confidence. Through qualitative data gathered from participant stories and interviews, a recurring theme is how these interventions foster a sense of belonging which in turn enhances individual confidence levels. However, there is variability in the extent to which different demographic groups benefit from such programs, suggesting that the effectiveness of arts-based interventions may depend on factors like age, gender, and previous experience with similar activities.

## Key Findings

- Arts-based programs can significantly enhance participants' self-confidence through a sense of belonging.
- The benefits of these programs seem to vary across different demographic groups.

## Open Questions

- How do demographic factors such as age and gender influence the effectiveness of arts-based interventions in building self-confidence?
