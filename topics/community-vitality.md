## Community Vitality Through Murals

The use of murals as an intervention to revitalize declining communities is explored across various sources. Murals contribute significantly to the sense of place and community vitality by reversing negative perceptions often associated with graffiti through reverse-graffiti techniques, thus fostering a positive identity for residents and visitors alike. However, while some sources highlight the transformative potential of mural projects in uplifting neighborhood spirits and economic conditions, others point out challenges such as sustainability and ensuring inclusive participation.

## Key Findings

- Murals enhance community vitality by improving sense-of-place through reverse-graffiti techniques
- Mural initiatives can lead to increased civic engagement and improved local economy
- Successful mural projects often require sustained community involvement

## Open Questions

- How can the long-term sustainability of mural-based interventions be ensured?
