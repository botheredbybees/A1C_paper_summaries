## Social Determinants Of Health

The provided sources emphasize the role of social determinants in shaping health inequalities and propose a holistic approach through creative health interventions. Both sources highlight the importance of equitable access to resources, whether they are healthcare facilities or broader societal supports. A consistent theme is that health should be viewed holistically rather than solely through medical lenses. Creative health approaches offer an innovative way to address social determinants by fostering inclusivity and accessibility in health initiatives.

## Key Findings

- Social determinants significantly impact health inequalities, underscoring the need for equitable access to resources.
- Creative health interventions can play a crucial role in addressing these inequalities.
- A holistic view of health beyond medical treatments is essential.

## Open Questions

- How effective are creative health interventions in achieving equitable access compared to traditional methods?
