## Registered Music Therapist

Music therapy is a specialized form of intervention that leverages the power of music to achieve therapeutic goals. Sources highlight the importance of a Registered Music Therapist (RMT) who possesses both theoretical knowledge and practical skills in utilizing music as a tool for communication, expression, and healing across various populations. The role of an RMT includes creating and implementing personalized treatment plans tailored to meet individual needs and objectives.

## Key Findings

- Music therapy is conducted by Registered Music Therapists.
- RMTs use music to communicate and express oneself in therapeutic settings.
- Personalized treatment plans are developed based on the unique needs of each client.
