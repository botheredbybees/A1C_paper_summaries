## Symbolic Representation in Therapy

Symbolic representation emerges as a pivotal technique across various therapeutic and creative arts interventions aimed at enhancing mental wellbeing. Sources highlight the use of externalization of emotions through symbolic means, such as art or play therapy, to facilitate emotional regulation and processing. These methods enable individuals to express complex feelings in a tangible form that is easier to discuss and manage, promoting deeper understanding and resolution of psychological issues.

## Key Findings

- Symbolic representation aids in externalizing internal emotions through creative outlets like art and play.
- Emotional regulation can be enhanced by engaging with symbolic representations, allowing for better management and expression of feelings.
- Therapeutic interventions utilizing symbolic representation help individuals process and resolve complex psychological issues.
