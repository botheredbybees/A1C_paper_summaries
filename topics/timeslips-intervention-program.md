## Timeslips Intervention Program

The Timeslips intervention program emerges as a creative expression initiative tailored to nursing home residents with dementia. Sources converge on the program's potential for enhancing resident engagement and positively influencing caregiver attitudes. The program leverages storytelling and creativity, which appears to provide meaningful interaction opportunities for individuals struggling with cognitive decline. This approach not only enriches the quality of life for those with dementia but also fosters a more supportive environment among caregivers who participate in or observe the therapeutic benefits. However, there is a noted tension around the sustainability and scalability of such programs within the broader constraints of nursing home settings.

## Key Findings

- Timeslips enhances resident engagement through storytelling and creative expression activities
- The program positively influences caregiver attitudes towards dementia care
- Creative interventions like Timeslips can improve quality of life for residents with cognitive impairments

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of Timeslips on both residents and caregivers?
