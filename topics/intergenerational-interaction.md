## Intergenerational Interaction

The synthesis of the provided sources highlights intergenerational interaction as a valuable intervention that fosters positive emotions and enhances social connections between different age groups. Source 1, 'Laughter Care' by The Humour Foundation, underscores the importance of humor and laughter in promoting positivity within these interactions, contributing to an enriched experience for participants from various generations.

## Key Findings

- Intergenerational interaction interventions can foster positive emotions among participants
- Humor plays a significant role in enhancing intergenerational relationships
