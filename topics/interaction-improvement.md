## Living Room Theatre for Dementia Interaction Improvement

The study 'Implementing Living Room Theatre Activities for People with Dementia on Nursing Home Wards' explores the Veder Method as an intervention to enhance interaction among residents in nursing homes. It identifies key factors that influence the implementation of these activities, focusing on how they can improve social interactions and quality of life for individuals with dementia. The research highlights both positive outcomes such as increased engagement and reduced isolation, alongside challenges like staff resistance and resource limitations.

## Key Findings

- Living Room Theatre activities significantly enhance interaction among people with dementia in nursing homes
- The Veder Method is a structured approach to implementing these interactive theatre activities
- Implementation of the intervention faces various challenges including staffing issues

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects of Living Room Theatre on residents' cognitive and emotional well-being?
