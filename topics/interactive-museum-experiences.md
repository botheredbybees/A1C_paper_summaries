## Interactive Museum Experiences

The exploration of interactive museum experiences focuses on enhancing engagement and understanding among diverse audiences, with a particular emphasis on individuals living with dementia. National Museums Liverpool's House of Memories initiative stands out as an example of how museums can serve as platforms for person-centered care and education. This approach not only aids in raising awareness about dementia but also provides valuable resources for caregivers and communities to improve quality of life through meaningful interaction with cultural heritage.

## Key Findings

- Interactive museum experiences enhance engagement among individuals living with dementia.
- Person-centered approaches are crucial for effective dementia care within museums.
- Museums can serve as educational hubs for raising awareness about dementia.

## Open Questions

- How scalable and replicable are person-centered interactive museum programs in different cultural contexts?
