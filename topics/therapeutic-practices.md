## Play and Art Therapies for Neurodivergent Children

The integration of Play Interventions and Art Therapy provides a holistic approach to supporting neurodivergent children's mental health. Both therapies emphasize the importance of creative expression, with Play interventions focusing on growth through structured play frameworks like AutPlay®, while art therapy utilizes visual arts for rehabilitation and emotional processing. Despite their distinct methodologies, both approaches share an emphasis on empathy-in-therapy and reflective practice to empower and affirm participants. However, there is a need to address gaps in understanding how these therapies can be effectively integrated or used concurrently to provide comprehensive mental health support.

## Key Findings

- Play interventions like AutPlay® facilitate growth and empowerment for neurodivergent children through structured play activities.
- Art therapy promotes mental health rehabilitation and emotional expression in a creative environment.
- Both Play Interventions and Art Therapy emphasize empathy-in-therapy and reflective practice to enhance therapeutic outcomes.

## Open Questions

- How can Play Interventions and Art Therapy be effectively integrated or used concurrently for comprehensive support of neurodivergent children's mental health?
