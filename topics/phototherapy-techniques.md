## Phototherapy Techniques

The utilization of photographic techniques for therapeutic purposes has gained significant traction in recent years. Both sources highlight the versatile and profound impact that phototherapy can have on individuals by enhancing self-efficacy, promoting emotional expression, and fostering personal growth through visual storytelling. Despite their distinct focuses, both documents emphasize the adaptability of these practices to modern digital environments, suggesting a broad applicability across various demographics and therapeutic needs. However, while source one delves into specific techniques that can be employed within phototherapy settings, source two provides a more comprehensive overview of the discipline as a whole, touching on its theoretical underpinnings alongside practical applications. This divergence in scope illustrates both the potential breadth and depth of research required to fully comprehend the efficacy and versatility of phototherapy techniques.

## Key Findings

- Phototherapy is increasingly recognized for its capacity to enhance individuals' self-efficacy through visual storytelling and emotional expression.
- The adaptation of traditional photography-based therapies into digital formats broadens their accessibility and applicability.
- A comprehensive approach to studying phototherapy should encompass both practical techniques and theoretical frameworks.
