## Integrative Medicine

The provided sources highlight the growing importance of integrative medicine in patient care. UT MD Anderson's support programs exemplify a holistic approach that incorporates various therapeutic modalities such as art therapy, aiming to enhance overall well-being and provide comprehensive treatment options beyond conventional medical interventions. Across these approaches, there is an acknowledgment of the need for personalized, multidisciplinary care that addresses both physical health and emotional needs.

## Key Findings

- Integrative medicine includes a range of practices such as art therapy designed to complement traditional treatments.
- Holistic patient care emphasizes addressing all aspects of a person's well-being including mental, social, and spiritual factors in addition to physical health.
- Support programs like those offered at UT MD Anderson recognize the therapeutic benefits of non-traditional therapies for enhancing quality of life.
