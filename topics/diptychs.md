## Diptychs

The method of using diptychs involves creating a pair of images that together convey deeper meanings than either could alone. The key focus is on how these pairs can be used to express contextual symbolism and create narratives through imagery. Across the sources, there is agreement on the power of diptychs in storytelling and evoking emotional responses. However, there are tensions regarding the interpretative flexibility—how viewers might derive different meanings from the same diptych based on their cultural or personal backgrounds.

## Key Findings

- Diptychs effectively convey complex narratives through a pair of images
- Contextual symbolism in diptychs enhances emotional and thematic depth

## Open Questions

- How do cultural differences influence the interpretation of diptych meanings?
