## Wellbeing Assessment

The sources collectively emphasize the importance of assessing and understanding children's wellbeing through a multifaceted approach. The first source underscores the necessity for a holistic and comprehensive method that captures various dimensions of development, including psychological, social, and physical aspects. This suggests that current methods may not be fully capturing all necessary factors contributing to child wellbeing.

## Key Findings

- Wellbeing assessment should include multiple dimensions such as psychological, social, and physical health
- A comprehensive data reporting system is crucial for understanding trends in children's wellbeing
- Current methods might lack balance, focusing too narrowly on specific aspects of development
