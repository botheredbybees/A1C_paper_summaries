## Music Therapy in Sustainable Development

The integration of music therapy into sustainable development initiatives highlights the multifaceted benefits of this approach. Projects that utilize music for addressing social, health, and economic issues underscore its role as a community-centered intervention. The application of music therapy within these projects emphasizes its capacity to enhance well-being, foster social cohesion, and contribute to broader developmental goals. These efforts reflect a growing recognition of the therapeutic value of music beyond traditional clinical settings. However, while there is agreement on the positive impacts of incorporating music in sustainable development frameworks, questions remain regarding scalability and sustainability of such initiatives.

## Key Findings

- Music therapy enhances social cohesion and community well-being
- Projects integrating music for health and economic issues show promise
- Community-based music interventions contribute to sustainable development

## Open Questions

- How can the scalability and long-term sustainability of music therapy projects be ensured?
