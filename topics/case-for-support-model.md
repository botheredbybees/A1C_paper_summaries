## Case-for-Support Model

The Case-for-Support Model and the Collaborative-Design Approach are pivotal frameworks in arts-health projects. These methods emphasize the importance of developing strong partnerships between artists and health organizations, focusing on creating comprehensive briefs to guide project development. The case-for-support model serves as a strategic tool for justifying and securing funding by outlining the necessity and impact of arts-interventions within healthcare settings. It highlights the collaborative process in tender processes, fostering mutual understanding and alignment among stakeholders. However, challenges arise from varying levels of engagement and commitment across different organizations.

## Key Findings

- The Case-for-Support Model is crucial for securing funding and support for arts-health initiatives.
- Collaborative design enhances project outcomes through better stakeholder engagement and alignment.
- Tender processes benefit from structured briefs that outline clear goals and methodologies.

## Open Questions

- How can the effectiveness of collaborative design be measured in terms of achieving public health outcomes?
