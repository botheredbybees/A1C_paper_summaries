## Virtual Puppet Play Therapy

The synthesis of the provided sources on Virtual Puppet Play Therapy reveals a unique approach to psychological intervention that leverages digital and symbolic projection techniques. This therapeutic method employs virtual environments and interactive online platforms to facilitate a safe space for clients, particularly children, to express their thoughts and emotions through puppetry. Central to this intervention is the concept of symbolic projection, which allows individuals to externalize their internal struggles in a less threatening manner. The use of technology enhances engagement and accessibility, providing an alternative channel for therapeutic assessment and intervention.

## Key Findings

- Virtual Puppet Play Therapy utilizes interactive online platforms to facilitate psychological expression through puppetry.
- Symbolic projection is a core component enabling clients to externalize their internal conflicts in a controlled environment.
- The method enhances engagement and accessibility, particularly beneficial for children.
