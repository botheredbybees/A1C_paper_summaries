## Creative Arts Intervention

The sources collectively emphasize the role of creative arts intervention as a multifaceted approach to enhancing mental health and well-being. This therapeutic modality is particularly noted for its capacity in neurodiverse populations, addressing social-model-of-disability concerns by fostering emotional regulation and social engagement. Creative arts interventions are seen as a potent tool for enabling individuals with diverse needs to express themselves and navigate challenging emotions effectively.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts intervention enhances mental well-being through emotional regulation
- The approach is particularly beneficial in neurodiverse populations
- Social-model-of-disability informs the application of creative arts therapy

## Open Questions

- What are the specific mechanisms by which creative arts interventions improve emotional regulation?
