## Creative Lighting in Photography

The use of creative lighting is highlighted as a critical element for enhancing the visual narrative and adhering to compositional rules like the rule of thirds. Across sources, there's an emphasis on how proper placement and manipulation of light can dramatically affect the mood and focus within images. While this aspect of photography garners widespread agreement in its importance, there remains a gap regarding standardized techniques for teaching creative lighting as distinct from basic lighting principles.

## Key Findings

- Creative lighting significantly impacts visual narrative.
- Proper use of creative lighting adheres to fundamental compositional rules like the rule of thirds.
- Educational material often merges lessons on creative and basic lighting, leaving gaps in specialized training.

## Open Questions

- What are the most effective methods for teaching distinct creative lighting techniques versus general lighting principles?
