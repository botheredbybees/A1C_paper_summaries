## Digital Manipulation Techniques

The topic of digital manipulation techniques highlights the intersection between advanced image editing and therapeutic applications. Source 1 outlines practical methods for merging and combining images in Photoshop, focusing on layer masking and selection tools as key processes to create seamless composite images. These technical skills are foundational for manipulating visual content but extend beyond mere artistry into psychological therapy contexts as seen in Source 2. This source explores the use of therapeutic self-portraiture, employing Jungian archetypes and color psychology to help individuals reimagine their traumatic experiences through the medium of photography. The integration of digital manipulation techniques serves a dual purpose: enhancing visual storytelling and aiding in personal healing and transformation.

## Key Findings

- Layer masking and selection tools are essential for merging images in Photoshop
- Therapeutic self-portraiture uses digital manipulation to aid trauma recovery through artistic expression
- Jungian archetypes and color psychology inform the therapeutic application of digital photography
