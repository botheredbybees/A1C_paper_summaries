## Selection Tools

The use of selection tools in digital image editing software like Photoshop is pivotal for isolating and manipulating specific parts of an image without affecting the rest. The key concept highlighted is layer-masking, which allows editors to selectively hide or reveal different areas within a single layer. This technique complements the use of selection tools by offering greater control over blending multiple images seamlessly. Across sources, there is agreement on the importance of mastering these techniques for creating professional and polished composite imagery.

## Key Findings

- Selection tools enable precise isolation of image components
- Layer-masking offers advanced control in compositing imagery
- Mastering these methods enhances digital editing proficiency
