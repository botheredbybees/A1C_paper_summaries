## Cultural Interventions

The concept of cultural interventions is explored across various sources, highlighting their impact on health and wellbeing through different lenses. Museums are presented as a key venue for cultural interventions that influence holistic health by addressing social determinants of health. These institutions offer immersive experiences that foster a sense of community and connection to history, culture, and identity. They serve not only as centers for education but also as therapeutic spaces, particularly in urban settings where access to nature is limited. Museums can play a crucial role in promoting mental and physical wellbeing by engaging visitors in meaningful cultural activities, thereby enhancing their quality of life.

## Key Findings

- Cultural interventions like museum visits positively affect holistic health
- Museums address social determinants of health through community engagement
- Engagement with cultural artifacts enhances individual's sense of identity and connection to history

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects of regular participation in cultural activities on overall wellbeing?
