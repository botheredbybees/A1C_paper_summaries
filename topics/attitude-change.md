## Attitude Change

The literature review on puppetry as a pedagogical tool highlights its effectiveness in facilitating attitude change among students. This approach is particularly noted for fostering a positive classroom climate and encouraging cooperative learning, both of which are conducive to changing attitudes. The use of puppets appears to enhance communication generation within educational settings, making it easier for teachers to address sensitive topics that could influence students' attitudes towards various subjects or social issues.

While the review emphasizes the beneficial outcomes associated with using puppets in education for attitude change, there is also an acknowledgment of the variability in how this method is implemented across different contexts. This variability can affect the success rate and generalizability of findings from one study to another.

## Key Findings

- Puppetry enhances communication generation.
- Promotes positive classroom climate
- Encourages cooperative learning
