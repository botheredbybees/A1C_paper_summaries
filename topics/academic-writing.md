## Academic Writing Protocols

The provided sources focus on different aspects of academic writing and research protocols. Source 1 discusses the importance of a well-structured rationale and feasibility in crafting a research protocol, which are crucial for effective communication and justification within an academic community. In contrast, Source 2 introduces various academic skills workshops and critical reflection models aimed at enhancing reflective practices among students. While both sources aim to improve writing quality and clarity, they diverge in their specific methods: one focuses on the technical aspects of research protocol creation, whereas the other emphasizes reflective writing through tools such as photo essays.

## Key Findings

- The need for a clear rationale and feasibility assessment is critical when writing research protocols
- Workshops designed to enhance academic skills can improve students' ability to engage in reflective practices

## Open Questions

- How might the principles of creating an effective research protocol (Source 1) be applied to other forms of academic communication, such as reflective essays and photo essays (Source 2)?
