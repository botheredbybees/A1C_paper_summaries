## Memories In The Making Program

The Memories in the Making (MITM) Program utilizes art therapy as a therapeutic intervention for Alzheimer's patients, focusing on non-verbal communication and creative expression. This program aims to engage individuals with dementia through painting activities that tap into long-term memory reserves and evoke emotional responses. The MITM approach is unique in its use of visual arts to facilitate reminiscence and connection among participants who may struggle with verbal communication due to cognitive decline.

## Key Findings

- Art therapy, specifically the Memories in the Making Program, enhances non-verbal communication for Alzheimer's patients.
- Creative expression through painting can evoke long-term memories and emotional responses in dementia patients.
- The MITM program supports social interaction and provides a meaningful activity for individuals with limited verbal ability.
