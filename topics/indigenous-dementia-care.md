## Indigenous Dementia Care

The sources collectively highlight the importance of culturally tailored interventions for individuals with dementia, particularly within Indigenous communities. Engaging traditional and cultural practices such as using photographic images in therapy shows promise in enhancing engagement and providing meaningful experiences for those living with dementia. While these programs demonstrate effectiveness, there is a need to further research and understand how best to integrate indigenous knowledge and practices into mainstream healthcare settings.

## Key Findings

- Photographic image-based therapies can enhance engagement among individuals with dementia within Indigenous communities.
- Culturally tailored interventions are more effective when aligned with traditional values and practices of the community.

## Open Questions

- What are the best practices for integrating indigenous knowledge systems into mainstream dementia care?
