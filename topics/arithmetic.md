## Arithmetic

The concept of arithmetic is foundational within the quadrivium, an ancient educational framework encompassing four disciplines. Along with arithmetic are three other subjects: geometry, music, and astronomy. Each subject in the quadrivium contributes uniquely to understanding the universe; however, this summary focuses primarily on arithmetic's role, leaving specific interconnections between these disciplines underexplored.

## Key Findings

- Arithmetic is a foundational component of the quadrivium.
- The quadrivium includes geometry, music, and astronomy alongside arithmetic.
