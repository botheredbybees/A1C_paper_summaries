## Super-Aging

The concept of 'super-aging' highlights a demographic trend where individuals are living longer and healthier lives beyond traditional retirement age. This phenomenon presents unique opportunities for enhancing quality of life, particularly through activities such as play that foster work-life balance and personal fulfillment. The integration of play into later life stages not only aids in maintaining physical and mental health but also contributes to the realization of one's authentic self. Challenges arise, however, with the need to adapt societal structures to accommodate this growing population segment.

## Key Findings

- Super-aging individuals benefit from activities that maintain their physical and cognitive vitality.
- Play can serve as a significant tool in enhancing mental health and personal fulfillment among older adults.
- The realization of one's authentic self is an important outcome for super-aging populations.

## Open Questions

- How can societies better support the needs and aspirations of super-aging individuals?
