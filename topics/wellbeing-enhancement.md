## Wellbeing Enhancement Through Arts and Music

Research demonstrates that arts and music interventions significantly enhance wellbeing across various populations. Visual arts programs aimed at dementia patients utilize multi-sensory stimulation to improve personal resilience, fostering dementia-friendly communities. Financial benefits of integrating arts into healthcare are also highlighted, suggesting positive returns on investment for health and wellbeing initiatives. Music therapy case studies further illustrate the therapeutic value of rhythmic auditory stimulation in promoting physical and mental health.

## Key Findings

- Visual arts programs improve personal resilience among dementia patients.
- Healthcare sectors report financial benefits from integrating arts interventions.
- Music therapy, particularly through rhythmic auditory stimulation, enhances both physical and mental wellbeing.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of sustained arts interventions on patient health outcomes?
