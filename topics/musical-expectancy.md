## Musical Expectancy

The concept of musical expectancy is pivotal in understanding how music evokes emotional responses and thrills. Sources emphasize its role as a mechanism that underpins emotional contagion and thrill experiences, suggesting it facilitates the connection between auditory stimuli and psychological outcomes. Both sources agree on the importance of examining underlying mechanisms, such as episodic memory and statistical learning, to fully comprehend the impact of musical expectancy on emotional reactions. The integration of these mechanisms offers a nuanced framework for appreciating how anticipatory processes in music influence our affective states.

## Key Findings

- Musical expectancy is crucial in generating emotional responses and thrills through auditory stimuli.
- Episodic memory and statistical learning are underlying mechanisms that support the role of musical expectancy in emotional contagion.
- Examining the nuances of how these mechanisms interact provides a deeper understanding of music's impact on emotion.

## Open Questions

- What specific neural pathways mediate the relationship between musical expectancy, emotional responses, and thrills?
