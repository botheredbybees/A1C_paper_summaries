## Music And Memory Project

The Music and Memory Project delves into the therapeutic uses of music in dementia care, focusing on how personalized musical experiences can enhance quality of life for individuals living with cognitive impairments. This project is part of a broader study known as the Power of Music and Dementia Study, which investigates the profound impact that tailored musical interventions can have. A key concept explored within this framework is rhythmic entrainment, which refers to the synchronization between an individual's internal rhythms (such as heart rate or breathing) and external stimuli like music. This synchronization has been observed to improve cognitive function and overall well-being in dementia patients.

## Key Findings

- Personalized musical experiences can significantly enhance quality of life for individuals with dementia.
- Rhythmic entrainment plays a crucial role in the effectiveness of music therapy for those suffering from dementia.
- The Power of Music and Dementia Study underscores the therapeutic benefits of tailored musical interventions.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects of sustained exposure to personalized music-based therapies on individuals with dementia?
