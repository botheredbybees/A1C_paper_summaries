## Tender Process in Arts-Health Collaborations

The synthesis of the sources indicates that the tender process is a critical mechanism for initiating arts-health collaborations. It serves as an entry point where organizations or entities outline their needs and solicit proposals from artists or arts organizations. This method often incorporates frameworks such as the case-for-support model, which emphasizes the necessity of demonstrating societal benefit and impact to secure funding and support. A collaborative-design approach is frequently recommended within these processes to ensure that all stakeholders are involved in the development phase, leading to more effective and sustainable projects.

## Key Findings

- The tender process is a foundational step for initiating arts-health partnerships.
- Incorporating a case-for-support model enhances the likelihood of securing funding.
- A collaborative-design approach within tender processes leads to better outcomes.

## Open Questions

- How can the tender process be adapted to better incorporate diverse community voices and perspectives?
