## Art Therapy Models

The integration of various art therapy models highlights their utility in addressing the unique challenges faced by individuals with dementia. These models emphasize non-verbal communication and provide a platform for self-expression, thereby enhancing quality of life and cognitive engagement. Despite the benefits observed across studies, there is variability in how these therapeutic approaches are implemented and their effectiveness in different cultural contexts remains uncertain.

## Key Findings

- Art therapy models enhance self-expression in dementia patients.
- Non-verbal communication through art supports cognitive function.
- Implementation varies widely, reflecting differing cultural practices.

## Open Questions

- How do cultural differences influence the efficacy of art therapy for individuals with dementia?
