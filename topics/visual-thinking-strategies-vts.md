## Visual Thinking Strategies

Visual Thinking Strategies (VTS) are an intervention that utilizes art to enhance observational skills and promote holistic well-being. Across the sources, VTS is used as a tool in medical education and professional development for healthcare professionals, emphasizing its role in fostering observational acumen and empathy. The integration of VTS into educational curricula aligns with broader trends towards incorporating health humanities and Artful Thinking approaches to improve both clinical skills and personal well-being among medical practitioners.

## Key Findings

- Visual Thinking Strategies enhance the observational skills of healthcare professionals.
- VTS is an integral part of professional development in health humanities, promoting holistic well-being.
- Art-based interventions like VTS can foster empathy and improve communication skills within healthcare settings.

## Open Questions
