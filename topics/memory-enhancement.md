## Memory Enhancement

The sources provided focus on the use of various methods to enhance memory and cognitive function in different populations. A central theme is the role of self-expression through arts-based projects, which not only aids in memory enhancement but also promotes social inclusion for individuals with dementia. Each source underscores the importance of tailored approaches that cater to individual needs and circumstances. However, there are gaps in understanding how these interventions scale across diverse cultural contexts and their long-term efficacy.

## Key Findings

- Memory-enhancement through arts-based projects benefits cognitive function.
- Tailored self-expression methods promote social inclusion for individuals with dementia.
- Arts interventions can be effective tools in addressing memory-related challenges.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects of arts-based memory enhancement on diverse populations?
