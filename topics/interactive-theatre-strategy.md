## Interactive Theatre Strategy

Interactive theatre and drama-based strategies are increasingly recognized as effective tools for promoting health knowledge among diverse youth populations. The Sharing Stories program, evaluated in the provided source, exemplifies such an intervention by using interactive theatre to enhance sexual health education among multicultural youth. This approach not only fosters a better understanding of sexual health issues but also encourages cultural sensitivity and engagement. Across studies focusing on similar interventions, there is agreement that these methods can lead to improved knowledge retention and behavioral change compared to traditional educational strategies.

## Key Findings

- Interactive theatre increases sexual health knowledge among multicultural youth.
- Cultural sensitivity and inclusivity are crucial components of effective interactive theatre programs in education.
- These interventions foster better engagement and knowledge retention than conventional teaching methods.

## Open Questions

- How can the impact of interactive theatre be sustained beyond the immediate program duration?
