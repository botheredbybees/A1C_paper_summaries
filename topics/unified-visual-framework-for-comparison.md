## Unified Visual Framework for Comparison

The sources collectively highlight the necessity of a comprehensive visual framework to compare and analyze the impact of art on individuals. The first source emphasizes current psychological models that depict emotional responses and physiological reactions to art, advocating for an integrated approach to understanding these impacts through unified visual representation. Despite differences in specific methodologies discussed in each article, there is consensus regarding the importance of visually comparing various models for a holistic interpretation of art's influence.

## Key Findings

- A need exists for a method that unifies different psychological and physiological reactions to art
- Visual frameworks are essential tools in facilitating comparative analysis among diverse art impact studies
