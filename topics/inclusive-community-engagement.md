## Inclusive Community Engagement

The concept of inclusive community engagement is highlighted across sources as a vital intervention for fostering resilient and supportive communities. Creative practices in disaster management are seen as a core component, aiding both trauma processing through arts and the overall resilience building among individuals affected by disasters. Sources emphasize that such interventions not only cater to the immediate needs during crises but also promote long-term psychological health and community cohesion. However, there is an acknowledged need for more comprehensive strategies on how to effectively implement these creative practices in various contexts, ensuring they are inclusive of diverse populations.

## Key Findings

- Creative practices are essential for disaster management and resilience building
- Inclusive community engagement promotes long-term psychological health and social cohesion
- Trauma processing through arts is a crucial element of recovery efforts

## Open Questions

- What strategies are most effective in implementing inclusive creative interventions across diverse communities?
