## Code Of Ethics For Arts In Health Professionals

The professionalization agenda for arts in health professionals underscores the development of a standardized ethical framework alongside core curricula and certification exams. Sources converge on the importance of establishing a code of ethics as fundamental to ensuring professionalism, accountability, and standardization within the field. Both sources highlight systemic capacity building through these mechanisms, which are seen as essential steps towards achieving parity with more established therapeutic professions.

## Key Findings

- The establishment of a Code of Ethics for Arts in Health Professionals is critical for professional recognition and practice integrity.
- Systemic capacity building includes both educational curriculum development and the implementation of ethical guidelines.
- Certification exams are proposed as an integral part of professionalization, alongside core curricula and codes of ethics.

## Open Questions

- How can the arts in health professionals effectively balance innovation and creativity with adherence to established ethical standards?
