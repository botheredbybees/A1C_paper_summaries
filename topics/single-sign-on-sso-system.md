## Single Sign-On Systems

The sources collectively highlight the role and importance of single sign-on (SSO) systems in modern digital identity management. Shibboleth is presented as a key SSO system, designed to facilitate secure authentication and authorization across various platforms and services without requiring users to remember multiple passwords or re-enter credentials repeatedly. The integration challenges associated with implementing such systems are noted but not detailed. A primary focus is the seamless user experience that SSO provides through its ability to manage identities efficiently over diverse web environments.

## Key Findings

- SSO Systems, including Shibboleth, significantly enhance security and convenience for users accessing multiple applications.
- Implementing an effective SSO system requires robust identity management capabilities and secure authentication mechanisms.
