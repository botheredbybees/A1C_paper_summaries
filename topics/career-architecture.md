## Career Architecture in Arts and Health

The concept of career architecture in the context of arts and health focuses on developing a structured framework for professionals to advance their careers through specific roles such as Arts in Health Practitioner-Certified positions. This involves establishing foundational competencies that are essential for practitioners to excel in their field, thereby creating a clear pathway for professional development. However, while there is an emphasis on the importance of certification and competency frameworks, some tensions arise regarding how these frameworks can be universally applicable across diverse artistic disciplines within health contexts.

## Key Findings

- Development of foundational competencies crucial for career progression
- Certified roles such as Arts in Health Practitioner-Certified are pivotal
- Career architecture provides a structured pathway for professional growth

## Open Questions

- How to ensure the universality and adaptability of competency frameworks across different artistic disciplines
