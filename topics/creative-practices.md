## Creative Arts for Community Wellbeing

The sources collectively emphasize the utility and importance of creative arts methodologies in fostering community engagement and well-being. Source 1 outlines a structured approach to research, underscoring the necessity of selecting appropriate methods that ensure feasibility and align with the research goals. It highlights the significance of methodology choice in shaping the effectiveness of interventions aimed at enhancing social connections and personal development through creative practices. Meanwhile, Source 2 provides an illustrative example of how arts-based research can be employed to address real-world issues by engaging migrant communities through storytelling and shared creative experiences. This application demonstrates not only the potential of such methods for promoting individual wellness but also for strengthening communal bonds across diverse populations.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts methodologies enhance community engagement and well-being
- Methodology choice is critical in ensuring research feasibility and effectiveness
- Arts-based interventions can address real-world issues by fostering social connections

## Open Questions

- How can the structured approach to selecting research methods from Source 1 be adapted for diverse creative arts projects aimed at community development?
