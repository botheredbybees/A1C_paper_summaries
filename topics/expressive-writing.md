## Expressive Writing

Expressive writing is an intervention recognized for its therapeutic and developmental benefits. The paradigm of expressive writing includes various models that evolve to incorporate creative writing as a means to foster personal growth and emotional healing. Studies suggest that engaging in expressive writing can enhance psychological well-being, with some research indicating positive effects on immune system functioning. Additionally, the practice supports narrative development and is often used in shared reading groups to facilitate deeper engagement and therapeutic interaction among participants.

## Key Findings

- Expressive writing has therapeutic value for emotional healing and personal growth.
- Engagement in expressive writing can positively impact psychological well-being.
- Creative writing is an evolving model within the expressive writing paradigm.
