## Stereotypes

The provided sources collectively emphasize the detrimental impact of stereotypes on marginalized groups such as individuals with disabilities. Stereotypes are pervasive and can create significant barriers to social inclusion. Through experiential exercises, one study aims to challenge these preconceived notions and foster a more inclusive environment by transforming perceptions. However, the effectiveness of such interventions in altering long-standing societal attitudes remains a point of tension.

## Key Findings

- Stereotypes can create significant barriers for marginalized groups.
- Experiential exercises may help transform perceptions related to stereotypes.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects of interventions aimed at changing stereotypes?
