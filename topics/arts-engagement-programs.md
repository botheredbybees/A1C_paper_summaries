## Arts Engagement Programs

Arts engagement programs are interventions aimed at enhancing community well-being through artistic activities. Across the provided sources, these programs are discussed for their potential to foster a sense of connection and provide immediate pleasure. These programs vary in focus but consistently highlight positive impacts on participants' social bonds and personal enjoyment. While there is general agreement on the benefits of arts engagement, specific mechanisms by which these programs achieve their outcomes remain underexplored.

## Key Findings

- Arts engagement programs enhance a sense of connection.
- In-the-moment pleasure is an immediate benefit of participating in arts activities.
- Social bonds are strengthened through communal artistic experiences.

## Open Questions

- What specific mechanisms within arts engagement programs contribute to fostering social connections?
