## Past-Tense Usage

The provided sources focus on the usage and implications of the past tense in narrative contexts. The first source discusses the distinction between using past tense versus present tense to recount events that have already occurred, emphasizing how each choice affects the reader's perception of time and engagement with the story.

## Key Findings

- Past-tense usage shifts the focus from immediate action to reflective recollection.
- Different narrative tenses can significantly alter a story's tone and emotional impact.
- The choice between past or present tense influences how readers perceive the immediacy of events.

## Open Questions
