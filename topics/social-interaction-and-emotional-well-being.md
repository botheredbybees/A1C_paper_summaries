## Social Interaction and Emotional Well-Being

The literature highlights the role of personalized interventions in enhancing social interaction and emotional well-being among individuals with dementia. A pilot study focusing on customized music playlists revealed significant improvements in quality of life, primarily due to increased engagement in social activities and better emotional regulation. However, there is a need for more extensive research that explores how these interventions can be tailored across diverse demographic groups and geographic locations to ensure broader applicability and effectiveness.

## Key Findings

- Personalized music interventions positively impact the quality of life for individuals with dementia.
- Music therapy enhances social interaction among those with dementia by increasing engagement in communal activities.
- Customized playlists can improve emotional well-being, aiding in better regulation of emotions.

## Open Questions

- How can personalized interventions be effectively adapted to different cultural and geographical contexts?
