## Reducing Anxiety Through Clown Doctors

The synthesis of literature on clown doctors reveals a consistent theme in the reduction of anxiety among children and their families. Source 1 highlights that the presence of clown doctors during medical rounds significantly contributes to alleviating stress for patients, enhancing communication between healthcare providers and parents/patients, and giving a sense of control back to those undergoing treatment. The intervention appears highly effective due to its non-invasive approach, which entertains, distracts, and provides emotional support.

## Key Findings

- Clown doctors are effective in reducing anxiety for children and their families during medical procedures.
- Enhanced communication between healthcare providers and patients is facilitated by the presence of clown doctors.
- Children gain a sense of control over their environment when engaging with clown doctors.

## Open Questions

- What long-term psychological impacts do clown doctor interventions have on pediatric patients?
