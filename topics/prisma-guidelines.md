## Prisma Guidelines In Research

The PRISMA (Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses) guidelines are a critical framework in the methodology of systematic reviews and meta-analyses. This review, focusing on play-based interventions for mental health, underscores the importance of rigorous reporting standards to ensure comprehensibility, reliability, and reproducibility of research findings. The integration of PRISMA enhances transparency by delineating clear criteria for selection processes, data extraction, and analysis methods, making contributions to the body of knowledge in mental health more robust.

## Key Findings

- PRISMA guidelines enhance clarity and rigor in reporting systematic reviews and meta-analyses.
- Play-based interventions offer significant benefits for mental health outcomes.
- The application of PRISMA facilitates comprehensive analysis and interpretation of research findings.
