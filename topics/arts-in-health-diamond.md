## Arts In Health

The concept of Arts in Health is explored through multiple theoretical frameworks, each offering unique insights into the intersection between arts and healthcare. The 'Arts in Health Diamond' and the continua model present comprehensive views on how arts can contribute to health outcomes by focusing on different aspects such as well-being, recovery, and community engagement. While the Venn diagram approach offers a more nuanced perspective by highlighting overlapping benefits and areas of synergy between arts and health initiatives. These theories aim to define and categorize the various ways in which artistic practices support or are integrated into healthcare settings, indicating both clear delineations and interdependencies among these approaches.

## Key Findings

- The Arts in Health Diamond provides a structured framework for understanding the relationship between arts and health.
- Continua models emphasize the spectrum of involvement from passive observation to active participation.
- Venn diagrams highlight areas where arts and health intersect, fostering holistic perspectives on their collaborative potential.

## Open Questions

- How do different theoretical frameworks integrate in practice to enhance both artistry and healthcare outcomes?
