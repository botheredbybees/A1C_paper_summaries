## Contextual Symbolism in Photography

The concept of contextual symbolism within photography is explored through the use of diptych images, which combine two separate photos to create a new meaning. This technique emphasizes narrative and metaphorical storytelling through visual imagery rather than explicit textual descriptions. Sources converge on the idea that by juxtaposing different scenes or moments, photographers can evoke deeper meanings beyond the literal content of each photo. However, there is a noted gap in how such symbolism translates into broader cultural narratives.

## Key Findings

- Diptych photography employs two images to convey complex ideas and emotions.
- Contextual symbolism relies on viewer interpretation to enrich narrative depth.

## Open Questions
