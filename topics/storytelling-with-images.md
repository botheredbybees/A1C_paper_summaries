## Storytelling With Images

The use of images to tell stories integrates both visual and textual elements that complement each other in conveying narratives. Sources highlight the importance of effective captioning, with Source 1 emphasizing direct and indirect quotes as crucial for authentic representation within photo essays. In contrast, Source 2 focuses on using captions creatively beyond mere descriptions, advocating for active voice and a focus on the five W's (who, what, when, where, why) to enrich storytelling through imagery. Both sources underscore the significance of attribution and narrative clarity, suggesting that successful image-based storytelling requires careful consideration of textual components alongside visual elements.

## Key Findings

- The effective use of direct and indirect quotes enhances the authenticity of photo essays.
- Creative captions leveraging active voice contribute to a richer narrative in photo essays.
- Attribution is key for maintaining credibility when using quotations or text in image-based storytelling.

## Open Questions

- What are the best practices for integrating creative captions while ensuring accurate attribution and representation?
