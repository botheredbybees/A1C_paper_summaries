## Holistic Health

The concept of holistic health focuses on the interconnectedness of mind, body, and spirit in achieving overall well-being. This approach is distinct from traditional medical practices that often address physical ailments without considering emotional or spiritual factors. In examining cultural interventions such as museums' impact on health and wellbeing, sources highlight how engaging with culture through arts and heritage can contribute positively to holistic health by addressing the social determinants of health. Museums provide a unique space for reflection and learning about oneself within broader societal contexts, potentially enhancing personal resilience against stress and promoting mental health.

## Key Findings

- Holistic health considers the interplay between physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being
- Cultural interventions like museum visits can positively impact holistic health by addressing social determinants of health
- Museums offer a space for reflection and learning that enhances personal resilience
