## Ethics in Research

The integration of ethics within research frameworks is a critical element across multiple sources. Each text emphasizes the importance of professional responsibilities and ethical considerations to ensure the credibility and impact of creative arts-based health research. Source 1 discusses the necessity for clear rationale, feasibility assessments, and adherence to guidelines set by ethics committees, reflecting on how these foundational elements support robust protocol development. Meanwhile, Source 2 addresses more specific aspects like general responsibility and conflict-of-interest management, highlighting the role of researchers in maintaining integrity and transparency throughout their projects. Lastly, Source 3 delves into practical issues such as confidentiality maintenance and ensuring meaningful participant involvement, underscoring that ethical challenges can significantly affect the validity and reliability of research findings.

## Key Findings

- Ethics committees play a crucial role in validating the feasibility and rationale of creative arts-based health research protocols.
- Confidentiality is an essential component to protect participants' privacy in such studies.
- Maintaining transparency and addressing conflicts of interest are critical for upholding professional responsibilities.

## Open Questions

- How can researchers ensure meaningful participant involvement while maintaining strict confidentiality?
