## Body-Memory in Non-Verbal Expression

The concept of body-memory is explored as a fundamental aspect of non-verbal expression and movement. Sources highlight the importance of stored physical experiences, often unconsciously, which influence current behavior and expression. This memory system plays a crucial role in dance and other forms of creative physical activity by facilitating spontaneous movements that are deeply personal and emotionally resonant. However, there is limited exploration into how this translates across different populations or cultural contexts.

## Key Findings

- Body-memory influences non-verbal expressions such as dance
- Stored physical experiences play a crucial role in movement and behavior
- There is an emphasis on the unconscious nature of body-memory

## Open Questions

- How does body-memory vary across different cultural or population contexts?
