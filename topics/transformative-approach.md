## Transformative Approach in Care

The topic of 'transformative approach' in care is explored across multiple sources that highlight the importance of shifting traditional paradigms to a more person-centered and sustainable interaction model. Central to this transformative intervention are concepts such as sustained interaction, which emphasizes the need for continuous engagement and support tailored to individual needs over time rather than episodic interventions. Person-centered care emerges as a critical component in these discussions, indicating that effective transformation requires practices that prioritize the patient's or client's preferences, values, and lifestyle in the planning of care processes.

## Key Findings

- The transformative approach in care emphasizes sustained interaction to foster long-term relationships between caregivers and recipients.
- Person-centered care is a cornerstone of effective transformative interventions, ensuring that care plans are personalized and responsive to individual needs.
- Shifting from traditional models towards transformative approaches requires significant changes in healthcare systems and practices.
