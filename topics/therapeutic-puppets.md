## Therapeutic Puppets

The use of puppets in therapeutic settings emerges as a versatile intervention for enhancing self-care and emotional expression. Sources highlight the application of shadow theatre and marionette performance techniques, suggesting their efficacy in addressing mental health issues through creative means. While these methodologies are detailed across various contexts, there is an acknowledgment that more empirical research is needed to fully understand their impact on patient outcomes.

## Key Findings

- Therapeutic puppets facilitate emotional expression
- Shadow theatre and marionette performance techniques can be used in therapy settings
- Further empirical research is required

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects of puppet-based therapies on mental health?
