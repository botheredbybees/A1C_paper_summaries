## Integration of Personality Through Dance Therapy

Dance therapy is presented as a potent intervention for integrating personality and building empathy. Donna Newman Bluestein's overview highlights the practice’s ability to enhance self-awareness, manage emotional dysregulation, and foster personal growth through movement. Sources concur on dance therapy’s capacity for violence prevention by addressing underlying psychological issues through embodied expression. However, there is a gap in understanding how specific personality types might benefit differently from such interventions.

## Key Findings

- Dance therapy promotes self-awareness and emotional regulation.
- It serves as an effective method for violence prevention.
- Enhances interpersonal skills and empathy building.

## Open Questions

- How do different personality types uniquely respond to dance therapy?
