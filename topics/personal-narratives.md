## Personal Narratives in Dementia Care

The integration of personal narratives into dementia care underscores a shift towards more human-centered and empathetic approaches. These narratives serve as powerful tools for enhancing dementia awareness among the public and healthcare professionals, fostering a deeper understanding of the lived experiences of individuals with dementia. Personal stories can also inspire innovative interventions such as interactive light sculptures designed to engage and comfort people living with dementia. However, these efforts face challenges in effectively translating personal narratives into scalable and sustainable public health initiatives that reach broader populations.

## Key Findings

- Personal narratives promote empathy and understanding in dementia care.
- Narratives are instrumental in raising public awareness about dementia.
- Creative interventions like light sculptures can be inspired by personal stories.

## Open Questions

- How to effectively scale up the use of personal narratives in public health campaigns for wider impact.
