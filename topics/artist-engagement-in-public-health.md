## Artist Engagement in Public Health

The integration of the arts into public health initiatives is seen as a powerful tool for enhancing well-being and promoting healing across diverse communities. Sources highlight the importance of removing institutional silos that often segregate artistic endeavors from medical or community services, emphasizing instead the interconnectedness between art and healing processes. Engaging artists in public health efforts can facilitate more holistic approaches to care, addressing both physical and mental health needs through creative expression and therapeutic practices.

## Key Findings

- Artistic interventions are increasingly recognized for their role in supporting health and well-being.
- Removing institutional barriers is crucial for the effective integration of arts into public health frameworks.
- Engaging artists in public health initiatives can lead to more comprehensive and holistic care approaches.

## Open Questions

- How can existing institutional structures be effectively reformed or adapted to better support the inclusion of artistic practices within healthcare systems?
