## Advance Care Planning

The topic of advance care planning (ACP) integrates personalized and integrated care models to enhance end-of-life care quality. Sources highlight the importance of ACP in addressing challenges such as fragmented healthcare systems, patient autonomy, and bereavement support. Creative arts therapy emerges as a supportive tool for patients to communicate their wishes effectively during the ACP process. Despite these advancements, tensions exist regarding the implementation of ACP due to varying cultural perceptions, legal frameworks, and patient engagement levels.

## Key Findings

- Advance care planning is crucial in enhancing personalized end-of-life care.
- Creative arts therapy supports effective communication of patients' wishes in advance care planning.
- Integrated care models support a holistic approach to advance care planning.

## Open Questions

- How do cultural and legal differences affect the implementation and effectiveness of advance care planning?
