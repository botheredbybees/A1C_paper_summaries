## Masking and Brushes

The use of masking and brushes in Photoshop provides a powerful set of tools for image manipulation and editing. These techniques are crucial in the merging and combining of images, where they enable precise control over which parts of layers blend together or remain distinct. The integration of these methods allows users to create complex compositions with detailed layer management.

## Key Findings

- Masking and brushes provide a versatile set of tools for image manipulation.
- Layer blending is facilitated by the application of masks and brushes.
- These techniques offer precise control over image composition elements.
