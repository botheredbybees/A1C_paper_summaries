## Cross-Disciplinary Framework for Emotional Skills in Healthcare

The integration of applied theatre and puppetry techniques with health care pedagogy has emerged as a promising framework to enhance emotional skills among healthcare professionals. Both sources emphasize the importance of a cross-disciplinary approach, combining arts-based methodologies with practical applications within healthcare settings. Source one elaborates on the application of puppetry and theatre exercises aimed at developing emotional resilience and active listening capabilities in medical practitioners. Meanwhile, source two highlights activities that similarly focus on nurturing emotional skills through creative engagement but places emphasis on mental wellbeing and broader aspects of play as therapeutic agents. While both sources converge on the value of integrating arts into healthcare training for fostering emotional competency, they differ slightly in their specific applications and areas of focus within this broader framework.

## Key Findings

- Cross-disciplinary frameworks integrating theatre and puppetry enhance emotional skills among healthcare providers.
- Creative arts play a significant role in developing mental wellbeing through skill-building activities.
- Active listening and emotional resilience are crucial components in healthcare professional training.

## Open Questions

- How can the effectiveness of cross-disciplinary pedagogical methods be measured in terms of their impact on patient care outcomes?
