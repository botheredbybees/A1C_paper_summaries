## Spect-Actors Framework in Musical Theatre

The 'spect-actors' framework is explored as an innovative approach within a musical theatre setting to enhance the social and artistic engagement of people with dementia. This intervention fosters collaboration between individuals living with dementia and performing arts students from the community, emphasizing autonomy and creative expression for those affected by cognitive decline. The primary objective is to provide participants with opportunities for self-expression and meaningful interaction, which can contribute positively to their quality of life.

## Key Findings

- Enhances social and artistic engagement for people with dementia.
- Promotes autonomy and creativity among dementia patients.
- Encourages collaboration between diverse community groups.

## Open Questions

- How does the 'spect-actors' framework impact long-term cognitive function and emotional well-being of individuals with dementia?
