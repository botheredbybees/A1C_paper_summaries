## Professional Responsibilities and Ethical Considerations

The provided sources emphasize the importance of professional responsibilities in conducting research, particularly within arts-in-health contexts. Both texts highlight the need for researchers to navigate potential conflicts-of-interest while ensuring ethical considerations are at the forefront. The first source introduces key concepts such as participant benefits and ethical research questions, indicating a broader focus on the well-being and rights of those involved in studies. Meanwhile, the second source narrows down these principles by discussing specific responsibilities within research design, reinforcing the significance of addressing conflicts-of-interest to uphold integrity in professional practices.
