## Musical Memories

The theme of musical memories emerges in both sources as a potent tool for cognitive and emotional engagement. The introduction to Week 2 highlights the intricate relationship between music and brain function, specifically focusing on how musical memories can assist individuals suffering from cognitive impairments such as dementia. Source two further elaborates on this with an examination of music-based interventions that capitalize on musical memories, noting their efficacy in enhancing the quality of life for those living with dementia through mechanisms like dopamine release which contribute to positive mood and cognitive stimulation.

## Key Findings

- Musical memories can be a powerful therapeutic tool for individuals suffering from cognitive impairments.
- Music-based interventions that leverage musical memories are effective in improving the well-being of people with dementia.
- The use of music in dementia care involves physiological benefits such as dopamine release, which enhances mood and engagement.
