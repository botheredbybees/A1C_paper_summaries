## Cross-Disciplinary Pedagogical Framework

The concept of a cross-disciplinary pedagogical framework is explored as an innovative approach to teaching and learning that integrates various disciplines for comprehensive education. This framework aims at enhancing emotional skills in healthcare contexts, focusing on the intersection between creativity, play, and mental wellbeing. The integration of diverse fields such as psychology, art therapy, and educational methodologies provides a holistic understanding that can be applied across multiple settings including healthcare environments to foster both personal and professional development.

## Key Findings

- A cross-disciplinary pedagogical framework combines various disciplines for comprehensive learning experiences.
- This approach is particularly useful in developing emotional skills within the context of mental health care.
- The incorporation of creative arts and play into educational strategies enhances overall wellbeing.

## Open Questions

- How can this framework be effectively implemented across different professional settings, including those beyond healthcare?
