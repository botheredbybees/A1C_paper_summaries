## Kolb's Reflective Cycle

Both sources emphasize the utility of Kolb's Reflective Cycle in understanding and improving arts in health interventions. Source 1 outlines how the cycle facilitates a structured approach to reflection, which is crucial for continuous learning and improvement in this field. It also highlights the use of discovery interviews as a means to gather data that can be reflected upon within the framework provided by Kolb's cycle. Meanwhile, Source 2 underscores the importance of PESTLE analysis alongside Kolb’s Reflective Cycle, suggesting that a comprehensive understanding of the external environment is essential for effective reflection and decision-making in arts-based practices. This integration of environmental factors through PESTLE enriches the reflective process, providing a broader context for practitioners to consider when reflecting on their experiences.

## Key Findings

- Kolb's Reflective Cycle provides a structured framework for learning and improvement in arts in health interventions.
- Discovery interviews can be used as a data collection method to support reflection within Kolb’s cycle.
- Integrating PESTLE analysis with Kolb's Reflective Cycle enhances the understanding of external environmental factors impacting reflective practices.
