## Collaborative Engagement

The concept of collaborative engagement emerges as a vital approach in enhancing health and wellbeing outcomes through inclusive project design. The key idea revolves around the empowerment of individuals to take active roles in decisions affecting their lives, promoting self-agency and community involvement. This method acknowledges that effective solutions are developed when all stakeholders engage in open dialogue and joint problem-solving, leading to more sustainable and impactful results.

## Key Findings

- Collaborative engagement enhances health outcomes through inclusive project design
- Empowerment of individuals through self-agency is central to collaborative practices

## Open Questions

- What are the barriers to effective implementation of collaborative engagement in diverse cultural contexts?
