## Holistic Healthcare Environment

The concept of a holistic healthcare environment emphasizes integrating various elements to provide comprehensive care beyond traditional medical practices. Sources highlight the importance of incorporating arts and other non-clinical approaches into hospital settings, advocating for bespoke projects tailored to specific needs rather than a one-size-fits-all model. While there is recognition that such interventions can significantly enhance patient well-being and staff satisfaction, challenges arise in defining clear standards or models due to the diverse contexts and individual requirements of healthcare institutions.

## Key Findings

- The holistic healthcare environment aims to integrate non-clinical elements like arts into medical settings for comprehensive care.
- Customized projects are preferred over standardized approaches to address unique institutional needs effectively.

## Open Questions

- What are the specific criteria or models that can guide institutions in implementing and measuring the impact of a holistic healthcare environment?
