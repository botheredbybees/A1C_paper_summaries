## Collaborative Design Approach

The sources collectively highlight the collaborative design approach as a method involving stakeholders in arts-health projects. This process is rooted in developing partnerships and briefs, employing models such as the case-for-support model and navigating tender processes to achieve effective outcomes. The integration of various stakeholders ensures that diverse perspectives are considered, contributing to more inclusive and impactful project designs.

## Key Findings

- Collaborative design approach involves multiple stakeholders
- Case-for-support model is utilized in arts-health projects
- Navigating tender processes is a critical aspect
