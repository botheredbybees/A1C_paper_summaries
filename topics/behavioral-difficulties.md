## Behavioral Difficulties in Adolescents

The sources collectively examine interventions aimed at addressing behavioral difficulties among adolescents from refugee backgrounds. Creative arts therapy emerges as a promising intervention to manage emotional symptoms and support cognitive development in this population. While there is consensus on the positive effects of such programs, variations in therapeutic outcomes highlight the need for tailored approaches considering cultural context.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts therapy can effectively address emotional symptoms in adolescents from refugee backgrounds
- Interventions must be culturally sensitive to maximize their effectiveness among adolescent refugees
- Behavioral difficulties are often linked with cognitive developmental challenges
