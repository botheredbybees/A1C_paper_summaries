## Creative Curriculum In Health Education

The integration of a creative arts curriculum within health professional education aims to foster unique learning experiences that encourage reflection, ethical thinking, and personal growth. This approach is highlighted in sources discussing its role in promoting compassion in practice among healthcare professionals (Source 1). However, the practical implementation may vary widely across different institutions, reflecting challenges such as institutional resistance to non-traditional teaching methods or skepticism about the applicability of arts-based interventions in a rigorous scientific environment (implied from Source 2 and Source 3). Despite these challenges, proponents argue that creative curricula can enhance empathy, resilience, and ethical awareness among students, contributing to more holistic and patient-centered care practices.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts integration enhances compassion and ethical awareness in healthcare professionals
- Implementation of creative curriculum faces institutional barriers
- Creative approaches improve professional resilience

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects on patient care outcomes from a creative curriculum?
