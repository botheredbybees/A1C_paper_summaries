## Challenging Societal Norms Through Art

Josie Webster's exhibitions challenge the traditional view of inclusion and self-expression in the art world. By integrating Down syndrome inclusion with a focus on individual artistic expression, her work disrupts societal norms that often marginalize individuals based on their intellectual differences. This intervention highlights the transformative power of breaking barriers within creative disciplines to foster more inclusive environments.

## Key Findings

- Down syndrome inclusion in the art world is enhancing self-expression and challenging exclusionary practices
- Artistic expression serves as a powerful tool for societal norm challenge
- Fostering an inclusive environment through art can significantly impact perceptions of diversity

## Open Questions

- How can other creative industries be similarly transformed to include marginalized communities?
