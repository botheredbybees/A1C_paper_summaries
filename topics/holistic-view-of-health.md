## Holistic View Of Health

The concept of a 'holistic view of health' emerges as an integrative approach that seeks to address the interconnectedness of physical, mental, emotional, and social aspects of wellbeing. Creative health is highlighted as a component within this framework, emphasizing the role of arts in promoting overall health beyond traditional medical treatments. However, there is also a recognition of significant disparities in access to these holistic healthcare approaches, particularly among marginalized communities. Sources concur on the importance of equitable distribution of creative and holistic health resources but highlight challenges in implementation.

## Key Findings

- Creative health is an integral part of the holistic view of health
- Holistic health approaches aim for comprehensive well-being including physical, mental, emotional, and social aspects
- Equitable access to healthcare remains a challenge when implementing holistic health theories

## Open Questions

- How can equitable access to holistic and creative health resources be achieved across diverse populations?
