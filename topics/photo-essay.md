## Photo Essays

The photo essay is a method of visual storytelling that integrates images and text to narrate stories. It emphasizes the use of visual elements for narrative expansion, enhancing traditional written narratives with dynamic imagery. This technique requires mastery in composition techniques such as color theory and framing, which are essential to convey depth and emotion effectively.

## Key Findings

- Photo essays utilize a combination of images and text to narrate stories vividly
- Color theory is crucial for evoking emotions and setting the mood in photo essays
- Effective composition techniques enhance the narrative quality of photo essays

## Open Questions

- How does the integration of text with imagery impact the audience's interpretation and emotional engagement?
