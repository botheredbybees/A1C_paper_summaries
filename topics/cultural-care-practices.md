## Cultural Care Practices in Dementia Care

The provided source highlights the importance of cultural care practices for individuals with dementia, particularly focusing on disparities in late-onset Alzheimer's disease prevalence. Cultural care practices encompass various interventions tailored to the unique needs and backgrounds of patients, aiming to provide more personalized and effective support. The document underscores the necessity of understanding and integrating these practices within broader care frameworks to address health inequities effectively.

## Key Findings

- Cultural care practices are essential in enhancing dementia care quality for diverse populations.
- Late-onset Alzheimer's disease prevalence varies significantly across different cultural groups.
- Understanding and implementing culturally sensitive care can help reduce health disparities.
