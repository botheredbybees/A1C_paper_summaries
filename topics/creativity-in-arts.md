## Creativity in Arts

The sources collectively highlight the role of creativity in arts as an intervention aimed at enhancing quality of life and fostering community engagement. Ethnomusicology and applied arts are presented as tools for addressing social issues, emphasizing their potential to create a 'better life' through creative expression. The literature underscores the importance of context-specific approaches that integrate local culture with artistic practices, aiming to empower communities rather than imposing external solutions. However, there is an underlying tension between the universal benefits attributed to creativity in arts and the need for tailored interventions that respect cultural nuances and community needs.

## Key Findings

- Creativity in arts can significantly contribute to improving quality of life
- Ethnomusicology and applied arts are powerful tools for fostering community engagement
- Tailored, context-specific approaches to integrating arts into communities are most effective

## Open Questions

- How do cultural nuances impact the effectiveness of creativity-based interventions in arts?
