## Ethical Research Questions

The sources highlight the importance of addressing various ethical considerations in research involving human participants. These include identifying and mitigating conflicts of interest that may influence the integrity of the research process, ensuring clear communication about potential participant benefits, and raising critical questions to ensure ethical rigor. Each source underscores the necessity for a robust framework that safeguards both the rights and welfare of participants while maintaining scientific validity.

## Key Findings

- Conflicts of interest must be disclosed and managed to preserve research integrity
- Ensuring participant benefits is crucial in ethical research
- Critical questioning enhances ethical rigor in research practices

## Open Questions

- How can researchers effectively balance the interests of participants with those of funders or sponsors?
