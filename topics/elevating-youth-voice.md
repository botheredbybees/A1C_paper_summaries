## Elevating Youth Voice Through Arts-Based Interventions

The integration of arts-and-culture-based strategies to support mental health among young people is gaining global recognition. A key finding across sources is the importance of elevating youth voice as a core element in these interventions, aiming to empower adolescents and foster resilience against mental health challenges. Each source emphasizes the transformative impact of engaging youth actively through artistic mediums, such as music therapy or visual arts programs. However, while there's consensus on the efficacy of these approaches in improving mental health outcomes among youth, tensions arise regarding how to best implement such strategies in diverse cultural contexts. Furthermore, questions remain about measuring long-term impacts and ensuring access for marginalized communities.

## Key Findings

- Elevating youth voice is integral to effective arts-and-culture-based interventions aimed at supporting mental health
- Arts-based approaches show promise in improving mental health outcomes among young people globally
- Engaging youths actively through artistic mediums can foster resilience and empowerment

## Open Questions

- How do we effectively implement arts-based strategies to support youth mental health across diverse cultural contexts?
