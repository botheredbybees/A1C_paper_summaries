## Confessional Writing

Confessional writing is a therapeutic intervention that facilitates the expression of personal experiences and emotions, often through unconventional mediums like photography. Source 1 delves into creating a photo essay on breast cancer diagnosis, emphasizing the use of confessional writing to convey deeply personal struggles.
