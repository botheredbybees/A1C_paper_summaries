## Rigorous Scientific Investigation

The sources collectively highlight the importance of rigorous scientific investigation as a cornerstone for evaluating and understanding mechanisms of impact in various fields. The concept is introduced as essential for distinguishing between evaluation and research, emphasizing the need for systematic methodologies that ensure reliability and validity of findings. However, there is a tension identified around how to operationalize rigor while maintaining flexibility in approaches to accommodate diverse contexts and populations.

## Key Findings

- Rigorous scientific investigation is crucial for establishing credible mechanisms of impact
- Distinguishing between evaluation and research requires methodological rigor
- Flexibility in methodologies can enhance applicability across different contexts

## Open Questions

- How to balance methodological flexibility with the need for rigourous standards?
