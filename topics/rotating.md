## Image Rotation Techniques

The method of image rotation is a fundamental adjustment in digital image processing. The GCFLearnFree tutorial highlights that image rotation is one of several basic adjustments, alongside cropping and resizing, which are essential for enhancing the visual appeal and effectiveness of an image without altering its intrinsic content. This source underscores the importance of maintaining the aspect ratio during these transformations to preserve the integrity of the original image's proportions.

## Key Findings

- Image rotation maintains the core subject matter while changing orientation.
- Cropping, resizing, and rotating are interrelated adjustments in digital image processing.
- Maintaining the aspect ratio is crucial when rotating images for quality preservation.
