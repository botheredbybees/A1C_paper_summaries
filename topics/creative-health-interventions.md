## Creative Arts Therapy in Healthcare

Sources highlight the potential of creative arts therapy as an intervention across various healthcare settings. Both sources underscore its value in personalized care and trauma-informed practices, contributing to holistic health outcomes for patients dealing with complex needs such as end-of-life issues or traumatic experiences. While Source 1 emphasizes the role of creative therapies within an integrated care model focusing on advance planning and bereavement support, Source 2 explores their application among children and young people in social care settings, noting positive therapeutic outcomes. However, both sources suggest a need for more robust research to fully understand how these interventions can be optimized and scaled.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts therapy is an integral part of personalized healthcare approaches.
- There are promising outcomes associated with trauma-informed creative therapies.
- Integrated care models benefit from the inclusion of advance planning and bereavement support via creative arts.

## Open Questions

- What specific mechanisms underpin the effectiveness of creative arts therapy in diverse healthcare contexts?
