## Role of Arts in Reducing Mental Health Stigma

Sources explore the transformative impact of integrating arts into healthcare and society to improve mental health outcomes. Both sources emphasize that creative activities like art therapy and workshops can enhance social cohesion, reduce stigma associated with mental illness, and provide a means for individuals to express their experiences in non-verbal ways. However, while Source 1 focuses more on practical applications such as social policy reform and data enrichment through artistic endeavors, Source 2 highlights the importance of arts integration at the community level to foster a supportive environment that encourages openness about mental health issues.

## Key Findings

- Creative activities can significantly reduce stigma around mental illness by providing non-verbal means for expression and understanding.
- Integration of arts in healthcare settings enhances social cohesion among patients, promoting positive mental health outcomes.
