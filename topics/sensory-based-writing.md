## Sensory-Based Writing

The concept of sensory-based writing is a therapeutic intervention aimed at enhancing creativity and communication in individuals with dementia. This approach utilizes the senses to evoke memories and stimulate creative expression, often through the use of life-story work and object-based reminiscence techniques. The key benefit highlighted across sources is the potential for sensory-based writing to improve quality of life by facilitating meaningful engagement and expression. However, there are varying degrees of implementation, with some approaches focusing more on structured exercises while others emphasize spontaneous creative processes.

## Key Findings

- Sensory-based writing enhances communication in individuals with dementia.
- Life-story work is an integral component of sensory-based writing interventions.
- Object-based reminiscence aids in evoking sensory memories that inspire written expression.

## Open Questions
