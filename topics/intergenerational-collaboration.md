## Intergenerational Collaboration

The synthesis of the topic on intergenerational collaboration highlights its potential to bridge generational gaps through collaborative activities and projects. A key case study underscores the importance of democratizing technology access for older adults, enabling them to participate actively in creative thinking initiatives alongside younger generations. Despite the positive outcomes seen in such interventions, there remains a need for further research into how these collaborations can be sustained over time and scaled up.

## Key Findings

- Intergenerational collaboration projects enhance mutual understanding and respect between different age groups.
- Democratization of technology access is crucial for ensuring equal participation from older adults in intergenerational initiatives.

## Open Questions

- How can intergenerational collaborations be sustained beyond the initial project timeframe?
