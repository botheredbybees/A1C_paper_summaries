## Creative Arts Engagement

The concept of Creative Arts Engagement (CAE) is a significant intervention that aims to enhance mental and physical well-being through participation in various forms of artistic activities. Sources highlight the importance of CAE as a therapeutic approach, with 'Arts on Prescription' being one notable example. This initiative underscores how arts engagement can positively influence health outcomes across diverse demographic groups. The integration of creative arts into community-based settings also reflects broader societal shifts towards recognizing the non-traditional benefits of arts involvement beyond mere aesthetic appreciation.

## Key Findings

- Creative Arts Engagement is recognized as an effective intervention for improving health and well-being.
- Arts on Prescription programs are increasingly being utilized to address mental health issues within communities.
- The demographic changes in society are influencing the adoption and acceptance of creative arts engagement initiatives.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of sustained participation in Creative Arts Engagement activities on various health outcomes?
