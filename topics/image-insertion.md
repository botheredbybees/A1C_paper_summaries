## Image Insertion in Powerpoint Presentations

The integration of images into presentations through software like Microsoft PowerPoint is a critical aspect of enhancing visual communication and engaging audiences. Both sources emphasize the importance of appropriate slide layouts to ensure that image insertion supports rather than distracts from the content's message. They highlight common practices such as choosing relevant, high-quality images that align with the presentation’s theme and maintaining consistency in design elements across slides. However, there is a lack of detailed discussion on the impact of different image types (e.g., photographs vs. infographics) or the role of audience demographics in selecting suitable imagery.

## Key Findings

- Image insertion should adhere to pre-established slide layouts to maintain visual coherence throughout presentations.
- Choosing high-quality and relevant images is crucial for effective communication in PowerPoint presentations.

## Open Questions

- How do different types of images (photographs, infographics) affect audience engagement and message comprehension?
