## Creativity and Community Engagement

The concept of using creative arts as a means to enhance community well-being is explored through the lens of ethnomusicology. The sources highlight how engaging with various forms of art, particularly music, can foster social cohesion and personal development within communities. There is an emphasis on active participation in creating arts rather than merely consuming them. While the benefits are evident, there remains a need to better understand how these interventions can be tailored to different cultural contexts.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts engagement fosters community well-being by promoting social connections and personal growth.
- Active participation in art creation is more beneficial for communities than passive consumption of art.
- Ethnomusicology provides a framework for understanding the role of music and other forms of creative expression in community development.

## Open Questions

- How can creative arts interventions be adapted to different cultural contexts while maintaining their efficacy?
