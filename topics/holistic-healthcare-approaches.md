## Holistic Healthcare Approaches

The concept of holistic healthcare approaches is increasingly recognized in various sectors beyond traditional medical practice. Museums, for instance, have been repurposed as spaces for promoting wellbeing through activities that align with the principles of holistic health, such as co-creation methods and green initiatives. These museums are not only places of cultural education but also venues where individuals can engage in self-care practices that promote mental and physical wellness by addressing the interconnected nature of human existence. The integration of these healthcare approaches into non-clinical settings underscores a shift towards more inclusive and community-oriented models of health support.

## Key Findings

- Holistic healthcare extends beyond medical facilities to include unconventional spaces like museums, reflecting a broader societal understanding of well-being.
- Co-creation methods encourage active participant involvement in the design and delivery of health interventions, fostering a sense of ownership and empowerment among individuals seeking care.
- Green initiatives within these settings highlight the importance of nature-based solutions for mental and physical health improvement.
