## Arts Activities for Youth Resilience

The reviewed literature emphasizes the role of arts activities in fostering resilience and mental well-being among children and young people. Both sources highlight structured group arts programs as a means to enhance self-efficacy, resilience, and overall psychological health. However, while there is consensus on the positive impact of such interventions, the specific mechanisms through which these benefits are realized remain unclear. The integration of self-efficacy concepts underscores the importance of skill-building and confidence in nurturing resilient youth.

## Key Findings

- Structured group arts activities positively influence resilience in children and young people.
- Self-efficacy is a critical component in the effectiveness of arts-based interventions for mental well-being.

## Open Questions

- What are the specific mechanisms through which arts activities enhance self-efficacy and resilience?
