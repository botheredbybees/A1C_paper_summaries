## Arts Therapies and Resilience in Children

The integration of arts therapies such as music, dance, drama, and visual art into mental health support for children and young people is shown to have a significant positive impact. These therapies not only serve as expressive outlets but also aid in building resilience against psychological stressors. Each modality of arts therapy offers unique pathways for emotional exploration and cognitive development, enhancing the overall therapeutic efficacy.

## Key Findings

- Arts therapies provide children with an alternative means to express emotions that may be difficult to articulate verbally.
- Diverse forms of arts therapy contribute uniquely to mental health support in children, fostering resilience against future stressors.
- The combination of multiple arts therapies can offer a more comprehensive approach to addressing the complex needs of youth.

## Open Questions

- How does the efficacy of different types of arts therapy vary across various cultural and socioeconomic backgrounds?
