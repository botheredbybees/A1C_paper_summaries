## Community Cohesion

The integration of arts in communities aims to enhance community cohesion by fostering a sense of belonging and engagement. Sources highlight the role of cultural activities in promoting social connections, particularly among marginalized groups. Cultural engagement through artistic interventions is seen as an effective strategy for mental health recovery and overall well-being. However, there are tensions regarding the efficacy of these programs across different demographic contexts.

## Key Findings

- Cultural activities foster a sense of community and belonging
- Arts-based interventions contribute to mental health recovery
- Mental health benefits from cultural engagement vary among populations

## Open Questions

- How do arts-based programs impact community cohesion in diverse demographic contexts?
