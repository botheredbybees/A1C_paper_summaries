## Cross-Cultural Dialogue Through Art and Poetry

The sources highlight the therapeutic and integrative power of cross-cultural dialogue facilitated through art and poetry. Source one underscores how wisdom poetry can serve as a platform for exploring universal themes, transcending cultural boundaries to foster deeper mutual understanding among participants. It emphasizes that poetic expressions can encapsulate cultural wisdom and facilitate emotional healing beyond linguistic barriers. Conversely, source two delves into the tangible benefits of arts collaborations in refugee communities, focusing on the preservation of cultural identity while promoting intercultural understanding. The art-based interventions not only provide a medium for expressing trauma but also aid in community building by bringing diverse groups together through shared creative processes. Despite differences in context and specific modalities (poetry vs. broader artistic practices), both sources converge on the importance of transcendent elements that bridge cultural divides, thereby enhancing intercultural dialogue and communal resilience.

## Key Findings

- Poetic and art-based interventions facilitate emotional healing by expressing universal themes beyond linguistic boundaries.
- Intercultural understanding is significantly enhanced through collaborative artistic practices involving diverse communities.
- Artistic expressions aid in the preservation of cultural identity while fostering social cohesion among refugees.
