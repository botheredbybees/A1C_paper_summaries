## Pubertal Hormones

The impact of pubertal hormones on adolescent brain development and behavior is a complex topic explored across several sources. Pubertal hormones are shown to have significant organizational effects, structuring the neuroendocrinology landscape during puberty. This process influences various aspects of cognitive and emotional development in adolescents. While there is consensus on the broad influence of these hormones, specific mechanisms and long-term outcomes remain areas for further investigation.

## Key Findings

- Pubertal hormones significantly influence adolescent brain organization.
- Organizational effects extend to behavioral changes during puberty.
- Understanding pubertal hormone impacts requires interdisciplinary approaches combining neuroendocrinology with cognitive science.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term psychological and physiological consequences of pubertal hormonal changes?
