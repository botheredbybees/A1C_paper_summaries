## Art in Healthcare Environments

The integration of arts and creative interventions into healthcare settings has been highlighted as a significant shift towards holistic healing practices. Source 1, focusing on 'Contemporary Art in Healing Environments,' emphasizes the role of arts-in-healthcare initiatives that aim to improve patient experiences and therapeutic outcomes through aesthetic and emotional engagement with art. This source underscores the importance of incorporating therapeutic design elements into hospitals and clinics to reduce stress and enhance overall well-being. Meanwhile, Source 2 introduces a broader framework by discussing 'The Fifth Wave of Public Health,' which identifies cultural and artistic contributions as pivotal in addressing emergent societal problems within public health strategies. Both sources converge on the idea that art can serve as an intervention to improve healthcare outcomes beyond traditional medical treatments, suggesting a comprehensive approach to patient care that includes social, emotional, and environmental factors.

## Key Findings

- Arts-in-healthcare initiatives enhance therapeutic environments.
- Incorporating artistic elements in healthcare settings improves patient well-being.
- Public health strategies are increasingly recognizing cultural contributions as essential for addressing societal challenges.

## Open Questions

- How can arts-based interventions be effectively integrated into diverse public health systems worldwide?
