## Torres Strait Islanders' Health and Wellbeing

The health and wellbeing of Torres Strait Islanders is heavily influenced by their connection to culture, which includes family/community connections, self-determination, and cultural engagement. Both sources highlight the importance of these factors in improving health outcomes but note that there are varying levels of evidence supporting different aspects of indigenous culture's impact on health and wellbeing. Mixed-methods research approaches are suggested for a more comprehensive understanding.

## Key Findings

- Torres Strait Islanders' connection to their culture is crucial for their health and wellbeing.
- Self-determination plays a significant role in the health outcomes of Torres Strait Islanders.
- Mixed-methods research provides a more nuanced approach to understanding indigenous cultural determinants of health.

## Open Questions

- What specific components of mixed-methods research are most effective for understanding the impact of culture on Torres Strait Islander health?
