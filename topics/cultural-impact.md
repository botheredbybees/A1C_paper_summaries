## Cultural Impact of Artistic Wellbeing

The synthesis integrates findings from the National Arts Participation Survey and research in music psychology to explore how artistic wellbeing impacts cultural contexts. The survey emphasizes the positive effects on individuals' lives during challenging times such as a pandemic, indicating that creative activities contribute significantly to emotional health and resilience. Meanwhile, music psychology studies highlight culturally-specific expectations around musical expressions and their role in evoking emotions unique to different societal groups. Both sources underscore the importance of artistic engagement for personal development and cultural identity. However, there is a noted gap regarding how these impacts vary across diverse socio-economic backgrounds within cultures.

## Key Findings

- Artistic activities positively influence emotional health during crises like pandemics.
- Cultural contexts shape expectations around music's emotional expressiveness.
- Creative engagement contributes to individual and collective resilience.

## Open Questions

- How do socio-economic factors within cultures affect the benefits of artistic wellbeing?
