## Public Baths and Mental Health

The integration of public baths into urban landscapes highlights the therapeutic benefits derived from engaging with natural elements. Source 1 underscores the role of such facilities in enhancing mental health through communal bathing experiences that are influenced by historical architecture, personal memories, and seasonal changes like winter storms. These settings not only provide physical relief but also foster social cohesion, thereby contributing to psychological well-being.

## Key Findings

- Public baths have a positive impact on mental health.
- Historical architectural elements enhance the therapeutic value of public baths.
- Seasonal changes, particularly in winter, affect the use and perception of public bathing facilities.

## Open Questions

- How do contemporary urban planners incorporate the principles of natural elements into new public bath designs to optimize their mental health benefits?
