## Communication Improvement

The sources provided emphasize the role of therapeutic interventions in enhancing patient communication. Therapeutic clowning (Source 1) highlights that engaging in sessions with clowns not only reduces fear and anxiety but also improves communication, suggesting a direct link between emotional comfort and verbal expression. This approach underscores the importance of creating a relaxed environment for effective interaction.

## Key Findings

- Therapeutic interventions can reduce patient fear and anxiety, leading to improved communication.
- Creating a comfortable setting is crucial for better patient-physician communication.
