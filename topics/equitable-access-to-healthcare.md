## Equitable Access To Healthcare

The concept of equitable access to healthcare is integral across the presented sources, emphasizing a need for a more inclusive and creative approach to health services. Creative health introduces an innovative perspective that focuses on enhancing holistic well-being rather than merely addressing medical conditions. This framework underscores the importance of considering individual needs beyond clinical treatment in order to achieve true equity in health care access.

## Key Findings

- Equitable access to healthcare involves a comprehensive approach that goes beyond traditional medical services.
- Creative health initiatives aim at fostering a more inclusive and personalized approach to enhancing well-being across diverse populations.

## Open Questions

- How can creative health practices be effectively integrated into existing healthcare systems to promote equitable access?
