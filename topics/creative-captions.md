## Creative Captions

The concept of 'creative captions' as part of storytelling with image and text is explored across sources. The use of the five W's (who, what, where, when, why) in conjunction with active voice contributes to engaging readers by embedding narrative depth within visual content. Captions act not only as a bridge for understanding but also as a creative intervention enhancing engagement. Active voice emphasizes direct communication between caption and viewer, making storytelling more impactful and relatable.

## Key Findings

- Creative captions enhance the narrative impact of images
- Active voice is essential in crafting engaging creative captions
- The five W's are fundamental elements to consider when developing creative captions
