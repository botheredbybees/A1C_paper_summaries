## Therapeutic Process

The therapeutic process is explored through the lens of Roland Barthes' Camera Lucida, which emphasizes photography as a medium for reflection and introspection. The photo essay 'Critical Reflection on '28 Solomon Street'' captures moments that encapsulate emotional and psychological experiences in an environment where personal growth and healing occur. This source highlights the power of visual storytelling in conveying complex human emotions and the nuances of therapeutic work.

## Key Findings

- Photography can serve as a powerful tool for introspection and reflection.
- Environments like '28 Solomon Street' provide contexts rich with emotional cues that support personal growth and healing.

## Open Questions

- How do different mediums (e.g., photography, writing) uniquely contribute to the therapeutic process?
