## Memory Preservation Through Therapeutic Photography

The role of therapeutic photography in preserving memories and supporting family wellbeing is examined through the lens of 'Apples Never Fall.' This study highlights how food, a significant element in personal identity and cognitive development, serves as a medium for emotional expression and memory preservation. By integrating the arts into healthcare practices, such as through photographic narratives, individuals can revisit past experiences and emotions associated with specific meals or gatherings. However, while therapeutic photography offers a unique way to encapsulate family histories and moments, its effectiveness varies depending on individual cognitive abilities and access to resources.

## Key Findings

- Therapeutic photography helps in preserving personal memories linked to food.
- Food-related imagery contributes significantly to emotional health through the arts in healthcare.
- Photographic narratives can serve as a tool for cognitive development and family wellbeing.
