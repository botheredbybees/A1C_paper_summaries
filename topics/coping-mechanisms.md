## Coping Mechanisms Through Creative Expression

Both sources highlight the role of creative expression in fostering effective coping mechanisms. Source 1 employs narrative photography to illustrate an individual's journey, emphasizing artistic expression as a medium for processing and sharing personal experiences. In contrast, Source 2 focuses on how puppet integration within an art and storytelling program aids immigrant and refugee children in developing emotional well-being and a renewed sense of agency. Despite differing contexts—personal versus communal—the sources converge on the efficacy of creative methods to bolster mental resilience and provide narrative structure for challenging life situations.

## Key Findings

- Creative expression through photography and puppetry serves as an effective coping mechanism, enabling individuals to articulate their experiences and foster emotional well-being.
- Narrative frameworks in creative interventions can empower participants by providing them with a sense of agency and control over their narratives.

## Open Questions

- How do cultural backgrounds influence the effectiveness and form of creative expression as a coping mechanism?
