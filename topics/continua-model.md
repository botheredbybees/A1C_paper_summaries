## Continua Model In Arts In Health

The Continua Model is a theoretical framework within arts in health that seeks to define and distinguish the various roles of arts in healthcare settings. This model uses visual aids such as diamonds, continua, and Venn diagrams to illustrate the complex relationships between art forms and their applications in therapeutic and cultural contexts. It highlights the overlapping and distinct areas where arts intersect with health care provision, patient well-being, and community engagement, thereby providing a nuanced understanding of how different artistic expressions can be integrated into healthcare practices.

## Key Findings

- The Continua Model utilizes visual aids like diamonds, continua, and Venn diagrams to explain the complex relationships between art forms and their applications in health care settings.
- It delineates distinct areas where arts intersect with health care provision, patient well-being, and community engagement.
- The model provides a framework for understanding the integration of various artistic expressions into healthcare practices.
