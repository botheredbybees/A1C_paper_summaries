## Cultural Wellbeing

The sources collectively emphasize the multifaceted benefits of cultural activities such as music and dance for enhancing physical, mental, and cultural wellbeing. Active participation in these practices is highlighted as a systematic approach to promoting overall health, influenced by social determinants of health. The integration of cultural elements into healthcare and wellness programs underscores their role in addressing diverse aspects of wellbeing. However, there are gaps regarding the long-term sustainability and scalability of such interventions.

## Key Findings

- Cultural activities like music and dance contribute to physical and mental health benefits
- Active participation in cultural practices enhances overall well-being
- Social determinants significantly influence the efficacy of cultural wellbeing initiatives

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects and scalability challenges of integrating cultural interventions into public health programs?
