## Torrance Test Of Creative Thinking

The Torrance Test of Creative Thinking is a widely recognized tool for assessing creativity. The Puppet-Assisted Play Therapy framework explores the use of this test in conjunction with play therapy techniques to enhance children's creative thinking and conflict resolution skills, emphasizing the importance of encouraging imagination through playful activities. This approach highlights the strengths perspective by focusing on positive aspects of child development rather than deficits. Mindfulness practices are integrated into these sessions to help children become more aware of their thoughts and feelings.

## Key Findings

- The Torrance Test is a valuable tool for measuring creativity in children.
- Puppet-assisted play therapy can effectively enhance creative thinking skills in young participants.
- Mindfulness techniques support the therapeutic benefits of imaginative play.
