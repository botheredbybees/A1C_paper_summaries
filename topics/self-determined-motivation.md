## Self-Determined Motivation in Music Listening

The study highlights the positive correlation between self-determined motivation for music listening and global happiness among Canadian late adolescents. This relationship underscores the importance of intrinsic factors driving engagement with musical activities, indicating that individuals motivated by personal enjoyment and fulfillment report higher levels of well-being. The exploration of subjective well-being further reveals how music can serve as a significant source of pleasure and emotional support, contributing to an individual's overall sense of happiness.

## Key Findings

- Self-determined motivation in engaging with music is associated with increased global happiness.
- Intrinsic factors driving music listening contribute significantly to personal subjective well-being.

## Open Questions

- How does the relationship between self-determined motivation and subjective well-being vary across different cultural contexts?
