## Creative Coping Mechanisms

The concept of using creative coping mechanisms as a response to the challenges posed by seasonal changes is explored across multiple sources. These interventions involve leveraging artistic or imaginative activities to manage emotional and psychological stress associated with shifting seasons, such as winter depression (seasonal affective disorder) or summer anxiety. The sources agree on the efficacy of these strategies in enhancing mental health and well-being during periods of climatic transition, emphasizing their role in mitigating the negative impacts of seasonal variations on mood and behavior.

## Key Findings

- Creative coping mechanisms are effective interventions for managing psychological distress related to seasonal changes
- Seasonal affective disorder can be alleviated through the use of artistic or imaginative activities

## Open Questions

- How do cultural differences influence the effectiveness of creative coping mechanisms in addressing seasonal affective disorder?
