## Dual-eeg Technology in Emotion Processing

The utilization of dual-EEG technology in the context of Guided Imagery and Music (GIM) offers a unique window into the neural dynamics of emotion processing. This technology enables researchers to track simultaneous brain activity between two individuals, revealing how emotional markers are shared or mirrored during emotional tasks such as guided imagery exercises. While the primary focus is on the technological aspect of dual-EEG, its application highlights significant advancements in understanding the interconnectedness and interplay of emotions in social contexts.

## Key Findings

- Dual-EEG technology allows for real-time analysis of brain activity between individuals during emotional tasks.
- The use of GIM in conjunction with dual-EEG reveals unique insights into how emotions are processed and shared.
- Emotional markers can be observed and analyzed through the simultaneous recording of neural dynamics

## Open Questions

- What are the limitations of dual-EEG technology when applied to large groups or complex social interactions?
