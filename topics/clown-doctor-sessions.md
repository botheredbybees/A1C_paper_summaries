## Clown Doctor Sessions

Therapeutic clowning is an intervention involving professional clowns (clown doctors) in clinical settings to provide emotional support and engage patients through humor. Across various sources, this approach has been shown to significantly reduce fear and anxiety among pediatric patients. It also improves communication between healthcare providers and young patients or their families, making medical procedures more comfortable and less stressful. The use of clowning as a therapeutic intervention demonstrates effectiveness in creating positive patient experiences and improving the overall treatment environment.

## Key Findings

- Clown doctor sessions reduce fear and anxiety among pediatric patients
- Improved communication between healthcare professionals and patients through humor
- Enhanced patient experience and comfort during medical procedures
