## Creative Hobbies for Wellbeing

The sources collectively underscore the positive impact of engaging in creative hobbies on mental health and overall wellbeing. Both sources highlight how activities like painting, gardening, or other forms of expression can serve as therapeutic tools during challenging times such as lockdowns. They emphasize psychological resilience and the benefits derived from connecting with nature through these hobbies. However, while both suggest that creativity enhances emotional stability, they do not delve deeply into specific mechanisms by which this happens, leaving room for further research on the physiological aspects of creative engagement.

## Key Findings

- Creative activities positively affect mental health during challenging times such as lockdowns.
- Connecting with nature through hobbies can enhance psychological resilience.
- Engaging in creative pursuits is associated with improved emotional wellbeing.

## Open Questions

- What are the specific physiological mechanisms by which creative hobbies impact mental health?
