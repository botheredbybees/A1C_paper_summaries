## Refugee Integration

The topic of refugee integration is explored through a multidisciplinary lens in 'Arts and Refugees: Multidisciplinary Perspectives (Vol. 2)', emphasizing the importance of diverse approaches to support refugees' social, cultural, and economic inclusion. The book highlights how various sectors such as arts, education, and community development can play pivotal roles in fostering acceptance and belonging among refugee populations. Notably, it underscores the significance of recognizing alternative aesthetic norms that reflect the diverse backgrounds of refugees, which can enhance their sense of identity and connection within new communities.

## Key Findings

- Refugee integration benefits from a multidisciplinary approach involving arts, education, and community development.
- Alternative aesthetic norms are essential for reflecting refugee identities in host societies.
- Cultural expression through arts contributes significantly to social inclusion.

## Open Questions

- What are the most effective strategies for integrating alternative cultural expressions into mainstream societal practices?
