## Sustainable Puppet Making

The synthesis of the concept of sustainable puppet making reveals its dual nature as both an artistic practice and a therapeutic intervention. In therapeutic contexts, it functions as a coping strategy for individuals seeking to express emotions or address mental health challenges through creative means. The sustainability aspect emphasizes the use of environmentally friendly materials and processes, which not only reduces the ecological impact but also aligns with broader goals of long-term viability in both personal well-being and environmental stewardship.

## Key Findings

- Sustainable puppet making serves as a therapeutic intervention for mental health issues.
- The practice incorporates eco-friendly techniques to promote sustainability.
- It provides individuals with coping strategies through creative expression.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of sustainable puppet making on environmental and mental health outcomes?
