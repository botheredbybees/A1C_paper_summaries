## Quality Of Life Model

The Quality of Life Model (QoLM) is a theoretical framework that aims to enhance wellbeing by focusing on personal and community values through creative arts engagement. It underscores the significance of active energy projects, such as those involving older adults, in fostering social integration and psychological resilience. This model integrates concepts like 'community-value-of-experience' which highlights the collective benefits derived from individual participation in activities that enrich communal life. The QoLM also emphasizes the importance of creative arts in promoting holistic wellbeing, encompassing physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual health dimensions.

## Key Findings

- The Quality of Life Model promotes a holistic view of wellbeing through active engagement with community values and creative arts.
- Active energy projects that involve older adults contribute significantly to both individual and communal wellbeing by fostering social inclusion and psychological resilience.

## Open Questions

- How can the effectiveness of the QoLM be measured in diverse cultural contexts where perceptions of quality of life may differ?
