## Youth Theatre Practice in Ireland

The Centre Stage +20 reports highlight the transformative power of youth theatre practice in Ireland. Both sources emphasize the role of real-interaction and active-engagement as key components that contribute to the positive impacts observed among young participants. However, despite these benefits, significant challenges remain, such as underpayment for those involved in the sector, which can undermine long-term sustainability and development opportunities. The reports also underscore the importance of rigorous impact evaluation to document and communicate the value of youth theatre practice effectively.

## Key Findings

- Youth theatre practice fosters real-interaction and active-engagement among participants.
- Underpayment is a critical challenge facing those involved in youth theatre.
- Rigorous impact evaluation enhances understanding of the benefits of youth theatre.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of underpayment on the sustainability of youth theatre practice?
