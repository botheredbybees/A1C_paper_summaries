## Tactile Experience in Creative Expression

The integration of tactile experiences into creative expression is highlighted across sources as a valuable method for emotional processing and personal development. Clay work, specifically, stands out as an intervention that allows individuals to engage deeply with their emotions through the medium's malleable nature. The process of shaping and molding clay offers not only a means to express oneself creatively but also a therapeutic avenue for addressing psychological needs.

## Key Findings

- Tactile experiences such as working with clay serve as an effective method for emotional expression and personal growth.
- The sensory engagement in tactile activities can provide unique opportunities for creative and psychological exploration.
- Crafting with materials like clay may offer a therapeutic benefit by allowing individuals to physically manipulate emotions through tangible objects.
