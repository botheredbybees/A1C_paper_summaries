## Conflict Resolution Through Arts

The integration of arts in conflict resolution emphasizes the role of creative expression and community engagement as essential components for fostering reconciliation and cultural recovery. Sources highlight the 'Make Arts for a Better Life' process, which serves as a framework to facilitate dialogue and healing through artistic communication. This approach not only focuses on individual artistic expression but also on collective action aimed at cultural sustainability and identity recovery. While both sources underscore the importance of community-based interventions in addressing conflict, they diverge slightly in their emphasis: one prioritizes the therapeutic aspect for individuals recovering from conflict-related trauma, while the other highlights the broader impact of arts on community resilience and cultural continuity.

## Key Findings

- Arts-based approaches offer a unique platform for communication and reconciliation in post-conflict settings.
- Community engagement through artistic practices can contribute significantly to cultural sustainability and identity recovery.

## Open Questions
