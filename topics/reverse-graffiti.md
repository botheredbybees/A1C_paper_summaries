## Reverse Graffiti

The concept of reverse graffiti emerges as a unique urban intervention aimed at revitalizing and beautifying declining communities. This form of art contrasts sharply with traditional street art or vandalism, focusing instead on cleaning surfaces to reveal images rather than painting over them. Sources highlight that reverse graffiti can significantly enhance the sense of place within a community by transforming dull or neglected public spaces into vibrant communal assets. Moreover, it is argued that such interventions contribute positively to the community vitality, fostering social cohesion and pride among residents who see their environment transformed through creative means.

## Key Findings

- Reverse graffiti transforms unappealing urban surfaces into positive visual landmarks.
- This intervention fosters a stronger sense of place in communities.
- Enhances community engagement and local pride.
