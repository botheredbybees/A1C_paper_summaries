## Environmental Influences on Mental Health

The provided sources collectively highlight the significant role of environmental factors in shaping mental health. Specifically, they emphasize how ongoing brain development during adolescence is heavily influenced by the environment, affecting cognitive functions and decision-making capacities through creative arts and expression. This theoretical framework underscores the importance of understanding not just individual biological predispositions but also the dynamic interplay between personal experiences and societal context in the development of mental health issues.

## Key Findings

- Environmental factors play a crucial role in adolescent brain development, influencing cognitive functions and decision-making capabilities.
- Creative arts and expressive activities are important environmental influences that can positively impact mental health during critical developmental periods.
