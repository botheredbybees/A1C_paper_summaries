## Emotive Research in Aged Care

The concept of emotive research as explored through the lens of creative arts-based methodologies such as Photovoice, photography and poetry highlights a distinct approach to understanding aging experiences. This method emphasizes self-expression and personal narrative, allowing individuals to convey their lived realities in a deeply emotional and authentic way. Sources underscore how these techniques can empower participants by giving them control over their narratives while also providing rich qualitative data for researchers. However, there is an acknowledged challenge in maintaining objectivity when dealing with highly emotive content.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts-based methodologies such as Photovoice are powerful tools for capturing the lived experiences of older adults.
- These methods empower participants by allowing them to control their narratives and express themselves through visual or literary means.
- The use of creative methods can provide deep insights into emotional aspects of aging, beyond what quantitative data might capture.

## Open Questions

- How do researchers maintain methodological rigor while working with highly personal and emotive material?
