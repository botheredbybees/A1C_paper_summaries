## Societal Health Impact of Museums

Museums play a significant role in enhancing individual and community health and wellbeing through various activities. They serve as spaces for cultural engagement, learning, and social connection, fostering positive mental health outcomes such as reduced stress and improved mood. Moreover, museums can contribute to the societal health impact by promoting intergenerational understanding and inclusivity. However, while there is evidence of these benefits, the extent and mechanisms through which museums achieve their health impacts require further exploration.

## Key Findings

- Museums enhance individual mental health by reducing stress and improving mood
- Cultural engagement in museums supports community wellbeing
- Promoting intergenerational understanding can be a societal health benefit of museum activities

## Open Questions

- What are the specific mechanisms through which museums contribute to improved public health?
