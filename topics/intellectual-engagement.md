## Intellectual Engagement

The literature explores intellectual engagement as a therapeutic intervention for individuals with dementia. Art museum programs serve as innovative platforms that encourage self-expression and enhance the quality of life among participants. These programs not only provide cognitive stimulation but also foster social interaction, thereby addressing multiple aspects of well-being in people living with dementia. Despite the positive outcomes observed, there remains a need to further investigate how such interventions can be scaled up and adapted for diverse cultural contexts.

## Key Findings

- Art museum programs enhance intellectual engagement among individuals with dementia
- Self-expression through art can improve quality of life
- Programs facilitate social interaction

## Open Questions

- How can these interventions be tailored to different cultural backgrounds?
