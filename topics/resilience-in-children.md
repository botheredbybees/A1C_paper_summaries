## Resilience In Children

The provided sources emphasize the role of creative expression and artistic activities in fostering resilience among children. Across studies, art workshops that encourage self-expression are seen as therapeutic interventions that enhance emotional well-being and coping skills. However, there is a tension between the potential benefits highlighted by these interventions and the challenge of scaling such programs to reach larger populations effectively.

## Key Findings

- Creative expression through art enhances resilience in children.
- Art therapy workshops can serve as effective interventions for promoting mental health in youth.

## Open Questions

- How can creative arts interventions be scaled up to benefit a broader population of children, particularly those with limited access?
