## Organizational Audit

The provided sources emphasize the importance of organizational audits in assessing and enhancing practices within organizations, particularly focusing on trauma-informed care. These tools are designed to evaluate the current state of an organization's policies, procedures, and culture through a trauma-informed lens. The synthesis across sources highlights the critical role of such audits in identifying gaps, promoting resilience, and fostering environments that support recovery for individuals affected by trauma.

## Key Findings

- Organizational audits are essential tools for evaluating and improving practices within organizations to better serve those impacted by trauma.
- A comprehensive organizational audit includes assessing policies, procedures, and cultural aspects of an organization through a trauma-informed perspective.
