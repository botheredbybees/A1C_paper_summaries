## Verbal and Non-verbal Communication in Healthcare

The Elderflowers Arts-in-Health Program highlights the importance of both verbal and non-verbal communication in healthcare. The program emphasizes the significance of playful interaction as a mode of engagement, which is crucial for building rapport with elderly patients who might have difficulty expressing themselves verbally due to physical or cognitive limitations. The use of art and play allows participants to communicate their feelings and needs through alternative means, enhancing their emotional well-being and quality of life.

## Key Findings

- Playful interaction serves as a significant tool in facilitating communication with elderly patients who face verbal expression challenges.
- Non-verbal methods such as art and play can be highly effective in healthcare settings for building trust and understanding between caregivers and recipients.

## Open Questions
