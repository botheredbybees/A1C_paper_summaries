## Interactive Play Humor

The integration of humor and playful interaction in therapeutic contexts, particularly through hospital clowning, underscores the importance of play as a fundamental right for children undergoing medical treatment. Interactive play humor not only promotes emotional expression but also aids in building resilience among young patients. Sources highlight how clowns facilitate engagement that can alleviate anxiety and promote positive emotional states, contributing to overall well-being and therapeutic outcomes. However, while there is acknowledgment of the benefits of interactive play humor, gaps remain regarding standardized methodologies for its implementation and measurement of long-term effects on patient resilience.

## Key Findings

- Interactive play humor through hospital clowning promotes emotional expression.
- Play is recognized as a fundamental right in healthcare settings.
- Humor and playful interaction contribute to building resilience among young patients.

## Open Questions

- What are the standardized methodologies for implementing interactive play humor in therapeutic contexts?
