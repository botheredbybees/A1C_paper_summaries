## Organizational Support for Service User Involvement in Dementia Care

The sources emphasize the importance of organizational support in facilitating service user involvement within dementia care settings. Organizational structures and policies play a critical role in enabling personalized strategies that meet individual needs, thereby enhancing patient-centered care approaches. However, there is an evident gap regarding how to effectively implement such supportive frameworks across diverse healthcare environments.

- Effective policies and structures are needed to promote personalized care
