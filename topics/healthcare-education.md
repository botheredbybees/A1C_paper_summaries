## Healthcare Education and Emotional Skills

The integration of emotional engagement strategies in healthcare education highlights the growing recognition of empathy's role in therapeutic relationships. Source 1 emphasizes expanding clinical empathy through an activist perspective, underlining the importance of understanding patients' social contexts to foster genuine emotional connections. In contrast, Source 2 introduces a cross-disciplinary pedagogical framework that utilizes applied theatre and puppetry to enhance healthcare professionals' emotional resilience and active listening skills. Both sources underscore the necessity for healthcare education methodologies that transcend traditional didactic approaches by incorporating experiential learning techniques aimed at developing empathy and emotional intelligence.

## Key Findings

- Healthcare education benefits from integrating methods that promote emotional engagement with patients
- Cross-disciplinary frameworks can effectively enhance healthcare professionals' emotional skills through innovative pedagogical techniques

## Open Questions

- How can cross-disciplinary educational approaches be scaled and implemented in diverse healthcare settings?
