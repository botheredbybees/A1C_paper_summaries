## Holistic Care

The concept of holistic care is a key focus in both sources, emphasizing the importance of non-medical and creative health approaches to mental well-being across different life stages. Holistic care integrates various aspects of an individual's needs, including psychological, physical, social, and spiritual dimensions, beyond traditional medical interventions. Both texts highlight the significance of person-centered care that respects individuality and personal circumstances, fostering a sense of empowerment and self-determination among patients. The sources also underscore the role of policy in supporting holistic practices through creative health initiatives, indicating a growing recognition of these approaches' value.

## Key Findings

- Holistic care integrates multiple dimensions of an individual's well-being beyond traditional medical treatments.
- Person-centered care is integral to delivering holistic interventions that respect individual circumstances and needs.
- Policies are increasingly recognizing the importance of integrating creative health strategies into healthcare frameworks.
