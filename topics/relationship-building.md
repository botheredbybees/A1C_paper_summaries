## Relationship-Building in Ethnomusicology

The practice of ethnomusicology focuses on building meaningful relationships with communities to understand their music and its cultural context. This approach emphasizes ethical engagement, ensuring that interactions are respectful and beneficial for the community. The key concept of relationship-building highlights how trust and mutual understanding foster collaborative projects that aim to benefit society through arts initiatives.

## Key Findings

- Ethnomusicology prioritizes building trusting relationships with communities as a foundation for research and practice.
- The ethical engagement aspect in ethnomusicology is crucial, ensuring respect and equitable partnership between researchers and participants.
- Arts-for-social-benefit projects are more effective when they stem from strong community relationships.

## Open Questions

- How can the principles of relationship-building be effectively applied to ensure long-term social impact through arts initiatives?
