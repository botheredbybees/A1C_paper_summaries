## Emotional Healing Through Creativity

The concept of using creativity as a form of emotional healing is explored across various sources. Central to this idea is the therapeutic function of art in facilitating personal expression and emotional processing (Source 1). Sources highlight the collaborative teaching approaches that support individuals in harnessing their creative abilities for self-reflection and healing. Despite these affirmations, there remains a need for further research into how specific types of creative activities impact different populations.

## Key Findings

- Creativity facilitates emotional expression and healing
- Collaborative teaching methods enhance therapeutic benefits

## Open Questions

- How do specific creative activities influence diverse populations' emotional health?
