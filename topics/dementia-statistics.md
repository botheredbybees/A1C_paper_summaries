## Dementia Statistics

The provided sources highlight the increasing prevalence and significant impact of dementia in populations. Both sources emphasize that Alzheimer's disease is a leading cause within this broader category. Prevalence rates are noted to be rising, partly due to demographic changes such as population aging. The economic burden associated with caring for those affected by dementia is highlighted, indicating a pressing need for effective interventions and support systems.

## Key Findings

- Alzheimer's disease accounts for a significant proportion of all cases of dementia.
- The prevalence rates of dementia are rising due to demographic changes such as population aging.
- There is a substantial economic burden associated with providing care to individuals affected by dementia.
