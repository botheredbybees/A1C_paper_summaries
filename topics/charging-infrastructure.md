## Electric Vehicle Charging Infrastructure

The adoption of electric vehicles (EVs) in Australia is significantly influenced by the availability and accessibility of charging infrastructure. Drayton highlights that while there are government incentives to encourage EV purchases, these efforts are less effective without a robust network of public charging stations. The article underscores the importance of investment in charging infrastructure as a critical enabler for widespread EV adoption. It also suggests that improvements in residential access to chargers could further drive EV uptake.

## Key Findings

- Electric vehicle adoption is hindered by insufficient charging infrastructure.
- Government incentives are crucial but not sufficient without supportive charging networks.
- Improving residential charging accessibility can enhance EV market penetration.

## Open Questions
