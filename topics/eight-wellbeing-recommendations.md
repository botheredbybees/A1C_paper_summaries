## Eight Wellbeing Recommendations

The topic of Eight Wellbeing Recommendations centers around a comprehensive approach to enhancing mental and emotional health through creative and integrative practices. This intervention proposes an innovative framework for mental health care, emphasizing the role of creativity in promoting wellbeing and addressing psychological distress. Across various sources, there is agreement on the importance of incorporating arts-based activities and holistic strategies into therapeutic interventions. However, while the recommendations advocate for a personalized approach to mental health treatment, there remains a gap regarding practical implementation guidelines for practitioners aiming to integrate these creative methods effectively.

## Key Findings

- The Eight Wellbeing Recommendations emphasize the integration of creativity in mental health care practices.
- There is agreement on the benefits of arts-based activities and holistic strategies for improving mental wellbeing.
- A personalized approach to incorporating creative interventions is recommended.
