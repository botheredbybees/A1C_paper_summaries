## Self-Reflection And Introspection Through Photography

The provided source highlights the use of photography as a medium for self-reflection and introspection. It outlines how engaging with this creative activity can facilitate emotional expression, which is beneficial for individuals dealing with depression and trauma. The therapeutic benefits of photography are attributed to its ability to encourage users to explore their emotions and experiences visually, aiding in processing traumatic events and fostering personal growth. Despite the singular focus on photography as an intervention method, there is a strong agreement among studies regarding the role of self-reflection and introspection in psychological healing.

## Key Findings

- Photography can serve as a therapeutic tool for emotional expression and trauma processing.
- Self-reflection through creative activities like photography aids individuals in navigating mental health challenges such as depression.
