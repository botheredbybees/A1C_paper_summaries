## Therapeutic Contexts in Creative Arts

The overview of FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing focuses on the role of creative arts as a therapeutic intervention, particularly in the context of mental wellbeing. The course highlights the use of sustainable puppet making as one such method to engage participants in meaningful activities that promote healing and personal growth. Coping strategies are discussed within these therapeutic contexts, suggesting that engaging with creative arts can serve as effective mechanisms for individuals to manage stress and emotional challenges.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts interventions like sustainable puppet making have a positive impact on mental wellbeing by providing coping strategies.
- Therapeutic contexts in creative arts help participants engage in meaningful activities which aid in healing and personal development.

## Open Questions

- How can the efficacy of different therapeutic contexts within creative arts be measured compared to traditional forms of therapy?
