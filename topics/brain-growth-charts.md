## Brain Growth Charts

The exploration of how music impacts a child's brain development hinges on the application and interpretation of brain growth charts. These charts serve as critical tools in tracking neurodevelopmental milestones, allowing researchers to pinpoint areas where auditory training or rhythmic exercises may influence neural pathways differently than expected baseline trajectories. Iversen highlights the significance of these methodologies by discussing how specific types of music training, particularly rhythmic activities, can lead to deviations from standard brain development patterns, suggesting that such interventions are measurable through these growth charts.

## Key Findings

- Brain growth charts provide a framework for tracking changes in neurodevelopmental milestones induced by music or rhythm-based activities.
- Rhythmic training exercises show promise in altering developmental trajectories as measured by brain growth chart metrics.

## Open Questions

- How do variations in individual genetic makeup and environmental factors impact the interpretation of results derived from brain growth charts?
