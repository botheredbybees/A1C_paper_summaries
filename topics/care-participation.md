## Care Participation in Collaborative Performance

The concept of care participation is highlighted across sources as a critical aspect of fostering inclusivity and collaboration within performance arts. The 'Theatre of Wandering: Rehearsal and Devising Notes [Part 1]' underscores the importance of care participation in creating space for underrepresented voices to be heard through collaborative performances. This approach not only enhances the quality and richness of the resulting artistic output but also serves as a powerful method for social change by amplifying marginalized perspectives. However, while there is agreement on the value of such practices, questions remain regarding how to sustain these efforts beyond initial projects or collaborations.

## Key Findings

- Care participation is essential in collaborative performance arts.
- It helps amplify underrepresented voices within theatre and other performing arts.
- Theatre of Wandering exemplifies care participation through its rehearsal and devising processes.

## Open Questions

- How can the principles of care participation be sustained in long-term projects or across different artistic communities?
