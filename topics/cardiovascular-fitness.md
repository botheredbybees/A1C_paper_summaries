## Cardiovascular Fitness

The sources collectively highlight the multifaceted impact of dance interventions on health and well-being. Particularly focusing on children and young people, these studies emphasize the enhancement in cardiovascular fitness through regular engagement with recreational dances. This physical activity is also associated with positive outcomes such as improved bone health and body image. Moreover, beyond physical benefits, there is evidence suggesting that dance-based activities contribute to reduced anxiety levels among participants. While the findings are generally supportive of dance interventions for improving various aspects of health, inconsistencies remain regarding their long-term efficacy and broader applicability across different demographic groups.

## Key Findings

- Dance interventions improve cardiovascular fitness in children and young people.
- Regular engagement with recreational dances enhances bone health and body image.
- Reduction in anxiety levels is observed among participants engaging in dance-based activities.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects of dance interventions on various aspects of health?
