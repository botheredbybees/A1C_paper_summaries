## Arts and Disability

The integration of arts and disability practices seeks to promote inclusion and challenge negative attitudes towards individuals with disabilities within the arts community. Sources highlight issues such as underrepresentation, barriers to participation, accessibility, and representation in the Australian context. While both sources emphasize the importance of fostering a supportive environment for artists with disabilities, they differ slightly in their focus; one delves into recreational participation and the challenges faced by these artists, while the other explores pathways and support systems that can improve their visibility and opportunities within the arts industry.

## Key Findings

- Artists with disabilities face significant underrepresentation and barriers to full participation in the arts.
- Accessibility is a crucial component for ensuring equitable access and representation of disabled individuals in the arts.

## Open Questions

- What specific strategies can be employed to enhance accessibility and support systems for artists with disabilities?
