## Public Health Impact of Dementia

The provided sources highlight significant public health concerns related to dementia and its impact on aging populations worldwide. Source 1 outlines the global prevalence of dementia using data from the Cognitive Function and Ageing Study, emphasizing the importance of understanding demographic changes in relation to cognitive decline. Similarly, Source 2 provides a detailed overview of Alzheimer's disease, noting that it represents a substantial economic burden due to rising healthcare costs and increased longevity. Both sources underscore the critical need for effective prevention strategies and interventions aimed at mitigating the adverse effects of dementia on individuals, families, and societies.

## Key Findings

- Dementia is increasingly prevalent in aging populations worldwide.
- Alzheimer's disease constitutes a significant economic burden due to rising healthcare costs.
- Prevention strategies are crucial for reducing the impact of cognitive decline.
