## Sense Of Connection

The literature emphasizes the role of arts engagement programs in fostering a sense of connection among individuals. These programs are seen as effective interventions for enhancing community well-being and creating liveable environments by providing opportunities for in-the-moment pleasure and social interaction. However, there is an undercurrent of tension regarding how these transient moments of joy translate into long-term benefits for mental health.

## Key Findings

- Arts engagement programs effectively promote a sense of connection
- In-the-moment pleasure derived from arts activities contributes to immediate well-being

## Open Questions

- How do short-term positive effects from arts engagement translate into sustained psychological benefits?
