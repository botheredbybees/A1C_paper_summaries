## Prevalence and Incidence Data

The National Dementia Data Improvement Plan (NDIP) for 2023-2034 emphasizes the importance of accurate prevalence and incidence data in monitoring dementia across national levels. The NDIP highlights the necessity for consistent, reliable, and comprehensive data collection to better understand the scope and trends of dementia within populations. This underscores a methodological approach to gathering and analyzing health data that is critical for public health interventions and policy-making.

## Key Findings

- Accurate prevalence and incidence data are crucial for effective national monitoring of diseases such as dementia.
- The need exists for improved methodologies in collecting, standardizing, and sharing health data across different regions and populations.
