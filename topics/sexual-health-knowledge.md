## Sexual Health Knowledge Among Youth

The Sharing Stories youth theatre program utilizes an innovative approach to enhance sexual health knowledge among multicultural adolescents. This interactive theatre method not only educates but also engages participants from diverse backgrounds, fostering a more inclusive environment for learning sensitive topics like sex education. Across various evaluations, the program demonstrates significant improvements in young people's understanding of safe practices and their ability to communicate about sexuality confidently. However, there is a need to address potential cultural barriers that may impede full participation or comprehension by all demographic groups within multicultural settings.

## Key Findings

- Interactive theatre programs effectively improve sexual health knowledge among youth from diverse backgrounds.
- Engagement in participatory activities can boost communication skills regarding sexual health issues.
- The Sharing Stories program shows potential for reducing cultural barriers to sex education.

## Open Questions

- How can interactive theatre methods be adapted to overcome specific cultural barriers within multicultural groups?
