## Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder

The provided sources offer insights into OCD within different therapeutic contexts and populations. Source 1 discusses how individuals with autism experience identity formation challenges exacerbated by conditions like OCD, which complicates their integration within family systems and overall well-being. While these insights are crucial for understanding the complexities of OCD in specific populations, they highlight a need for more generalized interventions that can be adapted across various cases.

## Key Findings

- Identity formation is complicated for individuals with autism who also experience OCD.
- Family systems theory plays a significant role in managing OCD within families.
- Interventions for OCD should consider the unique challenges faced by people with comorbid conditions like autism.
