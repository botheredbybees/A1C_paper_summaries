## International Migration Studies

The field of International Migration Studies is increasingly recognized as a complex and multidisciplinary area that integrates various academic disciplines such as sociology, anthropology, economics, political science, and cultural studies to understand the dynamics of migration. The source emphasizes the importance of a multi-disciplinary approach in addressing the multifaceted aspects of migration phenomena, including artistic and cultural dimensions. This perspective underscores the need for scholars to transcend traditional disciplinary boundaries to capture the full scope of human mobility's impacts on societies.

## Key Findings

- A comprehensive understanding of international migration requires an interdisciplinary approach that incorporates various academic disciplines.
- Artistic and cultural aspects are integral components in studying international migration, highlighting the importance of considering migrants' contributions beyond demographic data.
- The complexity of migration phenomena necessitates a multi-disciplinary perspective to address its socio-economic, political, and cultural dimensions.
