## Long-Term Health Impacts Of Disasters

The overview provided from FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing - Week 9 discusses the concept of long-term health impacts of disasters with a particular focus on post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) and trauma-informed design. The narrative emphasizes the importance of understanding how disaster events can lead to prolonged mental health issues such as PTSD and highlights the need for therapeutic approaches that are sensitive to trauma. However, the overview does not delve deeply into specific methodologies or interventions beyond mentioning the concept of trauma-informed design.

## Key Findings

- Disaster events have significant long-term impacts on individuals' mental health.
- PTSD is a common long-term effect following traumatic disasters.
- Trauma-informed design is recommended to address the needs of disaster-affected populations.

## Open Questions

- What specific methodologies are effective in addressing PTSD and other long-term mental health issues arising from disasters?
