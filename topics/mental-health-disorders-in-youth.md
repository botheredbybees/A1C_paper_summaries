## Mental Health Disorders In Youth

The integration of arts and culture-based strategies to support the mental well-being of youth is gaining traction globally. These approaches not only aim to address mental health disorders but also elevate the voices of young people by providing them with creative outlets for self-expression and healing. However, there are challenges in ensuring these interventions reach all youth populations equitably and sustainably across diverse cultural contexts.

## Key Findings

- Arts-and-culture-based strategies can effectively support youth mental health.
- Elevating the voices of young people through these strategies is crucial.
- Cultural context plays a significant role in the efficacy of such interventions.

## Open Questions

- How to ensure equitable access and sustainability of arts-based interventions for all youth populations?
