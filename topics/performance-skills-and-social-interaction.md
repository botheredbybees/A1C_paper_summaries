## Performance Skills And Social Interaction

The study of creative arts interventions for children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) highlights the potential of these activities to enhance both performance skills and social interaction. Sources indicate that creative arts, such as music therapy or visual art projects, are beneficial due to their interactive nature and structured environment which supports skill development in a safe space. However, there is variability across studies regarding how interventions are tailored to individual needs and how they impact different aspects of social competence and performance abilities.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts interventions can significantly enhance both the performance skills and social interaction capabilities for children with ASD.
- Tailoring interventions according to individual needs yields better outcomes, suggesting a necessity for personalized therapy approaches.

## Open Questions

- What specific elements of creative arts interventions are most effective in improving social interaction and performance skills?
