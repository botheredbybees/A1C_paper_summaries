## Authenticity Realization

The concept of authenticity realization is explored through the lens of play and its impact on health across different life stages. Source 1 emphasizes the importance of integrating work-life balance to enhance super-aging, a process where individuals actively seek fulfillment in later years by engaging in activities that reflect their true selves (authenticity realization). This source underscores how play can serve as an intervention for older adults to rediscover personal interests and passions, fostering a sense of purpose and well-being. While the focus is primarily on older populations, the underlying principle suggests broader applicability across various life stages.

## Key Findings

- Play serves as a conduit for authenticity realization in later life by aiding individuals in reconnecting with their true selves.
- Work-life balance plays a crucial role in enhancing super-aging, which involves maintaining personal fulfillment and engagement throughout the senior years.

## Open Questions

- How can play be adapted or tailored to support authenticity realization in younger populations?
