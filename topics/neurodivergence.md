## Neurodivergence

The topic of neurodivergence encompasses a wide range of neurological differences, such as autism spectrum disorder and ADHD. The source material emphasizes the importance of Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) in creating accessible digital environments for individuals with neurodiverse traits. WCAG not only enhances accessibility for people with disabilities but also benefits everyone by promoting inclusivity and reducing barriers to information access.

## Key Findings

- Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) significantly enhance digital accessibility.
- Neurodivergence includes a variety of conditions such as autism spectrum disorder and ADHD.
- Inclusive design practices benefit not only those with specific needs but also the broader population.
