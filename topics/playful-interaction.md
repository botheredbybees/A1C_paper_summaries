## Playful Interaction

The Elderflowers Arts-in-Health Program emphasizes the use of playful interaction as a means to enhance communication between participants. The program underscores the importance of both verbal and non-verbal communication in creating an engaging environment that promotes well-being and social connection. Sources indicate that playful interaction can be a powerful intervention for fostering meaningful exchanges, reducing barriers, and increasing participant engagement. However, there is variability in how 'playful interaction' is defined and implemented across different settings, which may affect its efficacy.

## Key Findings

- Playful interaction enhances verbal and non-verbal communication.
- It fosters social connections among participants.
- Variability in implementation can impact the intervention's success.

## Open Questions

- How does variability in defining playful interaction influence its effectiveness?
