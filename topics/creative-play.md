## Creative Play Therapy

Creative play therapy leverages imaginative activities to enhance emotional and psychological well-being. Key techniques involve the use of puppets to encourage character development and therapeutic engagement, allowing clients to express themselves in a non-threatening way. This method emphasizes the importance of posture and body language while using puppets as a tool for communication. The use of creative play also helps therapists navigate vicarious trauma by enabling them to explore challenging emotions through symbolic means rather than directly addressing them verbally. Non-verbal elements like puppetry offer an alternative channel for expression, making it particularly effective in situations where verbal communication is difficult.

## Key Findings

- Creative play therapy uses puppets to foster character development and engagement.
- Posture and body language are crucial when using puppets as therapeutic tools.
- The technique offers a non-verbal means of addressing complex emotional issues.
