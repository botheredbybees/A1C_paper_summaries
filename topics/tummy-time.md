## Tummy Time

The practice of 'tummy time' is a cornerstone intervention in early childhood development, particularly during infancy. It promotes the physical and cognitive growth of infants by encouraging them to spend time on their stomachs, which aids in developing foundational learning abilities such as motor skills and hand-eye coordination. The importance of tummy time lies in its ability to create safe and stimulating environments that enhance an infant's overall developmental progress. However, there is a need for more research to understand the optimal duration and frequency of this practice.

## Key Findings

- Tummy time promotes motor skill development in infants.
- Proper implementation of tummy time requires safe and stimulating environments.
- There are benefits to cognitive development through increased sensory stimulation.

## Open Questions

- What is the optimal frequency and duration of tummy time for maximum developmental benefit?
