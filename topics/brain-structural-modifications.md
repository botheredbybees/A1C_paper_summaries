## Brain Structural Modifications

The provided source discusses the impact of internet use on human cognition, highlighting shifts in cognitive functions such as sustained attention and an increase in multitasking tendencies. The text explores how these behavioral changes may be indicative of brain structural modifications due to prolonged engagement with digital media. However, it also suggests that while there is evidence for changes in cognitive processes, direct evidence linking these changes to specific alterations in brain structure requires further investigation.

## Key Findings

- Internet use correlates with an increase in multitasking behavior among users.
- There is evidence suggesting a decline in sustained attention as a result of internet exposure.
- Behavioral changes related to internet usage may indicate underlying brain structural modifications.

## Open Questions

- What specific neural mechanisms underlie the observed cognitive shifts attributed to internet use?
