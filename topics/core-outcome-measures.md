## Core Outcome Measures

The concept of core outcome measures is central to the evaluation and standardization of interventions in various fields. In youth mental health support, these measures are essential for ensuring that arts-based strategies can be effectively implemented across different cultural contexts. Local cultural practices play a crucial role in determining which outcomes are most relevant and meaningful to assess, thereby influencing the development of core outcome measures. However, integrating diverse cultural perspectives poses challenges in standardizing these measures globally while maintaining their relevance locally.

## Key Findings

- Core outcome measures facilitate the evaluation of arts-based strategies for youth mental health support.
- Local cultural practices influence the relevance and applicability of core outcome measures.
- Standardization of core outcome measures is challenging due to varying local contexts.

## Open Questions

- How can global standards for core outcome measures be developed while respecting local cultural diversity?
