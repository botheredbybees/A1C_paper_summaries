## Chemokine Ligand CX3CL1

The role of chemokine ligand CX3CL1 in regulating brain-derived neurotrophic factor (BDNF) highlights its critical importance in maintaining cognitive functions. Sources indicate that visceral adipose tissue plays a vital role by modulating BDNF through the mechanism involving CX3CL1, underscoring the potential therapeutic implications for interventions targeting this pathway to enhance or preserve neural health.

## Key Findings

- Visceral adipose tissue influences cognitive functions through CX3CL1.
- CX3CL1 regulates brain-derived neurotrophic factor (BDNF).
- Interventions targeting CX3CL1 may benefit cognitive health.
