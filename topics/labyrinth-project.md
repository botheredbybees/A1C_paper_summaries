## The Labyrinth Project in Arts Therapy

The Labyrinth Project is discussed within the context of arts education and its role in promoting emotional wellbeing and social inclusion among young people. Sources emphasize the therapeutic value of creative expression through the use of labyrinths as a medium for self-expression, highlighting how this intervention can serve to alleviate stress and foster personal development.

## Key Findings

- The Labyrinth Project uses creative arts therapies to promote emotional well-being in youth
- Creative interventions like labyrinths facilitate social inclusion by encouraging shared experiences
- Self-expression through labyrinth projects is a key mechanism for fostering psychological resilience

## Open Questions
