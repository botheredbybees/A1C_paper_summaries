## Chronic Disease Management

The topic of chronic disease management is examined through the lens of type 1 diabetes and broader creative approaches to health. Source 1 focuses on a family's journey with type 1 diabetes, highlighting the importance of emotional resilience and spiritual support in managing this lifelong condition. This source underscores the psychological and spiritual aspects of living with chronic diseases, suggesting that holistic care is essential beyond medical treatments. In contrast, Source 2 introduces an executive summary emphasizing creativity as a determinant of health, advocating for integrated care systems that incorporate arts-based interventions to enhance patient outcomes. The creative health review suggests that innovative approaches can significantly impact the well-being and quality of life for individuals dealing with chronic diseases, aligning with the holistic view presented in Source 1 but expanding it to include broader societal and systemic changes.

## Key Findings

- Holistic care incorporating psychological and spiritual support is crucial for effective management of chronic diseases like type 1 diabetes.
- Creative health initiatives can complement traditional medical treatments by improving patient engagement and overall quality of life.
