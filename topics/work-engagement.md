## Work Engagement Through Workplace Choirs

The integration of workplace choirs as a form of intervention has shown promising effects on employee well-being and work engagement. Across multiple studies, participating in choir activities at the workplace not only reduces stress but also enhances resilience among employees. By fostering a sense of community and belonging, these choral groups help to mitigate feelings of burnout and absenteeism. The collective singing experience appears to provide an emotional outlet that complements traditional wellness programs by addressing social needs alongside physical and mental health benefits.

## Key Findings

- Workplace choirs reduce stress among employees.
- Participation in workplace choirs enhances resilience against job-related challenges.
- Employee engagement is positively influenced, leading to reduced absenteeism.

## Open Questions

- How can the integration of singing activities be tailored for different organizational cultures and industries?
