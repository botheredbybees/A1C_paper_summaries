## Policy Development in Arts and Health

The synthesis integrates findings from sources that focus on the theoretical framework of policy development within arts and health sectors. The National Australian Arts and Health Framework exemplifies how evidence-based practices are required for effective policy-making, emphasizing the importance of engaging with various stakeholders to ensure policies are comprehensive and inclusive.

## Key Findings

- Policy development in arts and health benefits from a robust framework that incorporates diverse stakeholder engagement
- Evidence-based approaches are crucial for establishing credible national frameworks in arts and health
- Sector engagement is vital for the success of policy initiatives within arts and health domains
