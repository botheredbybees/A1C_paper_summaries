## Cognitive Activation

The concept of cognitive activation primarily emerges from the interplay between genetic predispositions and environmental factors, as highlighted in 'Music and Medicine'. This interventional approach underscores the role of emotional engagement as a pivotal element in activating cognitive processes. The sources indicate that music can serve as an effective tool for enhancing emotional engagement and subsequently influencing cognitive functions through gene-environment interactions. Despite shared recognition of the importance of these mechanisms, there are uncertainties about the specific pathways through which emotional engagement translates into cognitive benefits.

## Key Findings

- Cognitive activation is facilitated by emotional engagement
- Music plays a crucial role in enhancing both emotional and cognitive processes

## Open Questions

- What are the exact mechanisms through which emotional engagement leads to cognitive enhancement?
