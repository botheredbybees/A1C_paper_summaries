## Population Segmentation Model

The Population Segmentation Model discussed in the source highlights a framework for understanding different segments within society based on their behaviors and attitudes towards arts participation. Originating from the National Arts Participation Survey, this model offers insights into various subgroups within the population that can be targeted for advocacy or engagement strategies in creative fields. Behavioral and attitudinal indices are integral to delineating these segments, aiming to provide tailored approaches to increasing arts accessibility and interest across diverse demographics.

## Key Findings

- Population segmentation models utilize behavioral and attitudinal data to categorize subgroups within a population.
- The National Arts Participation Survey provides foundational data for creating population segmentations in the context of arts engagement.
- Targeted advocacy tools can be developed based on insights from population segmentation models.
