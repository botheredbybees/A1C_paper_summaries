## Layer Blending Techniques

The synthesis explores the method of layer blending in digital image editing as presented through a single source. Layer blending techniques are crucial for combining and merging images to create seamless visual compositions. The technique allows for various blending modes that affect how two layers interact, creating complex effects without altering the original elements. This process is foundational in applications like Photoshop where users can manipulate image components with precision and creativity.
