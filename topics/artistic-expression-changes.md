## Artistic Expression Changes in Dementia

The sources collectively explore the impact of dementia on artistic expression and its potential as a therapeutic intervention. They highlight how brain functions change with dementia, leading to alterations in art production techniques and styles for individuals with neurological disorders. These changes present opportunities for assessing cognitive decline through analysis of artworks produced by patients during art therapy sessions. However, there is variability in the extent of these changes due to differences in disease progression among individuals with dementia.

## Key Findings

- Dementia significantly alters artistic expression.
- Art production can serve as a diagnostic tool for monitoring neurodegeneration.
- Therapeutic interventions using art are beneficial for patients with neurological disorders.

## Open Questions

- How consistent and predictable are changes in artistic expression across different stages of dementia?
