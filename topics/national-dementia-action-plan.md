## National Dementia Action Plan

The National Dementia Action Plan is a comprehensive intervention aimed at improving the quality of life for individuals living with dementia and their caregivers. It includes a set of policy evaluation indicators designed to track progress and measure success in achieving its goals. Sources indicate that the plan encompasses various aspects such as care services, research funding, public awareness, and support for caregivers. However, there is a need for consistent monitoring and updating of these indicators to reflect changes in healthcare needs and advancements in dementia care.

## Key Findings

- The National Dementia Action Plan aims to enhance the quality of life for individuals with dementia and their caregivers through improved services and awareness
- Policy evaluation indicators are critical for assessing the efficacy of the plan's implementation and outcomes
