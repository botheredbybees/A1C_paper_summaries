## Neuronal Health

The study of neuronal health focuses on the impact of antidiabetic drugs in combating Alzheimer's disease. The review highlights that these medications may offer a new therapeutic avenue by addressing tau-pathology and insulin signaling disruption, both critical components affecting neuronal health. A key finding is the potential for antidiabetic drugs to mitigate oxidative stress, which exacerbates neurodegenerative processes. However, while the research suggests promising effects on neuronal health through modulation of metabolic pathways, there remains an underexplored area regarding long-term efficacy and safety profiles in neurological applications.

## Key Findings

- Antidiabetic drugs may ameliorate tau-pathology
- These medications could address insulin signaling disruption
- Potential to reduce oxidative stress

## Open Questions

- Long-term efficacy and safety of antidiabetic drugs for neuronal health
