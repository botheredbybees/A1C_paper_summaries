## Professional Conduct Principles in Arts in Health

The exploration of research ethics within arts-in-health interventions highlights the critical importance of adhering to ethical considerations and ensuring informed consent. Sources emphasize that professional conduct principles are paramount in maintaining integrity, fairness, and respect for participants' autonomy. These practices not only protect individuals but also enhance the credibility and effectiveness of such interventions. However, there is a noted tension between the creative flexibility required by arts-based approaches and the rigidity inherent in traditional ethical frameworks.

## Key Findings

- Ethical considerations are central to arts-in-health interventions
- The informed consent process must be thorough to respect participants' autonomy
- Professional conduct principles uphold integrity and fairness

## Open Questions

- How can traditional ethical guidelines be adapted to accommodate the unique nature of creative practices in health interventions?
