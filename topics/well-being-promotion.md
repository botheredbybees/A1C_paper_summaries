## Well-Being Promotion

The Happy Museum Project principles highlight the role of museums in promoting well-being and sustainability. These principles emphasize engagement with local communities to address social and ecological challenges. Across sources, a common theme is the importance of community involvement and sustainability for effective well-being promotion interventions. However, there is less detail on how these principles are implemented in diverse cultural contexts.

## Key Findings

- Community engagement is crucial for promoting well-being.
- Sustainability is integral to long-term well-being initiatives.
- Museums play a unique role in fostering community well-being and environmental sustainability.

## Open Questions

- How can the Happy Museum Project principles be adapted for different cultural contexts?
