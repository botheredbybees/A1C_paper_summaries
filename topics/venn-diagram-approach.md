## Venn Diagram Approach In Arts In Health

The concept of a Venn diagram approach is explored within the context of Arts in Health as part of the Arts in Health Diamond and continua model. This theoretical framework aims to illustrate overlapping areas between arts, health, and well-being dimensions, allowing for an intersectional understanding where benefits are synergized through collaborative work across different disciplines. Key themes include the visual representation of relationships between various elements contributing to holistic health improvement strategies.

## Key Findings

- The Venn diagram approach facilitates a multi-dimensional analysis of arts integration within healthcare settings.
- It highlights intersecting areas among arts, health, and well-being domains, indicating potential for enhanced therapeutic outcomes.
