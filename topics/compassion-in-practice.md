## Compassion-in-practice

The integration of creativity into health professional education is discussed as a method to foster compassion in practice. The emphasis on non-normative ethics and values within the creative curriculum aims to cultivate an environment where healthcare providers can develop more empathetic approaches towards their patients. This approach challenges traditional medical education, which often focuses on technical skills at the expense of emotional intelligence and patient-centered care.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts in health professional education helps foster compassion in practice
- Non-normative ethics and values play a crucial role in promoting empathy among healthcare providers

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of integrating creative arts into medical training on patient outcomes?
