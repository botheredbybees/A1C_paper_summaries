## Brightness Adjustment

The concept of brightness adjustment is explored within the context of enhancing digital images. According to the Week 8 Web Lecture on Editing Your Images, adjusting an image's brightness is a crucial step in achieving high-quality visual output. This process involves changing how light or dark an image appears, often as part of broader adjustments that include contrast enhancement and low-light capture improvements. While the lecture focuses primarily on practical aspects like software tools and techniques used for enhancing images through brightness adjustment, it highlights the importance of this intervention in digital photography and graphic design. The sources collectively emphasize the technical implementation and visual impact of brightness adjustment in image editing.

- Brightness adjustment is a critical technique in digital image editing.
- Contrast enhancement often accompanies brightness adjustments to improve overall image quality.
