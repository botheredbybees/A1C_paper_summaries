## Research Protocols

Research protocols are essential documents in the field of arts-in-health and other disciplines that guide the conduct of research. They provide a structured framework for researchers to outline their rationale, objectives, methods, and ethical considerations. Both sources emphasize the importance of having a clear rationale to justify the proposed study, ensuring its relevance and necessity. Additionally, both texts highlight the significance of obtaining ethical approval from an ethics committee as part of the protocol development process. This ensures that all aspects of the research adhere to ethical standards and guidelines.

## Key Findings

- Research protocols are crucial for guiding the conduct of scientific studies in arts-in-health and other disciplines
- A clear rationale is necessary to justify a study's relevance and necessity
- Ethical approval from an ethics committee is essential before conducting research
