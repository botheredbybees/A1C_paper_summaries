## Sensory Experiences in Mental Health

The role of sensory experiences as a therapeutic intervention is highlighted across both sources. Neuroaesthetic principles are discussed in relation to health and well-being, indicating the importance of sensory engagement for psychological benefits. Source 2 emphasizes self-regulation and brain development through sensory experiences, aligning with the therapeutic use of these stimuli. However, there's a gap in specifying how different types of sensory stimulation can be tailored to individual needs and developmental stages.

## Key Findings

- Sensory experiences are integral to mental health interventions.
- Neuroaesthetic principles underpin the therapeutic value of sensory engagement.
- Self-regulation and brain development benefit from sensory-stimulating activities.

## Open Questions

- How can sensory experiences be tailored for different developmental stages and individual needs?
