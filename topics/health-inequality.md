## Health Inequality

The sources discuss the multifaceted issue of health inequality through various lenses. A significant focus is on the role of culture and arts in public health initiatives aimed at addressing social determinants of health such as obesity, which disproportionately affect marginalized populations. Contributions from cultural and artistic interventions are seen as essential in combating these disparities by providing accessible and engaging methods to educate communities about healthy lifestyles and address structural barriers to equitable healthcare access.

## Key Findings

- Cultural and arts-based public health initiatives can mitigate the impacts of obesity and related health inequalities.
- Marginalized populations face significant barriers to accessing quality health care, exacerbating existing health disparities.

## Open Questions

- What are the most effective cultural and artistic approaches for addressing specific aspects of health inequality such as obesity?
