## Submission Process

The sources collectively discuss the critical reflection process and associated hurdles faced in academic settings. The primary focus is on preparing students to navigate the submission requirements for a specific task or assignment. A significant part of this discussion revolves around understanding the procedural intricacies involved, such as knowing when and how tasks should be submitted. One key aspect highlighted is overcoming the challenge posed by these processes, often termed 'hurdle-task', which can vary widely in complexity depending on the institutional requirements. However, there is a noticeable gap in providing detailed guidance on navigating diverse submission systems.

## Key Findings

- Critical reflection involves understanding and engaging with specific procedural hurdles.
- Preparation for submission tasks includes familiarization with institutional requirements.
- Navigating the submission process can be challenging due to varying institutional norms.
