## Autplay Framework

The AutPlay® framework is highlighted as a pivotal therapeutic intervention designed specifically for neurodivergent children and adolescents. This framework emphasizes the importance of play in facilitating growth, empowerment, and affirming practices within therapy sessions. It underscores the role of empathy-in-therapy to build trust and understanding between therapist and client, while also encouraging reflective-practice among therapists to enhance their effectiveness. The AutPlay® approach is notable for its holistic view towards addressing both emotional and behavioral challenges faced by neurodivergent youth, integrating play into therapeutic strategies to make interventions more engaging and accessible.

## Key Findings

- The AutPlay® framework focuses on the use of play as a core component in therapy sessions with neurodivergent children and adolescents.
- Empathy-in-therapy is crucial for building trust and effective communication between therapists and their clients, enhancing therapeutic outcomes.
- Reflective-practice among therapists within the AutPlay® context aims to improve professional competence and tailor interventions to individual needs.
