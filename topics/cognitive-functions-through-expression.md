## Cognitive Functions Through Expression

The integration of expression within therapeutic and educational contexts emerges as a critical approach to enhancing cognitive functions. Across multiple sources, the role of creative arts in promoting mental health is highlighted. Environmental influences are shown to significantly impact mental health outcomes, with expression serving as a conduit for self-understanding and emotional regulation. Adolescents particularly benefit from activities that enhance decision-making capacities through structured creative outlets. While there is consensus on the benefits of expressive interventions, gaps remain in understanding their long-term effects and optimal implementation strategies.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts and expression are effective tools for enhancing mental health among adolescents.
- Environmental factors play a significant role in shaping cognitive functions through exposure to creative activities.
- Expression-based therapies can improve decision-making capacities during adolescence.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of ongoing brain development facilitated by expressive interventions?
