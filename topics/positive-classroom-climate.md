## Positive Classroom Climate

The literature reviewed highlights the use of puppetry as a tool to enhance various aspects of classroom dynamics. Puppetry is noted for its ability to foster effective communication and generation, promoting cooperative learning environments that are conducive to positive classroom climates. It serves as an innovative approach to changing attitudes towards learning among students, encouraging engagement and interaction in educational settings. The integration of puppets into teaching methodologies underscores the potential impact on fostering supportive, inclusive learning spaces.

## Key Findings

- Puppetry enhances communication skills within a classroom setting
- Promotes cooperative learning environments that are conducive to positive climates
- Serves as an innovative approach to changing attitudes towards learning
