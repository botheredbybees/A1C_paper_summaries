## Social Relations Among Refugees

The study of social relations among refugees highlights the importance of music in enhancing their emotional expression and fostering a sense of identity and agency. Across multiple contexts characterized by protracted displacement, music serves as a medium for refugees to articulate personal struggles and aspirations, contributing to both individual and communal well-being. Music facilitates an environment where cultural differences are celebrated, thereby strengthening social bonds within refugee communities. However, the extent to which these benefits translate across varying socio-economic backgrounds remains unclear.

## Key Findings

- Music plays a pivotal role in enhancing emotional expression among refugees.
- The sense of self-identity and agency is bolstered through musical engagement.
- Social relations are strengthened within refugee communities via music.

## Open Questions

- How do varying socio-economic backgrounds affect the impact of music on social relations among refugees?
