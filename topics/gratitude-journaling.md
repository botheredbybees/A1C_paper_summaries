## Gratitude Journaling

The practice of gratitude journaling emerges as a beneficial intervention for mental health according to the provided sources. Sources highlight that regular engagement in expressing and reflecting on moments of gratitude can enhance emotional processing, mindfulness, and overall psychological well-being. However, there is an acknowledgment that while expressive writing such as gratitude journaling has clear benefits, its effectiveness might vary among individuals depending on their personal circumstances and motivations for engaging with it.

## Key Findings

- Gratitude journaling enhances mental health by improving emotional processing.
- Mindfulness practices are closely associated with the act of regular gratitude reflection in journals.
- Expressive writing interventions like gratitude journaling contribute to psychological well-being.

## Open Questions

- How does individual variability impact the effectiveness and utility of gratitude journaling as an intervention?
