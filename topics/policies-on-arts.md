## Policies on Arts

The provided sources emphasize the importance of arts-based interventions in promoting health and wellbeing within communities. They highlight how governmental and non-governmental organizations are increasingly recognizing the value of integrating art into community programs, leading to a growing body of policies that support such initiatives. The integration of arts into public health strategies is seen as an effective way to address various social determinants of health and foster community engagement. However, there is variability in the extent to which different regions or countries have adopted these policies, indicating a need for more comprehensive frameworks that can be universally applied.

## Key Findings

- Arts-based interventions are increasingly recognized as beneficial for public health and wellbeing.
- There is growing evidence supporting the integration of arts into community programs through supportive government policies.
- Policies on arts vary significantly across different regions, suggesting a lack of uniformity in implementation.
