## Narrative Structures in Visual Storytelling

The analysis of narrative structures in visual storytelling underscores the importance of specific elements such as establishing and ending shots. These components are crucial for setting up the context, themes, and emotional tone of a story. Across various sources, there is agreement on the foundational role of these narrative building blocks in creating effective visual narratives. However, there remains an open question regarding how these structural elements can be adapted or innovated upon to cater to diverse storytelling needs and contexts.

## Key Findings

- Establishing shots are critical for setting up a story's context.
- Ending shots help reinforce themes and emotional resonance of the narrative.
- Narrative structures in visual storytelling involve specific techniques like establishing and ending shots.

## Open Questions

- How can traditional narrative structures be innovated to accommodate diverse storytelling styles and contexts?
