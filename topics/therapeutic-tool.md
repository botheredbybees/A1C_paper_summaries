## Laughter Prescription as a Therapeutic Tool

The 'laughter prescription' is presented as a novel therapeutic intervention in lifestyle medicine. It leverages both spontaneous and self-induced laughter to address various health issues. While the efficacy of this tool is highlighted, there are considerations regarding its applicability across different populations and contexts.

## Key Findings

- Laughter can serve as an effective non-pharmacological intervention.
- Both spontaneous and self-induced laughter have therapeutic benefits.
- The 'laughter prescription' is a unique approach in lifestyle medicine.

## Open Questions

- How does the effectiveness of laughter prescriptions vary across different populations?
