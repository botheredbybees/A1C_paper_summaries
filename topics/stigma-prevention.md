## Stigma Prevention

The sources collectively highlight the role of language in promoting recovery and preventing mental health stigma. Recovery-oriented language is emphasized as a critical component in fostering an environment that supports individuals with mental health issues, enabling them to reclaim their identity beyond illness labels. This approach not only aids in the reduction of societal stigma but also enhances personal resilience and empowerment among those affected by mental health conditions.

## Key Findings

- Language choice is a key factor in promoting recovery and reducing stigma
- Recovery-oriented language fosters an empowering environment for individuals with mental health issues
- Effective use of language can enhance personal resilience
