## Holistic Healthcare

The concept of holistic healthcare encompasses various therapeutic interventions that integrate the physical, emotional, and social aspects of health. Two key sources highlight different yet complementary approaches to this philosophy: the use of reminiscence arts in elderly care and the practice of clowning therapy for children undergoing rehabilitation. Both emphasize the importance of engaging individuals in creative activities as a means to enhance their overall well-being. Reminiscence arts focus on utilizing memories, stories, and past experiences through visual and performing arts to promote mental health among seniors. Conversely, clowning therapy introduces humor and playfulness into medical settings to alleviate anxiety and pain in children. Both interventions illustrate the therapeutic potential of arts-based practices within healthcare contexts. However, while reminiscence arts aim for personal reflection and emotional healing, clowning therapy focuses more on creating a joyful environment that distracts from discomfort and illness. Despite these nuances, both approaches share an underlying belief in the power of human interaction and creative expression to improve quality of life.

## Key Findings

- Holistic healthcare interventions can significantly impact mental health through engagement with arts.
- Reminiscence arts promote emotional healing by encouraging reflection on past experiences.
- Clowning therapy uses humor and play to alleviate anxiety and pain in medical settings.

## Open Questions
