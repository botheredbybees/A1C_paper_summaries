## Individual Experience and Motivation in Heritage-Based Health Interventions

The study of heritage-based health interventions highlights the multifaceted benefits of engaging with cultural heritage for improving mental health. These interventions not only foster social interaction but also cater to individual motivations, thereby enhancing overall well-being. The integration of cultural activities into therapeutic settings underscores a personalized approach that resonates with participants' unique experiences and interests. While these initiatives offer promising outcomes, they require careful consideration of the diverse motivational factors among individuals to ensure inclusivity and effectiveness.

## Key Findings

- Heritage-based health interventions contribute significantly to mental health benefits through increased social interaction.
- Individual motivations play a critical role in determining participation and engagement with heritage activities.
- Personalized approaches that align cultural practices with individual needs enhance the therapeutic impact.

## Open Questions

- How can heritage-focused interventions effectively address the diverse motivational factors of different individuals to maximize their mental health benefits?
