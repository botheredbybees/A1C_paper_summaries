The concept of body language is explored across various contexts, including puppet manipulation. Source 1 focuses on the nuances of developing excellence in puppet manipulation, emphasizing the importance of accurate eye contact, head movements, mouth synchronization, and emotional expression to convey believable character interactions and narratives through puppets' body language.
