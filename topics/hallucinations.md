## Creative Puppet Therapy

The synthesis of the provided sources highlights Creative Puppet Therapy as an innovative intervention aimed at reducing hallucinations in patients diagnosed with schizophrenia. This therapy employs creative and engaging methods through puppetry to interact with the auditory and visual delusions experienced by individuals, fostering a therapeutic environment where these phenomena are addressed and managed. Preliminary findings suggest that this approach is effective in diminishing the frequency and intensity of hallucinations.

## Key Findings

- Creative Puppet Therapy is an emerging intervention for managing hallucinations associated with schizophrenia.
- Preliminary data indicates significant reduction in hallucination severity following the application of Creative Puppet Therapy.
