## Youth Theatre in Ireland

Centre Stage +20, a report on youth theatre in Ireland, highlights the importance of real interaction and active engagement within this intervention. The report identifies several challenges such as underpayment issues that hinder the sustainability and growth of youth theatre programs. Despite these obstacles, there is widespread recognition of the positive impact of youth theatre on personal development, social skills, and community involvement.

## Key Findings

- Youth theatre in Ireland fosters real interaction and active engagement among young participants.
- Underpayment challenges threaten the sustainability of youth theatre programs.
- The intervention positively impacts the personal and social development of its participants.

## Open Questions

- How can underpayment issues be addressed to ensure the long-term viability of youth theatre in Ireland?
