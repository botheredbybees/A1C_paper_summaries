## Equitable Access To Resources

The sources collectively underscore the critical role of equitable access to resources in mitigating health inequalities. Social determinants of health are highlighted as a foundational concept influencing individual and community health outcomes. Creative health interventions offer promising pathways to address these disparities by providing holistic approaches that integrate social, environmental, and economic factors. However, there is a tension between the theoretical benefits of such interventions and their practical implementation challenges in diverse contexts.

## Key Findings

- Social determinants of health play a significant role in shaping health inequalities.
- Creative health interventions can offer innovative solutions to address these inequalities.
- Equitable access to resources is crucial for effective intervention strategies.

## Open Questions

- How can creative health interventions be effectively scaled and adapted across diverse contexts?
