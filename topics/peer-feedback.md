## Peer Feedback

The integration of peer feedback in various educational and professional settings highlights its multifaceted benefits and challenges. Source one discusses the application of peer feedback within a photo essay context, emphasizing the importance of caption editing and journaling as means to enhance critical thinking and reflection skills among participants. While these sources do not directly align in subject matter—ranging from visual arts to writing—they collectively underscore the versatility and effectiveness of peer feedback across different domains. However, they also reveal inconsistencies in how peer feedback is implemented, with varying degrees of success depending on the context and execution.
