## Self-Expression in Dementia Care

The exploration of self-expression in dementia care through arts-based interventions highlights the significant role such activities play in non-verbal communication and enhancing quality of life for individuals with dementia. Art therapy models underscore the importance of allowing patients to express themselves creatively, which can be particularly beneficial when verbal communication becomes challenging due to cognitive decline. Sources emphasize that art-focused therapies provide a medium for emotional expression, fostering a sense of self-identity beyond the limitations imposed by the disease.

## Key Findings

- Art therapy models are effective in facilitating non-verbal communication among individuals with dementia.
- Creative activities help maintain and enhance the quality of life for those affected by dementia.
- Self-expression through art supports emotional well-being and helps preserve a sense of identity.
