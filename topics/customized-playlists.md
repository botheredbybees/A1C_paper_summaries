## Customized Playlists

The use of customized playlists emerges as a significant intervention in enhancing the quality of life for individuals with dementia. Sources highlight the therapeutic benefits of music tailored to individual preferences, which can improve mood and emotional well-being. The pilot study suggests that personalized playlists can facilitate social interaction and provide comfort by evoking positive memories and emotions linked to specific songs. However, while there is agreement on the potential of customized playlists to enhance life quality for dementia patients, questions remain about their broader applicability and long-term effectiveness in managing symptoms.

## Key Findings

- Customized playlists can improve emotional well-being among individuals with dementia.
- Personalized music interventions facilitate social interaction and evoke positive memories.
- Tailoring music to individual preferences enhances the therapeutic benefits of musical therapy

## Open Questions

- How does the long-term use of customized playlists affect cognitive decline in persons with dementia?
