## Integrated Care Model

The sources highlight the importance of an integrated care model in enhancing patient-centeredness and holistic support. Personalized care, as mentioned in Source 1, underscores the need for individualized treatment approaches that consider the unique needs of patients, particularly at the end-of-life stage. Creative arts therapy is identified as a valuable component within this model, providing emotional and psychological support to both patients and their families during bereavement. Furthermore, advance care planning emerges as a crucial element in facilitating seamless transitions through various stages of end-of-life care.

## Key Findings

- Personalized care is essential for effective integrated care models.
- Creative arts therapy contributes significantly to the emotional well-being of patients and their families during end-of-life care and bereavement.
- Advance care planning facilitates smoother transitions in end-of-life care.
