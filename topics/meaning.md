## Meaning in Music Experiences

The study of university students' experiences with music highlights the interplay between pleasure, engagement, and meaning. Sources converge on the idea that meaningful music experiences are deeply personal, often tied to emotional connections and self-expression. However, while pleasure is a common motivator for engaging with music, its role in creating a sense of meaning varies. There is an emphasis on how active participation in music can lead to more profound meaning than passive consumption alone.

## Key Findings

- Music experiences are enriched when they involve both pleasure and engagement.
- Meaningful experiences often stem from emotional connections formed through music.
- Active engagement with music facilitates a deeper sense of personal meaning.

## Open Questions

- What role does active versus passive music engagement play in the creation of meaningful experiences?
