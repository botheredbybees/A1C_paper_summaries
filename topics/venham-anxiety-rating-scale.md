## Venham Anxiety Rating Scale

The Venham Anxiety Rating Scale is a tool used to measure anxiety levels in pediatric patients. It plays a crucial role in the evaluation of therapeutic interventions such as puppet play therapy, which aims to reduce children's anxiety during medical procedures. The scale provides valuable quantitative data that helps healthcare professionals assess and adjust their approaches accordingly. Across various studies, it has been observed that while puppet play therapy can significantly alleviate anxiety (as measured by the Venham scale), there is variability in its effectiveness depending on factors such as the patient's age and previous experiences with medical treatment.

## Key Findings

- The Venham Anxiety Rating Scale is a reliable tool for quantifying pediatric dental anxiety.
- Puppet play therapy can be an effective intervention to reduce anxiety levels among pediatric patients.
- The effectiveness of puppet play therapy varies based on individual patient characteristics.
