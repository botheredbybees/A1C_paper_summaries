## Therapeutic Focus in Inclusive Arts

The concept of a therapeutic focus within the context of inclusive arts is explored through various sources. Dr Brendan Lamb's lecture highlights how the social model of disability plays a crucial role in shaping interventions that are not only rehabilitative but also empowering for individuals with disabilities. The inclusion of arts as a medium in therapy underscores its potential to foster creative expression and enhance quality of life, thereby moving beyond traditional medical models. However, there is variability among sources regarding the precise definition and application of therapeutic focus within inclusive arts practices.

## Key Findings

- Inclusive arts interventions are informed by the social model of disability.
- The arts have a significant role in enhancing the quality of life for individuals with disabilities.
- Therapeutic focus is not consistently defined across different contexts.

## Open Questions

- What specific elements define therapeutic focus within inclusive arts practices?
