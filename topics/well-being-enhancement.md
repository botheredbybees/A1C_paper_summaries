## Well-Being Enhancement

Sources indicate the significant positive impact of arts-based interventions on the mental and emotional well-being of individuals living with dementia. Source 1 highlights how incorporating artistic activities can enhance the quality of life for people with dementia through improved engagement, expression, and social interaction. This aligns with findings from Source 2 which reports a mixed-method study that demonstrates an increase in quality of life, communication skills, and overall well-being among participants who engaged in a visual arts program. Both sources emphasize the importance of engaging individuals living with dementia in meaningful activities to support their mental health and emotional stability.

## Key Findings

- Arts-based interventions enhance the quality of life for people living with dementia
- Engagement in artistic activities can improve communication skills among those with dementia
- Well-being is positively impacted by participation in visual arts programs

## Open Questions

- What specific elements of arts-based interventions are most effective in enhancing well-being for individuals with dementia?
