## Communication Generation through Puppetry

The literature review on puppet as a pedagogical tool highlights the intervention's effectiveness in generating communication among students. The use of puppets facilitates a positive classroom climate, encourages cooperative learning, and can influence attitudes towards subjects or peers. Despite these benefits, there are varying degrees of success depending on how puppets are integrated into lesson plans and by whom they are used, indicating that teacher training and context-specific application may be crucial factors in the intervention's efficacy.

## Key Findings

- Puppetry is an effective tool for fostering a positive classroom climate.
- The use of puppets can enhance cooperative learning among students.
- Attitude change towards subjects or peers can occur through puppet-mediated communication.

## Open Questions

- What are the specific contexts in which puppetry proves most beneficial, and what factors determine its success?
