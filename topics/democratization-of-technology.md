## Democratization Of Technology

The sources highlight the Active Energy project's success in engaging older people with creative thinking through technology, emphasizing democratization and social innovation. Both texts underscore how this initiative fosters intergenerational partnerships and collaboration, facilitating a more inclusive technological landscape where age is not a barrier to participation or creativity. However, while the first source explicitly mentions 'intergenerational partnerships,' the second focuses on broader 'intergenerational collaboration.' This minor distinction suggests a shift from specific pairings between generations toward a wider network of interactions among different age groups.

## Key Findings

- The Active Energy project effectively democratizes technology by involving older adults in creative thinking processes.
- Intergenerational partnerships play a crucial role in enhancing social innovation and inclusivity through technological engagement.
- There is potential for broader intergenerational collaboration beyond specific partnerships to further enhance the impact of technology democratization efforts.

## Open Questions

- How can initiatives like Active Energy be scaled up while maintaining their focus on personalized, meaningful interactions between individuals from different generations?
