## Democratization Of Technology

The concept of democratization of technology is a critical aspect of modern society's efforts to ensure that technological advancements are accessible and beneficial for all individuals regardless of age, socioeconomic status, or cultural background. The Active Energy project exemplifies an intervention aimed at engaging older people in creative thinking through the use of technology, thereby challenging traditional barriers to technological access and usage by this demographic. This initiative not only fosters intergenerational partnerships but also promotes social innovation as it encourages new ways of integrating technology into everyday life for all age groups. Integrating technology with community-building activities can serve as a model for broader societal initiatives that seek to bridge the digital divide.

## Key Findings

- The democratization of technology aims to make technological advancements accessible and beneficial to everyone, including older adults.
- Intergenerational partnerships are crucial in breaking down barriers to technology adoption among older populations.
- Social innovation can result from integrating technology into community-building activities for various age groups.

## Open Questions

- How can the democratization of technology be furthered to ensure that all segments of society, including those historically marginalized or underserved, are able to fully participate in and benefit from technological advancements?
