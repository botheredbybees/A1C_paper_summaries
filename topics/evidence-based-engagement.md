## Evidence-Based Engagement

The concept of evidence-based engagement emphasizes the integration of empirical data and qualitative feedback to inform the design and evaluation of interventions. The Arts for EveryBody: The One Nation/One Project Impact Report highlights the importance of mixed-method research in assessing the impact of cultural programs on community engagement. This approach combines quantitative measures with rich, context-specific insights from participants to develop a comprehensive understanding of program efficacy.

## Key Findings

- Evidence-based engagement utilizes a combination of qualitative and quantitative data to inform intervention design and evaluation.
- Mixed-method research is crucial for capturing the full scope of impact in community-based cultural programs.

## Open Questions

- How can evidence-based engagement methodologies be adapted for diverse cultural contexts?
