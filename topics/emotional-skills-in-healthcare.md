## Emotional Skills in Healthcare

The integration of emotional skill development into healthcare practices presents a nuanced approach to patient care and practitioner well-being. Source 1 emphasizes the importance of fostering emotional literacy and skills within both patients and healthcare providers through cross-disciplinary pedagogical frameworks. This holistic strategy not only enhances the therapeutic relationship but also supports mental health outcomes for all stakeholders involved in healthcare provision.

## Key Findings

- Emotional skill development is crucial in enhancing patient care.
- A cross-disciplinary approach integrates emotional literacy effectively into healthcare practices.
- Healthcare providers' well-being benefits from the inclusion of emotional skills training.
