## Educational Outcomes

The sources indicate that educational outcomes are positively influenced by various interventions, particularly those involving arts participation and community engagement. The National Arts Participation Survey suggests that involvement in digital arts can lead to enhanced learning experiences and improved educational results among participants. However, there is a need for more research into the specific mechanisms through which these benefits accrue and how they might be optimized.

## Key Findings

- Arts participation improves educational outcomes.
- Digital arts enhance engagement with education.
- Community engagement is linked to better academic performance.

## Open Questions

- What are the specific ways digital arts contribute to improved educational outcomes?
