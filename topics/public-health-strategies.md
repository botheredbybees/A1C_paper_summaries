## Arts-Infused Public Health Strategies

Public health strategies that incorporate arts-based interventions show promise in enhancing healthcare enrichment and community well-being. Sources indicate the integration of artistic endeavors into medical settings can provide significant benefits, such as reducing stress and promoting healing through sensory-friendly spaces and interdisciplinary approaches. Both sources highlight how art can be a transformative force in public health by fostering a supportive environment for patients and communities alike. However, there is variability in the implementation strategies, with museums and galleries offering unique spatial practices that go beyond traditional healthcare settings to impact community well-being.

## Key Findings

- Artistic interventions enhance healthcare enrichment through sensory-friendly spaces.
- Interdisciplinary approaches involving arts improve patient outcomes and community health.
- Museums play a critical role in public health by providing spatial designs conducive to health.

## Open Questions

- What are the best practices for integrating artistic elements into diverse public health settings?
