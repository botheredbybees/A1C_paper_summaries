## Puppet Play Therapy

Puppet play therapy emerges as a versatile therapeutic approach that facilitates emotional expression and regulation through the use of puppets. Both sources highlight the technique's efficacy in addressing emotional dysregulation, but each emphasizes different aspects: Source 1 underscores the role of cultural sensitivity and projection-in-therapy via the 'hidden puppet' method to foster deeper emotional exploration and understanding. Conversely, Source 2 focuses on symbolic representation and externalization of emotions as key mechanisms through which clients can safely express internal struggles and develop better coping strategies. Both sources agree that puppet play therapy is a valuable tool for enhancing mental wellbeing by providing children with an alternative way to communicate their feelings.

## Key Findings

- Puppet play therapy effectively aids in emotional regulation among children
- The hidden puppet technique supports deeper exploration of emotions and cultural context
- Externalization through symbolic representation helps externalize internal conflicts

## Open Questions

- How does the efficacy of puppet play therapy vary across different cultural contexts?
