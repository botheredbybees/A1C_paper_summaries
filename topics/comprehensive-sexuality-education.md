## Comprehensive Sexuality Education

Comprehensive sexuality education (CSE) is an intervention aimed at empowering adolescents with knowledge and skills related to sexual and reproductive health. Across the provided sources, CSE is highlighted as a critical component in promoting adolescent health through life-skills development and equitable access to healthcare services. These programs are designed to be holistic, addressing not only biological aspects of sexuality but also social norms, relationships, values, rights, and cultural contexts. The integration of life skills into these curricula aims at enhancing personal responsibility and decision-making abilities among youth, thereby fostering a healthier generation capable of navigating the complexities of modern society. However, there is an ongoing debate regarding the effectiveness of CSE in culturally diverse settings where certain taboos or conservative attitudes may hinder its implementation. Despite this tension, evidence suggests that when properly adapted to local contexts, CSE can significantly contribute to positive health outcomes among adolescents.

## Key Findings

- Comprehensive sexuality education aims to empower youth with knowledge and skills related to sexual and reproductive health
- Life-skills development is an integral part of comprehensive sexuality education programs
- Equitable access to healthcare services complements the objectives of CSE

## Open Questions

- How can comprehensive sexuality education be effectively adapted for implementation in culturally diverse or conservative settings?
