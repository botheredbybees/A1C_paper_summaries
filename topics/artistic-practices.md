## Artistic Practices in Healthcare

The National Arts and Health Framework highlights the integration of artistic practices into healthcare settings as a means to enhance patient well-being and promote recovery. This document underscores the importance of collaborative relationships between artists, healthcare providers, and patients, suggesting that such collaborations can lead to holistic approaches in delivering care. Across various sources, there is agreement on the therapeutic benefits of incorporating art within health interventions, but tensions arise regarding the standardization of these practices and their effectiveness across diverse populations.

## Key Findings

- Artistic practices are increasingly recognized for their potential to improve patient outcomes and enhance quality of life in healthcare settings.
- Collaborative relationships between artists, healthcare professionals, and patients are crucial for effective integration of artistic interventions into therapeutic programs.
- Holistic approaches that include art can offer a more comprehensive model of care compared to traditional medical treatments alone.

## Open Questions

- How can the effectiveness of artistic practices be standardized across different cultural and demographic populations?
