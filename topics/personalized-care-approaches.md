## Personalized Care Approaches

The concept of personalized care approaches is highlighted through the utilization of creativity and non-verbal communication to tailor interventions effectively. Sources emphasize creative engagement as a method for healthcare providers to understand and cater to individual patient needs, transcending traditional verbal methods. This approach supports the notion that every patient's journey is unique, necessitating adaptable strategies. However, while sources underscore the importance of personalized care through creativity, they also reveal gaps in standardizing these approaches due to the subjective nature of creative expression.

## Key Findings

- Personalized care approaches enhance patient engagement and satisfaction by accommodating individual differences.
- Creative methods provide a non-verbal alternative for expressing needs and preferences in healthcare.
- Non-standardized application poses challenges in the widespread adoption of personalized care interventions.

## Open Questions

- What are the best practices for integrating creative expression into standardized healthcare protocols?
