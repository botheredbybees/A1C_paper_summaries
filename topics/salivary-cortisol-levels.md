## Salivary Cortisol Levels

The examination of salivary cortisol levels serves as a crucial biomarker in assessing the physiological impacts of various interventions and conditions on health and wellbeing. Across different contexts, such as cultural activities at work or fine arts for elderly people, these studies indicate that changes in stress hormones like cortisol are reflective of both psychological and physical responses to stimuli. Although not all sources directly discuss salivary cortisol levels, their role is pivotal in understanding the impact of interventions designed to enhance wellbeing. Notably, the variability observed in cortisol levels across different age groups and activities underscores the complexity involved in interpreting physiological data.

## Key Findings

- Salivary cortisol levels serve as a biomarker for assessing stress and health impacts across various populations.
- Cultural activities at work can influence salivary cortisol levels, indicating potential benefits to wellbeing.
- Fine arts interventions may affect cortisol levels differently in elderly people compared to other age groups.
