## Creative Aging

The integration of creative aging activities aims to enhance the mental well-being and quality of life for older adults. Sources highlight that engaging in artistic pursuits can provide a sense of purpose, social connectedness, and cognitive stimulation, which are crucial elements for maintaining mental health as one ages. The concept of creative aging encourages the active involvement of seniors in arts-related programs designed to foster self-expression, creativity, and community engagement. However, there is variability in the types and accessibility of these activities across different communities, which may affect their efficacy in promoting mental well-being among older adults.

## Key Findings

- Creative aging activities can improve mental health by providing social connections and cognitive stimulation for seniors.
- Arts engagement is recognized as a valuable intervention for enhancing self-expression and purposeful living among older populations.
- The accessibility of creative aging programs varies, impacting their reach and effectiveness in different communities.

## Open Questions

- How can the accessibility and inclusivity of creative aging activities be improved to ensure they benefit all senior populations?
