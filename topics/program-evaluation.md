## Program Evaluation in Healthcare Settings

The sources provided emphasize the importance of program evaluation within healthcare settings. The key concept highlighted is the need for thorough evaluation methods to ensure participant welfare and collection safety, particularly when integrating cultural elements such as museum collections into medical environments. These evaluations are crucial for understanding the impact of these programs on patient outcomes and satisfaction, as well as ensuring that cultural artifacts are handled with appropriate care.

## Key Findings

- Program evaluation is essential in healthcare settings to ensure participant welfare.
- Evaluation methods must include considerations for collection safety when integrating museum collections into hospitals.
- The integration of cultural elements into healthcare can enhance patient outcomes and satisfaction.

## Open Questions
