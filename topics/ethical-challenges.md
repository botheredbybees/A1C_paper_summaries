## Ethical Challenges in Arts-Based Health Research

Arts-based health research presents a unique set of ethical issues that traditional medical research may not fully address. Key among these is the maintenance of confidentiality, given the often personal and revealing nature of artistic expressions used in such studies. Another significant concern revolves around ensuring meaningful involvement of participants throughout the process, beyond mere inclusion as subjects but rather active engagement in the creative process and decision-making regarding their data. Sources underscore the need for a robust ethical framework that can accommodate these challenges while maintaining respect for individuals’ rights and well-being.

## Key Findings

- Confidentiality is crucial in arts-based health research due to personal nature of artistic expressions.
- Meaningful involvement of participants beyond mere inclusion is essential.
- Current frameworks often fail to adequately address the unique ethical challenges posed by arts-based practices.

## Open Questions

- How can existing ethical guidelines be adapted or expanded to better suit arts-based health research?
