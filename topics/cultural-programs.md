## Cultural Programs

The evaluation of cultural programs through Arts for EveryBody highlights the importance of evidence-based strategies and mixed-method approaches in assessing their impact on communities. This report underscores how integrating qualitative insights with quantitative data provides a comprehensive understanding of the benefits derived from such initiatives, including social cohesion, community engagement, and personal development.

## Key Findings

- Evidence-based cultural programs contribute to improved social outcomes
- Mixed-method research is essential for capturing full impact of cultural interventions
- Community engagement through arts programs fosters social cohesion

## Open Questions
