## Economic Benefits of Music Engagement for Alzheimer's Patients

The analysis reveals that music engagement in patients with Alzheimer's disease can significantly improve their quality of life. Monetization pathways derived from these activities, such as enhancing participation in daily living activities and reducing the need for professional caregiving services, suggest substantial economic benefits. However, there is a gap between recognizing the potential for monetizing these interventions and effectively implementing scalable models to realize such benefits.

## Key Findings

- Music engagement improves quality of life for Alzheimer's patients.
- Monetization pathways can be derived from enhanced daily living activities and reduced caregiving needs.
- There are economic benefits associated with music therapy for Alzheimer's disease.

## Open Questions

- What scalable models exist to monetize the benefits of music engagement in dementia care?
