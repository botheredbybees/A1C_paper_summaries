## Performance Studies

The field of performance studies encompasses the study of live and recorded performances across various cultural contexts. Sources emphasize the importance of understanding performance as a dynamic social process that reflects and shapes societal values, identities, and power dynamics. Sustainability in music cultures is highlighted as a critical aspect of performance studies, focusing on the preservation and evolution of traditional practices within modern frameworks. Additionally, arts-based community development emerges as an interdisciplinary approach to fostering cultural engagement and economic growth through local initiatives.

## Key Findings

- Performance studies examines live and recorded performances across diverse cultural contexts.
- Sustainability in music cultures is a key theme, emphasizing the balance between tradition and innovation.
- Arts-based community development integrates performance practices with social and economic benefits.

## Open Questions

- How can performance studies effectively bridge theoretical frameworks with practical applications in community settings?
