## Cognitive Skills Improvement

The sources discuss the role of arts engagement as a method for improving cognitive skills and enhancing mental health. While Web Lecture 3 emphasizes the importance of community-based arts programs in promoting both cognitive skills improvement and mental well-being, it highlights that these benefits vary across different demographic groups. The integration of artistic activities into therapeutic practices suggests potential avenues for expanding traditional interventions to address cognitive decline and other neurocognitive issues.

## Key Findings

- Arts engagement can improve cognitive skills.
- Community-based arts programs enhance mental health and cognitive functions.
- Artistic activities are increasingly integrated into therapeutic methods for cognitive improvement.

## Open Questions

- How do demographic factors influence the effectiveness of arts interventions in improving cognitive skills?
