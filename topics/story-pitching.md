## Story Pitching

The process of pitching articles to editors involves understanding the publication's interests and aligning them with the writer’s profile. Sources emphasize the importance of research in identifying suitable publications for submission and tailoring pitches accordingly. Additionally, knowing one's unique selling points as a writer enhances the likelihood of securing publication opportunities.

## Key Findings

- Understanding publication's interests is crucial
- Tailored pitches increase success rates
- Writer profile boosts pitch effectiveness
