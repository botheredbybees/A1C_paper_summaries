## Case Study Analysis

The sources emphasize the use of case studies in analyzing the impacts and processes within therapeutic practices such as photography. Therapeutic photography, specifically, is highlighted for its ability to facilitate emotional resolution through creative expression. Case study analysis offers a detailed insight into individual experiences and outcomes, thereby contributing valuable qualitative data to the broader understanding of therapeutic methods like photography. However, there's an evident reliance on subjective interpretation due to the nature of case studies, which can limit generalizability.

## Key Findings

- Case study analysis provides deep insights into individual therapeutic processes.
- Therapeutic photography aids in emotional resolution through creative expression.
- Qualitative data from case studies enriches understanding of therapeutic methods.

## Open Questions

- How can the subjective nature of case study analysis be addressed to enhance generalizability?
