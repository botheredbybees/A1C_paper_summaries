## Adolescent Health

This synthesis focuses on the diverse health considerations of adolescent populations. Source 1 highlights a dance intervention study aimed at female adolescents, emphasizing improvements in aerobic capacity and self-esteem among participants. Sources 2 and 3 provide broader overviews of adolescent health issues, including mental health disorders, communicable diseases, environmental factors, comprehensive sexuality education, life skills development, and equitable healthcare services. While Source 1 focuses on physical health and psychological well-being through a specific intervention, Sources 2 and 3 underscore the importance of addressing multifaceted health needs that encompass various socio-environmental determinants. The integration of these perspectives reveals the necessity for holistic approaches to adolescent health care that incorporate both targeted interventions and comprehensive educational programs.

## Key Findings

- Dance-based interventions can enhance physical fitness and psychological well-being in female adolescents.
- Adolescents face a range of health challenges including mental health disorders, communicable diseases, and socio-environmental factors.
- Holistic approaches to adolescent health care are essential for addressing diverse needs.

## Open Questions

- How can dance-based interventions be adapted or integrated with comprehensive sexuality education and life skills development programs?
