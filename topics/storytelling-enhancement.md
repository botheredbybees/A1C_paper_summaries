## Storytelling Enhancement

The use of images as writing prompts is highlighted as a tool for enhancing storytelling skills. Across the sources, there is an emphasis on how visual stimuli can act as catalysts to stimulate creativity and aid in memory association, contributing to more vivid and engaging narratives. While there are shared benefits noted across the sources regarding the enhancement of storytelling abilities through creative processes triggered by visuals, there remains a need for further exploration into how different types of images impact specific aspects of narrative creation.

## Key Findings

- Images act as effective writing prompts that enhance creativity in storytelling
- Visual stimuli help with memory association and narrative construction

## Open Questions
