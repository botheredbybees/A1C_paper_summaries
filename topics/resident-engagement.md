## Resident Engagement in Dementia Care

The synthesis of the provided sources emphasizes the importance and impact of engaging nursing home residents with dementia through creative expression interventions such as TimeSlips. These programs enhance resident engagement by providing a platform for self-expression, storytelling, and social interaction. Studies indicate that such initiatives not only benefit residents by reducing isolation and improving quality of life but also positively influence staff attitudes towards caregiving. However, there are tensions regarding the scalability and implementation of these interventions across diverse settings.

## Key Findings

- TimeSlips intervention increases resident engagement
- Enhanced quality of life for dementia patients due to creative expression activities
- Positive impact on caregiver/staff attitudes

## Open Questions

- Scalability and adaptability of TimeSlips in different care environments
