## Arts On Prescription

Arts on Prescription (AoP) is an intervention that seeks to improve mental health and well-being through engagement with arts activities. Both sources highlight the positive impact of AoP on reducing symptoms of anxiety and depression, as well as enhancing overall psychosocial well-being. The observed changes in anxiety, depression, and general well-being suggest a broad improvement in quality of life for participants. However, while both studies emphasize the beneficial aspects of AoP, there is less detail provided about how specific art forms contribute to these outcomes or which subgroups benefit most.

## Key Findings

- Arts on Prescription can reduce symptoms of anxiety and depression among participants.
- Engagement in arts activities through prescription improves overall psychosocial well-being.
- Participants generally report positive changes in their mental health.

## Open Questions

- What specific aspects of different art forms contribute to the observed improvements in psychological outcomes?
