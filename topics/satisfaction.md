## Satisfaction in Healthcare Settings

The provided sources explore the use of medical clowns as an intervention to elevate patient satisfaction and reduce aggressive tendencies in both pediatric and adult hospital settings. Medical clowning is highlighted for its therapeutic benefits, suggesting that it can positively influence patients' emotional well-being and overall experience within healthcare environments. Studies reveal mixed but generally positive outcomes regarding the effectiveness of this approach, indicating a need for further investigation into its long-term impacts and broader applicability across different demographic groups.

## Key Findings

- Medical clowns improve patient satisfaction in hospitals.
- Reduction in aggressive behavior is observed with medical clown interventions.
- Therapeutic benefits extend to both pediatric and adult patients.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects of medical clowning on patient satisfaction?
