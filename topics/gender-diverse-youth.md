## Creative Arts and Therapeutic Programs for Gender-Diverse Youth

The integration of creative arts interventions for gender-diverse youth highlights the multifaceted benefits these programs offer. Both sources underscore the importance of supportive communities in enhancing mental health, self-esteem, and emotional expression among gender-diverse individuals. Through art-based therapeutic practices, participants can explore their identities, gain a sense of belonging, and develop resilience. However, there is a need to further investigate how these interventions vary across different cultural contexts and age groups within the gender-diverse community.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts programs significantly contribute to mental health improvement for gender-diverse youth.
- Supportive communities are crucial for self-esteem enhancement in this demographic.
- Cathartic emotional expression through art can enhance self-efficacy and foster a sense of belonging.

## Open Questions

- How do cultural contexts influence the effectiveness of creative arts interventions for gender-diverse youth?
