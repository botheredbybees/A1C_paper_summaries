## Ongoing Brain Development

The concept of ongoing brain development throughout adolescence and into early adulthood is a focal point across sources. Environmental influences significantly impact mental health, with creative arts and play being particularly beneficial in fostering cognitive functions through expression and enhancing decision-making capacities during this developmental phase. These activities provide a supportive environment for the brain's plasticity, aiding in emotional regulation and social competence development. However, the extent to which these interventions can mitigate negative environmental impacts remains an area of exploration.

## Key Findings

- Ongoing brain development is crucial throughout adolescence.
- Creative arts and play positively influence cognitive functions and decision-making capacities during adolescence.
- Environmental factors significantly affect mental health during brain development stages.

## Open Questions

- What are the specific mechanisms by which creative arts and play enhance ongoing brain development?
