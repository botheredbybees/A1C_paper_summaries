## Arts-Based Therapy

The integration of arts-based therapy across diverse communities highlights its potential as a powerful intervention for health and wellbeing. Source 1 delves into Community Cultural Development (CCD), emphasizing how artistic practices can foster social inclusion and community cohesion by engaging local cultural narratives and traditions. Meanwhile, Source 2 explores an Indigenous methodology grounded in ethical systems, which underscores the importance of decolonization and reclamation of traditional knowledge for Native communities in the U.S., illustrating how arts-based therapy must be culturally resonant to be effective and respectful.

## Key Findings

- Arts-based interventions can enhance social inclusion within community contexts through CCD practices
- Indigenous methodologies require a deep understanding and respect for cultural resilience and traditional knowledge

## Open Questions

- How can arts-based therapy effectively balance the universal application of creative techniques with the specific needs and cultural values of diverse populations?
