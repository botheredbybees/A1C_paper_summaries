## Work-Life Balance

The concept of work-life balance is explored as a critical component for enhancing overall health and well-being across different stages of life. The integration of play into daily routines is presented as an effective strategy to achieve this balance, particularly among the aging population. Authentic realization, or the process by which individuals become more true to themselves, plays a crucial role in maintaining work-life harmony. However, there are tensions between traditional societal expectations and personal desires for balance, suggesting that further research is needed to develop tailored interventions.

## Key Findings

- Play is an important tool for enhancing work-life balance across the lifespan.
- Authentic realization supports individuals in achieving a better work-life balance.
- The aging population benefits from strategies that integrate play into daily life.

## Open Questions

- What are effective methods to challenge societal expectations and support individual needs for work-life balance?
