## Cognitive Processing Therapy

The sources discussed highlight the role of cognitive processing therapy in enhancing the quality of life for individuals with dementia. Both sources emphasize the importance of creative therapies and arts-based interventions as methods to improve mental well-being among this population. They suggest that these approaches can act as effective critical evaluation tools, allowing patients to process their thoughts and emotions constructively. However, while there is agreement on the potential benefits of such therapeutic modalities, there remains a need for further research into how they can be most effectively implemented in clinical settings.

## Key Findings

- Creative therapies are beneficial in improving quality of life for individuals with dementia.
- Arts-based interventions can enhance mental well-being among those affected by cognitive decline.
- Critical evaluation methods are integral to the effectiveness of these therapeutic approaches.

## Open Questions

- How can arts and creative therapy be best integrated into existing clinical practices for dementia care?
