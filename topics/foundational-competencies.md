## Foundational Competencies In Arts In Health

The document 'Advancing Practice in Arts in Health' discusses the development of foundational competencies and certification processes for arts in health practitioners. It outlines a career architecture that seeks to standardize practice through training programs, emphasizing the need for foundational competencies. This approach aims to professionalize the field by offering structured pathways for individuals interested in practicing as arts therapists or artists within healthcare settings.

## Key Findings

- Certification processes are vital for establishing standards and professionalism in arts in health practices
- Foundational competencies serve as a basis for specialized training programs
- Career architecture provides clear progression paths for practitioners
