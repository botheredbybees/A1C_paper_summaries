## Paradigm Of Aging

The concept of a paradigm of aging is explored through various theoretical frameworks, with the notion that how we think about aging profoundly influences our societal and individual experiences. This perspective highlights the importance of shifting from a disease-oriented model to one that embraces holistic views of later life stages, emphasizing participation and agency in older adults' lives. Sources like 'Dementia and Ageing: A Broad Perspective' advocate for participatory action research methodologies as essential tools for understanding and addressing the multifaceted challenges faced by aging populations, reflecting a paradigm shift towards more inclusive and equitable practices.

## Key Findings

- The way society conceptualizes aging influences its attitudes toward older adults.
- There is a need to move from disease-focused models of aging to holistic perspectives that emphasize participation and agency.
- Participatory action research methodologies are vital for advancing understanding and addressing the challenges faced by aging populations.

## Open Questions

- How can we effectively implement participatory action research methods across diverse communities to ensure broad inclusivity in aging paradigms?
