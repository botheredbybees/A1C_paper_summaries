## The Transformative Power of Theatre

The summary highlights the potential of theatre to effect social change and individual transformation, positioning it as an intervention with profound possibilities. However, this is explored in relation to a continuum of applied theatre practices which suggests variability in its effectiveness and application across different contexts.

## Key Findings

- Theatre can serve as a powerful tool for societal change.
- Applied theatre operates on a spectrum, offering various ways to engage communities and individuals.
- Transformation through theatre requires intentional practice within specific frameworks.

## Open Questions

- How does the context in which applied theatre is used affect its transformative power?
