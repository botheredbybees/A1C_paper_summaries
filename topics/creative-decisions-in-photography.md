## Creative Decisions in Photography

The integration of creative decisions within photography emphasizes the importance of manual mode and understanding the exposure triangle (aperture, shutter speed, ISO). Sources highlight how mastering these elements allows photographers to control light and depth of field, influencing both technical quality and artistic expression. There is a consensus on the transformative potential of learning to manipulate these settings manually for enhanced creativity but also tension regarding the accessibility and usability for beginners.

## Key Findings

- Mastering manual mode enhances creative control in photography.
- The exposure triangle (aperture, shutter speed, ISO) are critical elements for achieving desired effects.
- Manual mode can significantly improve technical quality and artistic expression.
