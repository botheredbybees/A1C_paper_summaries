## Social And Emotional Wellbeing Among Aboriginal Youth

The sources collectively highlight the significance of cultural programs in enhancing the social and emotional wellbeing (SEWB) among Aboriginal young people. These initiatives focus on strengthening cultural connections, which foster resilience building and encourage help-seeking behaviors within these communities. While specific methods vary, they share a common goal of integrating traditional practices into modern contexts to address mental health challenges faced by Aboriginal youth.

## Key Findings

- Cultural programs contribute significantly to the social and emotional wellbeing (SEWB) of Aboriginal young people.
- Strengthening cultural connections is vital in building resilience among Aboriginal youth.
- Help-seeking behaviors are positively influenced by the integration of traditional practices.

## Open Questions

- How can the effectiveness of these cultural programs be measured in a way that respects and incorporates indigenous knowledge systems?
