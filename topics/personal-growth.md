## Therapeutic Creative Arts for Mental Health

The case studies of therapeutic creative arts interventions underscore their role in mental health support. These interventions emphasize social bonding, which fosters a sense of community and connection among participants, potentially reducing feelings of isolation and loneliness. Emotional regulation is another key benefit, as engaging with art allows individuals to express and process complex emotions in a non-verbal manner. Personal growth is facilitated through these activities, promoting self-discovery and development. While the effectiveness of creative arts therapy is clear from individual case studies, there are discrepancies regarding which specific aspects contribute most significantly to personal growth.

## Key Findings

- Therapeutic creative arts enhance social bonding among participants.
- Creative arts provide an effective means for emotional regulation in mental health contexts.
- Personal growth can be facilitated through regular engagement with therapeutic creative arts.

## Open Questions

- Which components of therapeutic creative arts contribute most to personal growth?
