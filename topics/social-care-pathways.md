## Social Care Pathways

The topic of social care pathways emphasizes the importance of tailored interventions for children and young people who have experienced trauma. Sources highlight the use of creative therapies as part of trauma-informed care, suggesting that these approaches can lead to better outcomes. However, there is a recognition of the need for more integrated care systems that facilitate smoother transitions between different stages of support, from initial intervention through ongoing therapeutic engagement.

## Key Findings

- Creative therapy interventions are effective in supporting children and young people who have experienced trauma.
- Trauma-informed care should be central to social care pathways.
- There is a need for more seamless integration among different sectors of support.

## Open Questions

- How can creative therapies be better integrated into mainstream social care systems?
