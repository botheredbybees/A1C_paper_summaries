## Multidisciplinary Approaches to Migration Studies

The integration of multidisciplinary approaches in the study of migration and cultural change is highlighted across all sources. These studies emphasize the importance of visual arts, participatory research methods, and broadened definitions of culture beyond traditional linguistic or ethnic markers to encompass migrant experiences more comprehensively. Key concepts such as rehumanization through arts-based practices are proposed as essential for understanding migrants' narratives on their own terms. Visual studies, in particular, offer unique insights into the lived realities of migration that may be less accessible through conventional text-based research methods alone.

## Key Findings

- Multidisciplinary collaboration is crucial for advancing international migration studies
- Visual arts and participatory methods enrich understanding of migrants' experiences
- Broadened cultural definitions are necessary to fully capture the complexities of migration

## Open Questions

- How can visual and participatory research methods be effectively integrated with traditional approaches in migration studies?
