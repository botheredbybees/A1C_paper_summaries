## Trauma-Informed Design

The sources collectively highlight the concept of trauma-informed design as an intervention that integrates creative arts and play to support mental wellbeing in individuals affected by traumatic events such as PTSD and long-term health impacts from disasters. This approach emphasizes understanding the impact of trauma on a person's life, creating safe spaces where they can express themselves through various mediums, including storytelling and adventure playgrounds. Both sources emphasize the importance of providing environments that foster resilience, healing, and empowerment for those recovering from traumatic experiences.

## Key Findings

- Trauma-informed design supports mental health recovery by integrating creative arts and play into therapeutic interventions
- Adventure playgrounds provide safe spaces for individuals to engage in activities that promote emotional expression and healing
- Storytelling is a powerful tool used within trauma-informed design to help individuals process their experiences
