## Nursing Home Care Interventions

The reviewed literature highlights the effectiveness of personalized activities and clown care interventions in enhancing the quality of life for nursing home residents, especially those with cognitive impairments. Activity personalization emphasizes tailoring daily activities to align with individual preferences and past experiences, thereby improving emotional well-being and overall satisfaction among cognitively impaired individuals. Similarly, clown care as an intervention aims to boost emotional well-being through joyful interactions, humor, and creative engagement. Both studies underscore the critical role of tailored approaches in addressing the unique needs of nursing home residents, indicating a shift towards more personalized care strategies that can significantly enhance resident well-being.

## Key Findings

- Personalized activities improve the well-being of cognitively impaired individuals in nursing homes.
- Clown care interventions positively affect emotional and overall quality of life for nursing home residents.
- Tailored approaches to care are essential for enhancing resident satisfaction and health outcomes.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of personalized activities versus clown care on resident well-being in nursing homes?
