## Creative Arts in Healthcare

The integration of creative arts into healthcare is increasingly recognized as a significant approach to enhancing patient care and improving well-being. Sources highlight the role of narrative medicine in fostering connections between literature, medical education, and health humanities. Both sources underscore the importance of engaging patients through various forms of artistic expression to foster healing and personal growth. The concept of narrative medicine serves as a bridge between traditional healthcare practices and creative interventions, encouraging practitioners to engage with patient stories to better understand their experiences and needs. However, there is a notable gap in defining clear methodologies for integrating creative arts into routine medical practice.

## Key Findings

- Narrative medicine bridges literature and medical education through storytelling and reflection
- Creative arts engagement contributes significantly to health humanities
- Patient-centered care can be enhanced through the use of narrative and artistic expression

## Open Questions

- What specific methodologies are effective for integrating creative arts into routine healthcare practice?
