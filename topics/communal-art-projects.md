## Communal Art Projects

Communal art projects have been explored as therapeutic interventions in various aged care settings. The MAC.ART program exemplifies a structured approach to incorporating person-centered care through communal art activities, which engage residents in creating art together. This intervention not only fosters social interaction and emotional well-being but also enhances the quality of life for participants by providing a creative outlet and sense of accomplishment.

## Key Findings

- Communal art projects can serve as effective therapeutic interventions in aged care settings.
- Person-centered care is essential to the success of communal art projects.
- Social interaction and emotional well-being are positively impacted through such activities.

## Open Questions

- How do communal art projects impact participants outside of aged care settings?
