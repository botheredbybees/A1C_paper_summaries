## Creative Arts Therapy

Creative arts therapy encompasses various modalities such as puppet play, dance/movement, and artistic expression to improve psychological well-being. Source 1 highlights the use of creative arts in dementia care for cognitive rehabilitation through artistic expression. Similarly, Source 2 emphasizes the therapeutic utility of movement in Dance/Movement Therapy, focusing on its efficacy as a psychological intervention. In contrast, Source 3 delves into Puppet Play Therapy, underscoring its child-centered approach and solution-focused brief therapy techniques. While these therapies share an overarching commitment to non-verbal expression and experiential learning, their specific methodologies vary considerably.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts therapies offer a range of interventions tailored to different populations and therapeutic goals
- Artistic expression and movement are fundamental components in cognitive rehabilitation and psychological support
- Child-centered approaches focus on solution-focused brief therapy techniques

## Open Questions
