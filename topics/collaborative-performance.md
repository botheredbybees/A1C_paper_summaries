## Collaborative Performance

The concept of collaborative performance emerges as a dynamic practice where multiple individuals engage in the creative process collectively. This approach is highlighted within the context of Theatre of Wandering, emphasizing rehearsal and devising processes that prioritize care-participation and amplify underrepresented voices. The integration of these elements fosters an inclusive environment where each participant's input is valued, leading to richer performances and more authentic storytelling.

## Key Findings

- Collaborative performance prioritizes the involvement of all participants in the creative process.
- Care-participation ensures that every individual feels supported and heard during collaborative efforts.
- Emphasis on underrepresented voices enriches the narrative and artistic output.

## Open Questions

- How can care-participation be effectively implemented to ensure all voices are equally represented?
