## Climate Change Concerns Among Youth

The survey report underscores a significant concern among young people about the impact of climate change on their lives and the future. The findings highlight how environmental issues intertwine with mental health struggles, cost-of-living pressures, and experiences of discrimination. Across sources, there is agreement that youth are deeply worried about the consequences of global warming but often feel powerless to effect meaningful change. Tensions arise from the disconnect between high levels of concern and perceived low efficacy in addressing these challenges.

## Key Findings

- Youth express significant anxiety over climate change's impact on their future lives and well-being.
- Concerns about climate change are closely linked with mental health issues among young people.
- Environmental worries compound existing economic pressures faced by youth.

## Open Questions

- What strategies can effectively empower youth to participate in mitigating climate change despite feelings of helplessness?
