## Elderly Wellbeing Through Creative Arts

The integration of creative arts interventions for elderly wellbeing highlights the importance of participatory activities in enhancing both physical and emotional health. The Active Energy Project demonstrates how such initiatives can significantly improve quality of life through engagement with art, emphasizing community value as a key component. Similarly, case studies like El Sistema and Big Noise illustrate that structured programs aimed at diverse populations can yield substantial benefits for older adults when adapted to their specific needs. These projects underscore the versatility of creative arts in addressing various aspects of elderly wellbeing but also highlight challenges such as sustaining engagement and accessibility.

## Key Findings

- Participatory art initiatives significantly enhance quality of life among the elderly.
- Community value plays a crucial role in the success of these programs, fostering social connections and support networks.
- Creative arts interventions can address both physical and emotional health needs of older adults.

## Open Questions

- What are effective strategies for sustaining long-term participation and engagement in creative arts projects for elderly populations?
