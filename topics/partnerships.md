## Partnerships

The concept of 'partnerships' is explored across various sources as a collaborative approach that emphasizes the importance of involving individuals with lived experience in the development and implementation of services. This practice not only seeks to enhance service design but also ensures that interventions are more relevant and effective by incorporating authentic voices from those who have directly experienced the issues at hand. However, there is variability in how these partnerships are established and maintained, leading to questions about the sustainability and scalability of such collaborative efforts.

## Key Findings

- Partnerships involving individuals with lived experience enhance service design and effectiveness
- Authentic voice is crucial for meaningful partnerships
- Collaborative approaches can lead to more relevant interventions

## Open Questions

- How can the sustainability of partnerships be ensured over time?
