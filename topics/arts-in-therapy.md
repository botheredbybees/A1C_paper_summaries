## Creative Arts in Therapy

The utilization of creative arts as a therapeutic intervention emerges consistently across both sources, indicating its potential efficacy for various populations. In 'FXA303 Creative Arts, Play and Mental Wellbeing Week 10', the focus is on cultural adjustment and mental wellbeing through creative therapy. Conversely, 'Developing Creative Approaches in Youth Justice: A Guide for Youth Justice Professionals' emphasizes the design and delivery of creative arts programs within youth justice contexts, highlighting the importance of integrating participant feedback to enhance service effectiveness. Both sources underscore the adaptability and versatility of creative arts as a therapeutic approach across different settings and populations.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts therapy can be effective for cultural adjustment in diverse groups.
- Participant feedback is crucial for designing tailored creative arts programs.
- Creative arts interventions are adaptable across various social service contexts.

## Open Questions

- How does the integration of participant feedback impact long-term outcomes of creative arts therapies?
