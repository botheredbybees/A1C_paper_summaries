## Institutional Transformation

The Happy Museum Learning Evaluation Report discusses the implementation of wellbeing initiatives within a museum setting as part of an institutional transformation towards cultural sustainability. The report highlights several key themes, including the need for museums to adapt their roles beyond traditional preservation and education functions to incorporate community engagement and social impact measures. There is consensus among sources that successful institutional transformation involves a shift in organizational culture from internal processes to external relationships with local communities and stakeholders. However, there are tensions regarding how these transformations can be achieved without compromising the core missions of cultural institutions.

## Key Findings

- Institutional transformation towards cultural sustainability requires museums to adopt new roles centered on community engagement and social impact.
- Wellbeing initiatives play a crucial role in facilitating institutional change within cultural organizations.

## Open Questions

- How can cultural institutions balance the pursuit of transformative changes with maintaining their core missions and values?
