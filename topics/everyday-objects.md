## Everyday Objects in Memory Care

The utilization of everyday objects as therapeutic tools in memory care interventions emerges consistently across the sources provided. Everyday objects play a critical role in facilitating meaningful conversations and aiding reminiscence therapy, particularly for individuals experiencing cognitive decline or dementia. The iRemember Loan Resource Flyer emphasizes how such objects can serve as touchstones to evoke memories and engage residents in meaningful dialogue. Although the flyer focuses on practical implementation through loanable resources, the broader literature suggests that these tangible items can significantly enhance quality of life by stimulating mental engagement and emotional connections.

## Key Findings

- Everyday objects are crucial for reminiscence therapy.
- They aid in triggering memories and facilitating conversation.
- Loanable resource programs facilitate access to a variety of everyday objects for therapeutic use.
