## Underpayment Challenges in Youth Theatre

The Centre Stage +20 report highlights significant challenges faced by youth theatre practitioners, particularly concerning underpayment. This issue not only affects the financial sustainability of young artists but also impacts their engagement and interaction within the industry. The report underscores that despite high levels of active engagement among young theatre makers, the economic viability remains a critical barrier. There is an evident tension between the passion-driven nature of youth involvement in theatre and the harsh realities of professional underpayment.

## Key Findings

- Youth theatre practitioners face significant financial barriers due to underpayment.
- Active engagement and real interaction remain high despite economic challenges.
- Underpayment affects the long-term sustainability and career prospects for young artists in theatre.

## Open Questions

- How can the youth theatre sector address the issue of underpayment to ensure sustainable careers for its members?
