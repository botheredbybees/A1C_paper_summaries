## Emotional Expression Promotion

Hospital clowning is a unique intervention that leverages the power of humor and play to promote emotional expression in healthcare settings. The key idea here is that interactive play facilitated by clowns can provide children with opportunities for emotional release, resilience building, and overall psychological well-being. While hospital clowning primarily targets pediatric populations, its application suggests broader implications for emotional expression promotion through playful interactions. This approach resonates with the concept of play as a fundamental right, highlighting its therapeutic benefits in fostering emotional health.

## Key Findings

- Hospital clowning promotes emotional expression in children
- Interactive play and humor are central to this intervention
- Resilience building is an observed outcome
