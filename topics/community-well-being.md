## Community Well-being Through Creative Recovery

The Afloat Project highlights the potential of creative recovery and pedagogies of visual literacy to enhance community well-being. This project underscores the importance of innovative educational strategies that integrate artistic expression into therapeutic practices, thereby fostering a sense of collective healing and personal growth among participants. The integration of these methods not only aids in individual mental health but also strengthens communal bonds through shared experiences and collaborative learning.

## Key Findings

- Creative recovery interventions can significantly contribute to community well-being by addressing individual and collective needs through visual literacy education
- Pedagogies that incorporate visual arts offer a unique platform for self-expression and therapeutic engagement, which in turn supports psychological resilience among participants

## Open Questions

- How scalable are such creative recovery projects given the varying levels of artistic resources available across different communities?
