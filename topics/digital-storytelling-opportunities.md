## Digital Storytelling Opportunities

The synthesis of digital storytelling opportunities emphasizes the importance of understanding the audience and their engagement with technology. Across sources, there is a consistent theme that effective digital storytelling requires thorough research on both story concept and target audience. However, there are tensions regarding how to balance traditional journalistic values with new forms of media presentation. While some sources advocate for adapting to emerging digital platforms to enhance reach and impact, others caution against losing the essence of quality journalism in pursuit of technological novelty.

## Key Findings

- Effective digital storytelling requires a deep understanding of both story concept and target audience.
- There is a balance between leveraging new technologies and maintaining journalistic integrity.
- Audience engagement is enhanced by tailoring content to digital platforms.

## Open Questions

- How can journalists maintain high standards of journalism while adapting to digital storytelling trends?
