## Engagement With The Arts

The sources collectively emphasize the positive impacts of engaging in artistic activities among various groups, particularly older adults. Each source highlights how participation in arts programs can lead to enhanced quality of life by fostering social connections and providing a sense of purpose. Despite these commonalities, some discrepancies arise regarding the specific mechanisms through which engagement with art contributes to well-being—whether it be through cognitive stimulation or emotional support. Additionally, while there is agreement on the benefits, questions remain about how to sustain such programs over time and ensure equitable access.

## Key Findings

- Engagement in arts activities improves quality of life among older adults.
- Arts-based interventions foster social connections and provide a sense of purpose.
- Participation in artistic pursuits offers cognitive stimulation alongside emotional support.

## Open Questions

- How can the benefits of engagement with the arts be sustained over long periods?
