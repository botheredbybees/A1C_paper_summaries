## Cultural Asset Index

The Cultural Asset Index is a method employed to measure and understand the collective value of arts and culture within community settings. This index serves as a tool for assessing the presence and impact of social engagement through creative arts interventions, such as storytelling programs. Sources discuss its effectiveness in identifying strengths and resources that communities can leverage for health promotion and well-being. While there is agreement on its potential to enhance communal vitality and resilience, some tensions remain regarding how best to quantify cultural assets quantitatively versus qualitatively.

## Key Findings

- Cultural Asset Index measures the collective value of arts within a community.
- Creative storytelling programs are highlighted as effective social engagement tools.
- The index aids in identifying community resources for health promotion.

## Open Questions

- How to balance quantitative and qualitative approaches in measuring cultural assets.
