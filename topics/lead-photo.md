## Lead Photo

The concept of the 'lead photo' is a critical component in visual storytelling as highlighted across various sources. It serves not only as an introduction but also as a hook to engage viewers with the story's theme or setting, often serving the same function as an establishing shot in traditional narrative structures. According to source 1, the lead photo sets up expectations for what follows and is crucial for capturing viewer interest right from the start. While the sources do not extensively cover differences between lead photos and closing shots, both are essential elements of story structure that guide the audience through visual narratives with precision and emotional resonance.

## Key Findings

- A lead photo introduces a narrative's theme or setting effectively to captivate viewer interest
- The role of a lead photo is comparable to an establishing shot in traditional storytelling
- Both lead photos and closing shots are integral parts of story structure
