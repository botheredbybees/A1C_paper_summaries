## Social Outcomes of Community Arts

The evaluation guide for community arts highlights the multifaceted impact these programs have on societal well-being. It underscores how community arts initiatives can be instrumental in fostering social cohesion and resilience, enhancing individual mental health, and promoting educational attainment among youth. The document emphasizes the importance of continuous improvement to maintain the positive social outcomes achieved through sustained engagement with community arts activities. Additionally, it points out that understanding these impacts is crucial for securing funding and justifying resource allocation towards such programs.

## Key Findings

- Community arts initiatives significantly enhance social cohesion and resilience in communities.
- Engagement in community arts contributes to improved mental health among participants.
- Educational benefits, particularly for youth, are a notable outcome of community arts activities.
