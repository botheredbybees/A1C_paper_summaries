## Lived Experience Narratives in Dementia Awareness

The use of lived experience narratives in dementia awareness initiatives is highlighted through the Sundowner play by KAGE Physical Theatre. This approach aims to raise public understanding and empathy towards those suffering from dementia, leveraging storytelling as an effective method for humanizing complex medical conditions and enhancing societal compassion.

## Key Findings

- Lived experience narratives are utilized to foster greater empathy in audiences regarding dementia patients
- The Sundowner play serves as a powerful medium to educate the public about the realities of living with dementia
- Storytelling through theatre can be an impactful intervention for raising awareness on sensitive health issues

## Open Questions
