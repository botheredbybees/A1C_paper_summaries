## Interrai Home Care Instrument

The InterRAI Home Care (HCC) instrument has been recognized as a vital tool for assessing and managing the needs of older adults receiving home care services. Its application across multiple European countries highlights its potential to identify best practices in home care delivery, ensuring consistent quality standards irrespective of geographic or cultural differences. The development of a benchmark method using this instrument emphasizes the need for standardized assessment tools that can effectively capture the diverse aspects of home care requirements and facilitate inter-country comparisons. This approach not only enhances understanding but also aids in identifying gaps and promoting improvements in home care services.

## Key Findings

- The InterRAI Home Care Instrument is crucial for assessing older adults' needs in home care settings.
- Standardized assessment tools like the HCC instrument are essential for identifying best practices across different countries.
- Benchmark methods using the HCC can aid in comparing and improving home care quality internationally.
