## Behavioral Therapy

The provided sources emphasize the role of behavioral therapy in treating conditions such as autism. Pivotal Response Treatment (PRT) is highlighted within a Behavioral Therapy framework, which underscores the importance of parent involvement and active engagement from caregivers to enhance treatment efficacy. This method integrates principles of applied behavior analysis but adapts them to be more naturalistic and responsive to the child's motivations and social context.

## Key Findings

- Behavioral therapy includes pivotal response treatments like PRT, which are tailored for autism spectrum disorder.
- Parent involvement is critical in enhancing the effectiveness of behavioral interventions such as PRT.
- Naturalistic teaching methods within behavioral therapies aim to increase motivation and engagement.
