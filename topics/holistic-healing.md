## Holistic Healing

The exploration of holistic healing through the arts as a therapeutic tool highlights its multifaceted nature. From an Australian Aboriginal perspective, the use of cultural sensitivity and traditional practices is paramount in promoting holistic healing, indicating that such interventions are deeply rooted in cultural context. The integration of traditional practices underscores the importance of addressing physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual health simultaneously to achieve true healing.

## Key Findings

- Holistic healing encompasses a comprehensive approach to health and well-being that addresses multiple aspects of an individual's life.
- The use of arts in therapy can serve as a powerful tool for holistic healing when integrated with cultural sensitivity and traditional practices.

## Open Questions

- How can the principles of holistic healing be effectively adapted across different cultural contexts to maintain therapeutic integrity?
