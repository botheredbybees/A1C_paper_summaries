## Arts-Based Strategies for Youth Mental Health

The integration of arts-based strategies into mental health support systems shows significant promise globally. These interventions not only cater to the therapeutic needs of youth but also incorporate local cultural practices, making them more relatable and effective. However, there is a lack of standard core outcome measures which hinders the uniform evaluation of these initiatives across different regions. Despite this limitation, studies affirm that arts-based approaches are instrumental in enhancing mental health outcomes among young people by fostering self-expression, social interaction, and emotional resilience.

## Key Findings

- Arts-based strategies effectively support youth mental health globally.
- Local cultural practices enhance the relevance and effectiveness of these interventions.
- Standard core outcome measures are lacking for evaluating arts-based programs.

## Open Questions

- What standardized core outcomes should be used to evaluate the efficacy of arts-based mental health interventions?
