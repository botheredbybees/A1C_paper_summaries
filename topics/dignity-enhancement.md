## Dignity Enhancement

The concept of dignity enhancement is explored through various contexts, notably in the care and support for individuals with dementia. The theatre setting, as exemplified by 'Brightness in the Theatre,' offers a unique space for enhancing dignity. This intervention seeks to provide an aesthetic experience that fosters identification with characters, thereby promoting a sense of self-worth and respect among participants. Across studies and interventions focused on dignity enhancement, there is a consistent recognition of the importance of engagement through meaningful activities that resonate personally with individuals suffering from dementia. These practices aim to affirm individual identity and foster positive relationships, contributing significantly to quality of life for those affected by cognitive decline.

## Key Findings

- Dignity-enhancing interventions are vital for improving the quality of life among individuals with dementia.
- Engagement in meaningful activities that resonate personally can foster a sense of identification and dignity.
- Theatre-based programs offer a unique setting to promote dignity enhancement through aesthetic experiences.

## Open Questions
