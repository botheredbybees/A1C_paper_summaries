## Rescue Alzheimer Trial

The RESCTUE-Alzheimer trial focuses on the use of music therapy as a treatment for individuals with Alzheimer's disease. Research highlights that creative music-making activities can significantly reduce agitation in patients, enhancing their quality of life and providing relief to caregivers. Multiple sources agree on this intervention's potential benefits but also highlight gaps in understanding long-term efficacy and mechanisms behind these observed improvements.

## Key Findings

- Music therapy is beneficial for reducing agitation in Alzheimer's patients.
- Creative music-making activities contribute positively to the quality of life for individuals with dementia.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects and sustained benefits of music therapy on Alzheimer's symptoms?
