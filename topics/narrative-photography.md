## Narrative Photography

The concept of narrative photography is explored through its ability to convey complex narratives and emotions through visual storytelling. Source 1 emphasizes the role of photographic essays in capturing personal journeys, such as Donna's journey, using a combination of images and text. This approach not only serves an artistic expression but also acts as a coping mechanism for individuals dealing with life challenges. Across these sources, narrative photography is presented both as a therapeutic tool and a means to document and share stories that might otherwise go untold.

## Key Findings

- Narrative photography can serve as a powerful method of personal storytelling and emotional expression.
- Photographic essays often combine images with text to provide deeper context and meaning.
- Using narrative photography can act as a therapeutic tool, offering coping mechanisms for individuals dealing with life challenges.
