## Story Concept Research

The integration of story concept research across the provided sources highlights its significance in modern journalism. Sources emphasize the need for thorough preliminary research to identify engaging stories that resonate with a specific audience, leveraging digital storytelling opportunities effectively. However, there is a tension between identifying impactful story concepts and ensuring journalistic integrity and objectivity are maintained throughout the process.

## Key Findings

- Story concept research should focus on identifying topics relevant and interesting to a target audience
- Digital platforms offer new avenues for engaging audiences with compelling narratives
- Journalistic integrity must be upheld despite the pressure to generate buzzworthy content

## Open Questions

- How can journalists balance innovation in digital storytelling with maintaining traditional journalistic values?
