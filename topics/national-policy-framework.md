## National Arts and Health Policy Framework

The development of a national arts and health policy framework is highlighted as a strategic initiative to integrate arts into healthcare systems across different nations. Sources emphasize the importance of evidence-based practices, stakeholder involvement, and comprehensive frameworks that support both artistic expression and health outcomes. The need for robust collaboration between policymakers, healthcare providers, artists, and community leaders is underlined as crucial for the successful implementation of such policies. However, there are tensions regarding how to effectively measure the impact of arts in health settings and ensuring that policy remains flexible enough to accommodate diverse cultural contexts.

## Key Findings

- The creation of a national arts and health policy framework is essential for integrating arts into healthcare practices.
- Evidence-based practices should be central to the development of such frameworks.
- Stakeholder involvement, including artists, community leaders, and healthcare providers, is critical.

## Open Questions

- How can the impact of arts in health settings be effectively measured and standardized?
