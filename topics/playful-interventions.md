## Playful Interventions

The synthesis of sources highlights the potential of playful interventions to enhance quality of life and cognitive functioning across different stages of life. Sources suggest that incorporating elements like humor therapy into daily routines can positively influence mental health by providing a sense of enjoyment and engagement, thereby increasing resilience against negative outcomes such as frailty in aging populations. The concept of 'cognitive reserve' is emphasized as being enhanced through playful activities which might slow down the cognitive decline process, making it a promising area for further exploration.

## Key Findings

- Humor therapy can improve mental health and cognitive functioning across age groups.
- Cognitive reserve may be increased through engaging in playful and humorous activities.
- Playful interventions offer a versatile approach to managing frailty and promoting well-being.

## Open Questions

- How do different types of playful interventions (e.g., humor therapy, physical play) compare in their effectiveness for enhancing cognitive reserve?
