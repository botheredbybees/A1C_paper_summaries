## Mental Health Issues Among Youth

The 'Youth Survey Report 2025' highlights the prevalence and impact of mental health issues among young people. It identifies financial strain, exacerbated by rising costs of living, as a significant stressor for youth today. School-related pressures further compound these challenges, contributing to an elevated risk of mental health disorders in this demographic. While the report emphasizes the economic burden on families, it also underscores the need for comprehensive support systems and interventions tailored specifically to address the unique needs of young individuals navigating their academic and social worlds amidst financial insecurity.

## Key Findings

- Financial strain due to high cost of living is a major contributor to mental health issues among youth
- School-related stress intensifies existing mental health challenges for young people
- There is an urgent need for targeted interventions and support systems addressing the unique needs of financially stressed youth

## Open Questions

- What are effective strategies for mitigating school-related stress in the context of rising living costs?
