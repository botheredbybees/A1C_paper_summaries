## Multidisciplinary Perspectives on Arts and Refugees

The volume 'Arts and Refugees: Multidisciplinary Perspectives' (Vol. 2) integrates multiple approaches to understanding the role of arts in refugee integration. The work highlights how various disciplines offer distinct yet complementary insights into the challenges faced by refugees and proposes arts-based interventions as a means to foster social cohesion and identity reformation among displaced populations. However, it also underscores the need for alternative aesthetic norms that cater to diverse cultural backgrounds, emphasizing that effective integration strategies must be inclusive of these nuances. Despite the overarching agreement on the importance of multidisciplinary approaches in addressing refugee issues through arts, there is a noted tension regarding the universality and adaptability of art-based interventions across different contexts.

## Key Findings

- Arts-based interventions play a crucial role in facilitating social integration for refugees
- Multidisciplinary perspectives are essential to comprehensively address the challenges faced by refugee populations
- The success of arts programs hinges on their ability to accommodate diverse cultural expressions and norms

## Open Questions

- How can art-based interventions be adapted to effectively serve a wide array of cultural contexts?
