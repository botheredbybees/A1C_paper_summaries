## Fine-Motor Control

The sources collectively highlight the multifaceted role of fine-motor control in enhancing sensory experiences and supporting self-regulation and cognitive abilities. Through various interventions, including creative arts therapies such as play therapy, children can improve their motor skills specifically in the realm of fine motor control, leading to better overall developmental outcomes. However, there is a tension between the extent to which these specific interventions can be generalized across different populations or settings without tailored adaptations.

## Key Findings

- Fine-motor control plays a significant role in sensory experiences and self-regulation.
- Creative arts therapies such as play therapy can enhance fine motor skills.
- Improving fine motor control contributes positively to cognitive abilities.
