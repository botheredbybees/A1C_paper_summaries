## Creative Research Methodologies

The sources provided delve into the methodologies and frameworks utilized in creative research. Source 1 outlines a four-stage framework for conducting rigorous scientific research that emphasizes creativity within its steps, such as hypothesis formulation and data collection. This framework highlights the importance of stakeholder engagement to ensure research relevance and applicability. In contrast, Source 2 discusses more specific applications of creative research methodologies through initiatives like Creative Futures Fund, which supports innovative arts projects, and the Street Dance Survey, an example of how community-driven research can be impactful in understanding local cultural dynamics. Both sources indicate that creative research is not only about the methods themselves but also about engaging stakeholders to make meaningful contributions to society.

## Key Findings

- Creative research methodologies benefit from structured frameworks like the four-stage process mentioned in Source 1, which integrates creativity and scientific rigor.
- Community engagement is crucial for effective creative research as highlighted by both sources through different contexts such as funding initiatives and community surveys.
