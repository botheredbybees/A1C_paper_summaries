## Mindfulness in Play Therapy

The integration of mindfulness into play therapy has emerged as a promising approach for enhancing the therapeutic benefits for children. The use of puppet-assisted techniques can facilitate creativity and conflict resolution, encouraging children to engage with their imagination and emotions more openly. Incorporating mindfulness practices such as the Torrance Test of Creative Thinking helps in assessing and fostering the child's innate strengths and creative abilities. However, there is a need for further research on how specific mindfulness interventions interact within the broader context of play therapy.

## Key Findings

- Mindfulness techniques enhance conflict resolution skills and creativity in children.
- Puppet-assisted therapy can be an effective method to integrate mindfulness practices.
- The Torrance Test of Creative Thinking is a useful tool for assessing creative abilities.

## Open Questions

- What are the specific mechanisms through which mindfulness integrates into play therapy to improve outcomes?
