## Self-Care Strategies

The sources explore different self-care strategies that individuals can adopt to enhance their emotional well-being and creativity. Source 1 emphasizes the importance of making space for one's own creativity as a form of self-care, suggesting that engaging in creative activities allows for better emotional processing and empowerment through creativity. This strategy focuses on the psychological aspect of self-care by encouraging personal expression and reflection. In contrast, Source 2 introduces the use of therapeutic puppets, shadow theatre, and marionette performance as more interactive and theatrical forms of self-expression and care. These methods not only provide a physical platform for emotional exploration but also offer a communal aspect where individuals can share their stories through puppetry, potentially fostering greater social connections and support networks.

## Key Findings

- Creative activities such as making space for one's own creativity and using therapeutic puppets serve as effective self-care strategies.
- Emotional processing is enhanced when engaging in creative or theatrical activities that allow for personal expression.

## Open Questions

- How do the communal aspects of puppetry-based interventions compare to more solitary forms of creative self-care?
