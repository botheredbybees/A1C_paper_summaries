## Drama Workshops

The integration of drama workshops as a therapeutic intervention for health and wellbeing emerges from several sources. These workshops utilize performance arts to foster emotional expression and social interaction, enhancing mental and physical health among participants. Shared reading groups and creative writing are also employed alongside drama workshops to provide multi-faceted support systems that cater to diverse individual needs within a group setting. However, the effectiveness of these interventions varies based on factors such as participant engagement, facilitator expertise, and the specific therapeutic goals set for each session.

## Key Findings

- Drama workshops are effective in enhancing mental health through emotional expression and social interaction.
- Combining drama with other arts therapies like shared reading and creative writing provides a comprehensive approach to well-being.
- The success of drama interventions is highly dependent on the facilitator's ability to engage participants effectively.
