## Reminiscence Therapy

The synthesis integrates the use of technology and music in reminiscence therapy to enhance engagement and recall for individuals with dementia. While Source 1 explores how technological tools like computers, mobile phones, cameras, and audio recorders can foster creativity and self-expression among those living with dementia, Source 2 highlights the therapeutic potential of music in evoking vivid memories and emotional responses. Both sources underscore the importance of personal connection through familiar objects or melodies, demonstrating that technology can serve as a bridge to past experiences when used creatively or musically. However, there is a noted gap in how these methods might be effectively integrated into broader healthcare practices.

## Key Findings

- Technology and music are potent tools for reminiscence therapy, enhancing engagement and emotional well-being among individuals with dementia.
- Familiar technological devices and musical melodies can evoke vivid memories and encourage creative expression.
- Personal connection through familiar objects or sounds serves as a crucial element in therapeutic interventions for dementia care.

## Open Questions

- How can the integration of technology and music be optimized to improve cognitive function and emotional well-being within broader healthcare practices?
