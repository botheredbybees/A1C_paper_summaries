## Policy Recommendations for Creative Health

The 'Creative Health Review Report' highlights the importance of integrating creative activities into public health policies to promote holistic improvements and preventive healthcare approaches. The report underscores how supporting creativity can enhance mental health, social cohesion, and overall wellbeing in diverse populations. However, it also acknowledges challenges such as limited evidence on long-term impacts and the need for tailored initiatives that address specific community needs.

## Key Findings

- Supporting creative activities through policy has potential benefits for public health, including mental health improvements and enhanced social connections.
- There is a need to tailor policy recommendations to fit diverse community contexts and specific cultural requirements.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts of integrating creative practices into public health policies?
