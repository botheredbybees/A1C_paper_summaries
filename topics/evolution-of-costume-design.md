## Evolution of Costume Design

The history of Punch and Judy shows a rich tapestry of costume design evolution intertwined with social commentary. This puppet show's subversive nature has been maintained throughout its transformations, reflecting societal changes while serving as a vehicle for critique. The integration of evolving costumes not only enhances the visual impact but also deepens the narrative layers to address contemporary issues. Over time, costume design in Punch and Judy shifted from traditional attire to more dynamic and expressive outfits that align with modern sensibilities, thereby broadening its appeal across generations.

## Key Findings

- Costume design in Punch and Judy has evolved to reflect societal changes and maintain relevance over time.
- Subversive elements remain integral to the show through evolving costume designs.
- The integration of costumes into social commentary enhances the narrative's impact.

## Open Questions

- How do contemporary costume designs contribute to the perpetuation or adaptation of Punch and Judy's subversive nature?
