## Neuroendocrinological Effects of Pubertal Hormones

The sources collectively elucidate the profound impact of pubertal hormones on brain development and behavior. They highlight that during puberty, hormonal changes play a crucial role in organizing neural circuits that influence adolescent behavior and cognitive development. These organizational effects are critical for understanding how sexual maturation influences psychological and social behaviors. However, there is variability across studies regarding the extent to which specific hormones contribute differentially to these processes.

## Key Findings

- Pubertal hormones have significant organizational effects on the brain during adolescence.
- These neuroendocrinological changes influence adolescent behavior and cognitive development.

## Open Questions

- What are the differential contributions of specific pubertal hormones in organizing neural circuits?
