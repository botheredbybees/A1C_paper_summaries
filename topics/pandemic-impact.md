## Impact Of Pandemic On Artistic Wellbeing

The sources collectively highlight the profound effect of the pandemic on artistic practices and creative well-being. The shift towards digital platforms was evident as traditional arts engagement faced disruptions. Despite these challenges, there were instances where community resilience manifested through increased online participation in creative activities, indicating a transformative phase for arts consumption and production. However, the overall trend suggests significant strain on artists' livelihoods and mental health due to reduced opportunities and financial instability.

## Key Findings

- The pandemic significantly disrupted traditional forms of artistic engagement.
- Shift towards digital platforms has been necessary but not always sufficient in maintaining levels of creative activity and community interaction.
- Resilience was observed among communities who adapted through online channels, suggesting a positive transformation within the arts ecosystem.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term impacts on artists' livelihoods and mental health due to the pandemic-induced changes?
