## Resident Well-being Through Personalized Music Therapy

The synthesis of findings from the Wisconsin Music & Memory Program implementation survey reveals a multifaceted impact on resident well-being through personalized music therapy. Sources underscore that this intervention enhances emotional, cognitive, and social aspects of care for elderly residents. Personalized music playlists tailored to each individual's preferences can alleviate feelings of isolation, depression, and agitation, fostering a sense of identity and connection. However, there are noted challenges in implementation, including the need for ongoing staff training and the integration of technology into daily routines.

## Key Findings

- Personalized music therapy significantly improves emotional well-being among elderly residents.
- Cognitive function may be positively influenced by engaging with personalized musical content.
- Integration of music-based interventions requires sustained support from care providers.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term effects of personalized music therapy on overall resident health and longevity?
