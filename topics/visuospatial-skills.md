## Visuospatial Skills

The impact of Alzheimer's disease on visuospatial skills is a significant area of research. The case study highlights how an individual with Alzheimer's experiences changes in artistic expression, which can be attributed to the decline in visuospatial abilities often observed in patients with this condition. This work underscores that cognitive impairments in Alzheimer's specifically affect areas such as visual perception and spatial awareness, leading to alterations in artistic output that reflect these deficits.

## Key Findings

- Alzheimer's disease leads to a decline in visuospatial skills.
- Artistic expression can be an indicator of changes in visuospatial abilities due to Alzheimer's.

## Open Questions

- What specific aspects of visuospatial skills are most affected by Alzheimer's and how do they manifest in various activities?
