## Visual Complementarity

The concept of visual complementarity is explored through the lens of editing and selection for photo essays, highlighting its importance in creative expression and reflective practice. Sources discuss how this method involves selecting images that work together to create a cohesive narrative or theme, enhancing each other's impact beyond what they could achieve individually. The role of uncensored writing as part of the reflective process is also mentioned, suggesting that deeper understanding emerges from examining the visual choices made in photo essays. However, there are gaps in how these methods can be applied across diverse creative mediums and contexts.

## Key Findings

- Visual complementarity is a method used to enhance narrative coherence through image selection in photo essays.
- Uncensored reflective writing aids in understanding and critiquing visual choices in photo essay creation.

## Open Questions

- How can the principles of visual complementarity be effectively applied across different creative mediums beyond photography?
