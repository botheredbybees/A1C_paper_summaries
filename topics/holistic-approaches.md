## Holistic Approaches

The concept of holistic approaches in health and wellness emphasizes the integration of various forms of expression, such as artistic practices, into therapeutic frameworks. The National Arts and Health Framework highlights collaborative relationships between artists and healthcare providers to enhance patient care through a multi-faceted approach that considers not only physical ailments but also psychological and social factors. This framework underscores the importance of holistic interventions in improving overall health outcomes by addressing the whole person rather than isolated symptoms or conditions.

## Key Findings

- Holistic approaches integrate various forms of expression, such as artistic practices, into therapeutic frameworks.
- Collaborative relationships between artists and healthcare providers are crucial for effective implementation of holistic therapies.
- Holistic interventions aim to improve overall health outcomes by addressing multiple aspects of a person's well-being.
