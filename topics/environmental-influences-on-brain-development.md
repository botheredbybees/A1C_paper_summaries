## Environmental Influences on Brain Development

The sources collectively emphasize the critical role of environment in shaping brain development and function. The concept of neuroplasticity underscores how experiences, including creative arts and play, can alter neural pathways throughout life. Sources highlight that these environmental factors are pivotal not only for childhood development but also for maintaining mental wellbeing across different stages of life.

## Key Findings

- Neuroplasticity allows the brain to change and adapt in response to various environmental inputs.
- Creative arts and play contribute significantly to positive neurodevelopmental outcomes.
- Environmental factors are crucial for both initial brain development and lifelong mental health.

## Open Questions

- What specific aspects of creative arts and play environments most effectively promote neuroplasticity?
