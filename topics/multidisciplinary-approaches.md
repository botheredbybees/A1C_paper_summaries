## Multidisciplinary Approaches in International Migration Studies

The integration of multidisciplinary methodologies, including those from the humanities and social sciences, is increasingly recognized as essential for understanding international migration. Sources emphasize the importance of visual studies and participatory arts-based research within this framework to capture nuanced cultural dimensions of migration phenomena. While these approaches enrich our understanding by incorporating diverse perspectives and methods, they also present challenges in terms of interdisciplinarity and methodological rigor.

## Key Findings

- Multidisciplinary approaches are vital for comprehensively studying international migration.
- Visual studies provide unique insights into the cultural dynamics of migration.
- Participatory arts-based research facilitates engagement with migrant communities.

## Open Questions

- How can interdisciplinary research methods be standardized to ensure methodological rigor?
