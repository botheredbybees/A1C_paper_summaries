## Arts for Health

The integration of arts into health interventions in community settings offers a promising framework to enhance social engagement and cultural development. Both sources highlight the importance of creative practices, such as storytelling, which can serve as powerful tools for healing and personal growth. Additionally, there is an emphasis on developing robust evaluation frameworks that employ theories of change and logic models to effectively measure outcomes. However, while these approaches underscore the potential benefits of arts in health settings, they also reveal challenges in systematically demonstrating their efficacy.

## Key Findings

- Creative arts practices can significantly enhance social engagement and cultural development in communities
- Evaluation frameworks utilizing theories of change and logic models are crucial for assessing the impact of arts-based interventions
- Creative storytelling is identified as a promising practice within arts-for-health initiatives

## Open Questions

- How to systematically demonstrate and measure the long-term efficacy of creative arts practices in health settings?
