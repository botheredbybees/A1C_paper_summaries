## Informed Consent

The concept of informed consent is central to the discussion on research ethics in both arts and health interventions as well as general research methodologies. Both sources emphasize the necessity of ethical considerations and adherence to professional conduct principles, highlighting that these factors are integral to ensuring the integrity of the research process. However, while source 1 delves specifically into the context of arts and health, source 2 provides a broader perspective on research ethics, touching upon the importance of informed consent as a principle of ethical scrutiny in all forms of research. This integration shows a common ground between specialized fields and general methodologies regarding the crucial role of informed consent.

## Key Findings

- Informed consent is a critical component of both arts and health intervention studies and broader research ethics principles.
- Professional conduct principles are essential for maintaining ethical standards in research, including the process of obtaining informed consent.
