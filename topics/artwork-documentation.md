## Artwork Documentation Practices

The provided sources highlight the importance of consistent and controlled environments for capturing accurate visual records of artwork. Both texts emphasize the use of consistent light sources to ensure true color representation, while also detailing specific camera settings and lighting techniques to enhance documentation quality. Safety considerations are a recurrent theme, with both sources offering guidelines on safe handling practices to protect art pieces during the documentation process. The integration of these elements underscores the methodical nature of artwork documentation, combining technical aspects such as equipment calibration and environmental control with practical concerns for art preservation.

## Key Findings

- Consistent light sources are essential for accurate color representation in artwork documentation.
- DSLR camera settings and lighting techniques significantly impact the quality of visual records.
- Safe handling practices are crucial to prevent damage during the documentation process.
