## Emotional Expression Through Puppetry

The integration of puppetry as a medium for emotional expression highlights its utility in non-verbal communication and therapeutic settings. Across the source material, puppetry is recognized not only as a creative outlet but also as an effective tool to mediate complex emotions in individuals who may struggle with verbal articulation. The use of puppets serves as a bridge between internal feelings and external expression, offering participants a safe and sustainable way to communicate their emotional states without direct confrontation or the pressure of immediate verbal response.

## Key Findings

- Puppetry facilitates non-verbal communication by providing an indirect means of expressing emotions.
- Mediated expression through puppetry can be particularly beneficial for individuals with speech difficulties or anxiety-related conditions.
- Sustainable practices in emotional therapy are enhanced when mediated tools like puppetry offer long-term engagement opportunities.
