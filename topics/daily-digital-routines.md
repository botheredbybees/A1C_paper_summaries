## Daily Digital Routines

The integration of daily digital routines as an intervention for self-care and well-being is a multifaceted practice that leverages technology to enhance personal health. Sources indicate the significant role of such routines in fostering relational well-being, suggesting that these activities are not merely individualistic but also contribute to broader social connections and support networks. However, there exists variability in how different populations engage with daily digital practices, leading to questions about the effectiveness and accessibility of such interventions across diverse groups.

## Key Findings

- Daily digital routines can serve as a form of self-care by enhancing personal well-being through technology
- The impact of these routines on relational well-being highlights their potential beyond individual benefits
- Variability in engagement with daily digital practices suggests the need for tailored approaches

## Open Questions

- How do cultural and socio-economic factors influence the effectiveness of daily digital routines as a self-care practice?
