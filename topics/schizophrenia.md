## Creative Puppet Therapy

Creative Puppet Therapy has emerged as a potential intervention for managing hallucinations in patients with schizophrenia. This therapy is innovative and non-traditional, utilizing puppets to engage individuals in the process of expressing their experiences related to auditory and visual hallucinations. Studies suggest that this form of expressive arts therapy may help reduce the intensity and frequency of these symptoms. However, further research is needed to understand its long-term efficacy and how it compares with other established treatments for schizophrenia.

## Key Findings

- Creative Puppet Therapy shows promise in reducing hallucinations in patients diagnosed with schizophrenia.
- The use of non-traditional therapeutic methods like puppetry can provide new avenues for symptom management.
- Expressive arts therapies are gaining recognition as viable options alongside traditional pharmacological approaches.

## Open Questions

- What is the long-term efficacy and sustainability of Creative Puppet Therapy in managing hallucinations?
