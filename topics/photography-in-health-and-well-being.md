## Photography In Health And Well-being

The integration of photography as an intervention for health and well-being is highlighted across the sources. The practice of mindfulness through a photo walk, as mentioned in Source 1, suggests that photography can serve not only as an artistic expression but also as a therapeutic tool promoting mindfulness and self-reflection. This aligns with broader theories proposing that creative practices can enhance psychological resilience and emotional regulation by fostering connection between individuals and their environment.

## Key Findings

- Photography can be used as an intervention for enhancing mental health.
- Mindfulness is integrated into photography practice to foster well-being.
- Creative practices like photography contribute to stress reduction and emotional expression.
