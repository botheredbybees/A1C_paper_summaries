## National Arts Participation Survey

The National Arts Participation Survey provides a comprehensive framework for understanding the demographic segmentation of arts audiences in Australia. This survey uses behavioral and attitudinal indices to categorize different population segments based on their engagement with the arts, including frequency of attendance at cultural events and personal interest levels. The Population Segmentation Model derived from this data serves as an advocacy tool for arts organizations by identifying key audience groups that can be targeted through tailored marketing strategies and community outreach programs.

## Key Findings

- The National Arts Participation Survey employs a detailed segmentation model to categorize diverse populations based on their interaction with the arts.
- Behavioral and attitudinal indices are crucial in defining distinct segments within the broader population of arts attendees.

## Open Questions
