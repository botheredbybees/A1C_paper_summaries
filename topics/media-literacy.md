## Media Literacy

The concept of media literacy is explored across various sources, emphasizing its role in equipping individuals with the skills to critically analyze and engage with diverse forms of media. It is often intertwined with creative expression as a means for individuals to not only consume but also produce content that reflects societal issues and personal perspectives. While media literacy is seen as crucial in navigating today's complex media landscape, there are varying approaches and frameworks discussed, leading to different emphases on the skills and knowledge required.

## Key Findings

- Media literacy enhances critical thinking about media consumption
- Creative expression complements media literacy by encouraging content creation
