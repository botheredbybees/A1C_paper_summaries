## Educational Storytelling

The synthesis of the sources on educational storytelling reveals a cohesive narrative around integrating local arts and culture into educational curricula to enhance learning experiences. The intervention involves embedding stories that are culturally relevant, thereby making education more relatable and engaging for students. A significant finding is the emphasis placed on evaluating these interventions not just through traditional academic metrics but by considering their impact on cultural literacy and student engagement. This approach acknowledges the importance of storytelling in preserving and transmitting local knowledge and culture. However, there is a tension between ensuring that educational content remains culturally specific versus being universally applicable, which poses challenges for scalability and generalizability.

## Key Findings

- Educational interventions that incorporate cultural stories enhance student engagement and learning outcomes.
- Evaluation of such programs should consider both academic performance and cultural literacy improvement.
- Cultural storytelling in education helps preserve local knowledge and traditions.

## Open Questions

- How to balance the specificity required for effective educational storytelling with the need for broader applicability?
