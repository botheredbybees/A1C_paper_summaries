## Culturally Sensitive Puppetry

The integration of therapeutic puppet play and puppet therapy highlights the profound impact these interventions can have on adults by fostering a sense of childlike wonder. Key to this approach is the facilitation of dialogue through culturally sensitive methods that resonate with diverse populations, aiming to engage participants in meaningful self-reflection and emotional exploration. Across various sources, there is an emphasis on how puppetry as therapy aids in creating safe spaces for expression and understanding complex emotions. However, a tension arises regarding the challenge of maintaining cultural sensitivity while ensuring broad applicability of these techniques across different communities.

## Key Findings

- Puppet therapy facilitates emotional engagement through the use of childlike wonder
- Culturally sensitive puppetry enhances dialogue facilitation in therapeutic settings
- Safe spaces for expression are created through puppet-based interventions

## Open Questions

- How can cultural sensitivity be maintained while ensuring broad applicability of puppet therapy techniques?
