## Personal Expression Through Crafting

The sources highlight the therapeutic and expressive potential of crafting activities like ceramics and knitting. Tactile experiences with materials such as clay offer a unique medium for personal expression, allowing individuals to channel their emotions into tangible forms. This process can serve not only as an outlet but also as a means of communication, enabling creators to articulate feelings that may be difficult to express verbally.

## Key Findings

- Crafting activities provide a tactile and sensory experience that facilitates emotional expression.
- Activities like ceramics offer individuals opportunities for creative self-expression through the manipulation of materials.

## Open Questions
