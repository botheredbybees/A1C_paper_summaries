## Theatre of Wandering

The Theatre of Wandering intervention emphasizes the importance of care-participation and collaborative performance to amplify underrepresented voices. Sources highlight its role in creating inclusive spaces for marginalized communities through devising performances that reflect their experiences. However, there is a tension between the theoretical ideals of inclusivity and practical challenges faced during implementation.

## Key Findings

- Theatre of Wandering fosters care-participation by encouraging active involvement from all participants
- Collaborative performance is central to the Theatre of Wandering approach, enabling diverse perspectives

## Open Questions

- How can Theatre of Wandering overcome barriers to inclusivity in practice
