## Social Benefits of Art and Music

The social benefits derived from art and music interventions highlight their multifaceted impact on communities. 'Making Art With Communities' focuses on inclusivity, cultural sensitivity, and the intrinsic social benefits that arise when artists engage constructively with diverse community groups. Conversely, 'A Music and Health Perspective On Music's Perceived Goodness' delves into the intrinsic value of music and its perceived goodness, suggesting a tension between the commodification of music and its genuine societal contributions. While both sources underscore the positive impacts on communities through art and music, they also highlight potential challenges in maintaining these benefits as practices evolve.

## Key Findings

- Art and music interventions can foster inclusivity and cultural sensitivity within diverse communities.
- The intrinsic value of music plays a crucial role in enhancing social well-being beyond mere economic gains.

## Open Questions

- How can the intrinsic values of art and music be maintained while resisting their commodification?
