## Caregiver Distress Scale

The Caregiver Distress Scale is integral in evaluating the efficacy of interventions designed to alleviate stress and improve quality of life for caregivers. A study focusing on personalized music interventions, as seen in the Music and Memories iPod project in Eau Claire, utilized this scale to measure the impact on caregiver well-being. The results indicate that such interventions can significantly reduce distress levels among caregivers by providing tailored musical experiences that enhance emotional and psychological comfort. This aligns with broader research highlighting the role of personalized therapeutic approaches in mitigating stress for those in caregiving roles.

## Key Findings

- The Caregiver Distress Scale is a critical tool in assessing interventions aimed at reducing caregiver stress.
- Personalized music interventions, as exemplified by the Music and Memories iPod project, show promise in improving quality of life for caregivers through emotional comfort provided by tailored musical experiences.
- Tailored therapeutic approaches are effective in alleviating distress among caregivers.
