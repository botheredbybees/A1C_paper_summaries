## Foundational Learning Abilities

The concept of foundational learning abilities during infancy and the early years emphasizes the importance of providing children with safe and stimulating environments to foster developmental milestones such as tummy time. Sources highlight that engaging in activities like tummy time are crucial for motor skill development, but there is a need to understand how these activities can be tailored to meet individual child needs while ensuring safety.

## Key Findings

- Foundational learning abilities during infancy and early years significantly impact later developmental milestones.
- Activities such as tummy time are critical for motor skill development in infants.
- Creating safe yet stimulating environments supports the growth of foundational learning abilities.

## Open Questions

- How can foundational learning activities be individualized to cater to unique needs while maintaining safety standards?
