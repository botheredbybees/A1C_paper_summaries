## Stress Management Techniques

The sources highlight various approaches to managing adolescent stress, emphasizing the importance of parental support and emotional resilience. Effective strategies include fostering a supportive environment through balanced parenting and incorporating specific stress management techniques like mindfulness and physical activity. While the sources agree on the foundational role of parental involvement, they also reveal gaps in identifying comprehensive stress reduction methods tailored specifically for teens.

## Key Findings

- Parental support is crucial for adolescent mental health.
- Mindfulness practices are effective stress management tools.
- Balanced parenting contributes to emotional resilience in teenagers.
