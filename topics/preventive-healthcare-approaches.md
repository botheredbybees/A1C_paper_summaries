## Preventive Healthcare Approaches

The review report emphasizes the importance of creative and integrative approaches in preventive healthcare. These strategies aim to improve overall health by addressing multiple factors that influence well-being, such as social, economic, and environmental determinants. The document highlights policy recommendations that underscore the necessity for comprehensive interventions which go beyond traditional medical practices to incorporate lifestyle modifications and community support systems.

## Key Findings

- Preventive healthcare approaches benefit from a holistic perspective that considers various factors impacting health
- Effective preventive strategies often require creative solutions and integrative frameworks
- Policy recommendations are crucial in supporting the implementation of preventive healthcare measures
