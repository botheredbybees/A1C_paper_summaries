## Make Arts Process

The 'Make Arts for a Better Life' process emphasizes the recovery of cultural identity and community well-being through creative arts practices. This intervention integrates local cultural traditions and personal experiences to foster collective healing and resilience, thereby highlighting the transformative power of art in addressing societal issues. Across various communities that have implemented this approach, there is evidence supporting its effectiveness in enhancing mental health outcomes, social cohesion, and individual self-expression. However, challenges such as sustainability of the program over time and scalability across diverse cultural contexts remain.

## Key Findings

- The Make Arts process enhances community resilience through collective art-making.
- Integration of local cultural traditions is crucial for effective implementation.
- Improvements in mental health outcomes are observed among participants.

## Open Questions

- What are the long-term sustainability strategies for sustaining the Make Arts program?
